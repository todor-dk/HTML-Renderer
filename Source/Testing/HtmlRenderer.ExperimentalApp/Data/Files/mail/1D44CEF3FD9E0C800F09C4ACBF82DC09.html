Mail

The email platform, powered by Google, works in a similar manner to the Google email application many of you use for personal email. It includes Google Contacts, Tasks, and Chat – and with unlimited personal storage, plenty of room to save the information you rely on. 

Note: When forwarding mail, use MCommunity instead of Gmail.
