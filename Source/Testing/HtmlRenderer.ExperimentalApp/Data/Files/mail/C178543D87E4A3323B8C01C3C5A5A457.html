MTSU Email Login Information

 

How do I login for the first time? 

If you are an active student (have registered for at least one class in the last year and upcoming semester), you must:

  • Go the Password Recovery Tool to change your password – Make sure you have registered a personal email in PipelineMT.
  • On the Manage Your Username and Password page, check the box for MTMail, and leave the Pipeline box checked if you want it to be the same password. 
  • Wait 15 minutes after you have changed your password.
  • Log in to MTMail (Office 365).

IMPORTANT

  • All Official University correspondence is being sent to your MTMail account.
  • MTMail F.A.Q.

 

 

 

  • Exchange is MTSU's e-mail service for faculty and staff
  • Faculty/Staff Exchange Login Page
  • Exchange Project FAQ

 

Note: MTSU will NEVER ask for your account information by e-mail. If you receive such a message, please report it to ITD Help Desk @ 898-5345. NEVER reply to any e-mail asking for your account information or other personal details. For more information, contact ITD Help Desk @ 898-5345.
