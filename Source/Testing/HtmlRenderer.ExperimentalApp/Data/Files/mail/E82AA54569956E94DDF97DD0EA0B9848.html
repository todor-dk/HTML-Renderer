Umail

Umail is a partnership between IU and Google that provides all students with access to email hosted by Gmail and other Google Apps such as Alerts, Calendar, Drive, Scholar, and Sites.

Log in


Note: University employees may NOT use this account for university business.

Need to take advantage of Umail's advanced features? Be sure to set a password on your account.
