G Suite for Education is the University's official student email system and offers most of the same tools and features as a personal Google account. G Suite for Education is also available to all faculty and staff to collaborate with students in their courses and departments.

 Students and Alumni

Click here for more information
G Suite for Education is the official student email system at the Northern Arizona University. G Suite gives you access to many exciting features that can be used to better facilitate your educational experience at NAU. Some of these apps include a fully featured, web-based office suite, a robust calendaring system, and a website development toolset. To make collaborating with your classmates easier, you can also request a Google Group.

You will need your user ID and password in order to log into your NAU Google account. If you are a current NAU student, faculty, or staff member or have received a grade in at least one NAU course, you are eligible for an NAU Google account (including NAU Gmail). If you have ever had access to an NAU Google account, you can log into it after retrieving your NAU user ID and password.

If you are an NAU alumnus and do not already have NAU Google account, you can request one by filling out the form here and then contacting the Student Tech Center once your credentials are set up. Keep in mind that if you attended NAU between 2009 and today, you will already have an NAU Google account.

 Faculty and Staff

Click here for more information

Information Technology Services supports G Suite for Education (formerly Google Apps for Education) for NAU faculty and staff.. This useful tool supplements our enterprise Exchange email and provides extensive collaborative features. Use of G Suite for Education for NAU business must be performed through an NAU Google account.

Because of the collaborative features available, there is a potential for improperly providing access to sensitive or proprietary NAU data. Account holders are responsible for setting appropriate access.

To request an NAU Google account, please visit this page.

The full policy, including limitations on support for some services, can be found at this page.

Please contact the Solution Center at (928) 523-1511 for G Suite for Education assistance.
