The Daily Mail

Profile:
This is a pseudo-label which groups together all releases given away free with the British newspaper, "The Daily Mail". The newspaper is published by Associated Newspapers.

Do NOT confuse with the Sunday edition, The Mail On Sunday, or its Irish counterpart editions, The Irish Daily Mail / The Irish Mail On Sunday.

Some releases with no visible catalog# can instead use the number on the matrix ring that may appear in this format: ##-##-##/## (eg. 23-4-04/02).
Sublabels
The Irish Daily Mail, The Irish Mail On Sunday, The Mail On Sunday
Parent Label:
Associated Newspapers
Sites:
dailymail.co.uk, Wikipedia, associatednewspapers.com
