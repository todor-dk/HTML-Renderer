Saving Money with Daily Mail e-Gift Cards

The Daily Mail provide e-Gift Cards which can be spent at the Mail Shop, however they cannot be purchased for cash. This is how to obtain a www.dailymail.co.uk e-Gift Card:

  1. Purchase the Daily Mail and locate the unique code on the back of the paper.
  2. Log on to your Mail Shop account or register as a new user.
  3. Click the yellow “Enter Unique Number” button on your homepage.
  4. Type in the number.
  5. You will earn 20 Mail Points for a weekday code, 75 for a Saturday code and 100 for the Sunday code.
  6. To buy a £5 Mail Shop e-Gift Voucher you will need 2000 points.
  7. For a £10 Mail Shop e-Gift voucher it’s 3900 points.
  8. For a £25 Mail Shop e-Gift Voucher it’s 9500 points.
  9. When you have the correct number of points, navigate to the Rewards page.
  10. Click the e-Gift Voucher you want and press "Redeem".
  11. The voucher will be e-mailed to you within 24 hours.

Spending a Daily Mail Gift Voucher

You can redeem Daily Mail e-Gift vouchers in their online Mail Shop, this is how to start:

  1. Log on to your account.
  2. Add the items you want to your basket.
  3. Click "View Basket" and check the summary.
  4. Enter your e-Gift Voucher code in the space provided.
  5. The amount will be debited from your balance.
  6. Click through to the Secure Checkout and pay any remaining costs.

Redeeming a Daily Mail Discount Voucher

Daily Mail discount vouchers printed in the paper can be redeemed instore by taking the voucher with you and presenting it at the till. For online vouchers, this is how to spend them:

  1. Log on to your account and visit the My Coupons section.
  2. Select the coupons that you’d like to use.
  3. Click the printer icon to print them out together.
  4. Cut them out and present them at the supermarket.
