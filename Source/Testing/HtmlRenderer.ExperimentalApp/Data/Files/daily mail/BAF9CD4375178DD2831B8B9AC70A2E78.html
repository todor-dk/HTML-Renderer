Worth reading in what way?

It's worth reading:
  • As a way to discover propaganda techniques that would make Goebbels proud.
  • To discover how xenophobic little-Englanders think.
  • As a kind of time travel back to the attitudes of the nineteen fifties.
  • As a reverse fact check (i.e. if it's in the Mail it's probably not true).
  • If you want to discover the latest unsubstantiated rumours about what causes cancer.
  • If you are a racist, a sexist, a homophobe, a climate change denier, xenophobic or  islamophobic, you want your prejudices confirmed and you aren't to fussy about how.

It's not worth reading if:
  • You have any respect for the truth.
