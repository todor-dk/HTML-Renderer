Breaking the Silence

Bladder Sensitivity seems to be one of the last taboos in our society meaning many women feel too embarrassed to confide in their friends, partners or even their GP, leaving them feeling isolated and alone. Recent research found that 3 out of 4 women who experience frequent bladder leaks have not spoken to a health professional about their condition. This means they are not getting the help and advice they need. Always Discreet is on a mission to break the silence around AI so that women can feel empowered to live the age they feel inside with nothing holding them back.
