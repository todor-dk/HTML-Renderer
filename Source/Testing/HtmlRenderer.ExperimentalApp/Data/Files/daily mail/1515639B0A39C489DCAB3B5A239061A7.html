Daily Mail: 0843 504 0513

You can contact the Daily Mail via their parent company’s head office switchboard: 0843 504 0513 / 020 7938 6000. This is the number for Associated Newspapers Ltd which owns the MailOnline, the Daily Mail and the Mail on Sunday.

The Daily Mail is the UK’s second most popular daily tabloid newspaper after The Sun. Its stance on both economic and social issues tend towards right-wing conservatism.

With a varied history spanning over a century, the Daily Mail is strongly popular across the UK. Its present readers are diverse: it is read widely by women across all age groups, and has adopted its output and staff to cater to perceived feminine desires. As such the paper publishes much lifestyle, fashion and columnist content aimed at satisfying this demographic.

Under the command of its editor Paul Dacre, the Daily Mail’s place as an enduringly popular daily publication is firm in spite of a general shrink in the newspaper market.
