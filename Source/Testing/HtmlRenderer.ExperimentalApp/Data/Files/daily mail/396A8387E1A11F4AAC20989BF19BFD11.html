Daily Mail Free Hotel Promotion

The West End's waiting for you in 2015, and we are here to help you hit it in style, with exceptional package offers that include a free London hotel stay when you buy top-price theatre tickets from just £50 per person†.

With more than a dozen new and classic world-class productions on offer, your only problem is deciding which one to choose. Just book your tickets and get an overnight stay with continental breakfast in a 3* hotel – absolutely free. That's all there is to it.

If you prefer, you can treat yourself to an upgrade to a 4* hotel for just £25.00 per person or 5* hotel for just £50.00 per person†. Make sure you book your train with us too as you can also save up to 40% on your tickets.

For a list of participating shows please see below or click here (PDF).
