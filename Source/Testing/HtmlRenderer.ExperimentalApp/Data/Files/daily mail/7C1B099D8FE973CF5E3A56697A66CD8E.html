Daily Mail Newspaper

Daily Mail Epaper

The Daily Mail epaper is a British national daily middle market tabloid newspaper published from United Kingdom. It was first published on 4 May 1896 by Alfred Harmsworth and his brother Harold Harmsworth. It is owned by the Daily Mail and General Trust.  Daily Mail second largest selling daily newspaper after The Sun. It has sister paper The Mail on Sunday was launched in 1982. It editions are publishing Scottish and Irish. It was the first British newspaper to sell a million copies a day. It has daily circulation 1,548,349.
