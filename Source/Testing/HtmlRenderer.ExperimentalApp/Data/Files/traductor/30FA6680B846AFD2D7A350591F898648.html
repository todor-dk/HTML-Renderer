Beca #Traductor

Sponsor: SpanishDict

Submission Deadline

August 31, 2016

Field(s) of Study

Unrestricted

Award Amount

$1,000 USD

Description

SpanishDict.com, the world's largest Spanish-English dictionary, translation, and language learning website, is hosting an inaugural #Traductor scholarship to give students the chance to win $1,000. The scholarship competition is open now through Wednesday, August 31, 2016 on www.spanishdict.com/traductor.

Other Criteria

Geared towards English or Spanish learners. The application is open to all students from around the world, 13 and older. Students can simply navigate to www.spanishdict.com/traductor, and fill out the form, with a short essay prompt describing a time when they had to translate words from Spanish to English or English to Spanish. The winner will receive $1,000 to help cover education expenses.
