With Direct Deposit, your money will automatically be added to your card on payday. You will no longer receive a paper check. That means no more check cashing fees or trips to the bank. To enroll, just give your direct deposit and bank routing numbers to your payroll or benefits provider. Many times, your employer or benefits provider will notify our bank of your incoming deposit in advance of your actual payday. If they do, we will credit your pay or benefits to your card when they give us that advance notice instead of waiting for pay day. This way, you can have access to your money sooner! (Enrollment in Direct Deposit is subject to your Cardholder Agreement. Other fees apply. See fee plan for details).
