Walmart Black Friday 2017 Ads and Deals

Walmart is the most anticipated Black Friday ad every year – and for a good reason. With a variety of mind-blowing deals and discounts both online and in stores, Walmart is your one stop shop for the Biggest Shopping Day of the Year.

Not only does Walmart deliver incredible Black Friday savings, it also offers amazing pre-Black Friday events as well as deep discounts during Cyber Week. While the retailer discounts different products during each holiday shopping season, there are some trends that you can expect to see year after year.

Walmart Black Friday Deals

Walmart is famous for offering Black Friday deals and doorbusters that make customers line up outside of the stores hours – and sometimes even days – before the shopping extravaganza. The amount of people who visit Walmart stores for Black Friday is unbelievable. In fact, during a four hour period on Thanksgiving night in 2014, Walmart saw 22 million shoppers in their stores. That’s more than the amount of people who visit Disneyland in one year! Walmart online shopping is equally popular on Black Friday, with the store always appearing in the list of the top five most popular online shopping destinations for the Biggest Shopping Day of the Year.

Walmart Savings Catcher and Price Matching

Since 2010, Walmart has been a leader in price matching. If you find a lower price at certain local brick-and-mortar and online retailers, you can ask for the difference on a Walmart Gift Card. For maximum convenience, customers can use Walmart Savings Catcher to compare prices and apply any difference to a gift card. Here’s a look at the basics of Walmart price matching:

  • Walmart will not Price Match Thanksgiving Day through Cyber Monday.
  • The price match item must be in-stock on the online retailer's website and at Walmart.com when the price match is requested.
  • The item must be sold and fulfilled by specific online retailers or by Walmart.com.

Walmart Black Friday 1-Hour Guarantee

For years, the Walmart Black Friday ad was famous for featuring a handful of products that were eligible for the Walmart 1-Hour Guarantee. Walmart guaranteed that products in this category would be available in-store for one hour. However, the retailer did not offer any 1-Hour Guarantee products in 2016. It’s unknown whether Walmart will bring back the guarantee in 2017, but we will keep you posted.
