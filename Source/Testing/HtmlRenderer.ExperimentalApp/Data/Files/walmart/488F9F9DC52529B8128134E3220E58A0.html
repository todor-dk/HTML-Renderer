Nothing arrived; I checked the website the day after, and it still said they would arrive the day before (it hadn't been updated). I tracked the package and Fedex said it was "in transit" with no date given. So I waited. A couple of days later, still no packages, I drilled down on the Fedex site to find that both packages had been damaged in transit and were being returned to the sender. No one notified me, no one updated the Walmart order page. I emailed them; they apologized and replaced the order -- by then Ebates rebate period was gone so I went back and forth with them to NOT refund and reorder but to replace (I was told we were sent the wrong email "update" that had said refund). I was also told that I should have been notified by Fedex, but it was clear from the emails that had I not contacted them to ask they would have simply refunded and canceled the order, if that, even. Then one of the replacement boxes arrived -- apparently the Fedex person threw it onto my concrete porch because one of the four bottles was broken but it hadn't leaked through until I got it into the house, immediately after delivery. The broken glass punctured the bag the bottle was in and tomato juice went all over my floor. The bottles were packed with lots of packing around them and over them but absolutely NO PACKING under them -- just glass bottle sitting on cardboard box bottom, an accident waiting to happen. I contacted Walmart again, asking for a replacement for the broken bottle. They sent it out. The 2nd replacement box arrived in good shape, no thanks to the absence of packing on the bottom -- I just got lucky with that one. Then today the replacement bottle arrived, and they sent the wrong thing. When I contacted them to send the right bottle I was told I couldn't order something that wasn't on the original order, even though it WAS on the original order. So, bottom line -- give these guys another year to sort out the bugs in their system -- their website didn't update, their packers didn't pack right, Fedex clumsily handled the boxes, no one notified us of the delay, we got erroneous email "updates", and customer service got the replacement wrong. It was a total clustercluck and since time is money I would have saved more by just buying it from the Kroger down the street."
