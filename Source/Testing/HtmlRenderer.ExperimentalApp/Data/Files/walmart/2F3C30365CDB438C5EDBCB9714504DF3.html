Walmart Locations Near Washington DC

Walmart Stores in DC, Maryland and Northern Virginia

By Rachel Cooper

Updated 01/16/16

  • Share
  • Pin
  • Email

Walmart Stores (or Wal-Mart Stores, Inc.) are known all over the country for cheap prices on a variety of goods including housewares, clothing, sporting goods, groceries, pharmaceuticals and much more. There are a number of Walmart Stores in the Washington DC metropolitan area, but there are not yet any locations within the city limits. In November 2010, Walmart announced its plans to open stores in Wards 4, 5, 6 and 7 in Washington DC.

In November 2011, two new store locations were added to the plan to create more than 1,200 jobs and stimulate local economic development while improving local residents’ access to fresh, affordable groceries. The new stores offer customers a full grocery selection, including fresh produce, a bakery, delicatessen and organic food items as well as a full service pharmacy and a wide variety of general merchandise.

Update January 2016 - Walmart is closing dozens of stores around the country and has announced that they will not be building the planned stores in Skyland Town Center in Southeast Washington and in the Capitol Gateway Marketplace in Northeast Washington.
