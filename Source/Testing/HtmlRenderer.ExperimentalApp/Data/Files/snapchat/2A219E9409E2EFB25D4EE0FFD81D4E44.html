Follow Inc. Magazine on Snapchat!

Want the inside look at entrepreneurship? Follow Inc. on Snapchat for the unfiltered version of running a business, thanks to weekly takeovers from top founders.
By Valerie Heinmets
Valerie Heinmets is the social media producer for Inc. and a graduate of Fordham University.
Social Media Producer, Inc.com
WRITE A COMMENT

Looking for Inc. on Snapchat? Follow along @inc, or add us using the Snapcode above (or at this link) to catch all the action. The premise? Some of our favorite entrepreneurs are taking you behind the scenes during week-long takeovers on the Inc. Snapchat handle.

What's on deck:

  • June 26-July 2: Mike Radenbaugh, co-founder of Rad Power Bikes, will take over the feed from his company's free-wheeling office in Seattle. The company, which sells its bikes directly to customers online, is expected to bring in $30 million in revenue this year.
  • July 3-9: Shama Hyder, founder and CEO of Marketing Zen Group, will be snapping from Europe, where she'll be traveling around Vienna for work. Shama is a marketing influencer and social media expert.
  • July 10-16: The duo behind watch company MVMT, Jake Kassan and Kramer LaPlante, are taking over the account from their HQ in Los Angeles. Their affordable timepieces landed them a spot on Inc.'s 30 Under 30--and helped them raked in $60 million of revenue last year.
  • July 17-23: Tenor co-founder David McIntosh will take you on a business trip from his company's headquarters in San Francisco to beautiful Aspen, Colorado. Use GIFs in conversations? It's likely Tenor is powering that search.

What'd you miss?

We've been all over the world. Han Jin, founder of Lucid VR, took over the account while traveling for business in Taiwan. Away's co-founders Jen Rubio and Steph Korey showed off their headquarters in New York City. And Stem co-founders Milana Rabkin, Tim Luckow, and Jovin Cronin-Wilesmith traveled from their headquarters in Hollywood, California, to parties and meetings in Nashville, Tennessee.

Schedule and Snappers are subject to change, so check back here to stay up-to-date on the latest information.
