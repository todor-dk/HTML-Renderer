About Snapchat

Snapchat first launched on July 2011 and has recently grown into one of the most popular apps among young people all over the world. The app allows users to take pictures or videos. Users can add text and drawings to the pictures that they make. A user can then select a specific group of people to which they can send the image or video to.

Users can also set how long the pictures are available for viewing with a range of between 1 to 10 seconds. The apps are currently only available for iOS and Android.

Snapchat’s mascot is called “Ghostface Chilla” which is a name derived from a rapper called Ghostface Killah of the Wu Tang Clan.

Research has shown that allot of the pictures on Snapchat are of  inappropriate gestures of position. And that the apps owe their popularity to the fact that users don’t always want to leave a trace of their online identity.
