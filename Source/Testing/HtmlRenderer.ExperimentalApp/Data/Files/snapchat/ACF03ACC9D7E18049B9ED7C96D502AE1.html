Some of Snapchat Emojis Meaning are explained below:

🌟 Gold Star – This next to a person’s contact indicate that particular person’s snap has been played in the last 24 hours. Snapchat Star Emoji also indicates that the person has many fascinating contents to share.

💛 Yellow Heart – Also know as a #1 BF symbol, these Yellow Heart indicate that this person is your best friend on the app. The actual meaning is that the maximum number of snaps have been sent and received from this person.

❤️ Red Heart – This Red Heart comes beside the contact name if you have been #1 BF with this person for exact 2 weeks without breaks. These Snapchat Symbols are beautiful

💕 Pink Hearts – These Pink Hearts come beside the contact name if you have been #1 BF with this person for exact 2 months without breaks. Shows the level of commitment!

👶 Baby – You just now became friends with this person.

😎 Face With Sunglasses – These Snapchat Emojis indicate that one of your best friends is also their best friend! Means with whom you have shared lot of snaps, this person has also shared in the same way.

😬 Grimacing Face – This Emoticon just indicates that you have one common #1 BF with this person, with whom you and this person share a lot of snaps.

😏 Smirking Face –  This indicates that you have been a BF to this contact. Even though you don’t share much to this person, they share a lot of snaps with you.

😊 Smiling Face – Included on your best friends list. You sent a lot of snaps to this person but still not your #1 BF on the list.

🔥 Fire – This Snapchat Emoji is used to indicate a Snapstreak. That means you have sent your snaps to this person continuously for some days, and they have also sent some snaps in return. The count will increase with an increasing number of days.

💯 Hundred – This comes near to the fire emoji when you have reached the mark of 100 days in Snapstreaking! That means you have snapped and also received the snaps from this person for 100 successive days.

⌛ Hourglass – This means that your Snapstreak count is going to end soon, so better send a snap now to keep it alive. Its fun time to use Snapchat Symbols.

🎂 Birthday Cake – This indicates that it is this person’s birthday today. It will only be displayed if the Birthday Party utility is turned ON in your friend’s setting option.

The above listed are some of the Snapchat Emojis Meaning with the Face given. Do remember this description as you will need it whenever you see any kind of Emoticon on the Application.
