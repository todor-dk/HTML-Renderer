What are the Snapchat Trophies?

Snapchat Trophies is a totally new and probably one of the most playful features added to Snapchat app during the latest update on September 15th, 2015. Snapchat Trophies are different emoji representing different achievements in the Snapchat app. It can be earned by completing various activities such as reaching a certain Snapchat score or using a particular app feature. As of today, there are 16 different Trophies and it’s worth noting some of them have multiple levels of achievement. We believe it’s very likely that Snapchat will update its trophies list regularly in the future.

How to get Snapchat Trophies?

Every single Snapchat Trophy is being earned for different in-app activities. For example, if you send a Snap with the temperature filter below freezing or a video Snap without audio, you will get a Trophy! Snapchat developers haven’t released any official guide how each Snapchat Trophy can be earned. However, some of the most dedicated app users already unlocked vast majority of them and we have compiled a full guide how to get all Snapchat Trophies.
