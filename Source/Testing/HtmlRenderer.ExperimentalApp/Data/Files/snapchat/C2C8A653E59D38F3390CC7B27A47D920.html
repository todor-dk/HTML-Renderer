Oxlade-Chamberlain’s Snapchat interesting considering LFC rumours

Posted by
Jordan Chamberlain
 July 6, 2017

Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain has told his Snapchat followers that he’s ‘pre-season ready’ ahead of a return to Arsenal.

This is despite the versatile Englishman rejecting a contract extension on his deal that expires in less than 12 months’ time.

The likes of Oxlade-Chamberlain, Virgil van Dijk & Naby Keita will start at a later date ?? pic.twitter.com/GfjPk82KHS

— Charlie (@AnfieldNotion) July 4, 2017

The Echo told us a week ago that Oxlade-Chamberlain had ‘no intention’ of agreeing to the Gunners’ £80k/week terms and that Liverpool would need to offer £25m to convince Arsenal to sell.

This seems fairly steep considering his contract situation, but it highlights the state of the market right now – which will be inflated further when two rivals are doing business together for a 23-year-old.

LFC have been working on ‘safe’ Keita backup option, reveals French journalist

Oxlade-Chamberlain would cover various areas of our squad, but would likely only move somewhere that guarantees him the first-team football he’s been denied at the Emirates under Arsene Wenger.

In this respect, it will be up to Jurgen Klopp to convince him of our project – as there’s no obvious spot for The Ox in our starting XI.

Obviously, he could come in and make a position his own, but competition for places will be tough – especially if we bring in Naby Keita – who’ll be guaranteed a spot in the central three.
