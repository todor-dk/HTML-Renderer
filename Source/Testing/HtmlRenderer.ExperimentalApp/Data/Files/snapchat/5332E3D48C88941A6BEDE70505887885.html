How to Sign In, Log In, Reset Password and Delete your Snapchat Account.


June 29, 2015June 8, 2017 snapchatlogin

How to Sign Up for a Snapchat Account

– Launch the web browser that is available on your computer or mobile device.

– Click on the web address bar and type www.snapchat.com and then press enter.

– On the website under ‘APPS’, you will see 2 options namely ‘Snapchat for iOS’ and ‘Snapchat for Android’. If you are using an iPhone or iPad, click on ‘Snapchat for iOS’. If you are using an Android device, click on ‘Snapchat for Android’. You will need to download the app before signing up for an account.

– Once you click on the appropriate option, you will be directed to the app store. Click on the ‘install’ option to start installing the app on your device.

– Once the app has been installed, open it by clicking on it.

– Click on Sign up

– On the sign up page, you will be required to enter some of your personal details including your email address, your birthday and also a password that you will be using to log in to your account. After entering the details, click ‘Continue’.

– On the next page, you will be required to enter your username. This is the name that other people will see and use to find you on Snapchat. Since the username cannot be changed, carefully think about the name you want to use. Afterwards, click ‘Continue’.

– You will be directed to another page whereby you will need to enter your phone number. This will help other people find you and Snapchat will also use this number to find people on your contact list who are on Snapchat.

How to Login to Snapchat

– Launch the Snapchat app

– Enter your snapchat login details which include your username and password

If you want to login to Snapchat without using the app, follow the steps below:

– Launch your web browser and type https://accounts.snapchat.com/ on the web address bar.

– Once you have been directed to the page, you will be prompted to enter your Snapchat login details which include your username and password.

When you Forget Your Password

Using the app

– Launch the Snapchat application

– On the Log in page, click on ‘Forgot your password?’

– You will be directed to a page whereby you will be required to enter your email address. After entering the email address, check the ‘I’m not a robot’ box and then click ‘Submit’.

– A link that will be used to reset your password shall be sent to your email address.

Using a web browser

– Go to snapchat.com

– Click on Support.

– Click on ‘Learning the basics’

– Click on ‘Account Settings’

– Click on ‘Reset a forgotten password’

– Enter your email address and click ‘Submit’.

– Follow the link that shall be sent to your email address. It will guide you to reset your password

How to Delete Your Account

– Using your web browser, go to Snapchat.com

– Click on ‘Support’

– Click on ‘Learning the basics’

– Click on ‘Account Settings’

– Click on ‘Delete an Account’

– You will be directed to a page whereby you will need to enter your Snapchat login details. Click Submit after entering the details

– You will be asked to enter your password again. After entering the password, click on ‘Delete my account’.
