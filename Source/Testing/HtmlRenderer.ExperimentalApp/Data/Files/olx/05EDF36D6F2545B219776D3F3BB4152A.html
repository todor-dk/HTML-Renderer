Description

The OLX app is a fun, cool and easy way to buy and sell things around you. Is there something you do not need anymore? Or is there something that you have always wanted? India’s largest classified marketplace, now brings you cool deals near you. Read through some of our salient features below.

Near you
We realize that buying and selling is easy when it happens nearby. Our App shows you cool items that are up for sale at great prices around you. Hop across to pick them up from your neighborhood.

All users 100% registered
We ensure that nobody is anonymous on OLX. You find only registered users. Get to know them by browsing their profile & common friends.

Snap & Post
Now post anything unused in 30 secs. Take a picture, Set a price and that’s it. Yes, that’s it. Our genie like image recognition technology automatically detects the product, titles your ad and posts it. Once posted, you can easily manage your ads and chats within the app on the go.

Chat First
We are now a Chat First app. So no SPAM calls. People can only contact you via chat and you decide whether you want to share your number with them. Use shortcuts to make your chatting experience effortless.


Everything Sells
Everything sells on OLX - anything from cars, two-wheelers, mobiles, furniture, baby products, books, fashion, sports, electronics, musical instruments, pets, toys, …phew…the list can go on.
