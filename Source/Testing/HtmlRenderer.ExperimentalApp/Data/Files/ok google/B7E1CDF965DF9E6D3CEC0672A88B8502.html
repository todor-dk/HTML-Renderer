      philliplavelle
      Hey All,

      Question for those of you who have the S7 Edge already (I have to wait until next week!)

      I use an iPhone at the moment in the car and it is invaluable to be able to yell 'Hey Siri, Play Adele' or whatever and it just does it using a combination of Apple Music and my own library. I can do this when the screen is off and it is always listening.

      I used an S6 Edge + for a few months last year and it drove me crazy not being able to do the same with OK Google.

      For some stupid reason, Samsung had locked down the functionality that lets the phone listen and respond at any time. From memory, the screen had to be on for it to listen which meant constantly having to grab the phone when driving and hit the home key. While 'pure' Google phones like the Nexus 6P had this 'always on, listening' mode even when the screen is off, the S6 seemed a bit crippled. Only way to get around it was to have the phone plugged into a charger, I think. Which wasn't ideal.

      I'm desperately hoping this has been fixed in the S7 Edge. Can anyone who has it please confirm whether OK Google works from anywhere even with screen off and locked just like the 6P?

      Thanks!
