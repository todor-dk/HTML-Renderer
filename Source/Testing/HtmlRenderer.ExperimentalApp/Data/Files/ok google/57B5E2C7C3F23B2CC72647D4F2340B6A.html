A Guide to Using the “OK Google” Voice Automated Command

November 25, 2015

A List of 42 Things That You Can Ask Google To Do With Your Voice

On your Android smartphone you may have noticed the little microphone on your search bar that says “Say ‘OK Google”. With this one command you can have your Android phone provide a variety of tasks. On an iPhone you will have to download and open the Google App first and then you can execute these commands as well.

Simply say,”OK, Google” followed by the command you want it to perform.

Try some of them for yourself! Feel free to enter your own nouns in the brackets.

General Search Commands

“How old is [Jon Voight]?”

“What is the [Urban Sombrero]”?

“When did [Shea Stadium] open?”

“Show me pictures of [dingoes]”

“Search for a [Medium Crab Bisque Soup] recipe”

“Define: [babka]”

“How many calories are in a [steak]?”

“Show me the menu for [Monk’s Diner]”

“What time does [Kramerica Industries] open?”

 

Reminders and Notes

“Note to self: [I parked in section E2 of the parking garage]”

“Set an alarm for [7 AM]”

“Remind me to call [Bob Saccamano tomorrow at 9AM].”

Communication

“Call [George Costanza]”

“Call [Vandelay Industries]”

“Send [text] to [Jerry], [don’t forget to pick up a marble rye]”

“Listen to voicemail”

“When is [Elaine’s] birthday?”

“Send [email] to [Newman], Subject: [Hello,…Newman], Message: [What day are you moving?]”

Time and Date

“What time is it in [New York]?”

“When is the sunset in [Boca Raton, Florida]?”

“Create a calendar event: [Festivus Party][Monday at 4 PM]”

 

Entertainment

“Listen to / play [Desperado] by [The Eagles]”

“Play some smooth jazz”

“Watch [Rochelle, Rochelle]”

“What movies are playing [tomorrow]?”

“Who was the actor in the movie [Superman]?”

“What song is this?” {when there is a song playing}

Maps and Directions

“Give me directions to [LaGuardia Airport]”

“How far is it from [Milan to Minsk]?”

“Map of [New York City]”

Control Your Device

“Open [Facebook]”

Take a picture”

“Record a video”

“Post to Twitter [these pretzels are making me thirsty]”

“Turn flashlight on”

 

Calculations and Conversions

“Convert [30 dollars] to [Euros]”

“How much is [75] divided by [3]?”

“Square root of [49]”

“How many [pounds] are there in [300 ounces]?”

“What is the tip for [34 dollars]?”

Weather

“Will I need an umbrella today?”

“What is the weather like tomorrow?”

These are just some of the useful and fun ways that you can control your smartphone using the Google App.   Using Google Now’s “OK Google” command is just like having your own virtual assistant. Try it out and you may be surprised at the number of tasks that it can do for you.
