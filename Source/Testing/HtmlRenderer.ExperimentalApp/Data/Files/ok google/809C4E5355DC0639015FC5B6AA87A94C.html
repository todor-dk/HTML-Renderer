OSU Campus Maps

Oklahoma State University’s main campus is located about 60 miles from both Oklahoma City and Tulsa in the city of Stillwater. OSU is one of the most beautiful campuses in the Big 12 Conference and the region. Picturesque landscaping and Georgian architecture highlight the more than 700 acres of its central campus. The Stillwater campus is home to Old Central, the university’s first permanent academic building; the Student Union, the nation's largest and most comprehensive student union; the Edmon Low Library, located in the heart of campus; beautiful Theta Pond; state-of-the-art Boone Pickens Stadium; and historic Gallagher-Iba Arena. Explore the links on this page to experience the Cowboy spirit, university pride and land-grant tradition that is Oklahoma State University.

Undergraduate Admissions Tours

Interested in attending OSU? A picture might be worth a 1,000 words, but it can’t tell you if you will fit in, who your friends will be or what the campus environment is really like. We take great pride in making our visitors feel like they are part of the Cowboy family from the moment they step on campus. But don’t just take our word for it. Come find out for yourself! Join us for a campus tour or one of our many events held throughout the year. For tours intended for prospective students and their families, please visit the Office of Undergraduate Admissions website.

Self Guided Tours

If you're unable to attend a scheduled tour, we offer a self-guided option that you can complete at your convenience.

Our self-guided walking tour will take you from the heart of campus, past our beautiful modified Georgian-style architecture, to the latest research buildings on campus. We’ve also included optional excursions for you to explore at your leisure.

Where's Pete Discovery Trail - Discover the beauty and history of OSU through a fun, interactive tour of America's Healthiest Campus®! Find 15 Pistol Petes hidden along a 2.15 mile trail while searching for answers to questions along the way.

Alumni Tours

Are you in the mood to reminisce? Are you saying to yourself, “What’s that building, that wasn’t there when I was in school?"

The OSU Alumni Association's Student Alumni Board is available to host free tours of the Oklahoma State University campus during regular business hours. The tours may be customized depending on what parts of campus you are interested in seeing.
