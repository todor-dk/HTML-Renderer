How to use “OK Google” on any screen on Galaxy S5 ?

October 14, 2014 by Ethan

If you wish to say “Ok Google” from any screen (including the lock screen) to have Google do something for you, please follow these steps:

  1. Ensure you have the latest Google app on your Galaxy S5. To confirm you have the latest version, you can go to the Google app page on the Play Store app and tap Update.
  2. On your Galaxy S5, find and launch the Google app.
  3. Tap  > Settings > Voice
  4. On the Voice screen, make sure the primary languages is English (US)
  5. Tap “Ok Google” Detection.
  6. Check the box next to ‘From Google Search app’ and ‘From any screen’.
  7. You’ll now be prompted to say “Ok Google” three times to teach Google what your voice sounds like.
  8. After you’ve successfully said “Ok Google” three times, tap OK.
  9. Getting back to the “Ok Google Detection” screen, you can check the box next to ‘From lock screen’.

Note: Enabling OK Google on the lock screen may allow someone to use voice commands to make calls, send texts or access your address contacts even when your screen is locked.

Note: The steps here were written for Android 4.4.2 on a Samsung Galaxy S5 (SM-G900F). If you’re having a different model of Galaxy S5 or other version of Android, there’s a possibility that the steps and screenshots will be different.
