    About this Add-on

    Google translate gadget for firefox. This extension translates entire webpages into a language of your choice with one click. Idea comes from Google Translation Bar for Chrome.

    You will use it:
    1.Similarity with the Google Translation Bar or Google Translate(for Chrome)
    2.Without having to install Google Toolbar
    3.User experience better than Google translate bookmark

    Tips: If you use Flashblock or NoScript in your browser, this extension will not function properly. (Thanks：marekjs 和 mehtuus )

    BUT, you can "add the specific url ' translate.googleapis.com ' in white list of flashblock, and gtranslator works 100% fine"(thanks Leecher05 )
