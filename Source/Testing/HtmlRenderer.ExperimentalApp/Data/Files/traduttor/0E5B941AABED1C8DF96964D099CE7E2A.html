    • Reverso Intranet Translation

    • The high performance translation solution


      Softissimo offers you the opportunity to develop a complete and customized translation solution adapted to your image and business activity.
    • Give your colleagues the chance to communicate easily without language barriers through Reverso Intranet translation solution.
    • Key points
    • Description
    • Features
    • Demo
    • Fast and effective translation software.
    • Installation directly on the server.
    • Access via a simple browser or directly from your Office applications (Reverso PowerTools).
    • Unlimited number of users.
    • Customization of the interface to your company's image
    • Linguistic customization: creation of your own dictionaries from your own glossaries, vocabularies...
    • Connection to dictionaries created with Reverso Pro
    • Reverso Corporate translation solution includes:
    • - applications available on the Intranet of your company to translate files, websites or other documents in real time.
    • - individual licenses for intensive or laptop users to translate directly from their Office applications
    • - professional tools to improve the efficiency and productivity of translation specialists and help them manage the vocabulary specific to your business.

    • A solution adapted to your needs:

      The Reverso Corporate Solution is based on standard software ( Reverso professional software for PC and Intranet) which can be adapted to your specific needs through our customized services.

    • Reverso PowerTools (optional module)

      Reverso PowerTools module allows you to have access to the translation functions of Reverso Intranet directly from your main applications: Microsoft Office (Outlook, Word, PowerPoint), Open Office (Writer and Impress), but also from Internet Explorer and Firefox.

      Main features

    • 3 translation modules:
      • Texts translation: e-mails, mails, web pages or files excerpts...
      • Web pages translation: for browsing foreign websites keeping the format and the links
      • Files translation: for keeping the page display of your files
    • Lexibase Intranet: the complete version of the online dictionary
    • A collaborative module where you can send translation suggestions or feedbacks of your personal user experience
    • A user guide to help you get familiar with the translation service
    •  
    • Administration module interface
    • Statistics made easy and faster to access
    • Management of the users'comments
    • Linguistic management module for specialized domains and dictionaries
