Sinhala  

Type: adjective, proper, noun;
  • nl.wiktionary.org
    singalese
    { noun masculine }
  • OmegaWiki
    cingalese
    { noun masculine }
An Indo-Aryan language of Sri Lanka.
The indigenous language of the majority people of Sri Lanka.
An ancient name for the island of Sri Lanka.
The kingdom in the island of Sri Lanka mentioned in the Mahabharata.
Indo-Aryan language of the Indoeuropean languages, mother tongue of the Sinhalese, the largest ethnic group of Sri Lanka along with Tamil.
An abugida script used to write the Sinhala language, spoken in Sri Lanka. It is also used to write Pali and Sanskrit.
of or relating to the Sinhalese languages; "the Sinhalese versions of the Ramayana"
the Indic language spoken by the people of Sri Lanka
