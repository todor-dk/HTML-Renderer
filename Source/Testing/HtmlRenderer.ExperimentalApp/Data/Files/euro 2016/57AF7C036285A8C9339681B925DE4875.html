    Euro 2016

    0shares
    0shares


    The Euro 2016 finals kick off on June 10th. We’ll be providing in-depth previews of the finalists as well as comprehensive coverage of the tournament itself. Each country will be previewed and each player will be profiled as we build up to the major football event of 2016.

    News updates will be accompanied by regular opinion pieces from our specialist European-based correspondents. Sid Lowe in Spain, Paddy Agnew in Italy, and Nick Bidwell from Germany will be among the writers keeping you up-to-date with the developments, while World Soccer editor Gavin Hamilton will be in France for the duration of the tournament.

    In-depth previews of all the countries taking part are now available online and we have also published profiles of every single player taking part at the finals. Over the coming days we will be publishing exclusive interviews with coaches and key players from each nation as we prepare for the footballing showpiece of 2016.

    For the full schedule of the tournament, click here, and for in-depth coverage of each country. including tactical analysis, player profiles, interviews etc, click on the country name in the right-hand panel.
