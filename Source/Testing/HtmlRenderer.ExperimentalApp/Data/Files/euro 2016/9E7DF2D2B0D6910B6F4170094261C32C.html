UEFA Euro 2016 - Fixtures

The 15th version of the European Championships Euro 2016 will be held in France from 10th June to 10 July. The Euro Football 2016 matches will be played across 11 cities, with the final being played in Saint-Denis in Paris. The teams will be divided into six groups of four and the top two teams make it to the knockout stages of the tournament. From thereon, the teams face each other in knockout format at the Euro 2016 venues.

This will be the second time that France will be hosting a European Championship 2016. Their maiden win was on home soil in 1984. So far, only Germany and Spain have won the tournament three times. The hosts have won the tournament twice. Greece were the last winners before the Spanish Armada sweeping the last two Euro Cup tournaments. Netherlands, USSR and Italy have also got their hands on the tournament. As many as five new teams are participating in this tournament. Wales, Slovakia, Albania, Northern Ireland and Iceland will be making their debut. More than ever before, there are top players. Every team has such star players and that will be the biggest draw in European Football Championship 2016.
