UEFA EURO 2016 Final Draw Simulator

It's a simulator of the UEFA EURO 2016 Group stage final draw. Actual draw is held in Paris on December 12.

2015-11-17
Simulate Draw

Draw procedure

  • There will be four pots:
  • Pot 1: five teams to include Spain, as the reigning European champions
  • Pots 2, 3 and 4: six teams
  • France are not included in the pots as they are automatically assigned position 1 of Group A.

Group Stage

  • The 24 teams taking part in the final competition will be divided into six groups of four teams.
  • The six group winners, the six group runners-up and the top four teams of third-placed side qualify directly for the final tournament.
