AUOL - At a glance

Founded

The American University Of London was originally established in 1984 as the American College of Science & Technology and became a University in 1986. AUOL, the international distance learning centre, was incorporated independently as an On-Line and Distance Learning University in 1999 and is not affiliated with any other institution.

Mission Of The University

"To bring closer to every student everywhere in the world the exciting prospect of higher education through Distance Learning. AUOL is the acknowledged expert in helping people study in their own time with the support of an accredited tutor".

Type Of Institution

American System Independent, not for profit. The Pioneers in Online & Distance Learning education since 1984. AUOL is also one of a few recognized Universities that award degrees based on work and professional experience.

Degrees

Associate of Arts, Bachelor of Arts And Science, Bachelor of Business Administration, Master of Business Administration, Master of Law LLM, DBA and PhDs Prof Nimier, the President awarding an Honorary Doctorate to Sheikh Mohammad Ibn Oboud Al-Amoudi of Saudi Arabia (Sept.2004)in the presence of Lord Ahmed of Rotherham & the Saudi Attache'

Schools

Currently AUOL has six Schools: School of Business, School of Information Technology, School of Law, School of Education & Liberal Arts, School of English & School of Humanities. The university has one Call Centre based in central London. Each School has its own members consisting of a Dean, tutors, assessors and accredited examiners.

Information Technology

Computer network links AUOL schools and connects to the Internet. Applications include Microsoft Office Professional 2000, e-mail and instructional Software.

Academic Calendar & Admissions

AUOL operates on a rolling admissions basis. We will accept an application at any time of the year, from anyone living anywhere in the world as long as the applicant has prior academic and /or work experience that can be considered suitable for the awarding of university credits

Scholarships

AUOL offers scholarships based on need and academic qualifications, including the AUOL Asia Studentship Award, the AUOL Africa Studentship Award & the Barbara George Theodory Award (for gifted students with specific academic interests in Middle Eastern Studies).

National Origin Of Applicants

Students from more than 60 countries have registered at AUOL. The Alumni are found around the world particularly in South East Asia, USA & Canada, and the Middle East & China. 15% Europe, 19% N. America, 11% Africa 22 % South East Asia, 18% Middle East 5% UK, 10% China

Undergraduate Programs

65 majors and 83 minors. Over 100 courses are available each semester.

Graduate Programs

26 graduate programs in Law, Business, Arts, Education, English and the Humanities.

World Wide Affiliates

AUOL has academic co-operation agreements, affiliation & representations with a number of educational establishments in USA, Canada, Malaysia, China, Greece, Syria, Indonesia, Egypt, Spain Saudi Arabia , Argentina, Hungary Iran, Yemen, Taiwan & S. Korea

Worldwide Student Support

Online Information desk available to students 7 days a week from 8 am to midnight. International students' counsellors specialized in addressing registered students' academic concerns

Degrees

In line with other Universities in the UK our degrees are notarised by a British commissioner of oaths.
