Thank You for Visiting Hanksters

My name is Gary Hankinson AKA "Hankster." I've been building, restoring and selling Hot Rods, Muscle Cars, Classic Cars and Motorcycles for 30 years. I would like to personally thank you for visiting our web site, where every one of our cars have been personally selected and handpicked by our team. We do not do consignment and we own 100% of our cars so we don't have to check with the seller, we can make a deal on the spot. With over 30 years of knowledge and hands on experience, We only purchase quality cars, Period. We keep 75+ vehicles in each of our showrooms in Pa. & South Daytona Florida. Specializing in quality Muscle cars, Classic Cars, Hot rods, Street rods, Resto Mods, & Pro Touring Cars, we mostly purchase our cars from individual owners or private collections. We’re very thorough in our listings by showing you many photos and videos of many different views, interior, exterior and underside. We take great pride in being able to provide our customers with their dream cars at an affordable price. All of our vehicles are available for inspection prior to purchase or bidding. If you are looking for something specific and do not see it in our inventory, please let me know. I have built relationships with many collectors throughout the United States so if you have a specific car in mind, we can find it!
