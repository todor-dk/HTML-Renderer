NY Traffic Ticket Lawyer

Getting a traffic ticket in New York may at first seem like it’s nothing more than a minor inconvenience.  Unfortunately, moving violations and related misdemeanors are serious business with serious consequences. You could be facing large fines and surcharges, increased insurance premiums, license suspension or even a criminal record as a result of a traffic ticket or misdemeanor.  Read More

If you want to avoid the penalties, you need to make informed decisions and pursue the proper course of action. The attorneys at Feifer & Greenberg have decades of combined experience fighting New York traffic tickets.  Let us explain what you are facing and, if you decide to move forward with our firm, help you fight your traffic violation or driving related misdemeanor. Typical traffic tickets for violations such as disobeying a traffic control device, speeding and cell phone use carry points. Too many points can lead to large surcharges and even license suspension once 11 points in an 18-month period have been accumulated on a driving record.  We can help you fight any moving violation and work towards having the charges against you reduced, if not dropped altogether, to avoid fines and/or points on your license as well as any potential insurance increases.

Not all driving related charges are traffic tickets alleging moving violations. Sometimes the charges rise to the criminal level.  Driving with a suspended license (Aggravated Unlicensed Operation) and Reckless Driving are two very common driving related misdemeanors in New York.  Of course, any charges alleging driving under the influence (DUI) could lead to misdemeanor or even more serious felony convictions.  Our experienced attorneys can help you navigate any of these cases effectively.

How Does This Work?  Will I Need To Go To Court?

When you contact us, we will give you a free, full evaluation of your case.  This includes any good news and any bad–our job is to accurately inform people, not just tell them what they may want to hear.  We’ll give a full consultation, let you know what consequences you are potentially facing and how we may be able to help.  Note that we can help absolutely anywhere in New York State.  Feifer & Greenberg has been doing this for a long time and we have an extensive network of attorneys across the state who have experience in local courts.  Our fees are low, fair flat fees and consistent throughout the state — they are not dependent on the location of your case relative to our main office in NYC.

During your consultation, we’ll discuss the procedure in the particular court at hand — sometimes plea bargaining and negotiation is an option, sometimes it is not (particularly with NYC TVB traffic violations).  We’ll discuss reasonable goals and how we intend to achieve them if we are hired as counsel.  In the vast majority of cases, you will not need to personally go to court.  If your case is an exception to the general rule, we’ll of course let you know as that might play a role in your decision regarding how to proceed.

If you decide to hire our firm to help fight your NY traffic ticket, we’ll walk you through a very quick client intake.  We have some introductory paperwork to send to new clients outlining what we do, giving you a recap of much of what was discussed during your consultation and giving you an opportunity to reiterate any special circumstances or information you’d like us to have regarding your case.  As your case progresses, we will be in full communication with you.  If there are any updates or developments in your case, we will contact you asap.  If you have any questions, we are very responsive to email inquiries and have a fully trained staff and attorneys available to take your call and a live person answering calls 24/7.

At Feifer & Greenberg, we handle traffic cases. It’s all we do. Trust us to expertly handle your New York traffic ticket or driving related misdemeanor.  Contact us today for a free case evaluation.
