Do-It-Yourself Wood Working Plans by U-Bild
America's favorite woodworking plans since 1948, U-Bild plans are ideal for woodworkers and do-it-yourselfers of all skill levels.

Their unique "trace, saw and assemble" designs feature simple-to-follow patterns that allow even beginners to achieve professional results. Each plan features detailed step-by-step instructions, full-size traceable patterns (where needed), in-progress photos, isometric diagrams and complete materials lists and cutting schedules. U-Bild woodworking plans are simply the best way for a new woodworker to gain confidence, experience and feel the joy of woodworking!
