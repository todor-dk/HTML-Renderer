Loewe Bild 5 OLED TV

  • 6th July 2017

Luxury German TV brand Loewe has unveiled an exquisite new addition to add to its portfolio – the Bild 5 OLED.

The latest design has been crafted, by renowned designer and Loewe’s creative director Bodo Sperlein.

With the new Bild 5 model, Loewe continues to breathe new life into the world of television with a striking aesthetic style inspired by retro Sixties décor.

The TV’s mixture of high-tech and natural materials produces a warm, homely feeling, and is available in two finishes – natural Silver Oak and high gloss Piano Black.

Each has been considered to complement the futuristic slim screen – which, at just 4.9mm thick, makes the TV appear almost weightless.

The Bild 5 OLED is available in either a 55″ (140cm) or 65″ (160cm) screen, with an Ultra HD 3840×2160 display, so you can be sure of a crisp, clean image.

Buyers can also choose between a range of 21 different stands and soundbars for a TV tailored to each individual’s needs from a wall-mounted monitor version to a table stand on the sideboard or free-standing on the real oak floor stand.

With the optional soundbar, wireless technology allows the speakers and subwoofer to communicate with the television without cables, and BlueTooth can be used to stream music from your smartphone directly to the television.

The integrated hard disk has 1,000 GB of storage space, so you can watch a football match while recording a crime thriller and a film at the same time, and the TIDAL app in the home screen gives access to streaming of over 40 million songs in super high quality.

The Bild 5 OLED TV, from £2,990, by LOEWE
