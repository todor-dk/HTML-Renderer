Based in Hamburg, Bild is the best-selling newspaper both in Germany and across Europe. Founded in 1952, it is a tabloid newspaper published Monday through Saturday. The German word 'Bild' means a picture or image. The newspaper was formerly called Bild-Zeitung ("Picture Newspaper"). Bild emphasizes coverage of popular sports, especially football from the German Bundesliga, along with celebrity gossip and photos.

Circulation: 3,548,000 copies

Bild is one of the largest circulation newspapers in the world. Mondo Times covers newspapers around the world.

This newspaper is owned by Axel Springer.

The website is presented in the German language.
