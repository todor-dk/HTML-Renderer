Drag and drop pieces from the palettes on the left onto the space on the right. Clicking on a piece brings it to the front.

Arrange them to make your city. (It might help to start by adding the roads)

If you don't like a piece you can delete it by putting it in the dumper truck in the right corner.

You can delete your whole town by clicking the 'Delete all' button.

Click 'Save & send' to send your unique city to your friends.
