Our Story

The story of a prayer bracelet, a voided ticket, an orphanage, and two men crying went viral. Our story received over 707,000 shares and 1,176,000 likes on Facebook. We met new friends from all over the world who wanted to help us share the love. We decided to form a non profit charity called “WeDo.Life”. The proceeds will go to the families of fallen officers, orphanages, and anyone else we can help… No matter where they are and no matter what it takes. Please donate today and receive a free bracelet as our gift to you. It’s time to build a better tomorrow. Let’s start today.
