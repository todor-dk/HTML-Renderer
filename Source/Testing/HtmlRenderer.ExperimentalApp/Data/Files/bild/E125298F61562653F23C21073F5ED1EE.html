The Region’s Leaders in Accessibility
We are Nationally Recognized leaders in accessibility, having instructed thousands of professionals on accessible modifications and solutions for Universal Design. BILD combines the expertise of Physical and Occupational Therapist accessibility experts along with our interior design and lift experts to bring solutions for accessibility, safety and comfort.

Visit our Accessibility Showroom
BILD offers a functioning accessibility showroom and design center which features the area’s largest display of functional grab bars, bathroom options, kitchen accessibility, stair lift, ceiling lift, automatic door opener, and other solutions for accessibility. Our showroom is located at 9209 W Bluemound Road, Milwaukee, WI 53226 – Please call us for an appointment at 262-671-2032
