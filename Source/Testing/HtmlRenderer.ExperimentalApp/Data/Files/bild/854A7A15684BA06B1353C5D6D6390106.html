Welcome to Van Bortel Ford

 

Van Bortel Ford is a Ford President's Award 5 Time Winner this year! Thank you all so much for making us the #1 Ford Dealership in Rochester, NY again and again. We couldn't do it without our loyal customers!

Van Bortel Ford, Serving Rochester, Fairport, Victor and Pittsford, is Home to All Your Ford Needs

In our experience, there's no resisting the beautiful craftsmanship of the Ford models you'll find in our showroom. If you happen to hail from East Rochester and are in the market for a new or used car, then you have come to the right place. Because if there's one thing we love more than our lineup of new and used cars, it's serving customers like you here at Van Bortel Ford.

The first thing everyone notices when they walk into our East Rochester showroom is that factory finish on models like the Ford Escape, F-150, Focus, Edge and Fusion. And if that alone can capture your attention, just imagine the feeling of taking a seat behind the wheel for a test drive. That's something we encourage everyone to do. That, and asking questions. After all, we could talk about new Ford models all day, and often do.

Beyond simply selling new cars, our dealership is also known for giving Ford models a second chance on the road by carrying an excellent selection of used cars as well. Each vehicle we receive from a previous owner is thoroughly inspected to make sure it's fit to be driven in East Rochester and the surrounding Rochester, Fairport, Victor and Pittsford areas, meaning you can count on a reliable vehicle when you shop at Van Bortel Ford.

From Finance to Parts and Service, Van Bortel Ford is About More Than Simply Sales

When the search for your next car ends, that's where the fun begins for our team of auto financing experts. They love searching for the best possible car loan or Ford lease solution for your needs in order to ensure you drive home completely satisfied, both with the vehicle you've chosen and the terms of your financing. Simply put, you're in good hands with Van Bortel Ford by your side.

Fantastic customer service from Van Bortel Ford doesn't end when the sale does; our dealership also staffs a fantastic, onsite auto service and repair center. Their goal: make sure every mile you spend behind the wheel is one that's free of worry. They do so by firmly knowing your specific Ford Escape, F-150, Focus, Edge or Fusion inside and out, only using high-quality Ford parts and by making sure you're aware of every service being performed on your car as well as why it's being done.

Here at Van Bortel Ford we're serving drivers in Rochester, Fairport, Victor and Pittsford with all of their automotive needs. Whether a new or used car is in order or a little maintenance is required, visit us at 71 Marsh Rd. in East Rochester, NY and we'd be happy to help you, too.
