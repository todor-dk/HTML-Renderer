Le Cache BILD Wine Cabinets

BILD wine cabinets by Le Cache offer superior quality and features at a fraction of the price of most wine cabinets. This is possible due to an inexpensive yet attractive and durable melamine exterior finish on Le Cache BILD cabinets, as well as the fact that the BILD series requires some assembly. You can expect the same high quality cooling and attractive durability you get from other Le Cache models, but priced for the more frugal wine enthusiast.

Le Cache BILD wine cabinets are offered in various sizes including walk-in wine rooms, single-deep and double-deep configurations. Efficient wine rack placement, advanced CellarPro cooling system with full 5-year warranty coverage, and a choice of melamine exterior finishes complete the Le Cache BILD package. BILD wine cabinets include tinted glass to protect your wine from light and heat, excellent insulation and wine racking that can accommodate larger bottles.

Le Cache BILD wine cabinets are shipped flat-packed and require assembly.
