Services

What’s your design dilemma?

Healthy businesses grow and change over time. You may find that your marketing materials have evolved without a strategy and that, as a result, your visual identity is conflicting or confusing.

That’s where I come in. I can create a clear, consistent identity that reflects your brand message.

I design:

  • marketing pieces to fit your existing brand
  • brochures, booklets and flyers
  • annual reports
  • business logos and stationery
  • signage and more

If you already have regular print suppliers, I can work seamlessly with them to produce your materials, giving you the freedom to focus on your own job.

If you have never had design work done before and don’t know where to start, I can guide you through each step of the design and production process.

Unique illustrations, made to order

Looking for unique illustrations or artwork? I create commissioned hand-drawn or digital illustrations to meet your brief and budget.

I try to bring a fresh insight to each of my projects, with well-crafted, thoughtful imagery.

Ask me about:

  • books and CD covers
  • editorial illustrations
  • posters and products
  • instructional manuals and more
