  • Designed and engineered to secure seams of Owens Corning™ housewrap products and for general construction repair
  • Acrylic based, pressure sensitive adhesive tape combined with polypropylene film
  • Seals against air and moisture infiltration
  • Formulated to resist mildew and mold growth
  • Resists UV and weather exposure
  • Designed to maintain its adhesive properties under high humidity conditions
