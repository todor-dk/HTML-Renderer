Search for U-Bild Woodworking Plans, Projects and Patterns

U-Bild has provided America's favorite woodworking plans since 1948. U-Bild woodworking projects are ideal for woodworkers and do-it-yourselfers of all skill levels. U-Bild project plans offer our unique "trace, saw and assemble" designs that feature simple-to-follow patterns (like sewing patterns).

Working With U-Bild Woodworking Plans
Even woodworking beginners can achieve professional woodworking results. Each U-Bild do-it-yourself woodworking project features detailed step-by-step instructions, full-size traceable patterns (where needed), in-progress photos, isometric diagrams and complete materials lists and cutting schedules.
