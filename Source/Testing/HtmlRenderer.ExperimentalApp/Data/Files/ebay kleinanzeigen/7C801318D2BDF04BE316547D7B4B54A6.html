Local Classifieds in Germany

eBay Kleinanzeigen was launched in 2009 as a simple, easy and free way to find what you need, locally. eBay Kleinanzeigen is an ideal format for those users who are looking for a simple, local online classifieds experience. Customers do not need to register and can place an ad on eBay Kleinanzeigen in less than two minutes. eBay Kleinanzeigen is a destination for German locals to connect and fulfill the core need of finding immediate value across a range of categories – goods, jobs, cars and more. Members of its communities share a neighborly spirit and know they can find great deals close to home on items they want to buy, sell or simply give away.
