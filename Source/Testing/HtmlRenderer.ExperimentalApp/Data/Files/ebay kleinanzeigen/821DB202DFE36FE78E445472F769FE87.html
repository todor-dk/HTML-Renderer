Own the Shopper Journey
Shopping happens everywhere these days and no one understands that better than eBay.

With 167M active buyers worldwide and 1B live listings on our platform 1, we have unique insight into the entire shopper experience. Our data tells a story about what fuels our users’ passions through their product research and purchase trends.

As a marketing partner, our deep understanding of our users’ interest and intent translates into opportunities to drive sales and actionable insights for your brand. Our powerful suite of tools, combined with this invaluable shopping intent data, helps you influence your target market’s purchasing path at every stage.

eBay is one of the world’s largest and most vibrant marketplaces. We are transforming connected commerce for brands and sellers:
Turn data into insights

Over 200M hours of shopping activity is analyzed monthly.2
Reach any audience segment

Over 60 predefined audiences—drive them to your products.
Find more customers

Reach shoppers who look like your current customers through rich data science and shop-alike modeling.
Implement custom integrations and solutions

We’ll craft custom marketing plans designed to surround your target audience.
