About GameScene

GameScene is the orginal Web site for web browser games! We started building our collection of free games back in 1995. Here you will find more than 160 games of every genre: puzzle games, action games, word games, card games, simulations and more.

What makes our site different is that all of the games here were created by us. While other sites borrow and steal low quality games from all over the Internet, these games are all our own creation.

The games are all free to play online right inside your Web browser using either Flash, Shockwave or HTML5. The HTML5 games will even work on devices like the iPad and iPhone. Click on a game to try it!
