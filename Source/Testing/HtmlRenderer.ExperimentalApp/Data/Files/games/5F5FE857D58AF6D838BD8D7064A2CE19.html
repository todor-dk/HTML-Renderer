Play It Your Way

There is an excellent selection of virtual reality headsets available, ranging from simple frames that work in conjunction with a smartphone to the advanced PS VR, which harnesses the incredible processing power of your PlayStation 4 to open up new vistas. Alternatively, shop for the Nintendo Switch, a fantastic way to switch up how you spend your free time. When it's docked, you get a full HD home entertainment experience, but when you need to game on the go, it transforms into a powerful portable handheld console.

The detachable Joy-Con controllers have built-in motion control and allow two friends to play face-to-face, while online functionality opens up exciting multiplayer opportunities. There are no game cartridges to worry about. If you are more into retro gaming, the Nintendo Mini is an ideal addition to your console collection. The miniaturized replica of the classic console comes pre-loaded with 30 games for countless hours of gameplay.

Power Up

If you thought the PS4 was powerful, you haven’t seen anything yet. Take gaming to the next level with the PS4 Pro (previously the PS4 Neo). This powerhouse console runs games faster and with fewer frame rate tears for a smoother, more immersive gameplay experience. It is also capable of 4K streaming and 4K auto-upscaling for video content. If you want blistering speed, spectacular graphics, and enhanced gameplay, this console has it all. Alternatively, consider the Xbox One S, the only console with 4K Blu-ray, 4K video streaming, and HDR. It offers up to 2TB of storage, is 40% smaller, and incorporates an IR blaster that makes it the heart of your home entertainment system.
