Skype (2011)

Microsoft's Skype is a software that allows you to instant message and make voice and video calls to fellow Skype users on the Internet for free. You can even get a phone number and make calls to phones around the world for a fairly affordable rate. Learn the many ways you can share with those who matter to you using Skype.
