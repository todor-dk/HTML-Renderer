Description

The Skype you know and love has an all-new design, supercharged with a ton of new features and new ways to stay connected with the people you care about most.

What can I do with Skype?

• Messaging made awesome: More than just text. Send photos, videos, voice messages, emoticons, Mojis and more. You can also respond to any message from your contacts with reactions.
• Great for groups: Add your favorite people and start chatting. Use groups to plan together, play together, for staying in touch or just for hanging out.
• Call everyone - Skype, mobiles and landlines: Free* video and voice calls make it easier to stay connected with friends and family. You’ll also enjoy low calling rates to mobiles and landlines worldwide with Skype. 
• Never miss a moment with quick capture: Quickly swipe to capture photos and videos. Make them your own by adding emoticons, stickers and annotations.
• Share Highlights: Keep others updated with Highlights from your day. React to any highlights with awesome emoticons or send a private message about that highlight. Your highlights only stay visible for 7 days to your followers.

*Skype to Skype calls are free. Operator data charges may apply. We recommend using an unlimited data plan or WiFi connection.

What's New in Version 8.1.2

Issue resolved: Skype now supports all iOS 9.0 (or later) iPhones.

Welcome to the all new Skype.

It's the Skype that you know and love but enhanced with new features to help you connect with the people you care about.

Some of the new features are:
- In-Call Reactions: You can now add live emoticons, live text and even real-time photos to your video and voice calls.
- Message Reactions: Let your contacts know how you feel by adding expressive reactions to their messages.
- Capture: Sharing photos and videos of precious moments are just a swipe away.
- Highlights: Share your day-to-day with friends and family while keeping up with what they are up to using highlights. You can easily respond to others' highlights by reacting with emoticons or even use them as a conversation-starter.

Customer Reviews

Ugly Disappointment
     
by MonstersHaveHeartsToo

This latest update is the most thrown-together looking thing I've seen in a long time. The layout looks and feels nothing like Skype, the chat system is a mess and the Highlights system just screams "we want to be as successful as SnapChat". Checking your own or other people's profiles is a pain and the mood messages are nowhere to be found. The camera system is messed up, I only have a front facing camera on my device and it won't let me turn my cam on during a call. The new colors are atrocious, why is there no option to turn everything blue again, instead of these ugly multi colored text bubbles? At least there's a black and white option, but it's still terrible to look at. Making calls is a pain, and this update makes the app feel like a failed SnapChat rip-off rather than Skype. The whole point of Skype is to make calls with a simple chat system, not these stupid Highlight mechanics. I can confidently say I will never use the Highlight system, it's pointless and stupid. Not having the mood messages or "online/away/do not disturb/invisible" options available really bothers me, that was one of the best parts of Skype. I'm extremely disappointed in this update.
The only good thing I can say about it is now it's faster and doesn't take 10 minutes to start the app up anymore. That's it.
I've had this app for years and this is by far the worst, most dysfunctional update I've seen. What were the developers thinking?

The update is rubbish
     
by felldyne

I've been using skype for the past three years and it has quickly become one of my favorite apps. Well that was before this stupid update. Nothing feels like skype anymore. Instead it looks like a Snapchat wanna be. I mean honestly after reading through a few other reviews, deciding not to switch back to the original skype would be a poor marketing choice on skype's side. The main problem with the app is that it's too busy! Skype has always been know for its simplistic and easy to use layout. Now however, I can't even figure out how to use the hold mechanic while I'm in a call. I was originally excited about this update. With all the data skype had been gathering from reviews and feedback I had expected this update to fix a good portion of the original problems. What I didn't expect was this trashy thrown together update which, from what I've seen, nobody in the Skype community likes. Not only does the app function horribly but somehow they've managed to make this version even slower than the last one! The only reason I updated in the first place is because skype forced me to. I'm also very upset that I can no longer alter my status and mood message. In trying to prevent skype from turning into an undesirable washed up app...this update has made skype more undesirable than ever. If I had one piece of advice to give to the developers of skype; It'd be that you can't fix something that doesn't need to be fixed.

Great App
     
by AussieKA

Update: A year ago I gave Skype an overall 4.5 to 5 star rating but since the update my opinion has changed. This new version is not user friendly which is the primary reason I chose Skype initially. I concur with all the other reviews, everything is so much more difficult and confusing than it has to be. I just want a simple easy way to check in daily with family overseas. Bring back the old Skype. Remember the motto "if it ain't broke don't fix it"

I've used Skype for the last 9 years to video, call and text from the USA to Australia to stay in daily contact with family. I even used it to stay in contact with family while they travelled throughout Europe and Egypt for 9 months a couple of years ago. Occasionally I get a bad video or call connection that is intermittent but this can sometimes be remedied by hanging up and connecting again. All in all I find Skype is mostly reliable, certainly convenient and easy to use. I now have an iPhone 6+ and have not noticed a difference between using Skype on it than on the last 2 iPhone models I had. I also use it on my PC with the same results. I'd give it an overall 4.5 to 5 star rating.
