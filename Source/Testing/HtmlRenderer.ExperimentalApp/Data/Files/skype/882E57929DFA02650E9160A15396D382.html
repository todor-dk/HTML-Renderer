Skype Hardware and Accessories from Today's Best Brands

VoIP Supply carries a robust line of Skype hardware and accessories including Skype phones, Skype headsets, Skype video phones and webcams for use with Skype.

All of the Skype hardware and accessories you will find in this category are Skype Certified or are known to work well with Skype so there's no worries about compatibility issues.

And of course every piece of Skype hardware sold by VoIP Supply is fully warranted and supported by our team of in-house experts.

Skype Free Edition vs. Skype for Business

There are two versions of Skype available, here's a quick breakdown:

Skype Free Edition

  • Download for free and connect with anyone
  • Up to 25 people on conference calls
  • Make calls to cell phones, landlines, Skype users
  • Available for Windows, Mac, Linux

Skype for Business

  • Meetings with up to 250 (even non-Skype users)
  • Microsoft Office integration - schedule meetings in Outlook and more
  • Record audio and video meetings
  • Security - strong authentication and encryption
