ooVoo Chains

Chains are a collection of short videos created by you, your friends, and all the other people on ooVoo. Videos, text and images come together to create something unique and original you can’t see anywhere else.

See top videos from around the world in 'Trending', watch your friends’ content in 'Network' or see a mix of other creations from the ooVoo community in 'Random'.

When creating a new chain or adding to an existing one, you will be able to choose between video and text messaging, we'll add more options as we go!

Messaging

Our new messaging puts a focus on what you’ve come to love us for – video.

Record up to 15 seconds and send to your friends. Don’t worry, if you can’t send a video you can send images or text just like always! And all your messages sync from one device to another so you can keep the conversation going on any device and on any platform.

  • Send HD video to your friends
  • Upload and send images
  • Send text when you can’t send a video
  • Quickly send messages from friends' profiles
  • Start a new group chat or join an existing one

Video Chat

ooVoo provides crystal-clear video chat so you can talk to the people who matter most anywhere and anytime - For FREE 💛

Are you looking to FaceTime with more than one person or someone on a different device? We’ve got you covered! Whether a 1-on-1 or a group chat, we allow you to chat with up to 8 friends at once.

Friend isn't available to talk? We’ve added the ability to leave them a video message they can view when they get the chance!
