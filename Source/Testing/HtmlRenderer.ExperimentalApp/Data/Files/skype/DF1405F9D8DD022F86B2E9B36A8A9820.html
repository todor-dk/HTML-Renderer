Skype

Connect with friends, family, and colleagues using Skype for HoloLens. They can see what you see and place holograms into your world.

Brings us together

Share moments with friends, family, and colleagues who are using Skype on other devices, and create new memories together.

Interact in my space

Collaborate with others, and make decisions more effectively, using Skype to draw and place images in your space.

Heads up, hands free

Get instruction without distraction. Keep your hands free to work on real-world projects while you get help from a friend or colleague.

Developer highlights

Here are some insights into the development of this app for Microsoft HoloLens that will help as you start creating your own.

Spatial mapping

Real-time spatial mapping lets users interact with an ever-changing, always up-to-date environment.

Spatial sound

Voices come from the direction of the chat window wherever it’s placed, even if it’s out of view.

Hand tracking

Consider using natural hand movements in your app, like you’d draw or place objects in real life.

Connect to any device

Like Skype, you can create apps that allow HoloLens users to interact with users on other devices.

Don't have HoloLens?

You can use Skype on any device to call a friend who's using Skype for HoloLens, but you'll need to use Skype for Windows to draw holograms and place images in their world.

Developed for mixed reality

See how the first apps developed for HoloLens apps showcase the promise of mixed reality.

  • Actiongram

    Actiongram enables you to move, resize, rotate, and record holograms in your environment, creating videos you can share with friends.

  • HoloTour

    Experience an immersive combination of 360-degree video, spatial sound, and holographic scenery with HoloTour.

  • RoboRaid

    Defend your home from a robot invasion in RoboRaid, the first mixed reality first-person shooter.

  • HoloStudio

    Communicate more effectively when you can show as well as tell.

  • Fragments

    You are the detective in Fragments, a crime thriller. Interact with life-sized characters in mixed reality.

  • Young Conker

    Guide a playful, pint‑sized hero through your world in Young Conker, an ever‑changing adventure game.
