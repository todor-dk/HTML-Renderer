Skype for iPad

By Skype Communications S.a.r.l

View More by This Developer

Open iTunes to buy and download apps.

Description

Stay in touch with free video calls, voice calls and messaging on Skype.

- Talk face to face with a video call.
- New group video calls with up to 25 people.
- Free voice calls to anyone else on Skype. Add up to 25 people on a group call.
- Message your friends in an instant and add up to 300 people to a group chat.
- Call mobiles and landlines at low rates (Skype to Skype calls are always free).
- Share photos, video messages, your location and add emoticons and Mojis to your chat.
- Skype’s available on smartphones, tablets, PCs, Macs, and even some TVs. Now it’s even easier to stay connected to friends and family – no matter the device they’re on.
- Available for Apple Watch – your notifications and chats available on your wrist.

System requirements:
iOS 8.0 or above (If you are using iOS 7 or earlier, you can download the last compatible version by tapping install/update and following the prompt to download.)

Customer Reviews

AN ESSENTIAL!!!
     
by voxfugit

Take some time to really look at all the features this app offers. You will be surprised at how many things you can do on the app and how many face-to-face meetings just this single app can eliminate from your life without the complexities and fees associated with some similar applications.

I have been a regular user of Skype since shortly after it was first released to the public in the early 2000's and you could only do live chatting. Since that time I have grown with the program and have regularly used the iPad app for giving and receiving video lessons with Skype users literally around the world. I've experienced a user sharing her screen to help me understand concepts she sought to illustrate. I have also regularly received phone calls on my cell phone from family in another part of the country which they made to me using Skype.

If the parties have even a decent internet connection, the quality is more than acceptable. (My connections with parties in parts of Africa and China were sometimes poor so we eliminated video and used strictly voice.) If the connection to the net is quite good, the quality of the voice and even video calls is just amazing.

It has so many features now, I won't name them all. Just know that I have at each stage of the app's development benefitted from the improvements and the quality of the support.

This is an essential in my personal and business tool box on both my iPhone and my iPad.
