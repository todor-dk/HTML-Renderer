Skype

Skype is an online chat, voice calling and video calling service that lets users contact other users with a Skype client application. Skype software is available for both computers (Windows and OS X) and smart phones and tablets, including the iPhone, iPad and Android devices. Microsoft acquired Skype in 2011 and in 2013 merged Messenger (also known as MSN Messenger and Windows Live Messenger) with Skype.
