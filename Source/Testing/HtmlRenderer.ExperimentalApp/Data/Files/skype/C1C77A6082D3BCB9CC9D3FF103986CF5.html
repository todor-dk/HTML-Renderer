Skype To Go

  • Keyboard Shortcuts

The Skype to Go feature allows you to place international calls at low rates by setting up phone numbers for contacts. Since the numbers are routed through Skype, you can take advantage of their low international rates.

  • Course Overview
  • Transcript
  • View Offline

- [Instructor] Another innovative feature…through Skype is Skype To Go.…This is a pay as you go service…that allows you to call people all over the world…for the price of a local call…by using Skype as the middle man.…Where as Skye numbers provide a great way…for friends and family to contact you,…Skype To Go is a great way…for you to be able to call your friends and family.…Especially if you're in a different country.…This can save you a lot of money because essentially,…once you have your Skype To Go number from Skype,…you'll dial that number, which will go through Skype,…which will then dial…the other number that you're trying to call.…

The call is placed from a phone and answered on a phone.…But because it goes through Skype,…it'll be considered a local call with cheaper rates.…Let's take a look at how to create a Skype To Go number.…From inside the desktop application,…again I'm going to go to the top,…on the toolbar, click on Skype, and then my account.…And this will bring me to my online Skype account.…

Released

Get up to speed with Skype, the tool for video and voice calling, instant messaging, screen sharing, and more. Oliver Schinkten walks you through the basics, from creating an account and adding contacts to placing video and audio callsone-on-one or group conversations. Oliver demonstrates the best way to set up your equipment to allow for high-quality audio and video. Next, he explains everything you need to know about initiating or receiving calls and chat messages. Oliver covers how to set up a Skype number, make calls to phones, set up voicemail and personal Skype numbers, and place international calls with Skype credits. Finally, he explores the advanced features, such as real-time translation, and the differences between the mobile and desktop versions of Skype.
Topics include:
  • Creating a Skype account
  • Adding contacts
  • Making video and audio-only calls
  • Sharing your screen
  • Messaging with Skype
  • Making calls to mobile phones and landlines
  • Using Skype Translator
