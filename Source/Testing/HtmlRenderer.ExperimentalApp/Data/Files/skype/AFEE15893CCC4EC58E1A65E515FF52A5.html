Who we are

Skype is for doing things together, whenever you’re apart. Our text, voice and video make it simple to share experiences with the people that matter to you, wherever they are. It’s all made possible by the people who work here. And you could be one of them. Our passion lies in engineering incredible products and sharing them with the world. So whether you're an engineer who lives for great code, an awesome program manager or a design guru we have something exciting for you.
