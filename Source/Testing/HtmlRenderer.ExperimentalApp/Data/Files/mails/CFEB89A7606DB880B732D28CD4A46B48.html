My Story: "Life is a choice"

There is nothing that brings me greater joy than helping others. Having an economic and business management degree, I thought I would be in the banking field all my life. As fate would have it, I joined Mains’l after the birth of my twins, looking to have a part-time job. I began working with some amazing people, from whom I have learned so much about life; I am still learning. Having risen from part-time to now a full-time assistant manager testifies to the joy and happiness my job brings me. Supporting people to strengthen their abilities is what keeps me going.

- Haja, current employee
