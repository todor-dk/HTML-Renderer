How to Automatically Forward Email from an Outlook 365 Account to an Off-Campus Account

Page address: https://www.mnsu.edu/its/mavmail/off-campus-email-forward/

Step 1: Log into your Outlook 365 account at https://www.mnsu.edu/its/mavmail/

Step 2: On the upper right-side of the page, choose, "Options"

Step 3: From the drop-down menu, choose "See All Options"

Step 4: In the top-middle of the page, select "Connected Accounts"

Step 5: In the middle of the page, under "Forwarding", type the email address that you would like your mail to be forwarded to

Step 6: Uncheck the box that says "Keep a copy of forwarded message in Outlook Web App" and then click "Start Forwarding"
