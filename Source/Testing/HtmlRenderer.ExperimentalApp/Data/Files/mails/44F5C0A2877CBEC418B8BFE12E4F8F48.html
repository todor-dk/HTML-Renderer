How It Works

MyEmail works by using POP3 and IMAP protocols to check your email, just like popular email clients such as Microsoft Outlook, Mozilla Thunderbird, and Mac Mail. However, unlike those programs MyEmail requires no extra software to download or install. Simply visit the MyEmail.com website and go! This makes MyEmail compatible with any email address as well as any computer or device connected to the internet, whether it be PC, Mac, or even a tablet!
