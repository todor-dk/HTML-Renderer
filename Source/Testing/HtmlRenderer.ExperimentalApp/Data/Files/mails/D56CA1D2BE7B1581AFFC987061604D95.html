Michigan State University is currently running two email systems.

  • Undergraduate students have been migrated to Office 365 (spartanmail.msu.edu).
  • All others are using our primary email service, referred to as webmail, at mail.msu.edu/imp/login.php.

After entering a NetID on this page, individuals are directed to the correct email system based on their role.

Email addresses in both systems are NetID@msu.edu (e.g., sparty@msu.edu where "sparty" is the MSU NetID).

Office 365 Details

Students who have been migrated to Office 365 should use their MSU email address (e.g. sparty@msu.edu) with their MSU NetID password to login to Office 365.

Note: Passwords in Office 365 should be the same password that was set up and used for your MSU NetID. If this is the first time logging into Office 365 and the MSU NetID password does not work, please change your NetID password so it syncs with Office 365. It may take up to 5 minutes to sync.

If you have trouble logging into Office 365, please contact the MSU IT Service Desk at (517) 432-6200 or ithelp@msu.edu.
