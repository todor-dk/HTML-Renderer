University Webmail Services

Email services for students, faculty and staff

The Ohio State University

For Students

BuckeyeMail

  • BuckeyeMail is Ohio State's student-only email service and uses Microsoft Outlook Live. Learn more about BuckeyeMail.
  • To log in, click the BuckeyeMail button and use your lastname.#@buckeyemail.osu.edu (Windows Live ID) and password to access your account. NOTE: To use this service, you first need to activate your Ohio State Username (OSU Identity) at my.osu.edu.

For Faculty and Staff

University Email Service

  • University Email Service is Ohio State's email and calendaring service for faculty and staff and uses Microsoft Exchange and Outlook Web App. Learn more about University Email Service.
  • To log in, click on the University Email Service button and use your Ohio State Username and password (OSU Identity) to access your account. To activate your Ohio State Username, go to my.osu.edu.

    More resources:

NOTE: The Ohio State University will NEVER ask for your account information by email.

If you receive such a message, please report it to report-phish@osu.edu. NEVER reply to any email asking for your account information or other personal details. For more information, contact the IT Service Desk by calling 614-688-HELP (4357).
