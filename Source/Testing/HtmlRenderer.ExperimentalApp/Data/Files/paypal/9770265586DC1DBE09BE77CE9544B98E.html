How to Connect to PayPal

Connect Stamps.com to PayPal and easily import, manage and ship your orders right from our easy-to-use interface.

• Import order data directly from PayPal
• Print shipping labels and packing slips in one step
• Automatically get the best rates based on preferred delivery speed
• Automatically send tracking info to your customers

NOTE:  A PayPal Business Plan is required to connect with Stamps.com

  • Online
  • Software

Step by Step

1. Log in to Stamps.com and select Orders.

2. In Orders, Click the Settings icon...

 and Select Add/Edit Stores.

3. The Manage Stores window will open.  Click Add.

4. Select PayPal.

5. The Connect your PayPal Store window will open.  Click the link in the window or go to: https://www.paypal.com/home

6.  The PayPal Log In window will open.  Enter your PayPal Email and Password, then Click Log In.

7. In PayPal, Select Profile.

8. Click My selling tools.

9. Click Update next to API Access.

10. Click Grant API Permission.

11. Enter the Third Party Permission Username: paypalaccount_api1.stamps.com,  then Click Lookup.

12. The Available Permissions window will open.  Check the indicated boxes, then Click Add.

13. Return to the Connect your PayPal Store window in Orders, enter your PayPal Email Address, and Click Test Connection.

14. The PayPal Email Verification window will open.  Click Send Email Verification to send a verification email to your PayPal Email Address.

15. Follow the instructions in the email to finish the store profile creation process.  If you can't find the email, check your email Spam folder.

16.  The PayPal Settings window will open.  Give your PayPal Store a personalized Nickname and Click Save.  This Nickname is how your PayPal Store will appear in Orders.

 

You can customize your PayPal Store now, or at any time in the future, with our advanced Settings features.  Learn more about Store Settings Details.

17. Your PayPal Store is displayed in the Manage Store window.  Close Manage Stores.

Congratulations!  Your PayPal Store is now connected to Stamps.com Orders and ready to retrieve your orders.  

How to Import Orders from PayPal

In Orders, Click the Refresh icon.

PayPal Store Information:

  • Stamps.com will only import orders with the Completed payment status.
  • PayPal does not have a shipping status.  Stamps.com will import orders with changed payment status since last import.
  • PayPal does not support automatic shipping information return to your account.  Copy the tracking number from your Stamps.com Print History Transaction Details, click Add Tracking Info at the bottom of your PayPal Order Details page, and paste the tracking number into PayPal.  The tracking number will be included in the shipment notification email sent to your customer.

 

Step by Step:

1.  Log in to your Stamps.com PC Software account and select the Batch feature.

2.  In the Batch feature, click Manage Data Sources.

3.  Click Add Source.

4.  Select PayPal and click Next.

 

5.  The PayPal Profile window will open.  Enter a nickname for your PayPal Profile.  

 

6.  Enter your PayPal ID.  

 

7.   Click Proceed to PayPal.

8.  Log in to your PayPal account.

9.  Click Grant Permission.  This will allow Stamps.com to retrieve orders from PayPal.

10.  Stamps.com will now connect with your PayPal account.  When finished, a confirmation window will open.

11.  Close the confirmation window.  The Profile Options window will open.  Set your shipping label preferences for:

  • Return Address
  • Sender Phone Number
  • Return Address Graphic
  • Hidden Postage fields

 

Click Done when finished. 

12.  Click Done again to close the Manage Profiles pop-up.

Congratulations! Your PayPal  account is now connected to Stamps.com and ready to retrieve your orders. 

Your PayPal Profile will appear in the Batch profile tree on the left side of the Batch Feature. 

How to Import Orders from PayPal.

In the Batch feature, simply click Import Orders.

Stamps.com imports orders from PayPal based on the following:

  • When importing orders from your PayPal account for the first time Stamps.com will retrieve orders up to 2 days old.  After that, orders up to 7 days will be retrieved.
  • Stamps.com will only import orders with payment status Completed.
  • PayPal does not have a shipping status.  Stamps.com will import orders with changed payment status since last import.
  • PayPal does not support automatic shipping information return to your account.  Copy the tracking number from your Stamps.com Print History Transaction Details, click Add Tracking Info at the bottom of your PayPal Order Details page, and paste the tracking number into PayPal.  The tracking number will be included in the shipment notification email sent to your customer.
