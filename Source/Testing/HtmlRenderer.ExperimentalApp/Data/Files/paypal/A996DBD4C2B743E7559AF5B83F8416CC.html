Streamline your payments and get paid sooner with PayPal and Xero

Accept more payment options – credit cards, debit cards and PayPal payments – and let customers pay online in a couple of clicks. Making it easy for your customers to pay means less time chasing money, so you can spend more time on other parts of your business.

With PayPal and Xero you can:

  • Be up and away in no time: Xero have made it easy to set up Paypal as your payment provider.

  • Get paid faster: Email customised invoices straight from your Xero account and get paid online instantly with PayPal.

  • Enjoy hassle-free follow ups: Set payment reminders from within Xero.

  • Manage your business on the go: Use Xero’s mobile app to send invoices and get paid with PayPal wherever you are.

  • Accept payments from around the world: Accept credit cards, debit cards and PayPal payments in more than 25 currencies

Helpful information about Paypal

Who is it for?

PayPal and Xero work together to support any small business that needs to invoice customers.

How does it work with Xero?

Once you’ve linked your PayPal and Xero accounts:

  1. A ‘Pay now’ button appears on your invoices.
  2. Your customers simply click the button and either pay with their PayPal account or enter their credit or debit card details.
  3. You receive the money in your PayPal account straight away.

How do you sync it with Xero?

You only need to link your PayPal and Xero accounts once. You can either do this in your Xero settings or the next time you send an invoice.

In your Xero settings:

  1. Go to General Settings and click Payment Services.
  2. Click Add Payment Service and select PayPal.
  3. Enter your PayPal account email address and click Save.

From a Xero invoice:

  1. In Accept Online Payments, click Set up.
  2. From the Select Provider drop-down, choose PayPal.
  3. Enter your PayPal account email address and click Save.

Once linked, all of your future invoices are sent with a Pay Now button so your customers can pay you immediately.
