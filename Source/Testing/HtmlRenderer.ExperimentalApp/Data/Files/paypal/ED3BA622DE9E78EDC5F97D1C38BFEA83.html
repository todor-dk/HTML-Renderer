Payment via

PayPal allows you to receive money electronically and then transfer the money to a regular bank account (or you may keep the money in your PayPal account). All we need to send you money via PayPal is your email address. If you do not yet have a PayPal account, PayPal will send you instructions on how to set one up. When selling your books to Cash4Books.net, you are given the choice of a PayPal or check payment preference. We recommend the choice of PayPal.

Reasons to choose PayPal for your Cash4Books.net payment option:

  • Faster than a check (once we receive your books and issue payment, the money is available in your PayPal account instantly).
  • More secure than a paper check traveling through the mail system.
  • More convenient - the money can be easily transferred from your PayPal account to your bank.
  • No fees. You will not be charged any PayPal fees when receiving money from Cash4Books.net, because we pay the fees for you! Thus, you get 100% of the funds by not having to pay the 2.9% plus $0.30 USD fee that PayPal usually charges to receive money.

Existing users may also choose to later change their payment preference to PayPal, by updating your preferences under your Account.

From PayPal's website:

"With more than 40 million member accounts in over 45 countries worldwide, PayPal is the world's largest online payment service. PayPal makes sending money as easy as sending email! Any PayPal member can instantly and securely send money to anyone in the U.S. with an email address."

Get cash for your used books by choosing PayPal as your payment option!

How it works

How does all this work?

We try very hard to make selling your books as simple as possible.

Following the three step process on our homepage, you start by simply entering the ISBNs of the books you want to sell into the form. Ship your books to us for FREE, and then collect your money via PayPal or check.

Easy, straightforward, and hassle-free!

When will I get my money?

On average, it takes 13 days from the time you click "sell my books" to when payment is issued. It can be shorter or longer depending on what shipping method is used, and your proximity to Hillsboro, Oregon. After your books are received, inspected and marked as 'processed', payment will be made. Payment is sent within one business day of 'processed' status.

We offer payment via check or PayPal.

What kinds of books do you buy?

We buy mostly college textbooks, but sometimes other books as well. View our example 500 list page, which shows examples of books that we commonly purchase.

Most of the books we purchase have a publication date within the past three years. What we buy can change day by day or week by week depending on resale values, our current inventory, demand, and other factors.

How will I ship my books to Cash4Books.net?

Shipping is always FREE with our pre-paid mailing label that you print right from your computer.

If you do not have a printer, simply contact us to request US Post Office shipping labels to be mailed to you.

Further instructions on FedEx or the US Post Office are provided after you submit your books to be sold. Also, if you need to know where to get a box to mail your books, please read our frequently asked questions.
