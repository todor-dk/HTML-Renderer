Rewarding Shopping with PayPal Extras MasterCard

The PayPal Extras MasterCard is PayPal`s solution to your credit needs. Not only will you receive a secure credit line backed by PayPal, MasterCard, and GE Money Bank, but you`ll also receive access to the PayPal Rewards program.

The PayPal Rewards program can offer you things such as PayPal Cash, gift cards, merchandise, travel vouchers, and more. With the PayPal Extras MasterCard you`ll earn reward points for every dollar spent on gas, at restaurants, on PayPal and eBay purchases.

With the PayPal debit MasterCard, you can withdraw money from your PayPal balance at any ATM and will receive 1% cash back on your purchases all while being backed by the same big names behind the PayPal Extras MasterCard. It`s a great option for those who have a balance in their PayPal account and don`t want to have to wait to transfer it.

To apply for PayPal credit cards, you can go online and begin the application process. In as little as ten seconds after the applying, you can be earning PayPal rewards.
