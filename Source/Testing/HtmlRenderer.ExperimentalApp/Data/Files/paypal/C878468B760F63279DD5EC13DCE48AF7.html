PayPal

A fast, safe and easy checkout

Pay with personal accounts without sharing secure information

On aa.com, once you select PayPal as your payment method, you’ll be redirected to their website to log in and confirm your payment. If your PayPal payment is declined for any reason, your reservation will be cancelled immediately.

If you don’t already have a PayPal account, you can create a free account today to get started.

Accounts you can use with PayPal

  • Credit card (Visa, MasterCard, and Discover)
  • Debit card (for UK customers, Maestro, Solo, and Visa Electron)
  • Bank checking or savings
  • Your PayPal account – any unused balance*

Countries that accept PayPal

  • U.S. (including Puerto Rico and U.S. Virgin Islands)
  • Canada
  • UK

*If you choose to pay using a UK billed PayPal account, a processing fee of £4.50 per ticket will apply.
