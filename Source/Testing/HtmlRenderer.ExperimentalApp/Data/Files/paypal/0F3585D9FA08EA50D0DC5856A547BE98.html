Offer good benefits, lots of advancement opportunities, learn a lot
Customer Service Representative, La Vista, NE - July 10, 2017
The company really cares about their employees. Although I didn't need any of them, they offer great benefits. Pay is good. The managers constantly work with you to help you improve and I learned a lot; I believe working here really enhanced my customer service skills.

Cons: Once I moved into a different department, I was constantly busy. Sometimes I had to go on a quick minute or 2 break just to take a drink of water. It can be stressful some days. Also, I really missed having face-to-face, more personal, interaction with customers.

About PayPal

PayPal allows any business or individual with an email address to securely, conveniently and cost-effectively send and receive payments online. Our network builds on the existing financial infrastructure of bank accounts and credit cards to create a global, real-time payment solution. We deliver a product ideally suited for small businesses, online – more... merchants, individuals and others currently underserved by traditional payment mechanisms.
The size of our network and widening acceptance of our product have helped us become one of the leading payment networks for online auction websites. PayPal is also being increasingly used on other ecommerce sites for the sale of goods such as electronics and household items, the sale of services such as web design and travel, and the sale of digital content. Offline businesses, including lawyers, contractors and doctors, have increasingly begun to receive payments online through PayPal. PayPal's service, which lets users send payments for free, can be used from computers or web-enabled mobile phones. – less
