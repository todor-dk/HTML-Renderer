Product description

Effective July 1, 2016, only the PayPal mobile web experience is available for Amazon devices. The latest PayPal app for Amazon will now take you to the mobile web experience where you can still send peer-to-peer payments and have many of the same features that were provided in the app previously. Older app versions of PayPal will no longer be supported.


SEND & REQUEST MONEY
Pay back a friend for lunch. Chip in for a gift. Collect money for a work outing. PayPal lets you transfer money between friends instantly.

MANAGE YOUR ACCOUNT
Check your balance, see your transactions, and more —anytime, anywhere.

EVEN MORE…
Online or on-the-go, your financial info is always secure with PayPal.
