What is Paypal

Paypal is known as one of the easiest and popular ways to pay online. If you ask yourself what is Paypal, than the simplest answer is this: a tool that allows you to safely send and receive money via email. The company is owned by eBay, and it is a global online payment system. With this online payment system you can send money from your credit/debit card, transfer money to and from your bank account. Use it whenever you need to send money to someone (settle a debt with a friend, pay for online purchase/auctions...) or when someone needs to send money to you. Paypal system is an easy way for individuals and merchants to send and receive money online for goods, services, charitable donations, and more. As stated on their official website adress.

How does Paypal work

Paypal works this way: You create an account (click here -> it will open in a new tab). After you register you fund your account. You send payments to anyone with an email address.

How to send money with Paypal

Log into your PayPal account and select Click the Send Money tab. Fill out the form and click Continue. Review the details of the payment, including the payment method. Click Send Money to send the payment. Here you can find more information about how to send money online using Paypal.

How to receive money from Paypal

You have to provide your email address to the person that wants to send you money. Anytime someone sends you money using Paypal, the funds will be instantly credited to your account balance. When someone sends you Paypal money, you receive a You've Got a Payment email. Open your account and click the History link to view your payment. After this you can withdraw the money from your account to the credit card or bank account. You can also withdraw a Paypal check. This way it takes longer, but the Paypal check is available for many users who can't validate their credit cards, or do not have a bank account. Here you can find more information about how to receive money online using Paypal.

Why should I use Paypal

You can securely send and receive online payments:
  • You can pay for online purchases without revealing your credit card information (Mastercard, Visa, Maestro...)
  • You can accept credit cards, and the one who will send the money will feel secure.
  • You can send and request money worldwide.
  • Track your online spendings

How much does it cost me?

As many people already know, it's always free to send money. You always send the same amount of money without having to pay extra. But personal accounts can receive a limited number of credit or debit card funded payments per year and are assessed a transaction fee. To view your Personal account limit, log in to your account and click the View Limits link. Members with a Paypal Business account or Paypal Premier account are charged a low transaction fee for receiving payments. For more information, consult the fee schedule. If you want to see it in numbers, consult the list with the transaction fees.

In what countries does Paypal work?

Many people ask themself: "Does Paypal work in my country?" The truth is that Paypal services work for many countries. With a network of more than 190 countries and regions, and in multiple currencies, the service reaches across the globe to give you or your business a way to pay or get paid. You can see all the available countries on the Paypal website here. You can see the countries in which you can send, receive and withdraw, and the ones in which you can only withdraw.

Does Paypal work in India?

Yes. It works just like in the other countries. For more information visit the Paypal India sectioin this site.
