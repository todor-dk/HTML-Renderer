The Global Leader in Fast, Easy and Secure Online Payments!

Overview

PayPal has several secure solutions to meet your business needs so that you can accept credit card, bank transfer, debit card and PayPal balance payments on your website and via email!

PayPal is just what you need to benefit both your customers and your business. You can add PayPal to your website in minutes--no downtime required.

Features

In just a few easy steps, you can add Buy Now buttons or a shopping cart, send professional invoices by email and customize your website checkout.

With a business account you have access to:
  • PayPal's reporting tools.
  • A multiple currency manager.
  • Shipping and tax calculators.
  • And more!

Benefits

PayPal gives your business access to a growing user base with millions of active online shoppers.

What are the other benefits of using PayPal?
  • It's Fast. Payments are made immediately so you get the funds quickly in your PayPal account.
  • It's Global. PayPal is accepted worldwide and can be used to make payments locally or internationally.
  • It's Easy. Customers can shop and pay directly on your website or check out in a few clicks on PayPal.
