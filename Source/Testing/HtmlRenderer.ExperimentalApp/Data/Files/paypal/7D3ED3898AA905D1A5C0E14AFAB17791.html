Use PayPal with your Microsoft account

PayPal can be used as a payment option in certain countries and regions to buy Xbox Live Gold subscriptions and content such as games, apps, music, HD movies, and TV shows on your Xbox 360 console, Xbox One console, and Xbox.com.
