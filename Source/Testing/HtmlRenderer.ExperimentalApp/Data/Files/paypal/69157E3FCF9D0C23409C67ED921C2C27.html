PayPal

Offer Your Customers Another Way to Pay with PayPal.

Get Started Today
Home › Payment Processing › Payment Gateway ›  PayPal

PayPal has become a household name for facilitating money transfers made over the internet for payment. BluePay supports PayPal as a method of payment in the form of a secure hosted payment page. BluePay lets you choose from standard or customizable starting and return pages.

By integrating PayPal, you can benefit from:

  • Ability to accept one-time or recurring payments from the millions of PayPal customers worldwide
  • All payment data processed on a single platform for ease of reporting and reconciliation
  • Increased sales by offering your customers an alternative way to pay

To use PayPal, you will be required to have a PayPal business account that can be linked to your merchant ID on the BluePay Gateway. All of your PayPal transactions will be conveniently reported alongside your other transactions for fast and simple reconciliation. Transactions are subject to PayPal’s standard fees.
