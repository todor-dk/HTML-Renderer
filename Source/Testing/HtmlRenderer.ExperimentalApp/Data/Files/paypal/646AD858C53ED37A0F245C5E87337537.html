PayPal

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

PayPal is a website that allows money to be transferred between various email addresses. Someone who is sent money with this system can send it to other people, or withdraw it into a bank account. PayPal was owned by eBay from 2002[1][2] to 2015.[3] PayPal can be used in more than 200 countries.[4]
