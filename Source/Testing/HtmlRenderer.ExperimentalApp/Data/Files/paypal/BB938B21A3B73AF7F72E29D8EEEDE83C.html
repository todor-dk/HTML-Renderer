PAYPAL FAQ

Is there a fee for depositing with PayPal?

Yes. There is a $5 fee on every PayPal deposit. PayPal deposits will not be refunded as part of any current refunding/waiver program.

Is there a minimum or maximum deposit amount for depositing with PayPal?

Yes. The minimum deposit is $10. The maximum amount is determined by PayPal’s proprietary risk-assessment.

Will all users automatically be able to deposit with PayPal?

Most users with a bank account confirmed PayPal account will be able to transfer funds automatically. PayPal may apply funding limitations.

Can I deposit with PayPal using credit/debit cards on my PayPal Account?

No. You may only deposit with PayPal using a PayPal balance, or the banking-account associated with your PayPal account.

Is there a delay in the funds being retrieved from PayPal?

Funds transferred from PayPal will be available immediately. Occasionally there may be a slight delay in reflecting the funds in your TVG PABets 4NJBets account balance. If funds are not available within five minutes please call 1-888-PLAY-TVG.

Can I deposit with PayPal using an international PayPal Account?

No. You must use a US-based PayPal account.

Can I deposit with PayPal using a business PayPal Account?

No. You must use a personal PayPal account.

Can I use multiple PayPal accounts to fund my TVG PABets 4NJBets account?

No. You may only fund one TVG PABets 4NJBets account with your PayPal account.

On what channels/platforms can I deposit with PayPal?

Users may deposit with PayPal on the NEW TVG.com PABets.com. 4NJBets and TVG Mobile. and 4NJBets Mobile.

Can I withdraw funds to PayPal?

Yes! You can withdraw funds from your TVG PABets 4NJBets account to your PayPal account anytime. Transferred funds may take up to 5 business days to clear.
