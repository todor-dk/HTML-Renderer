Description

The Sync with PayPal free app automatically imports your PayPal sales transactions, fees, taxes, and discounts into QuickBooks.

Securely import your PayPal sales transactions, fees, taxes, tips, and discounts into QuickBooks

Get started with just a few clicks. Once you allow QuickBooks to access your PayPal account, your data is automatically and securely imported into QuickBooks.

Sales receipts are automatically created

We'll create sales receipts in QuickBooks for you, while syncing other key details, such as the sale amount, discounts, fees, tips, and taxes.

Expenses and bank transfers are downloaded

Your expenses and bank transfers are downloaded into default categories within your QuickBooks account.

Historical import of your past transactions

You can import up to 18 months of your past transactions with just one click.

Sync your customer information from PayPal into QuickBooks

We give you the option to auto-sync your customer information from PayPal into QuickBooks, saving you time by filling in your customer information in QuickBooks for your convenience.

Manage how your data shows up in QuickBooks

You can update your app settings at any time to ensure your transactions are imported the way you want. You can also decide what information is important and should be imported.

How does Sync with PayPal work?
Sync with PayPal imports your sales transactions, bank transfers, and expenses from PayPal into QuickBooks. Each sale in PayPal is synced to QuickBooks as a sales receipt, with detailed information such as the item sold, quantity, discounts, taxes, and so on. PayPal fees are also imported as an expense, so fees are recognized and appear correctly in your accounts. Likewise, bank transfers (to or from PayPal) and PayPal expenses are also imported to keep your account accurate.
How is Sync with PayPal different from the bank download for PayPal in QuickBooks Online?
The main difference between the Bank Connection center in QuickBooks and the Sync with PayPal app is the way certain transactions are handled. Only one of these connections can be active at the same time. The PayPal integration app is automatically turned off if the Bank Connection center integration is turned on. We are working with PayPal to find a way to allow both integrations and merge the transactions.
How much does Sync with PayPal cost?
The best part is that this helpful app is free to all U.S. QuickBooks customers. Sync with PayPal is currently available in the U.S. only.
