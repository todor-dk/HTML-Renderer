Wences Casares

Wences Casares has served as a director of PayPal since January 2016. He is the Founder of Xapo Inc., a bitcoin wallet and vault startup, and has served as Chief Executive Officer since March 2014. From October 2011 to March 2014, Mr. Casares was Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Lemon Inc., a digital wallet platform.  From March 2007 to October 2011, Mr. Casares was Co-Chief Executive Officer of Bling Nation Ltd., a mobile payments platform.  He also serves on the Board of Directors of Kiva.org and Endeavor Global, Inc.

Member of the Compensation Committee
Jonathan Christodoro

Jonathan Christodoro has served as a director of PayPal since July 2015. He served as a board member of eBay Inc. from March 2015 to July 2015. Mr. Christodoro served as a Managing Director of Icahn Capital LP, the entity through which Carl C. Icahn manages investment funds, from July 2012 to February 2017.  He also serves on the Board of Directors of Cheniere Energy, Inc., Enzon Pharmaceuticals, Inc., Herbalife Ltd., Lyft, Inc., and Xerox Corporation. 

Member of the Compensation Committee
John J. Donahoe

John J. Donahoe has served as Chairman of the PayPal Board since July 2015. Since April 2017, Mr. Donahoe has served as the President and Chief Executive Officer of ServiceNow, Inc., an enterprise cloud company. He served as the President and Chief Executive Officer of eBay Inc. from March 2008 to July 2015, and a director from January 2008 to July 2015. Mr. Donahoe also serves on the Board of Directors of ServiceNow, Inc., and Nike, Inc.

David W. Dorman

David W. Dorman has served as a director of PayPal since June 2015. He served as a board member of eBay Inc. from June 2014 to July 2015. Mr. Dorman has been the Non-Executive Chairman of the Board of CVS Health Corporation, a pharmacy healthcare provider, since May 2011, and is the former Chairman and CEO of AT&T. He is also Founding Partner of Centerview Capital, a private investment firm, since July 2013.  Mr. Dorman is a Trustee for Georgia Tech Foundation, Inc.

Member of the Nominating and Governance Committee
Chair of the Compensation Committee
Belinda Johnson

Belinda J. Johnson has served as a director of PayPal since January 2017. She has been the Chief Business Affairs and Legal Officer of Airbnb, Inc., a global community marketplace which provides access to unique accommodations and experiences, since July 2015, having joined as General Counsel in December 2011. Prior to Airbnb until August 2011, Ms. Johnson served as Senior Vice President and Deputy General Counsel of Yahoo! Inc., a digital information platform. Ms. Johnson also served in other various positions at Yahoo! Inc. from August 1999. From November 1996 to August 1999, Ms. Johnson was General Counsel of Broadcast.com, Inc., an internet broadcasting company.

Member of the Audit, Risk and Compliance Committee
Gail J. McGovern

Gail J. McGovern has served as a director of PayPal since June 2015. She served as a board member of eBay Inc. from March 2015 to July 2015. Since June 2008, Ms. McGovern has served as the President and Chief Executive Officer of the American Red Cross, a humanitarian organization. She serves as a trustee of John Hopkins Medicine, and a director of DTE Energy Company and as an advisor to The Weather Company.

Chair of the Nominating and Governance Committee
Member of the Compensation Committee
David M. Moffett

David M. Moffett has served as a director of PayPal since June 2015 and as a Lead Independent Director of PayPal since July 2015. He served as a board member of eBay Inc. from July 2007 to July 2015. Mr. Moffett served as Chief Executive Officer of Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corp. (Freddie Mac) from September 2008 until his retirement in March 2009. Mr. Moffett serves on the Board of Directors of Genworth Financial, Inc. and he also serves as a Trustee for Columbia Atlantic Mutual Funds and University of Oklahoma Foundation and as a consultant to various financial services companies.

Chair of the Audit, Risk and Compliance Committee
Ann M. Sarnoff

Ann M. Sarnoff has served as a director of PayPal since June 2017. Since August 2015, Ms. Sarnoff has served as the President of BBC Worldwide North America, a media company that delivers high-quality, innovative and intelligent programming.  From 2010 through July 2015, she served as Chief Operating Officer of BBC Worldwide North America.  She also sits on the board, the operating committee and the editorial committee of BBC America, a joint venture with AMC Networks.  From June 2006 until joining the BBC Worldwide in 2010, Ms. Sarnoff was President of Dow Jones Ventures and Senior Vice President of Strategy for Dow Jones & Company, Inc. Ms. Sarnoff serves on the Board of Directors of HSN, Inc.  She is also a member of the board of Georgetown University as well as the vice chair of the McDonough School of Business at Georgetown.  She is the vice president of the board of The Women’s Forum of New York and on the board of the Harvard Business School Women’s Association of New York.

Member of the Audit, Risk and Compliance Committee
Daniel H. Schulman

Daniel H. Schulman has served as President and Chief Executive Officer of PayPal since July 2015. He joined PayPal as the company’s President in September 2014. Mr. Schulman previously served as Group President of Enterprise Growth at American Express where he led global strategy to expand the company’s alternative and online payments services. He also serves on the Board of Directors of Flex Ltd. and Symantec Corporation.

Frank D. Yeary

Frank D. Yeary has served as a director of PayPal since July 2015. He served as a board member of eBay Inc. from January 2015 to July 2015. Mr. Yeary has been the Chairman of CamberView Partners, LLC, a corporate advisory firm, since 2012. He also serves on the Board of Directors of Intel Corporation.

Member of the Audit, Risk and Compliance Committee
