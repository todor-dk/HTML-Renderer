CrickBuzz App Download For iPhone or iPad

Crickbuzz or Cricbuzz is the most in-demand live cricket app ever. This amazing app which is currently owned by Times Internet has over 50 million users. And it draws a revenue of 7.8 million dollars. There are way too many reasons for this enormous success that this app has seen in this time. With new apps being released every day it is quite difficult for an app to make it to the top these days. But Cricbuzz is an app that saw the light of popularity despite all odds when it was released by the independent creators Piyush Agrawal, Pankaj Chhaparwal and Pravin Hegde. These three had released the service in 2004 and developed the app version in 2010. Later in 2014, Times Internet bought the majority of the share.

There is more to this background of Cricbuzz. After Times Internet had acquired most of the stake in the company, they merged another service named GoCricket with this. And they did. After that, Cricbuzz has been the sole representative of the cricket front. Now that we have taken a look at the technical details let us have a brief overview of the app. After this overview, you will find a detailed step by step installation guide for iOS devices. So keep reading.

Cricbuzz or CrickBuzz:

There are way too many features to talk about. Crickbuzz has an amazing and optimized live score viewer. With the app open, the users can see ball by ball progress as opposed to the in-app notification service that gives an overview of an over. To add to this, there is also a Live Cricket Score and live commentary service.

You can also see the match highlights or read the news articles right from this app. And if you are looking for a complete fixture of the tournament or just keeping an eye out for the upcoming matches, Cricbuzz Cricinfo is where you need to look. Upon opening the app, you get to see a fixture of the ongoing tournament and the upcoming matches.

Cricket fans, brace yourselves for more. There is a separate section that is dedicated to the statistics and ranks of each and every team and individual player. You will get to see the performance of the teams as a whole in a year. That stands true for a particular cricketer too. All you need to know about cricket is right there at your fingertips with Cricbuzz app.

Installing Cricbuzz on iOS:

It is no wonder that Crickbuzz is available for downloads in the iTunes App Store for iOS devices. It is also available for Android devices in the Google Play Store. However, here is a detailed guide on how to get this app to run on iOS devices line iPhone, iPad, iPod Touch.

  •    Open the iTunes App Store on your iPhone or iPad or iPod Touch.
  •    Now search for the app using the search button located at the bottom of the screen.
  •    After you locate the app, click on it.
  •    The app details window will open.
  •    Locate and tap on the ‘Get’ button.
  •    You will be asked to sign in.
  •    Enter your Apple ID.
  •    Enter the password.
  •    Press OK.
  •    After the installation is complete, tap on the Open button to launch the app.
