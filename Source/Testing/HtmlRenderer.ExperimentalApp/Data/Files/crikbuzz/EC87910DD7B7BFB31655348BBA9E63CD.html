Cricket has millions of fans all over the world, being especially popular in such countries as India, United Kingdom and others. And, it goes without saying that many of them are also looking to play cricket games on their Windows 8 tablet and use apps to follow live cricket games. We found 4 of the best such apps to use.

If you will go right now in the Windows Store and will start looking for “cricket” related apps and games, a plethora of Windows 8 apps will appear in front of your eyes. The big problem is that so many of them are useless and not worth downloading on your Windows 8 tablet. We hand-picked 4 of the best Windows 8 cricket apps and games for you to use. ESPN Cricinfo

This is the best Windows 8 live cricket app that you need to download on your Windows 8 tablet. By using the app, you will get full cricket coverage from around the world and get real-time cricket results on your Windows 8 tablet. Besides this, you will get the most important cricket news, features and editorial content from the ESPN Cricinfo team. All wrapped under a great looking app. The latest update also brought ush notifications for wickets and results.

World Cricket Championship Pro

The World Cricket Championship Pro is the best Windows 8 cricket game for touchscreen devices. We will review it shortly in a separate article, but there’s a trial option available, so before paying the full price, you can see if this cricket game is for you or not. The game is a mix of World Cricket Championship, World Premier League and Fantasy Cricket League and is a must-download for cricket lovers all over the world.

Cricbuzz

Just like ESPN Cricinfo, Cricbuzz is a Windows 8 cricket app that you need to download if you want to stay updated with the latest news from the world of cricket. Get quick score updates and ball by ball text commentary, match alerts, latest cricket news and editorials, results of recent cricket matches, photos, audio commentary, player profiles and points tables for all major tournaments by installing Cricbuzz on your Windows 8 tablet.

Street Cricket


Published by the same company that has made the World Cricket Championship Pro Windows 8 game, the Street Cricket game comes with similar graphics in a game of only five.

The rough play, the dust, the nostalgia of cricket in your own gully; that is what Street Cricket is all about! In this version the rules are modified to suit the ground situation while the usual excitement of the game remains intact.
