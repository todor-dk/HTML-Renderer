I know what you’re thinking; “Why would I want to download all the videos in a YouTube playlist?”.

It’s a fair question after all, we live in an age where everything from your phone to your fridge has an internet connection. We can get any video we want in a matter of seconds.

If you want to download a whole TV series to watch when you’re traveling, or perhaps a playlist of videos about a hobby or a foreign language, it makes sense to download the files on your desktop then save them onto your mobile device.

In this article, I’m, going to introduce you to five apps and tools that will let you download an entire YouTube playlist in just a few easy clicks.

1. 4K Video Downloader

Available On: Windows, Mac, Linux

Lots of video downloading apps are saturated with adware, spyware, and aggressive toolbars. 4K Video Downloader is one of the few exceptions.

And it does a whole lot more than merely downloading playlists. You can download entire channels with a single click, ask the app to automatically download new videos from individual channels as soon as they become available, and even download YouTube’s new 3D and 360-degree videos The 10 Most Insane 360-Degree Videos From Facebook The 10 Most Insane 360-Degree Videos From Facebook You've probably seen a couple of Facebook's mesmerizing 360-degree videos already. Most are great, but some are downright incredible. Here are 10 of our favorites. Read More .

One of its noteworthyfeatures is the ability to download any associated subtitles. You can either embed them into the video file itself or save them as a separate SRT file. The videos themselves can be saved as MP4, MKV, M4A, MP3, FLV, or 3G.

If you decide to download a playlist, the app with automatically generate an M3U playlist file so you can easily play your content in sequence on an app. The downloading process is simple; just paste a YouTube link into the main window and let the app do its thing.

The app also supports downloading videos from other popular sites such as Vimeo, Facebook, and DailyMotion.

2. Gihosoft TubeGet

Available On: Windows, Mac

Like 4K Video Download, Gihosoft TubeGet covers a whole lot more than just YouTube. The app supports more than 10,000 sites, including Facebook, Dailymotion, Vimeo, Metacafe, Break, Veoh, and Blip.tv.

The app can download single videos and entire playlists. To download a playlist, just grab its URL and click +PasteURL in the top left-hand corner of the screen.

You’ll then be prompted to choose either the format (MP4, WebM, 3GP, FLV, AVI, or MKV) and the quality (4K, 1440P, 1080P, 720P, 480P, 360P, or 240P). When you’re ready, click Download. The app will download up to five videos at a time.

If you’ve got a slow internet connection but want to download a large playlist, don’t worry. You can limit the download speed so it does not interfere with any other online activities you’re working on.

Lastly, the app also includes an MP3 extraction tool; it’ll let you save the audio of a video without needing to download the whole video file.

Available On: Windows, Mac, Linux

To begin, fire up the app and head to Media > Open Network Stream. Paste the URL of the YouTube video you want to download and hit Play.

After the video begins, go to Tools > Codec and copy the link in the Location box.

Paste the link into your browser and press Enter. Once again, the video will start playing automatically. Right-click on the video and select Save Video As.

4. MegaVN.com

Available On: All web browsers

If you don’t have VLC player and you don’t trust third-party apps, you can try using a web app 18 Free Ways to Download Any Video Off the Internet 18 Free Ways to Download Any Video Off the Internet Video is the future, as it's now everywhere you look online. There are hundreds of web apps that will let you download individual videos, but ones that offer a way to download whole playlists are much harder to find.

At the time of writing, the best one is arguably MegaVN. Click the Playlist/Channel tab in the top right-hand corner of the screen and paste the link of the playlist you want.

There are a couple of other features that help make this the best web app. Firstly, like 4K Downloader, it will let you download the entire back catalog of a particular channel. Secondly, it gives you a way to download country-restricted videos using a proxy. The app even goes as far as to supply you with a list of proxies to choose from.

5. KeepVid

Available On: Windows, Mac

I’ll conclude the list with another downloadable app. KeepVid is in the same mold as 4K Video Downloader and Gihosoft TubeGet. You download the app on your desktop and paste in the links of any videos or playlists that you want.
