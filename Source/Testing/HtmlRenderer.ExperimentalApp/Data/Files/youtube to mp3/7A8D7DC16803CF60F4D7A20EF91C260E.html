YouTube songs to MP3 player


O.k. you have just downloaded a bunch of songs with the Music From YouTube program. How to put these songs on your MP3 player? In this tutorial you will learn how to download songs with the Music From YouTube program and how to put them on MP3 player.

 

Connect MP3 player to your PC

First connect the MP3 device to the computer via USB connection.You need a USB-cable for this, which was probably included with the MP3 player when you bought it. If this is not the case you can buy one at an electronics store or on the internet. Some MP3 players can be connected directly to the USB port without a cable.

You need to make sure that your phone is on, otherwise your computer will not be able to detect it! Most computers will automatically detect the connection and open the location, Go to "My Computer" (or just "Computer" in Windows 7) and you will see a new removable drive in the list of drives. Double click on this drive to open it.

 

Transfer

Now click on the Windows Start button at the bottom-left of the screen (just the windows logo in Windows 7) and click on "My Music" (or just "Music" in Windows 7). This will open the folder where the songs you downloaded with Music From YouTube are stored. Select the songs by holding the Ctrl key and then left clicking on all the songs you want to tranfer to your MP3 player. When you are finished selecting, hold the Ctrl key and press the letter "c". Now release the Ctrl key and click on the other window where you opened your MP3 player. To transfer songs you just selected, just hold the Ctrl button and press the letter "v" and then release Ctrl. Now the computer will start copying your music files to your MP3 player.

Unplug the USB cable and/or your MP3 player after the computer has finished copying. Now you can play and listen all the songs you just transferred to your MP3 player as much as you want!
