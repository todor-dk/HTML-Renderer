YouTube to MP3 Converter is RipTunes’ solution for extracting the audio from any YouTube video in an intuitive and straightforward way. The program will search for an artist or a song (or any other keyword) and will list all the YouTube videos that match your request. It will download all the videos selected and will extract their audio stream on the fly, saving them as MP3 files. You can then listen to the downloaded files and even create original ringtones without leaving the program.

One of the assets of this utility is its search engine. Though you are requested to enter the name of an artist or the title of a song, actually you can use any keyword to find your favorite tunes. It will perform its search among more than 20 million videos available in YouTube, listing all those matching your criteria. You can then select the ones whose audio you wish to download, and the program will list them to you separately together with a thumbnail image of the video. Though YouTube to MP3 Converter actually needs to download the selected videos in order to extract and save their audio, these video files cannot be accessed in any way. The program will extract the audio immediately after the downloading process has finished, sending the newly-created MP3 file to the program’s library. Here you can create your own playlists and use the program’s player to listen to the downloaded tracks. Together with the list of video files that match your search, the program will provide you with information about the artist selected and a list of similar artists that you may be interested to explore.

It is worth noting that this tool is fully synchronized with iTunes, meaning that all the MP3 tracks downloaded will appear on your copy of iTunes right away.

Finally, the program comes with a utility that you will not find easily in other download managers – the ringtone maker. Here you can open any of the MP3 tracks in your library, select and listen to the fragment you want to send to your mobile phone and save it as an MP3 or an M4R audio file (for Android and iPhone, respectively). All in all, an interesting free utility worth having installed on your system.
