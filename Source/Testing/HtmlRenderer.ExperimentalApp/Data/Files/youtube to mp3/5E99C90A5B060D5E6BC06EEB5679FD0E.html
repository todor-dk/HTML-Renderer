Xilisoft YouTube to MP3 Converter is a video downloader and converter for Windows. The application lets you download videos from YouTube and convert them to MP3, as well as to other audio formats like WAV and MP2. It is a one-step solution for those who want to get just the audio track of a video, movie scene, or song found on a popular video sharing site.

The application works in a very simple manner and offers two download methods. You can just paste a video URL and have the program download the video with its default quality, or you can use the application as a web browser and dive into YouTube in search of a video to download, with the advantage that you will be able to choose the best quality available. One feature I really liked is that the program supports batch downloads, that is, it is capable of downloading multiple videos at the same time.

There are many options that you can configure from the settings window. For one thing, you can tell the application to convert your current video as soon as the download is complete, that way, you get the desired audio file directly and in one single step. Of course, you can also keep the downloaded video in its original format: FLV or MP4. Besides, it is possible to determine the download speed, the number of simultaneous downloads and conversions, and other advanced settings. To check the status of your videos, the program has tabs that show you the current downloads, videos already converted, as well those which are being processed.

During my time using this program I can say it works as well as expected; I liked the fact that it supports simultaneous downloads and conversions, which is a time-saving feature. Videos download at a reasonable speed, and the same can be said about conversion times. Its interface is simple and lets you get started right away. The only drawback is that it works only with YouTube.

Mariel Rearte
