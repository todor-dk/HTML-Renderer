Vidtunez.com is a free online service which has four main features:

1. Repeat a youtube video clips click here.
2. Download and convert non-copyrighted youtube video
clips into mp4/flv (1080p supported) or mp3 file format.
3. Audio editor which can help you to edit your downloaded
audio or your local audio file click here.
4. Repeated playlist which you can assign the video from
youtube and the number of repeat in each video of
playlist items.

For converting to mp3 format, We need approximately 1 to
5 minutes (depend on audio quality) to finish the job. Please review and abide by our terms of service and privacy policy.

How to convert YouTube to MP3 or FLV:

1. Go to search page or click on the top right side search
button.
2. Type your keyword in the input text to search your video
on youtube.
3. Click find icon or press enter and you will see the result on
the bottom.
4. After you click the search result, it will redirect you to the
repeat page, and there is a download option in the right
side of page.
5. You can choose either audio or video file to download.
6. Wait until the system finish downloading and converting
your file. Please be patient.
7. Click download.
8. Done!
