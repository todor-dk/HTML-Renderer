9 Life Hacks That Make YouTube Better

There’s no doubt that plain old YouTube can be loads of fun, but when you get bored of watching clips the traditional way, there are a ton of sites that can help enhance your experience. Have a look at our 12 hacks of choice and let us know of any other YouTube hacks or apps you’ve used.

Download audio from videos.

There are tons of live performances on YouTube that you can’t buy on iTunes. Now you can add them to your playlist anyway. YouTube-mp3.org is the easiest online service for converting videos to mp3. You do not need an account, the only thing you need is a YouTube URL. They will start to convert the audiotrack of your video file to mp3 as soon as you have submitted it and you will be able to download it.
