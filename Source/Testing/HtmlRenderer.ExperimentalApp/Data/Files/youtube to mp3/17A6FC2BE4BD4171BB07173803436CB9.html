FSS YouTube MP3 Converter details

FSS YouTube MP3 Converter is an easy-to-use Windows tool that facilitates video downloading from YouTube, in order to extract the audio stream and convert it to popular formats, namely MP3, MP2, OGG, FLAC, M4A, WAV and WMA.

Fast install and approachable UI

The setup operation is quick. A single window with a clean structure, where you can paste URLs from the Clipboard with the click of a button.

Easily configure settings

All you have to do is specify the output directory, audio quality (low, normal, high) and preferred audio format, and start the download and automatic conversion operation with one click.

The file list shows the name and duration of the clip immediately after pasting its URL, along with a progress bar during conversion.

Performance and conclusion

Program has a good response time and carries out an extraction job in reasonable time, delivering quality sound. It may have few options, but FSS YouTube MP3 Converter delivers a simple solution to extracting the audio stream from YouTube clips and saving it to different formats, and it can be used by anyone.
