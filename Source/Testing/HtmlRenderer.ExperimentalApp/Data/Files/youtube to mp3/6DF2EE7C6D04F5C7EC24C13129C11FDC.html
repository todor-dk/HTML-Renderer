8 Best YouTube To MP3 Converters

22-11-2014 by Samantha Millets

Millions of people use YouTube every day to watch videos. But, this isn’t always so easy when you are on the go and you simply want to click “play” and have a video come on. So, you will want to download the videos to your computer or a mobile device.

#1. Flvto YouTube

It only takes two steps to convert videos into any other format. Past the link of the YouTube video, click “Convert to”, and enjoy all of your favorite songs in high-quality sound. You can upload the MP3 files to your smartphone, tablet, etc. so you can listen to them any place, and at any time. FLVTO converts music not only from YouTube, but also from Dailymotion, Vimeo etc. If you download a software, you can convert up to 99 songs at a time including the long tracks of 4-hours. But FLVTO doesn’t have Chrome add-on.

#2. 2conv.com

This is a simple converter to use. Just copy a link from a video portal, past it into the URL area on 2conv.com, and click on “Convert to MP3” (blue button). You will have high-quality music that you can take from the website to your personal device any time. You won’t even need an Internet connection to be able to listen to all of your favorite music, no matter where you are.

#3. FLV2MP3

This free YouTube to MP3 online converter is easy and fast to use. It stands out from others because all you have to do is paste the URL of the video you want to convert, and click on “next”. The track download right to your desktop so you can listen to it any time you like. You can get all of your favorite songs and build up an awesome music library with this converter.

#4. SoundDrain

It is a real pain in the you-know-what to try and download and store music from the cloud. You have to search for the download button, and there are formats that you probably can’t even use. Now, you can choose the format you want for the music downloads from the cloud. All you need is SoundDrain, the newest Soundcloud downloader. Save music in the formats you use for all of your devices, from desktops to iPhones.

#5. aTube Catcher

This conversion tool will literally “catch” any amount of videos you toss at it, and convert them in a AVI, MOV, WMV, MPG, FLV, MP4, and many others formats. It even supports formats that are not so common, such as PSP for Sony’s handheld games console. If you are looking for something that is really cool to look at, this definitely isn’t it. It’s functional, but not the most attractive converter in the world.

#6. Free YouTube to MP3 Converter

This is a freeware program that allows you to convert YouTube videos to other formats, including MP3 and other audio formats. You can queue many files to be converted, and you will see clear displays of the files that are waiting. This is more for professionals, and may be complicated for the average user.

#7. Freemake Video Converter

No matter what file format you want to use, this converter likely supports it. In fact, it supports more than 200 different formats, including MP4, AVI, WMV, WKV, RM, QT, DV, MTS, and TS. You can even convert auto to MP3. Unfortunately, even though it is supposed to be fast, it will all depend on how fast your computer is. If you have an older computer, be prepared to do some waiting.

#8. YouTube Downloader

Download and convert videos from your favorite websites, including Facebook, College Humor, YouTube, and many more. Convert to the most popular formats, including MP3, MP4, WNF, FLV, MOV, 3PG, and AVI, just to name a few. The biggest drawback to using this is all of the ads telling you how much better off you would be if you were using the paid version, and there is no way to get rid of them.
