Description


How Its Work: To download a YouTube video whenever you are on a video page, you will see the Red “Download” button under YouTube Video, click that button to populate the list of all available formats to download, click the desired format, save to your hard drive and that’s it!

To Convert a YouTube video in Mp3: whenever you are on a video page, you will see the Red “Download” button under YouTube Video, click that button to populate the list of all available formats Please select “Mp3-XYZkbps” it will take Max 20 sec To Convert Video in mp3 format depend on video duration
Note: For Video To Mp3 Conversion by default Its support under 20-Minute Long Videos Clips only If you want to convert long duration videos in mp3 please Change “MP3 Converter configuration” From extension option page

Video Supported Formats: It can allows user to download YouTube video with different video quality: 2160p(4k), 1440p(2k), 1080P(HD), 720P(HD), 480P, 360p, 144p etc. Supports all available formats (MP4, FLV, WebM, 3GP) for any video quality.

Dash Video and Audio Download:This extension also lets you download DASH video and audio streams. Please be aware that DASH files do not include either audio or video streams.

Embedded Video Support: You can download videos directly from embedded YouTube players. Just hover a cursor on it and a “Download” button appears on Top Right Corner of Video player. Click to save video in the desired quality.

Take Screenshots from YouTube Videos: This Extension also Help Users to take High quality screenshot of YouTube video. Just press camera button at the frame you want to capture.
Note: For best result Pause at the frame you want to capture and press camera button

Enhancement: improve your user experience on YouTube

From Extension option page you also can customize some highest demand features

  • CustomizeVideo and MP3 download formats to display in Downloadlist E.g. MP4, FLV, WebM, 3GP
  • Customizepreferable video Downloads qualityE.g.1080P, 720P, 480P, 360p, 144p
  • Enable YouTube cinema modus
  • Enable Dark theme
  • Enable Pop out video’s from YouTube function
  • Enable Float View (Smart view to see video while scroll down. video will be shown on right bottom corner)
  • Enable Smart Pause ( Auto pauses currently playing videos on YouTube once you leave the page and resumes when you return )
  • Removes video ads from YouTube ( Removes any banner & videos ad shown before your video as well)
  • Disable Annotations ( Watch YouTube Videos without the annoying Annotations )
  • Enable autoplay ( Stop auto loads a suggested video when the current video ends )
  • Hide YouTube Comments ( Hides all user comments on YouTube. Nothing less, nothing more )
  • Enable Copy to clipboard Function ( It will help to Download Videos with Download manager )
  • Show save-as dialog box when choose to download a video
