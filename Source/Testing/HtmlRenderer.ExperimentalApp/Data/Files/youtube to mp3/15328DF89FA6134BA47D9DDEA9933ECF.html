Free Video to MP3 Converter enables you to extract audio from video files and then convert it to MP3 and WAV Lossless Audio. You have the option to save in High, Standard or Economy value and the program can handle high-quality formats including OGG, FLAC and APE.

You'll find Free Video to MP3 Converter easy to use - as well as browsing through files, you can drag-and-drop or right-click to add files and change output names in a single click. It's also possible to convert files in batches. Title tags and artwork are automatically filled in for you because it takes a snapshot from the middle of the video.

A handy feature is the Preset Editor, which preconfigures different file formats for different devices. These can be selected quickly from the Quality menu.

Overall Free Video to MP3 Converter is easy-to-use and effective at extracting audio from video files.
