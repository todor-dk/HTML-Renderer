  Adding lyrics to MP3, WMA, MP4, M4A files.
Digital Music Pad will automatically show Lyrics if they exist on MP3, WMA, MP4 and M4A files.

From Tools - click 'Add Lyrics'

Adding lyrics to a file.
Make sure you have an internet connection.
Select a song in the play list, and click the Lyrics icon.
The program will request lyrics from the Amazon Song Lyrics Service.
Lyrics are submitted to the Amazon site by the public and accuracy can vary, so you have the option to edit the lyrics before adding them to the song.
