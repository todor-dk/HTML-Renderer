Download the free METSTRADE Show app from the Google Play or the iTunes stores! The app features all the exhibitors, the knowledge programme, the new products list, news and much more. Highlight your favorite companies and programme items so you don't miss anything during the event.

Prepare your visit

By creating a myMETSTRADE profile on the website or in the app within 10 seconds, you can favourite exhibitors, products and news as well as schedule sessions you plan to attend. An overview of favourites is stored automatically and readily available in both the app and on the website. With myMETSTRADE, your visit starts before the event and greatly facilitates your preparations.

Make sure you will download the app! You can log in with your myMETSTRADE or account in the app.

About METSTRADE

METSTRADE is the world’s leading platform for professionals in the leisure marine equipment industry. This global business platform and community focuses on innovation, market developments and on-site networking and knowledge events such as the METSTRADE show, the world’s largest leisure marine equipment trade show.
