Google pulls the indexes down - 2017-06-27

A negative opening is expected today on Wall Street, as the futures contracts on the leading indices are trading in the red territory before the opening bell. GOOG, which represents the giant technology company Google, is leading the decline in the NASDAQ, following a decision by the European antitrust authority to fine the company by $2.7 billion.


The reason for the fine is the Authority's decision that Google had manipulated the search results it presented to users in order to increase its revenues. This decision will cause Google to update its algorithms in accordance with the directives of the Authority (in Europe only), while at the same time two more investigations are being conducted against the Company.


Google is considering appealing the fine. At these moments Google shares are trading lower by about 1%.
