Effective 10 March 2016, ETRADE Securities (Hong Kong) Limited ("ETRADE Hong Kong") has discontinued its brokerage operations in Hong Kong and surrendered its Hong Kong Securities and Futures Commission licences.

Looking for information about a closed account? Simply log on using your most recent user ID and password to access prior account statements or other documents.
