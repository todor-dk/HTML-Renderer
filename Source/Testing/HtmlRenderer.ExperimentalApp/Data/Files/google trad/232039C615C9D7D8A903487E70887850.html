Submit a Trade-In

The Amazon Trade-In Program lets you trade in eligible items such as textbooks, electronics, video games, DVDs, and more for an Amazon.com Gift Card. Your trade-ins are sent with free shipping, and you receive an immediate offer with no required registration or listing.

To submit a trade-in:

  1. Go to the Trade-In Store and search for eligible items.

    Note: You can also submit a trade-in from the Amazon Mobile App on your Android or iOS compatible device.

  2. Click Trade in to add items to your trade-in submission.
  3. Select the condition of your item(s) based on the criteria listed, then click Continue.
  4. Select or enter an address from which you will ship your items.

    Note: This address will be used to return any items that aren't accepted. PO boxes, APO/FPO addresses are not eligible.
  5. To add more items, click Add another item.
  6. Follow the remaining on-screen instructions.
  7. Click Submit your trade-in to print your prepaid UPS shipping label.
  8. Ship your items within 7 calendar days of submitting the trade-in.

Note: For more information about printing shipping labels from Your Trade-In Account, go to Ship Your Trade-In.
