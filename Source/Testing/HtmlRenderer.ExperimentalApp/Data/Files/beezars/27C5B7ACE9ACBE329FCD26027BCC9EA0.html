Reviews of Beezer's

02/14/2015 - Juggler
After making a reservation for Valentines night, we showed up and they did not even know we were coming. We had to still wait quite awhile and THEN after waiting and getting seated, then they tell us they were out of Prime Rib, which is what we came there for! No one offered any comp drinks or such. So we left and went to Marty's(Valley Inn) in Elroy and they HAD Prime Rib and it was awesome! Marty himself served us.... we wasted a 10 plus mile car ride in negative 2 degree weather to Beezers. Marty's was quiet and not crowded... we say just go to Marty's in Elroy and avoid Beezers....
