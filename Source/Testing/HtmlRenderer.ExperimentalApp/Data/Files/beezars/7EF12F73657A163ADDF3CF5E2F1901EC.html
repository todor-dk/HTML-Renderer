Tacoma, Washington

Discipline Patience Effort


The St. Charles Chess Club provides instruction, match play and tournament experience for interested students at St. Charles Borromeo School.  Rob Smith currently coordinates a team of fellow parents who run the club as volunteers. The content for this site now reflects the 2011/2012 school year.
