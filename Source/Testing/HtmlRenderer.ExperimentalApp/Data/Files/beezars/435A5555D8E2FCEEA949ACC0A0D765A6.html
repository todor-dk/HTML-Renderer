About

Beezers Bar & Grill is a restaurant located in Hillsboro, Wisconsin at 1235 Water Avenue. They are open every day except Monday.

We make every effort to assure your stay is more than satisfactory. Our staff is here 24 / 7 to assist you. We offer the full service features of the large chains, with a small town touch. We are very capable of hosting events and large gatherings. The addition of Beezers Bar and Grill allows us to provide the complete level of service difficult to find in this area. We believe we are unique in our facilities and quality of service, we hope your stay leads to your return visit with us. Thank you. Relax, take a swim, enjoy an excellent meal and retire for the evening, all within the hotel and attached bar and grill. Beezers features 8 flat screen television sets and is geared toward the sports enthusiast. Click here to check it out. If you have any requests or questions that are not answered on this site, let us know. Just call 608.489.3000.
