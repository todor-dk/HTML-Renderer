Call Ahead

If you are travelling as a group, on motorcycle or snowmobile, call ahead and we can accommodate your group. Beezers can be contacted directly at 608.489.3007. We have a full menu including noon specials and breakfast on Saturday and Sunday.

Welcome to Beezers

The hometown atmosphere at Beezers is sure to make you feel welcome. Join your family and friends for a dining experience. Our extensive menu allows you to choose from appetizers, sandwiches, pizza or a full dinner. Enjoy a drink from our fully stocked bar.

Beezers on Route 3

Starting in Spring and ending with the Fall colors, biking through the Driftless is an addicting experience. The area has scenic bluffs, hidden valleys, and numerous small crossroad towns and villages. For those who love the ride, this area has much to offer. Some routes are forming or create your own, but be sure to stop at Beezers for excellent food or stay at the hotel after a day's ride.
