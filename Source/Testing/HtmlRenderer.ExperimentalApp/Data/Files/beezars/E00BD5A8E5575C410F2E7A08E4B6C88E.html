The 2016 annual meeting of the Elroy Fair will be held at Beezers Bar and Grill in Hillsboro. The meeting will start at 7:30 pm in the private room near the hotel desk, just off the bar. Join us as we have the election of directors and officers for the upcoming year. This event is open to the public and all who are intersted in supporting the Elroy Fair.
Please come early for social time. All drink and meal costs are the responsibility of the person attending.
