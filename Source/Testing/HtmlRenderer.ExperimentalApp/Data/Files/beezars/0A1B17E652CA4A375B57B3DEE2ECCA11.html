KITCHEN CLOSURES & THINGS FOR SHOPPERS TO KEEP IN MIND…

1. KITCHEN CLOSURES: None

2. If you have a lot of items on your list and would like to save some money, please send me a message with your list and I will do a custom listing for you. It will definitely save money on the S/H.

3. If you have a date of when you would like the product to arrive, please include a note in with your order.

4. Gift wrapping…. I can do “no frills” wrapping… tissue paper or a holiday themed poly-bag. Anything more than that will require alternate packaging. I am willing to do it (LOVE wrapping presents), but you need to be willing to pay for it. If you ask for more than “No frills” please provide a zip code for the final destination so I can determine the most economical shipping option for you… East Coast, I can probably ship cheaper with my own packaging and West Coast will be better off with a flat rate box.
