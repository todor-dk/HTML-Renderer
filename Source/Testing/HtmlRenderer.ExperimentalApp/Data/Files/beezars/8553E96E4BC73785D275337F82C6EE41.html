Bezares has a wide range of valves, tipping valves, open and closed circuit, check valves, circuit selector or relief valves. Available with pneumatic or electric actuators, and end of stroke, in pusher or puller versions.

 

Tipper: From 70 to 250 liters throughput. Pneumatic actuators with direct action or balanced relief valves up to 400 bar. Mechanical end of stroke is further incorporated with cable option in some of them.

 

Electric drive: In 40 liter flow and 1/2 ", both open and closed center.

 

Direct Mounting Units: Over our gear pumps, in "tandem", available for tipper applications as pressure relief.

Bezares takes very seriously the reputation for quality, customer service, product development, and delivery that it has developed in Europe. We, at Bezares USA are committed to developing and continuing this same reputation in the Americas.

Bezares factory and headquarters

Founded in 1954 by Eugenio and José Bezares in Madrid, Bezares takes very seriously its reputation for good customer service, quality products development and fast delivery of auxiliary hydraulic equipment for trucks and industrial vehicles such as Power Takeoffs (PTO), hydraulic pumps, fittings, winches, Power Packs, complete hydraulic systems, hydraulic adapters and other parts for all the major brands and models such as Allison, ZF, Toyota, Renault, Mitsubishi, Volvo, Man, Scania, Isuzu, Iveco, Daf, Hino among others.

 

World Reference

We are the third largest manufacturer of Power Takeoffs (PTO) in the world and a world reference in high quality pumps and hydraulic equipment parts and accessories.

 

Market Leaders

We are market leaders in many European countries such as Spain, Portugal, UK, Sweden among others.

We have a wide network of distributors in over 80 countries.
