Daniel P. Jul 14.

I've never actually been to Beezer's before dinner. As it is directly across from the Horseshoe, Beezer's is very popular with students. I would recommend going there late at night, especially on the weekends. The parade of inibriated students is almost as good as the food.

As for the subs, they are delicious and come in interesting combos (i.e. meatball sub with banana peppers). They are all reasonably priced, even for a student's budget.

Overall a great place to experience the college environment.

Joshua G. Aug 05.

I think Subway started a long time ago to put pretty good ingredients on good bread. Since then, Beezers has taken it to a new level. Their sandwiches use good ingredients, and their breads are fresh and very tasty. Beezers makes some of the best sandwiches around, and they're very reasonably sized. Instead of the 6 or 12 inch option, they give you 8 inches. Which turns out to be perfect for a refreshing meal. The only downside to Beezers is the parking... it's terrible. Especially when school is in.

PROS: Good ingredients, great bread
CONS: Parking
