STUDENT TRAVEL PERIODS

Breezes Bahamas - during select periods throughout the months of March, April, May & June - welcomes groups of Spring Breakers & Graduates. The median age of our resort guests will vary during this time. Breezes Bahamas happily welcomes couples, singles, groups of friends and families with all guests ages 14 and older year-round.

 

If you prefer to visit Breezes Bahamas when fewer students and younger guests are at the resort, be sure to select travel dates outside of normal nationwide Spring Break and Graduation travel periods.

Confirmed student travel periods

 

2018: March 3 - April 7 |  April 14 - 21
