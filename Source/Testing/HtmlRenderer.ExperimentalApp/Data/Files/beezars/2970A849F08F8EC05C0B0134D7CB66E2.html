About

Welcome to Beezer’s!

Helping you get a great shave every day!

 This is Beezer’s first time wet shaving.  From this point on I was totally hooked on getting a great shave.  Here in Colorado our options are limited in places to go purchase shaving supplies.  We launched Beezer’s to share this timeless tradition with others.

Our daughter Taylor was diagnosed with Autism when she was 2 years old.  She has had surgery on both eyes. Taylor is 6 now and goes to therapy almost every day. She is smart, funny, and so wonderful! She is the drive behind this business. Part of the proceeds will go towards funding Taylor’s therapies and other items she needs.  Thank you all for you love and support!
