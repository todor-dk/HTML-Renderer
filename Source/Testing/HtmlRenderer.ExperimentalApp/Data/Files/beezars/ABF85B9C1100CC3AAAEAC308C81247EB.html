Student Support

Jackets & Company West

Hours: 7:30 a.m. - 3:30 p.m. Monday- Friday
Location: next to the cafeteria
Phone: 247-3031

 

Jackets & Company West carries textbooks, supplies and required items for your classes. They also sell postage stamps and offer UPS services. Logo items such as clothing, coffee mugs, pens, and baseball caps; greeting cards for most occasions; and soft drinks and snack items are also available. If you're looking for software, if we don't have it we can special order it for you!

 

The copy center is also located in the bookstore.
