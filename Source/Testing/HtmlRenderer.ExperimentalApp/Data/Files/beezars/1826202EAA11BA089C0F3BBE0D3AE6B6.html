CITY OF SEATTLE  v.  BEEZER

A. L. Newbould, Richard S. White, William A. Helsell, Robert L. McCarty and Charles F. Wheatley, Jr., for appellants.

Alfred J. Schweppe, for appellee Beezer.

Clarence C. Dill, Joseph Volpe, Jr. and Bennett Boskey, for appellee Public Utility District No. 1 of Pend Oreille County, Washington.

Solicitor General Cox, Richard A. Solomon, Howard E. Wahrenbrock and David J. Bardin, for the Federal Power Commission, as amicus curiae.

PER CURIAM.
