Beezers Bar and Grill

The hometown atomsphere at Beezers is sure to make you feel welcome. Join your family and friends for a dining experience. Our extensive menu allows you to choose from appetizers, sandwiches, pizza or a full dinner. Enjoy a drink from our fully stocked bar. "The Shack", Beezers outdoor patio/deck bar, is the place to be for a relaxing, fun filled evening with family and friends. With easy access from Beezers Bar and Grill, "The Shack" offers a great outdoor atmosphere, with beautiful views where our guests can relax and unwind while enjoying a refreshing beverage. "The Shack" offers live music most Friday nights.
