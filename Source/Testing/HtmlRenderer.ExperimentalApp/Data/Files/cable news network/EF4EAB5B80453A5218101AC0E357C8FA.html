    Re: Cable News Network This is (CNN)

    While CNN may have been a news network under the leadership of Ted Turner back in the day, it has long since ceased to be one. It should more accurately be renamed the Continuous Indoctrination Network as it pushes "news" with an agenda and ignores many real news stories which need to be reported. It ignores the world and indulges in 24-hour political navel-gazing and political punditry. In my opinion it is almost worthless as a news source and just as useless as a window into the American gestal. I pine for real news coming out of America but there is so very little to find. Better to follow Breitbart and Democracy Now and to appreciate the cultural, political and moral fragmentation of an increasingly schizophrenic super-power as it unconsciously but wilfully slides beneath the waves like a new Atlantis. Maybe it's just irony that CNN was based in Atlanta for so long?

    What are Americans thinking that they let this kind of "news" indoctrinate and by extension infect them willingly. They're a smart people but in this regard they seem to have a continent-wide blind-spot. So, why do Americans tolerate this poison being poured into their ears by much of the MSM, like a digital remake of Hamlet on a society-wide scale? As an outsider, looking into the grand fishbowl of the great American Republic, I am befuddled and depressed to see this process unfold.

    Cheers?
