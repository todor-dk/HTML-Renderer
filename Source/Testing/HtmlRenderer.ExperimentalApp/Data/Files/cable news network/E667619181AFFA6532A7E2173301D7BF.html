Benefits

Why Comcast?

Diversity

At Comcast, you'll work alongside best-in-class innovators in a fast-paced and rewarding environment - but that's not all.
Full-time employees are eligible for a number of benefits, and special perks, including:



Career Development

Learn, grow and thrive professionally and personally with annual tuition assistance and a variety of development programs.

Medical & Dental

Comprehensive medical, dental and vision plans are just the start. Comcast employees have access to Flexible Spending Accounts, personal health consultants, benefits to support life milestones, and more.

401(k) Savings Plan

Our 401(k) plan offers automatic enrollment along with dollar-to-dollar matching up to the first 4.5% of eligible pay contributed.

Courtesy Services

Employees in serviceable areas enjoy free digital TV and Internet service as well as deeply discounted phone service, and other features.
