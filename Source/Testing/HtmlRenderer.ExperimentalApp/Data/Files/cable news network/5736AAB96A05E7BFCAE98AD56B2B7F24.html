Welcome to the Boston Neighborhood Network website where you can get information about our history and the services we provide Boston residents, nonprofit and community-based organizations and the general public. You will find our television channel schedules and many opportunities to participate in our programs and events. You can also sign up to become a member or register for a workshop.

Support BNN: Your support will help us continue our efforts to prepare the next generation of media makers and to give seniors the tools they need to participate in the digital age. Help us build an informed citizenry and ensure Bostonian's First Amendment Rights. We appreciate the generosity of all our supporters and hope you will consider adding your name to our supporter list.  Click to view the complete list of BNN Supporters
