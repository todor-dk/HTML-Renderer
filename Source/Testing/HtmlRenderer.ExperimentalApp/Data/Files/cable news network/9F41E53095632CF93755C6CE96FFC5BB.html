About Us

Asia News Network

The Asia News Network (ANN) is one of the world's biggest media alliances. Founded in 1999 with seven members, ANN now comprises 22 leading media, mostly leading national English-language daily, from South, Southeast and Northeast Asia. ANN's main activities are daily exchanges of news, photos and a host of other collaborations.
