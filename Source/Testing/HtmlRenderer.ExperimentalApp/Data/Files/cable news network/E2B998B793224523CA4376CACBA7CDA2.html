Transcript of The History of the Cable News Network

The Cable News Network, or CNN, is the first 24-hour news network. Founded by Ted Turner, CNN was the beginning of a whole new type of news station that would change the way the world received news forever.
The Cable News Network
The History of the Cable News Network
However, as CNN developed, the audience grew. People were tuning in more often to watch the 24-hour news network over the 30-minute evening networks that everyone had been accustomed t0. As the years went on, CNN eventually became well-known for describing events as they happened. More and more people were turning to CNN for coverage of major events. Soon, Ted bought the Satellite News Channel in 1983 and knocked out his main competitor. Ever since then, CNN has evolved to be one of the most viewed news networks in the U.S.
Progress
In 1980, Ted Turner founded the Cable News Network in Atlanta. At first, it wasn't very successful. It lost money over the next couple of years, and few people tuned into watch it. CNN was constantly ridiculed as the "Chicken Noodle Network", and it seemed that it wouldn't ever do well. But Ted did not give up.
The Start of CNN
Ted Turner started his career early in life when his dad committed suicide in 1963. During the lifetime of Ted's father, he
*
ran a successful advertising business in Georgia. Once he died, however, Ted took over and expanded the business. In 1970, he bought a failing Atlanta TV network and converted it into a "super station" (which was an idea Ted had where one station was broadcasted by satellites to TV's across the country). Over the next couple of years, Ted bought the Atlanta Braves baseball team and the Atlanta Hawks basketball team, broadcasting their games on TBS (a network he had started) whenever they played. But despite all of these accomplishments, his greatest achievement was yet to come.
Ted Turner
The Cable News Network has come a long way since the eighties. Today, CNN is immensely popular and is watched in millions of homes across the world. The Founder, Ted Turner, is now peace activist and environmentalist.
CNN Today
* CNN gained popularity when it covered the Persian Gulf War 1991.

*In 1984, CNN began broadcasting overseas.

*In 1982, CNN launched the companion network CNN2, which later came t0 be known as the CNN Headline News& now is called HLN.

*CNN's website is one of the most popular websites in the world.
