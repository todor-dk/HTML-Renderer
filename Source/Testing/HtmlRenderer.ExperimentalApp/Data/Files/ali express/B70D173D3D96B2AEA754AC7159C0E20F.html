Yes it is very save. The only issues with Aliexpress is that it still takes a long time for products to be shipped to the US, EU or UK. Things to keep in mind when buying from AliExpress:

  • Shipping time.
  • Buyer protection rules. Make sure you file a complaint on time. They offer almost the similar buyer protection rules as at eBay.
  • Make sure it’s very clear what you are buying. Don’t buy from the cheapest and don’t buy from the one most expensive.
  • Verify ratings.
  • Verify comments.
  • Make clear if you are responsible for paying all the duties and taxes.
  • Make sure you are not buying a fake product.
  • Make sure the product you bought may enter your county.
