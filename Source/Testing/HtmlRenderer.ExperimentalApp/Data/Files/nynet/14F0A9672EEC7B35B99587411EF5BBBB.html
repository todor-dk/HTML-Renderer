Inkspotwifi Receives NYnet Award

inkspotw June 20, 2012 Rural WiFi, Updates

Inkspotwifi Ltd have recently been awarded a position in the Supply Framework for the provision of next generation Community Broadband services for rural areas in North Yorkshire.

“NYnet is a public and private sector commercial venture created in order to provide a communications network that is capable of delivering multiple high quality services to citizens, business and public sector bodies.

North Yorkshire County Council set up NYnet, to help bring next generation broadband across the County.  NYnet is tasked to provide an ultra-high-speed Internet network to ensure North Yorkshire is not left behind as information technology advances.  For the public sector it offers massively improved ‘e-services’.  For the private sector it provides a world class, high-speed communications infrastructure across the region.”
