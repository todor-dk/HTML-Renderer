To access NY State of Health, you will need an NY.gov ID. This ID can be used to access other State services. If you already have an NY.gov ID, you can use it to log in to NY State of Health.

Click on the "Create a Personal NY.gov ID" button at the bottom of the page to create an NY.gov ID. You will need to enter your name and email address and choose a user name. You will receive an email with instructions on how to activate your account, set up your security questions, and choose your password. You can then log in to NY State of Health!
