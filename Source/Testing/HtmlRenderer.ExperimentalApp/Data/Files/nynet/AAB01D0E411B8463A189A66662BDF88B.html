Who is it for?

MTN HyNet is ideal for small and medium-sized businesses that require high speed Internet connectivity for multiple groups of staff. With no limit to download, MTN HyNet is suitable for businesses that have large data download requirements. Benefits

  • You can choose the bandwidth/speed that meets your peculiar need
  • You enjoy unlimited downloads
  • You can choose from an array of plans that suit your usage patterns
  • No software installations required. You can Do it Yourself
  • Flexible payment methods

Features

  • Wi-Fi enabled indoor modems
  • VoIP Capable
  • Choice of indoor or outdoor modems

How do I subscribe?

  • Visit any MTN Service Centre,
  • Call 0700MTNBusiness (070068628746337) or
  • Send an email to mtnbusiness@mtnnigeria.net and a Sales Consultant will assist you in signing up.

How do I Resubscribe?

  • Once you click MTN HyNet Portal
  • Log on with your username and password (username can be found in your modem manual. Default password is the MAC address found on the base of your modem)
  • Top Up and follow on-screen information.
  • Select the service you wish to subscribe, click “Submit” and you are connected to the Internet.
