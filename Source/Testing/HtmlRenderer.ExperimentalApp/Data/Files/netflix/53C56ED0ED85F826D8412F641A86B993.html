About Netflix

Netflix, Inc. is an online movie rental subscription service in the United States, providing approximately 7.5 million subscribers access to approximately 90,000 digital versatile disc (DVD) titles plus a growing library of more than 6,000 choices that can be watched instantly on their personal computers (PCs). It offers nine subscription plans. Subscribers – more... select titles at the Company’s Website aided by its recommendation service, receive them on DVD by United States mail and return them to the Company at their convenience using its prepaid mailers. After a DVD has been returned, The Company mails the next available DVD in a subscriber’s queue. It also offers certain titles through its instant-watching feature.
