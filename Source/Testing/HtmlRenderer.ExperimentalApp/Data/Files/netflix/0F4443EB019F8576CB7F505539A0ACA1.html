Here's Everything That's Coming to Netflix in June

By Kerensa Cadenas

Deputy Pop Culture Editor. Tweeting. 

May 21, 2015
  • Share
  • Tweet

It's almost June and while we're certain you won't be inside binge-watching all the movies and TV shows on Netflix (aside from when Orange is the New Black drops on June 12), you never know when that summer cold/Sunday hangover/mental health day is going to strike. Having a base knowledge of what Netflix has to offer you in June, from romantic flicks to comedies to horror films, is for the best. Noteables include Jennifer Aniston's award nominated turn in Cake, Gina Prince-Bythewood's slept-on music romance Beyond the Lights, and Jake Gyllenhaal's batshit crazy performance in Nightcrawler. 

You'll have plenty of viewing options for that inevitable early summer "too many tequila shots" weekend.
