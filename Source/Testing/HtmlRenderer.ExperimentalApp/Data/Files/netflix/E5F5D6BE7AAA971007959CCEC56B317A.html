What’s on Netflix

Welcome to What’s on Netflix, one of the largest Netflix fan-sites dedicated to bringing you the latest and greatest content from the Netflix library in both the United States and the United Kingdom. With daily updates of the latest movies and TV series now streaming on the US and a weekly round-up of all those added to Netflix in the UK we’re your number one destination to finding out what’s new on Netflix.

Beyond that we cover the Netflix goings-on in our news sections where we break and report on stories which could involve Netflix picking up a new TV series, breaking another world record or about it winning an award. Our previews give you a look into what’s coming in the coming month or year to Netflix. The last chance section will give you an opportunity to catch up on a title before it leaves. Finally, the other section does what it says on the tin – it’s the space for everything else.

Netflix is a streaming service operating all over the world allowing users access to instantly stream TV series and movies for a subscription each month.
