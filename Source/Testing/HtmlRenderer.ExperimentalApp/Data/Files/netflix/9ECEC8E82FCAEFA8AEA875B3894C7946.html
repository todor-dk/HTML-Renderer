Netflix
on Xbox One

Original series. Comedies. Dramas. Kids shows. Netflix streams your favorite TV shows and movies instantly to your Xbox One, and does so in stunning 4K. It’s fast. It’s easy. It’s changing the world of entertainment.


Instant entertainment.

Watch at the press of a button. Binge late at night. Play catch-up on the weekend. With Netflix you can stream as much as you want. Whenever you want. With no commercials.



Something for everyone.

It’s all here. Award-winning original content. Endless romance, sci-fi and comedy. Even a special section just for kids. From Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt and Narcos to The Adventures of Puss in Boots, there’s plenty of drama, adventure and giggles to go around.

Your first month is on us.

Ready to watch? We’ve got your first month covered. No commitments. No contracts. No hidden fees. And you can cancel anytime.



Personalize your experience.

Profiles allow different members of your household to have their own personalized Netflix experience, built around the TV shows and movies they enjoy. You can have up to five individual profiles within a single Netflix account.



Setup Overview

Get an Xbox Live Membership

Take your Xbox One online for games, HD movies and more.

Become a Netflix Member

Become a Netflix streaming member and instantly watch thousands of TV shows & movies on your Xbox One console. Not a member? Sign up now for your free one month trial.

Sign-in on your Xbox One

Once you're a Netflix member, download the Netflix app on your Xbox One, sign in to your Netflix account, and start streaming your favorite TV shows & movies.
