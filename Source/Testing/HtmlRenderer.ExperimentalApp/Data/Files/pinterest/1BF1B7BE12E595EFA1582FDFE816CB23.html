What is the Pinners Conference & Expo?

Pinners is two great events in one. It's a conference featuring 100 Pinterest-based classes taught by the best presenters in the nation. And it's also an expo with nearly 200 top businesses providing beautiful options in the worlds of DIY, crafts, cooking, self-improvment, photography, party planning, scrapbooking, holiday, beauty and fashion and all sorts of other great things. What's Pinners? It's where Pinterest comes to life. And it's your new favortie event!

VIPinners

Being a VIP at Pinners is awesome. You get a party the night before the main event with the bloggers, presenters and other VIP's. You'll also receive a boat load of cool swag, early-ins, a VIP room and all of the cool class options you can take! Check it out here.

Admission


FREE Expo admission (all exhibits and shopping) with pre-registered online ticket.
Expo and Class admission wristbands = $15 to $25
Some classes will have optional kits to buy once you're in the class.
Children = 12 years and under FREE to all events

There are some other options too, including being a VIP attendee complete with VIP/Blogger party the night before and some pretty cool swag. So if you're wondering what Pinners is all about, grab a free ticket from the Register page and stop by. You can always upgrade the fun online or at the show. See you at Pinners :)
