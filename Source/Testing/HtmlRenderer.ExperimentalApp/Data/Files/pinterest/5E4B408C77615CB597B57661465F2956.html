What can I do with Buffer on Pinterest?

Schedule content: Save time and schedule your pins to post consistently so that you don't have to be online all day. You can share content to Pinterest from the Buffer dashboard or from the Buffer browser extensions for Chrome, Firefox and Safari.

Connect multiple social networks: Connect as many Pinterest accounts as you'd like and easily publish your content to Twitter, Facebook, Google+ and LinkedIn all from the same dashboard.

Measure the performance of your pins: Measure the performance of your pins in order to understand how your audience is growing and how your followers are engaging with your content. Buffer helps you filter, export and sort your data so you can make smarter decisions with your social media campaigns.

How much does it cost?

You can schedule your pins with Buffer on the Awesome and Business plans, starting at $10 per month and $99 per month respectively. Buffer for Business provides richer analytics and team member collaboration.

You can get started on a free 14-day trial of Buffer for Pinterest if you start pinning today!
