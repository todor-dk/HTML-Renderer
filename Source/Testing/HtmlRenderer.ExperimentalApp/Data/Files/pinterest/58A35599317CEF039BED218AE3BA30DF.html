    The 8 Most-Pinned Cake Recipes on Pinterest are Seriously Scrumptious

    And baking them is a piece of cake (sorry, we had to!). 

      By Jessica Mattern
      Mar 17, 2016

      If you're looking for a recipe that's sure to be a hit with the masses, the best place to find it is on Pinterest, obviously. Pinners far and wide have excellent taste when it comes to dessert, which is why we've rounded up these most-pinned cake recipes for your baking and eating enjoyment. (Sweet tooth not satisfied? You can find even more amazing cake recipes on Pinterest!)

      Buttermilk Blueberry Breakfast Cake

      Buttermilk makes everything taste out-of-this-world good.

      Get the recipe at Alexandra Cooks.

      Alexandra Cooks
      Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

      Strawberry Cake

      Pretty and full of flavor, it's no wonder this pink cake is a crowd favorite.

      Get the recipe at Just a Pinch.

      Just a Pinch
      Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

      Chocolate Red Wine Loaf

      When three of your favorite things come together—chocolate, red wine, and cake—you know there's going to be some culinary magic happening.

      Get the recipe at French Press.

      French Press
      Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

      Crock-Pot Peanut Butter Cupcake

      You don't have to spend hours slaving away in the kitchen to create a crowd-pleasing dessert, and this easy slow cooker recipe is proof.

      Get the recipe at Moms With Crockpots.

      Moms With Crockpots
      Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

      Pecan Pie Coffee Cake

      The hint of cream cheese and pecan filling that soaks through this cake means every bite of this recipe is pure dessert perfection.

      Get the recipe at Bake or Break.

      Bake or Break

      Cookies & Cream Cupcakes

      Between the marshmallow cream filling, cookie-loaded frosting, and surprise cookie at the bottom, it's not at all surprising to know that these are a Pinterest favorite.

      Get the recipe at Confessions of a Cookbook Queen.

      Confessions of a Cookbook Queen

      Samoa Sheet Cake

      If you love those famous Samoas from your local Girl Scouts, then you're going to adore this easy-to-make sheet cake.

      Get the recipe at Chef in Training.

      Chef in Training

      Pineapple Sunshine Cake

      This light and fluffy cake is infused with pineapple juice, giving it some extra zest. Hello, new favorite summer party cake!

      Get the recipe at Belle of the Kitchen.

      Belle of the Kitchen
