Pinterest Crafts for Kids for Every Season, Holiday and Just for Fun

Crafts
Holidays
SeasonalAll AgesResources5 Comments

Pinterest is a favorite tool of mine for finding ideas. But when I look for Pinterest crafts for kids to do, they all look so perfect and pretty, did the kids really make them?

I have quite a hard time finding crafts for kids to do on Pinterest that the kids can actually do. Often they’re crafts intended for kids, but there’s really no kids involved in making them, so I have a hard time believing, or wanting, my kids to do them.

I’ve scrounged Pinterest and came up with 22 Pinterest boards with actual kids crafts.

To make this even better, I planned this list to cover the whole year. All four seasons and every major holiday.

Of course, I included some of my favorite Pinterest craft boards that just have some fun crafts for kids to make too, good for any time of the year!

I’ve been really enjoying having these lists of my favorite Pinterest boards now that I’ve done one for toddlers and everything for boys. They’ve come really handy in finding pretty much anything and everything on the topic!

Here they are! Don’t forget, you can simple repin from right here,  click on any pin listed in the boards and it’ll pop up with a chance for you to repin. And be sure to click the ‘follow’ button on any that interest you.
