Welcome!

What is The Pinterest Project all about? Basically, we thought it would be interesting to put all our Pinterest pins to good use and actually tackle some of the projects rather than wishing we were motivated enough to do them.

Pinterest is a really neat way to compile recipes, good ideas, DIY projects, clothing you love, and quotes that make you think all in one place. Not only can you keep track of the things you come across during an online search, but you can see other people's ideas too! You can search by topic or hobby or keyword - or you can follow friends and see what they're "pinning" to their boards. It's a lot of fun, and there are some seriously creative ideas to be found.
