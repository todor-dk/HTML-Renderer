Pinterest is a
high-potential ecommerce channel

With a focus on photography and built-in merchandising via boards, Pinterest was already a great way to highlight your products to potential buyers. Now Buyable Pins let users buy your products right from Pinterest with just a few clicks. Our native integration makes it fast and simple to turn your products into Buyable Pins.

  • 100 million active monthly users
  • 93% of pinners use Pinterest to plan purchases
  • 87% of users have purchased because of Pinterest

Connect your store to Pinterest

Seamless buying for your customers, seamless selling for you

Frictionless purchasing

Customers can discover and buy your products right from Pinterest, reducing friction and improving conversion for a seamless ecommerce experience.

No additional work

Any product pinned from your store will automatically become a Buyable Pin, with no need to create additional listings.

Built to convert

Pinterest’s optimized checkout saves shipping and payment info so purchases can be completed with just two clicks.

Streamline your processing and analytics

Our integration makes processing Pinterest orders and tracking data more efficient

Business as usual

Pinterest orders and customer information are sent back to BigCommerce so you can process them with your other orders, aggregating all ecommerce transactions in one place.

Measure engagement and sales

Pinterest Analytics offers you valuable insights like the number of Pins, repins and orders that have come in.
