Pinterest Buyable Pins

Sell your products right from the Pinterest app

We’ve partnered with Pinterest™ to let your users buy your products seamlessly through new and existing Pins of your products.

Free 14-day trial, no credit card required.

It’s easy and instant

Pinterest works with your online store

All products pinned from your Shopify store will automatically become Buyable Pins.

Buying is simple

When a customer decides to purchase your product they can checkout with a credit card or Apple Pay without leaving the Pinterest app. The low barrier to purchase means your new customers will enjoy a streamlined experience.

Measure engagement and sales

Measure and track the number of Pins, repins, and orders using the Pinterest Channel Dashboard in your Shopify Admin.

Grow your brand

Sell a lifestyle—not just products. Build stories around your product pins, with your customer persona in mind.

Descriptive copy in your pins, boards, and bio can help you reach a wider audience through search.

Grow your network

Tap into influencer marketing. Consider inviting Pinfluencers and bloggers to create guest Boards.
