Pinterest Master List

If you love Pinterest as much as we do – then this is the party for you!

This HUGE Link party is such an amazing resource – full of fabulous crafty Pinterest accounts! If you love creative things – or all things Pinterest – we’d love for you to link up to the party! All the instructions are below – check those out if you haven’t ever linked up to a party before.

Time for some “Pin-spiration”!!

Its time to link up to the official

Allow us to present your Pinterest Party hosts! We would love for you to follow along with us, as your hosts we are constantly going through the list and repinning from you, as well as featuring you in our weekly Pinterest posts!

Michele from The Scrap Shoppe, Malia from Yesterday on Tuesday, and — ME!!! {Bev from Flamingo Toes}!

The Pinterest Party is the brainchild of Tammy from Not Just Paper and Glue, but she had to step aside from the party hosting. We wish her the best of luck with her endeavors and hope she’ll come back to join us again sometime!  

The goal for this party is to create a Master List of Pinterest accounts for crafty folks like yourselves.

This is a one time link party that will remain open indefinitely.

In true blog hop fashion, you can link up here, on Malia’s blog, or on Michele’s blog.
All links go to the same place so there is no need to link up in all 3 places.

Each Friday Malia, Michele, or I will randomly select a Pinterest account to feature and highlight pin boards.

We also are inviting guests to visit once per month to host a feature Friday!

We have a button you are welcome to grab and put on your blog.
Feel free to blog about the party and/or spread the word by Facebook or Twitter.
The more the merrier!
Actually the more, the more inspiration!

All creative minds are invited to join the Master List including craft bloggers, foodies, and mom blogs, too!

Rules:

Link to your main Pinterest page
example: http://pinterest.com/yourusername
DO NOT LINK TO YOUR BLOG!
This is a PINTEREST party. We want to see your Pinterest boards!

Leave your name and/or blog/website in the text box when you link up
example: Michele @ The Scrap Shoppe

* If you don’t have a blog, but want to link up your Pinterest account, please feel free to do so!

* If you do have a blog, be sure that your website is linked in your Pinterest profile so when you are featured we can include it in the write up and give you a proper shout out!
(Click on the Edit Profile button under your profile picture to add your blog address to your Pinterest account.)

So let’s link up!

Note: If you notice your link is no longer on the list it is because you linked to your blog and not your Pinterest account. Feel free to link back up using the correct web address!
I do visit your blog to see if you have a link to your Pinterest account in your sidebar, but if not I have to delete your link if it goes to your blog. :(

If you have a hard time linking a photo to the party, please upload one from your computer if you can. There’s seems to be a few issues with Linky Tools!
