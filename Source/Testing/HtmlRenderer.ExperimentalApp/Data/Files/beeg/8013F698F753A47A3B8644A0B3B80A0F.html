Review:
The philosophy behind Beeg’s mobile website is simplicity, and we appreciate their approach. The site features a simple list of large thumbnails, 25 per page, that the user can click on to continue to play the video.
 
 

While it is an easy idea to comprehend, we did have to make a few adjustments for the best experience. First, a standalone video-player app is almost a must. We use MX Player and highly recommend it: its free from the Google Play Store and played any file we threw at it without a hitch. Trying to play the videos through Browser or Google Chrome was pretty lackluster (you can see the photo below).
 
   

Like Beeg promises, its simple: just tap the thumbnail and you’re taken to the play page. Simply tap the thumbnail again, and you are prompted to open with your default app. If you installed MX Player like we suggested, you should see that on your list and be able to open the video there. Do not select the ‘Play Video’ or ‘Download Video’ buttons on the bottom – they are ads which try to get you to subscribe to a fake Android app. One of the perks of Beeg is you can save videos to your phone’s memory: simply press and hold the video thumbnail and select “Save link as…” from the menu options. These can always be played back with MX Player or other video players.
 
   

Beeg’s no-frills approach could appeal to people who don’t want to specify categories, and particularly those who enjoy that specific content, but we found that we missed some of our favorite categories, and after a few days grew bored of the similar, repetitive content despite the website being updated consistently. The catalog of videos seems limited to selections from Naughty America, Brazzers, and affiliated companies; if you want Amateur or Celebrity content you won’t find it here. In fact, even narrowing your search to Asian or Teen or Anal or any specific category is not an option. This is Beeg’s biggest downfall in our opinion, and hopefully some option will be implemented soon to the otherwise clean and functional site.
