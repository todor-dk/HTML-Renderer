After School Football Coaching

Our 158 full time FA Lidl Skills football coaches run after school football coaching courses at over 50 Skills Centres throughout England to help 5-11s improve their football skills. Learn More

Holiday Football Coaching

During the school holidays our FA Lidl Skills football coaches run holiday football coaching sessions for children of all abilities to gain an introduction to the programme. Learn More

Football Coaching In Schools

The FA Lidl Skills Programme works in over 1,000 primary schools a year, providing specialist football coaching for children and supporting teachers in their delivery of sport. Learn More
