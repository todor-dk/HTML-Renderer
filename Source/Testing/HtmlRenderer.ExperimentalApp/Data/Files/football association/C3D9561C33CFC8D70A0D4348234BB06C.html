1st Annual MP Football Golf Outing 

Friday July 21st

(Tee times between 10AM -12:30)

Where: Mount Prospect Golf Club

Cost: $100 per golfer which includes 18 holes with a cart, lunch, 2 free drinks, door prize
and competition prizes. Also included is admission to the after party with appetizers and
entertainment.
After Party: $25 per person who do not golf during the day from 5:30 pm to 10:30 pm.

Hole sponsorships are available for $100 which includes a sign.
