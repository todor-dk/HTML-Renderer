Home

Rogers Youth Football is a non-profit organization focusing on youth development through the teaching of football fundamentals. Our 2nd-3rd grade league is our teaching, non-competitive league. Our 4th-6th grade league is our competitive house league partnering with the Elk River league, games are played in both cities.

Our 7th-8th grade league is highly competitive in the North Suburban League with several communities. All of our coaches are parent volunteers and without their support we could not be as successful as Rogers Youth Football has been over the last several years.
