    • Welcome

    Welcome to Cape Town Tygerberg Football Association Official Website

    The main objective being to supply you with up to date fixtures, league and cup results , logs as well as the latest news and events happening within the Association.

    In addition , the website will have the CTTFA constitution, affiliated club, SAFA CT and CTTFA details .

    Cape Town Tygerberg Football has a proud history, and this website is  a welcome addition .
    We welcome any assistance and/or suggestions on how we can improve even further and   with your support we can only go from strength to strength.

    CTTFA Mission Statement

    To provide professionally organized league and cup competitions and quality administration for all stake holders within Cape Town Tygerberg Football Association without compromise.

    Safa Cape Town

    South African Football Association Cape Town
    • The Association was established in 2006. SAFA CT is non government organization and controls the regional office under SAFA National. SAFA CT has provided the structures to take football to all levels of the Cape Town community. There are now age-group competitions from under-07 level up, assisted by vibrant and competitive amateur football programmes.
    • A democratically-elected executive committee headed by a President and 14 other REC members who volunteer their time to oversee the running of staff operation and football in Cape Town. To communicate with our clients and stakeholders, we hold Monthly Council Meetings, Monthly Sub-Council Meetings and Annual General Meeting
