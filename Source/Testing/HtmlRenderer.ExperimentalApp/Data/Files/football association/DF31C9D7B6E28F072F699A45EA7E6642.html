SP17 3v3 Grassroots Tournament concludes with 5 winners

[May 9, 2017 - Harmon, Guam] Real Guahan FC, Lady Islanders, Wings, Islanders, and Bombers Blue all emerged victorious in their respective divisions of the Spring 2017 Guam Football Association 3v3 Grassroots Tournament at the GFA National Training Center Saturday.

Real Guahan FC, featuring a mix of players from Wings Red and Wings Black in the Aloha Maid Minetgot Cup Elite Youth League, won the U13 Boys divisions after finishing off the Islanders in the day's finale. Coached by Martin Iseke, Gavin Baker, Kai Pahl, Raif Sablan, Kyle Takano, CJ Takano, and Christopher Na, received winners trophies during the tournament's awards ceremony.

In the U13 Girls division, the Lady Islanders defeated The Force to claim the division's championship title. Coach Walter Benavente guided players Sarah Benavente, Kailey Quidachay, Olivia Leon Guerrero, Jada Han, and Jadyn Palomares to victory in the division's championship match.
