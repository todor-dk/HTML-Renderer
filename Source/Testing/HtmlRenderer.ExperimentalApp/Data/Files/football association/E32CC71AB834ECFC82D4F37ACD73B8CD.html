The Federation

The Youth Football and Cheer Federation organizes a progressive football and cheer program for youngsters ages 4-13 years old. We also emphasize the importance of education and community service to our participants.

Welcome to the Fed !!! _

The Federation is the premier youth football and cheer organization based in Houston, Texas. The Federation is focused more on substance than hype, and more on the positive impact of our programs on parents, participants, and the community. Each and every member organization is treated with dignity and respect from the leadership of the Federation. "Football Is More Than A Game" !!!

Recent News / Information

2017 Season Coming Soon
As the Federation wrapped up probably its most successful season ever, things have not slowed down as several new teams have formed and other existing organizations have expressed interest in joining the YFF for the 2017 season. Planning is underway to ensure continued improvement and operational excellence in our programs.
Texas Championship Alliance
The results are in...the Federation and Houston swept the inaugural Texas Championship Alliance Bowl Championship Series. Alliance Weekend was fun-filled and provided an opportunity for the Federation coaches, staff and parents to forge new friendships with our new partners from the "U"nited Youth Football League of North Texas.
