2017 Hall of Fame Announement

CONGRATULATIONS TO THIS YEARS USFTL NATIONAL HALL OF FAME CLASS! Welcome to the Family! The USFTL NATIONAL HALL OF FAME (CLASS OF 2017) The USFTL National Hall of Fame Class of 2017 has just been announced! This year we had 4 coaches, 8 players, 1 contributor, & 1 referee inducted. There were a total of 37 nominations and this year, 14 will be inducted in the National Hall of Fame: Anthony Buckman Player (Fort Washington, MD) Buck Cureton Coach (New York City, NY) Timothy Fobbs Contributor (Washington, DC) Paul Feldhausen Player/Coach (Milwaukee, WI) Lorenzo Fludd Player (Fort Washington, MD) Tom Flynn Player (Chicago, IL) Robby Hudson Referee (Charlotte, NC) Marcus Johnson Player (Raleigh, NC) Rod Johnson Coach/Contributor (Jacksonville, FL) Lynn Lewis Coach/Contributor (New York City, NY) Billy McCue Player (Chicago, IL) James Sanders Coach/Contributor (Mechanicsville, MD) Patrick Walker Player (Fort Washington, MD) Delvecchio Wilson Player (Tampa, FL)
