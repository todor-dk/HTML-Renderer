8th Grade Tackle Football Update

By Rochester Youth Football Association 04/18/2017, 9:00am CDT

Attention future 8th graders wishing to participate in Rochester Youth Football Association.  Starting in school year 2017-2018 – Rochester Youth Football Association will no longer offer a tackle football program for 8th grade students.  Instead, Rochester Public Schools in partnership with the local high schools will be absorbing the 8th grade program which will be tied to the respective high schools.  Please refer to the following websites for information about the high school programs:

If you are a student who will attend Century High School please visit:
http://www.centurypanthers.org

If you are a student who will attend John Marshall High School please visit:
http://www.johnmarshallrockets.org

If you are a student who will attend Mayo High School please visit:
http://www.mayospartans.org

2017 Spring NFL Flag Football Registration Now Open

By Rochester Youth Football Association 01/02/2017, 9:45am CST

Registration for the Spring 2017 NFL Flag Football season is now open.  Registration runs from today through March 31st and is only $75.00 which includes an NFL jersey.  Sessions start in late April and run through May.  All of the details can be found on our Flag Football Page.
