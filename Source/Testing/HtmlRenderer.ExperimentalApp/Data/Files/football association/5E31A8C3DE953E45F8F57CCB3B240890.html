UNION YOUTH FOOTBALL ASSOCIATION (UYFA) is a 501(c)(3) non profit organization. We are dedicated to serving the boys and girls of the Union PS school district here in Tulsa, Oklahoma. We provide a multi division tackle football and cheer/spirit association for our kids in 1st-7th grades. UYFA is a member of the INDIAN NATIONS FOOTBALL CONFERENCE (INFC)

The UYFA promotes the education, knowledge and training of football for players, players parents, coaches, as well as other interested parties in an atmosphere of mutual respect and encouragement.
