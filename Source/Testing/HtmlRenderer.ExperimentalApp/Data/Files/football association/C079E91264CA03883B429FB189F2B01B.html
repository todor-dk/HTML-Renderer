Calgary Bantam Football Association

The Calgary Bantam Football Association is a full contact tackle football league for youth’s aged 13 to 15 years.

The league is comprised of 13 teams, 10 of which are in Calgary, and 3 which encompass the surrounding communities. The rural teams are based in Airdrie, Okotoks, and Cochrane . All teams play their games at Shouldice Athletic Park in northwest Calgary.
