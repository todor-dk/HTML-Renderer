Welcome to The Foxboro Midget Football & Cheerleading Website!

This is Foxboro's 61st year participating in the Pop Warner program. We pride ourselves in diligently serving our community in this vital and fun activity for our children. The objective of Foxboro Midget Football & Cheerleading (FMF) is to offer every child the opportunity to participate in the sport of football or cheerleading and to ensure that they do it in a safe, enjoyable, yet disciplined environment. We as parents, board members and coaches feel it is essential to emphasize teamwork, sportsmanship, camaraderie, and physical fitness throughout all phases of our program.

FMF has many dedicated volunteers that keep the league running, but as the program grows, so does the need for more volunteers. Please contact us today if you can help in any way. FMF is a proud member of    The Hockomock League.
   
We urge you to check back on our website often for updates and new information.

Football Equipment Pick-up Dates

Football equipment pick-up will take place at the Ken Goodwin Sr. Fieldhouse (Ernie George Field).  All required paperwork must be turned in in order to receive equipment.  A parent must accompany their child to sign the equipment receipt form.

Wednesday, July 26th (5:30 - 8:00 p.m.) for last names starting with the letters A thru L

Thursday, July 27th (5:30 – 8:00 p.m.) for last names starting with letters M thru Z

  • Siblings with different last names can attend on either night to make it convenient for parents.

  • If you are unable to attend your scheduled night you may attend the other.  If you miss both nights then equipment will be issued on August 1st before camp starts.

 

FMF Board of Directors
