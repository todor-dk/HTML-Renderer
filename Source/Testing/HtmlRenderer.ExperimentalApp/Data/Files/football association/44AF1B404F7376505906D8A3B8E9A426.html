Our Goal

North Metro Football League (NMFL) was formed to serve the areas of the Northern Metro Region of Atlanta. NMFL's mission is to provide a league to address the needs of the youth organizations in the community we serve embodying the following initiatives:

  • In it for the love of the game
  • Believes the most important character trait is integrity and sportsmanship
  • Providing USA FOOTBALL Certification trained Coaches that can properly teach correct fundamentals of the game.
  • Providing a Safe fun-filled environment
  • League Teams to follow USA Football's Safety Requirements

North Metro Football League (NMFL) Receives Recognition from GA House of Representatives

Representatives of the North Metro Football League (NMFL), State Bank and Sports Care Connect were formally recognized by the Georgia House of Representatives on March 20, 2013 with a Resolution for the groups’ leadership efforts on Concussions.

The NMFL was recognized as the first league in Georgia to offer such a benefit to its players and the first to take such a significant step toward addressing the issue of sport induced concussions in Georgia.
