“BOCHK Community Football Partnership Programme”

The Hong Kong Football Association (HKFA) is delighted to announce that BOCHK Charitable Foundation will donate a sum of HKD50,000,000 to establish “BOCHK Community Football Development Fund” in which the interest from investment will be used for the “BOCHK Community Football Partnership Programme”, with the objective to enhance football development in Hong Kong, particularly for disadvantaged young people. A series of regular events will be held to promote the football development as well as to improve the standard of local football. Thus a ceremony has been held today (7 July) during the BOCHK Centenary Celebration to kick off the programme.

KBZ Bank Cup 2017 - Hong Kong U-22 1:0 Cambodia U-22

Hong Kong U-22 squared off against Cambodia U-22 in the final match day of the KBZ Bank Cup 2017 in Yangon, Myanmar on Sunday afternoon. Teenage sensation Jordan Lam found the back of the net for Hong Kong U-22 in the first-half and that was all the side needed to secure a spirited 1:0 victory against Cambodia U-22.
