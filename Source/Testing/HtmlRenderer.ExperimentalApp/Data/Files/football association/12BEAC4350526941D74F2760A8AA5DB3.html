How our fundraisers work: All the details

Fundraisers in a Few Simple Steps!

1. Order tickets

Pay only for the printing (about $0.19 per ticket) plus shipping and handling up front.

2. Sell tickets – keep 70%

Sell 1 ticket or 10,000 tickets, and you keep 70% of the money you collect.

3. Pay your invoice – 30%

Pay only for the tickets that you sell. If you don’t sell a ticket, you don’t pay for it.

4. Winners get paid $$$!

We calculate the winning game cards and work with you to pay the winners.
