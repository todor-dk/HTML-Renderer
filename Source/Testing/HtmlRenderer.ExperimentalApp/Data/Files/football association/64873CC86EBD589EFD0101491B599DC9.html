About the Armstrong Cooper Youth Football Association (ACYFA)

The Armstrong Cooper Youth Football Association is a non-profit tackle football organization for kids in grades 1 through 8.

  • Grades 1-2 practice and play in a six-week in-house development league 2 days per week. Practice days are determined by the coach in early August.
  • Grades 3-6 play a 9-10 game season in the Twin Cities North Youth Football League. Practice is 3 times per week with games typically on Saturdays. Practice days are determined by the coach in early August.
  • Grades 7-8 play a 9-10 game season in the North Suburban Football League. Practice is 3 times per week with games typically on Saturdays. Practice days are determined by the coach in early August.
  • Registration fees include all tackle football equipment (except shoes, mouth guard and cup), fields, referees, and a practice jersey that the players get to keep. 
  • Practice starts Monday, August 7th and the regular season will run through late October.  Click the links on the left to find out more about each program.
  • All coaches and board members volunteer their time to make this program successful.

2017 ACYFA Registration now open!

Click on the link above to register for the 2017 season

Cost: The cost to register for the 2017 ACYFA season is $100.00 for grades 1-2 and $240.00 for grades 3-8.

Early Bird Discount: If grades 3-8 register before June 10th (must register by June 9th), you will receive an early bird discount of $20.00.

Family Discount: If you are registering more than one player in your family for grades 3-8, you will receive a $20.00 discount on the second (and third, if applicable) child's registration fee.  In order for the registration system to recognize that your family is registering multiple players, please register all of your players at this time.  On the Review Page, there is a link near the bottom to "Register Another Player" - please click on this link to ensure that all of your family's players are registered within the same order. Failure to do so will result in a forfeiture of your family's discounted rates.  Also, please note that the Family Discount does not apply if you are receiving a hardship discount.

Payment Plan: ACYFA offers a payment plan. $120.00 is due at initial sign up and the second installment of $120.00 is due by July 1st. You will be responsible for a $5.00 registration fee charged to ACYFA by Sport Ngin for each installment (total will be $10.00 in addition to the registration fee).  Early bird discount does apply if registering before June 10th.
