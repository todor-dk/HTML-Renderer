FINAL FOOTBALL EQUIPMENT HANDOUT

Our final equipment handout will be Monday July17 6:00-8:30pm at the Field House in Cranberry Community Park. This is the FINAL handout, no others will be offered. The Field House is the brick building located between fields #1 & #5, at the top of the gravel drive.

Only players who are signed up and PAID will be given equipment. If registered but not paid, please pay via the website, or by check at equipment handouts. To receive equipment, please arrive with two checks made out to SVJFAC.  NO EQUIPMENT WILL BE GIVEN OUT UNLESS BOTH CHECKS ARE RECEIVED AT HANDOUTS. There will be NO exceptions. One check will be for the $125.00 association fundraising fee. Each family in the association will be given one book of raffle tickets that you can sell to defer this cost. The second check will be for $200.00, and will be used as a security deposit for your equipment. This check will NOT be cashed unless you fail to turn in your equipment and game jersey at the end of the season. The actual cost to replace these items is over $250.00 per player.
 
Each player will receive a helmet, shoulder pads and game pants. Game jerseys will be given out at a later date. If you have any questions about equipment or equipment handouts please contact Pat Brugos - pbrug74@yahoo.com.

Heads Up Pittsburgh 2017

Heads UP Pittsburgh 2017 Registration is Now Open!

UPMC Sports Medicine, The Pittsburgh Penguins Foundation, Community College of Allegheny County, and Community College of Beaver County are pleased to announce the Heads Up Pittsburgh Program for the Summer of 2017. In addition to our main program, we are excited to add limited dates in Greensburg,PA and Erie,PA over the coming weeks for our 2017 program. Please go to www.upmc.com/baselinetesting to create a profile and schedule for all available sessions or click the links below:

Click Here to Register Ages 7-9 for Pediatric Baseline Testing (Free)

Click Here to Register Ages 10-14 for Adult Baseline Testing (Free)

Click Here to Register Ages 15+ for Adult Baseline Testing ($25.00)


If you previously registered, we thank you for your support and look forward to seeing you soon. If you did not register yet, space is still available to get your test completed and we encourage you to register early! Registration is now open for Individuals and ATHLETES OF ANY SPORT. PLEASE take a moment to read the specifics of the program below:

* Individuals ages 7-14 can test for FREE.

* Individuals ages 15 and over (including adults) can test for a nominal fee of $25.00

* All test takers must be at least 7 years of age for pediatric testing session or 10 years of age for adult sessions on the date of the test to participate. No Exceptions.

* The LAST DATE of testing will be July 28. Please register early to assure your seat as there will not be any sessions added later in the summer.

* Pediatric Testing (ages 7-9) will only take place at the Rooney and Lemieux Sports Complexes. Adult Testing (ages 10+) will take place at all of our locations.

* The first 3,000 athletes will receive a FREE 9” Pilates Ball in addition to the baseline test.

* First-time participants will receive a neck strength kit and balance disc.

* Individuals ages 7 - 12 should have a new baseline every year. Individuals over the age of 13 and adults should have a new baseline every 2 years.


No Phone Registrations will be accepted. Please utilize the online scheduling system using the links provided in this email.

PLEASE REGISTER AT WWW.UPMC.COM/BASELINETESTING BY CLICKING ON THE APPROPRIATE AGE GROUP IN THE RIGHT-HAND COLUMN UNDER “ADDITIONAL INFORMATION”.

If you do not wish to receive emails from us, you can unsubscribe or manage your subscriptions.

2017 RAIDER PRIDE FOOTBALL CAMP

2017 Raider Pride Football Camp

Date: Tuesday July 18, 2017

Time: 9:00am-12:00pm

Where: Nex Tier Stadium ( Seneca Valley High School football stadium)

128 Seneca School Road

Harmony PA, 16037

Cost: $25.00

INFO:

The Seneca Valley Coaching Staff and SV Varsity Players will be holding this Raider Pride Camp. 

This camp is opened to all kids who are entering grades 3,4,5,6,7 and 8. 

We will be featuring Non-contact, individualized instruction of fundamental football skills at all positions. Player will be grouped according to age for all drills. 

Requirements: Players should wear football cleats or tennis shoes, shorts or sweatpants and T-shirt. 

Campers will be responsible for all items he/she bring to camp. 

Drink and a snack will be provided.
