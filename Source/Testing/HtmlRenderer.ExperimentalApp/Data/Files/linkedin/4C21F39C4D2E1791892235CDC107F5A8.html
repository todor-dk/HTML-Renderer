LinkedIn for Good

Connecting underserved communities to economic opportunity

We realize that economic opportunity isn't distributed evenly.  Some communities face greater obstacles than others when it comes to building networks and accessing opportunity.  What if everyone could be connected to people who could guide and inspire them and to the knowledge and opportunities that could transform them?  At LinkedIn, we believe this is possible.

In 2016, LinkedIn for Good reached 1 million underserved youth, veterans, refugees, employees and nonprofits. Join us on our mission to unlock greater opportunity for all.
