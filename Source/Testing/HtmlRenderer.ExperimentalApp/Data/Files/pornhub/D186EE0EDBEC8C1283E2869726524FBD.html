          There are currently significant efforts underway to de-anonymise the blockchain and link payment addresses to specific individuals, which would be trivial if sensitive data were to leak from any of the exchanges. Individuals using bitcoin on dark net markets go to extreme lengths to dissociate themselves from their bitcoin addresses.

It is a younger brother of bitcoin but its usage is growing significantly, with hundreds of millions of dollars currently held in Monero.
