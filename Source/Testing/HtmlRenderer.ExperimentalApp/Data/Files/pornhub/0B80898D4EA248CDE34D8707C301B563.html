PornHub's Thanksgiving Search Trends Prove Americans Are Super Freaky Horndogs

Clay Skipper
November 25, 2015
Facebook
Twitter
Email

Turkey: America's most underrated aphrodisiac.

Here's the thing: America is full of kinky weirdos. We all know it. You find yourself looking at polite, punctual Bob three desks down and thinking: I wonder what kind of crazy sex shit that guy's into. It's why Fifty Shades of Grey was such a mega hit. It tapped into the lusty, intercourse-obsessed vein of the country's bloodstream, providing the catharsis the USA's freaky hornballs needed. Oh, okay, so he's into chains too? Cool.

Well, my randy countrymen, it's kinkier than we thought. PornHub just released some stats on their Thanksgiving traffic and it reveals that the appetite for food is not as great as the appetite for, er, off-beat pornography. For many, it appears Thanksgiving isn't just a return to the ancestral home place where you can find Mom's comforting laugh and Grandpa's strange, stale must. A little family time, it appears, means a lot more "me time," where Thanksgiving feasters find themselves back at their adolescent keyboards tap-tapping out searches on PornHub that provide a revealing window into America's sexual subconscious.

Here are the biggest takeaways:

Nothing turns us on quite like a good Thanksgiving-themed sex party.
The most searched terms on Thanksgiving include Thanksgiving, Thanksgiving Blowjob, Thanksgiving Sex, and Thanksgiving Dinner, which begs a few questions. What is a "Thanksgiving Blowjob"? Does it involve leaves and gourds? Do the sweaters and corduroys come off, or is it more Thanksgiving-y with them on? Must you politely thank them for the oral favors provided? Did the pilgrims do this? What's the erotic fascination with Turkey Day? Sure, there's plenty of topical, punny dirty talk to be done (gravy; stuffing; breasts; giblets; meat), but it's hard to imagine looking at that bountiful spread and seeing active sex on a bed, not passive indigestion on a couch.

We like—no, like, really, really like—Native Americans.
As you can see above, other terms among the most searched include "Native American", "Pocahontas", "Navajo", and "American Indian". Pocahontas? Fair. But then you remember that's not really Pocahontas. That's a sexualized, American reproduction of the exotic queen we want Pocahontas to be. And she's animated, and waaayyy too young for John Smith. And then you remember that she wasn't animated. She was real. And so was the tribe and band of people who we once ate a very friendly, delicious dinner with—and then massacred. Not so horny now, are you?

America is way more incestuous than we thought.
Among search terms that showed the most increased usage on Thanksgiving: Granny, Step Mom, Step Sister, Southern Cougar, and House Wife. If you're looking for a positive spin here, I guess at the very least it's a step mom. But Granny? I mean, do you, America.

"Pawn Shop" is a term people type into search bars on porn websites.
This is not even Thanksgiving-related. What a time to be alive.

The food coma is real. So real, in fact, we can't even pull ourselves out for the sake of a little porn-viewing.
This is either a testament to the power of food, the power of football, or the power of what happens when the two collide head-on with concussive force, like a wide receiver and a human-seeking linebacker.

From the report: "On Thursday we found that mobile traffic dropped the least (-9.9 percent), but increased more significantly on Friday (+9.8 percent) compared to other devices. This is most likely due to people travelling and relying more on their portable devices while away from home."

In a minivan somewhere...

Dad: "Hey, Mikey, what you doing back there, man? You've been on your phone ever since we left Grammy's, like, three hours back."

Mikey: "Candy Crush? Yeah, Candy Crush. Great game!"

New Hampshire is our most Puritan state. Nebraska...is not.
Thanksgiving sees New Hampshire's PornHub traffic drop by 40 percent. Nebraska's drops by 1 percent. One of those states truly respects the sanctity of Thanksgiving and the warmth of sharing a fireside living room with rarely-seen loved ones. The other is hiding in the dark corners of their house, full of food but searching for "Pocahontas Thanksgiving blowjob" on their phone.
