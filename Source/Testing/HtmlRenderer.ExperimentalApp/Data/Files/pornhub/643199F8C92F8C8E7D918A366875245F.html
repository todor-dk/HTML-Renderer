      Pornhub Premium Review

      We Went Undercover To Find Out If Paying For XXX Material Is Really Worth It

      Bobby Box
      February 15, 2017
      Share
      Tweet
      0
      Shares

      I’ve been a long-time connoisseur of pornography. Not in a cooped-up-in-a-dark-basement-without-socializing kind of way, but more of a graphic means of stimulation when the self needs some loving. As a result of all this explicit consumption, I like to think I’ve discovered the best free websites to visit. Because nobody pays for porn these days, do they?

      Evidently no, they do not. Internet accountability website Covenant Eyes released a collection of pornography statistics in 2013 that proved quite interesting. The website discovered that roughly a decade ago, global porn revenues were estimated at a resounding $20 billion, with half of this profit coming from U.S. consumers alone.

      By 2011, both of these numbers decreased by 50% due in large part to the amount of free pornography available online. Covenant Eyes estimates that 80% to 90% of internet users solely access free pornography. Which makes sense. I mean, why pay for pornography when there’s ample resources available for free?

      Well, there is an answer: So you can continue to watch porn. In a previous interview I’d had with Chauntelle Tibbals, embedded sociologist and author of Exposure: A Sociologist Explores Sex, Society, and Adult Entertainment, porn-streaming websites host stolen clips for viewers to watch for free. These websites then generate revenue for clicks — none of which go toward those creating the film, but instead to the websites hosting the clips.

      Since it isn’t the website that populating the site and instead the site’s viewers, the hosting companies aren’t held legally responsible. By paying for porn, you’re giving porn production companies the resources to create new porn. By not paying, you’re hurting porn industry profits and therefore hurting porn’s future potential.

      Enter Pornhub Premium(NSFW, obviously). It exists to marry the simplicity and ease-of-use of the porn-streaming websites with the actual porn content of the porn production companies (which, in Pornhub Premium’s instance, include Brazzers, Reality Kings, Digital Playground, and Fake Taxi). Why those sites? Well, they're all owned by MindGeek — which also owns Pornhub.

      What Is Pornhub Premium?

      Pornhub Premium is a streaming, subscription-based service that the media has coined the “Netflix for porn.” While similar services have been available for adult content in the past, none have had the brand recognition Pornhub has, which is exactly why this is the only Netflix-style service for porn that people are talking about.

      For a subscription fee of only $9.99 a month, members have access to ad-free videos, mobile-friendly browsing, 1080p video quality, HD videos (added daily), and over 13,000 full DVDs. But are these perks worth the almost $10 a month? I decided to reach out to Pornhub for a Pornhub Premium membership to write this article, which they graciously sent me. Thus started my month of paid porn — that I didn’t technically have to pay for.

      My Month Of Paid Porn

      From mid-February to mid-March, I indulged in what Pornhub Premium had to offer.

      At first, I couldn’t differentiate between Pornhub and Pornhub Premium and thought I had to click some special menu option that took me to a more luxe-looking website. It turns out that the premium version of the website was essentially more of the same. Nothing — appearence-wise — made it look any better than it had when it was free.

      The first thing I noticed when accessing the premium version of the site was that the clips were much longer — meaning users actually get the opportunity to watch the full-length clip, which is great. Except over time, you discover (like I did) that these shortened clips tend to contain the best shots that the clip has to offer, and you end up skipping two-thirds of the video anyways.

      Being able to watch full DVDs, however, was much a much more satisfying perk, and allows you to better appreciate a well-written script and promising adult actors than you do when a clip is edited down to cut out the dialogue entirely. If you're the kind of guy who appreciates some sexual tension build-up, you'll really appreciate this aspect of Pornhub Premium.

      Pornhub Premium

      Premium members are also granted access to exclusive, members-only programs, similar to Netflix — their latest being “Full Holes,” a Full House parody, which was damn good, as far as porn parodies go.

      Something I wasn’t so keen to discover was that many videos still had the unnecessary studio watermarks in the corner of the clip, which was something I figured would be eliminated when paid for. Also, by clicking "Related Videos" to watch after you’ve finished watching a clip, it’s easy to find your way back to the low-quality videos that non-paying members have access to. Too easy, really. It really wasn’t that big of a deal, but for the sake of this review, I’m being picky.

      Is Paying For Porn Worth It?

      So, is it? Ethically, yes. As a writer, I believe all creative efforts should be compensated. Plus, if we want porn to continue creating smutty versions of our favorite TV shows and films, they need to see more money. But as for whether the benefits of Pornhub Premium are enough to excuse a monthly fee, I’m less inclined to say yes.

      HD porn is a plus, for sure, but it isn’t enough for me to consider paying for it — though I’m sure many would. I’ve found there to be plenty of websites you can visit for similar quality and quantity. Personally, the biggest advantage for Pornhub Premium is in the value of full DVDs, which, when prices are compared to the subscription fee, is one hell of a deal.

      There's still very much a perceived shame in porn viewing and the industry, which could be a big reason people don’t want to spend money on it. This is probably the reason similar services for movies and television (Netflix, Hulu, Shomi, etc.), as well as gaming (like Playstation Plus) have managed to find more mainstream success.

      However, with more original content being added to the lineup and word of potential VR integration in the works, features like these will only add to the value that comes with a Pornhub Premium account. And with value comes customers.
