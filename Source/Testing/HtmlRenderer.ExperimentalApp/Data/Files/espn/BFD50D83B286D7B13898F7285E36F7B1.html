ESPN unveiled the cover stars of ESPN The Magazine’s seventh annual Body Issue Monday. Washington Nationals star Bryce Harper is joined by Olympians Amanda Bingson and Chantae McMillan, Cleveland Cavaliers’ Kevin Love, 12-time Olympic medalist Natalie Coughlin, and New York Giants wide receiver Odell Beckham, Jr. on the six different Body Issue covers.

U.S. national team defender, and new World Cup champion Ali Krieger also features in the issue, and discussed her desire to pose in the spread.

“It’s important to show your vulnerabilities. I’m proud of my body, I’m proud of my sport, I’m proud of being a female athlete. Being naked is just another aspect of that. It’s real. It’s raw. It’s honest.”
