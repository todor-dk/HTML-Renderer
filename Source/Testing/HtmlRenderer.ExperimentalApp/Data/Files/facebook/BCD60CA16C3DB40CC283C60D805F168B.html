The National Archives on Facebook

You can stay connected to the National Archives through Facebook! Several organizations within the National Archives maintain Facebook accounts to share information with the public about our activities. We encourage your input, but please be aware of our policies concerning comments on Facebook.

Add a piece of history to your Facebook profile! Download one of these National Archives themed Cover Photos to use on your own Facebook timeline.

You can visit our various Facebook pages by clicking on the links below.
