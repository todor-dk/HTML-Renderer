Nike Tiempo

Despite being thought of as a young buck in the world of football boots, the Nike Tiempo can truly lay claim to being one of the most storied names in football boot history.

Having been worn by the likes of Ronaldhino, Socrates, Ian Wright, Eric Cantona, Andrea Pirlo, Patrick Kluivert and dozens more, the Tiempo is regarded highly for offering incredible touch and feel an combining it with equally-dazzling innovation.

The boot that started it all for Nike, the Tiempo name was first introduced in the 70’s, but it wasn’t until the early 90’s that it became iconic.

Through a combination of high-profile endorsees at Italia ’90 and USA ’94 and an incredible array of adverts on television through the 90’s, the Nike Tiempo name became household in a time when a renewed focus was being put on what players had on their feet.

Today, the Tiempo VI is still holding onto it’s roots. Featuring a thin kangaroo leather upper with a versatile soleplate, it is still the boot of choice by legends like Andrea Pirlo and Francesco Totti and other such as Gerard Pique and Sergio Ramos.
