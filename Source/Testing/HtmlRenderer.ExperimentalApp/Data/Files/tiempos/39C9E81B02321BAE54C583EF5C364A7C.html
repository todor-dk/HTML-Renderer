Amar en tiempos revueltos (Spanish for "To Love in Troubled Times", or literally “Loving in Turbulent Times”) is a Spanish soap opera set in the times of the Spanish civil war and the early Francoism. The serial has been aired from 2005 and has won prizes in Spain and a Silver Medal in the «New York Festivals» [1].[1] Since January 2013 has aired on Antena 3 with new name, Amar es para siempre (Spanish for "To Love is Forever").[2]
