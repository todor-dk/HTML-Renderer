Mailen Auroux (born 25 July 1988) is an Argentine tennis player. As of 26 April 2010, she is ranked World No. 346 in singles[1] and No. 469 in doubles.[2] She has enjoyed success at the ITF Women's Circuit, winning nine singles and seven doubles titles, and made her debut for Argentina Fed Cup team in the 2010 Fed Cup World Group II, scoring a doubles win against Estonia.[3]
