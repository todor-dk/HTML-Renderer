Dutch[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /meːlə(n)/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Etymology[edit]

From English e-mail with -en appended and the e- dropped.

Verb[edit]

mailen

  1. to email, e-mail
