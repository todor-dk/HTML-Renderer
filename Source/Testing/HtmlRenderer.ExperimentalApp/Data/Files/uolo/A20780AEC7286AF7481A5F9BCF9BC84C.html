How much Social Media is good for my kid?

Social Media provides a great platform for kids to express themselves. It can provide a genuine support network for them. It can strengthen their friendship and they can get a sense of belonging from it. It also provides awareness about the real issues in this world.
But there are issues which needs to be addressed. It takes lot of time from kids which can be used for studying or physical activities. If they’re spending five hours in ...front of digital screen, not outside playing, doing homework, or interacting with the parent, that’s a problem. Other risks are cyber bullying, access to pornographic material, acute FOMO (Fear of Missing out) which means their activity should be monitored and they should be regularly counselled.
American Academy of Pediatrics’ recommendation that screen time should be limited to two hours per day. Prolonged digital screen time can lead to physical problems like slouching posture, carpal-tunnel, neck strain and eye problems. Excessive media use can also lead to attention problems, school difficulties, sleep and eating disorders, obesity and changes in social behaviour.
So, social media is something which cannot be avoided but has to be continuously monitored by parents and guardians.
