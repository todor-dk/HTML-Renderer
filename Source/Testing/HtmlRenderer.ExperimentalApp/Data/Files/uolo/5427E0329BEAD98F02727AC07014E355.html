Nanbantei Japanese Restaurant (Far East Plaza)
14 Scotts Road, Singapore

Yakitori Bento Lunch Set ($13.80)

Famous for its affordability and quality, it is no wonder why this popular yakitori restaurant remains as one of the best go-to places when one craves for some good and smokey Japanese grilled skewers.

It was like a walk down memory lane as the bento was still as oishii (delicious) and looked the same as it was when I last had this many years ago. 😋 The bento comes with 5 assorted sticks of yakitori with ume (plum) sauce by the side, japanese rice with sweet braised minced pork, braised mushrooms and a big seaweed.

Savour the tastiness of the meat, they were still moist and tender with all that fragrant smokiness and slight burnt crisp. 👍 Let's start from the top, there were tsukune (chicken meatballs), uzura tamago (quail eggs), negima (chicken with leek), shiso maki (pork with shiso leaf) and asparagus maki (asparagus wrapped with pork). 😍

P.S: Try to make reservations beforehand as they do have regular lunch and dinner crowds. You can do that easily via Burpple App now. 😘
