Job Description

“How many golf balls can fit in a Boeing 747; Why are manholes cover round?”...
What the heck??

Thankfully, at Uolo we won’t waste your time asking questions which are neither relevant to your skill set nor pertinent to our business. We are a successful start-up that is transforming education. We have had an amazing year - partnering with 700+ schools and serving 700,000+ parents and their kids. Now, we are on the lookout for a coding guru who will: script our growth story, decode our growing pains, and execute our next level strategy!

Our mission is simple: Create world class technology products that improve the quality of childhood education and at the same time make life easier for the educators.

Responsibilities:

You'll be an all-rounder responsible for maintaining, expanding, and scaling our product portfolio. You'll work on everything from complex algorithms, database design to configuring new servers on cloud.

Skills & Requirements:

• 0-2 years of best in class programming experience in any language but preferably PHP ( Don’t worry if you don’t know PHP, we’ll give you time to ramp up)
• Advance knowledge of Threading, Data Structure & Algorithms

• Good knowledge of MongoDB, HTML 5 and CSS

• Working knowledge of Redis, RabbitMQ and AWS

• Ability to do quick Proof of Concept on Open Source Technologies

Compensation:

• Salary will not be a constraint for the right candidate
• Attractive stock options

About Uolo (www.theuolo.com)

Uolo Technology is founded by paranoid parents who want to make a difference to the schooling experience. The founders graduated from top business school in India (IIM A and ISB).

We are looking for passionate people who want to make a mark in industry and have a fast progression career.
