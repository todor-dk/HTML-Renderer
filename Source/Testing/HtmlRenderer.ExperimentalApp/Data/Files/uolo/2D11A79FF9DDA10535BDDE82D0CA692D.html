Welcome to Pediatric Urology Associates (PUA)

We are a group of physicians who specialize in the care of children and adolescents with urologic and gynecologic problems. All of the surgeons in our group have been fellowship-trained at prestigious children’s hospitals in the United States. Pediatric Urology Associates, as a group, has over 100 years of cumulative clinical experience caring for complex urologic problems in children.
