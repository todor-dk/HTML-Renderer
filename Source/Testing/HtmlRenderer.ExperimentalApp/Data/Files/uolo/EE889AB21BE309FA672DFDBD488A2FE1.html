    UOL Group Limited (UOL) is aware that persons unknown have represented themselves to be officers or employees of the Group and expressed or offered investment opportunities in various industries and countries, and there are websites purporting to be the official website of UOL.

    The public is advised to take notice that these persons, representations and websites are unauthorised and bear no association with UOL, and that www.uol.com.sg is the only official website of the Group.

    UOL is closely monitoring the situation and advises the public to be vigilant and exercise all necessary caution and circumspection when approached by anyone purporting to be an officer, employee or representative of UOL or its affiliated companies. If you have been approached by such individuals, please seek verification and independent consultation before proceeding further.

    UOL and its subsidiaries and affiliates disclaim any and all responsibility for any form of loss or damages occasioned directly or indirectly by any form of reliance on the fraudulent representations.
