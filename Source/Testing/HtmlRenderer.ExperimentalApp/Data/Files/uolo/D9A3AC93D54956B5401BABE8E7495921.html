YOLO Watersports provides all the fun you’ll need on Captiva Island. Waverunner Excursions & Rentals, Parasailing, Flyboarding,  Scoot Coupes, Motor Scooters, Bicycles for the entire family, Beach Chairs & Umbrellas , Stand Up Paddleboard Sales & Rentals, Hobie Wave Sailboat, Surfboards, Skimboards, Bodyboards, Banana Boat Rides, GoPro Camera Rentals, Yeti Cooler Rentals & Beach Wagons.

Our Retail Store includes all the goods you’ll need for a day at the beach and more including, no-see-um repellent, shelling accessories, sunscreen, hats, water shoes & sandals, skateboards, sunglasses by Oakley, Costa del Mar, Maui Jim, Peppers & Ray Ban, Freestyle watches.  Bluetooth speakers by Scosche & Ecoxgear, Leatherman tools, Corn Hole, Kan Jam, Spikeball,   GoPro camera accessories & rentals,  West Indies Wear sarongs & tunics, kids clothes by Tank Stream Designs & YOLO Gear. Skimboards by Zap, Bodyboards, SUP’s by Bic & North 2 Boards.

Open every day from 9am – 5pm.
