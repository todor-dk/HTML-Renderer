TexasUrology.com is the educational website of Dr. Ken Goldberg and Dr. John Fairbanks. Dr. Ken Goldberg is a board-certified urologist formerly of the practice Texas Urology and now with UT Southwestern Medical Center. Dr. Goldberg practices out of Lewisville. Dr. John Fairbanks, formerly of Texas Urology, is also a board-certified urologist. He practices now with Urology Clinics of North Texas in Flower Mound and Carrollton. Both Dr. Goldberg and Dr. Fairbanks provide diagnosis, treatment and care information for urological disorders, such as erectile dysfunction, kidney stones and prostate cancer.
