      Twitter is a public message service that allows anyone to broadcast a short real-time text and/or picture update on what they're doing, thinking, or feeling. It's kind of like the "announcements" part of the Classifieds section of a newspaper: messages can only contain up to 140 letters, numbers, symbols, or spaces, so they're usually straight and to the point.
