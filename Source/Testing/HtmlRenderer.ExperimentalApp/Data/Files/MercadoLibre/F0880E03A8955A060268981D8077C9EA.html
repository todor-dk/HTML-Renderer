Selling on MercadoLibre Cross-Border Trade

MercadoLibre Cross-Border Trade (CBT) is your hub to MercadoLibre in Latin America. The program is currently open for buyers in Brazil and Mexico, and is soon to be expanded to all 14 MercadoLibre countries throughout Latin America. MercadoLibre CBT is your gateway to the thriving Latin American e-commerce market, which grew by 11% year over year and is the fastest growing internet market. MercadoLibre is the #1 e-commerce site in Latin America, where the Brazilian and Mexican marketplaces represent 60% of the total MercadoLibre GMV. The MercadoLibre CBT program supports merchants in four areas:

  1. International logistics: There are three ways you can ship your products to MercadoLibre buyers:
    1. You can ship your products to MercadoLibre's local warehouse(s) in the United States. They will take care of the international portion of shipment and deliver the item to buyers. They currently operate from two warehouses, one in Florida (for Brazil, Chile, and Colombia) and another in Texas (for Mexico). This option is only available for US sellers.
    2. You can ship your products directly to buyers using your shipment company of choice. This option is available to all sellers. Under this option, you can ship using a local postal service, such as USPS, Hong Kong Post, or Royal Mail or a courier service such as UPS or FEDEX. Local postal services do not require prepayment of taxes (DDU method), whereas couriers usually request a prepayment of taxes (DDP method).
    3. You can use DHL to ship items directly to buyers. MercadoLibre has preferred DHL rates, and you will be using their DHL account to ship items directly to buyers.
       
  2. Payments: MercadoLibre CBT, through its payment unit - MercadoPago - , can take any local payment method in Brazil and Mexico. As a result, US merchants will be able to sell products and receive payments locally without opening a local branch. Payments are settled twice a month in the merchant's US bank account.
     
  3. Localization of listings: MercadoLibre CBT makes sure the content and price of your listings are localized to each country participating in the CBT program. They offer services to translate titles and descriptions. Regarding listing prices, MercadoLibre CBT calculates all costs related to the transaction before the product is listed. Those costs are: duties & taxes, international shipping, and MercadoLibre fee. The ‘Total Landed Cost’, or price the buyers see has the following components: merchant price, US domestic shipping cost, duties & taxes, international shipping cost, MercadoLibre fee.
     
  4. Fees: You don't pay listing fees or sales commissions when listing and selling your products on MercadoLibre CBT.

Is selling on MercadoLibre Cross-Border Trade a Good Fit?

If you are considering selling on the MercadoLibre CBT marketplace please be aware of these important requirements:

  1. You need to be a US-based seller with a US banking account.
  2. You need to have a ChannelAdvisor US locale posting account.
  3. You need to be able to ship your products quickly to the MercadoLibre CBT warehouse in the US – MercadoLibre CBT fulfills all shipments to Latin America from their warehouse in the US. The package should arrive at the US warehouse within five days after purchase to avoid long shipping times to the buyer.
  4. You need to be able to print provided DHL label for orders that you will use MercadoLibre’s DHL account for shipping direct to buyer – This is easy with ChannelAdvisor as we will provide the link to download the label with in the order details.
  5. Be ready to answer buyers' questions – MercadoLibre CBT provides a tool to reply to listing and order questions in English, Spanish, or Portuguese.

    How a customer request is answered at MercadoLibre CBT

  6. Offer competitive product prices – You will send your US product prices and domestic shipping cost to MercadoLibre CBT. MercadoLibre CBT then adds additional costs like listing fees, customs, importation taxes and international shipping to the price and lists the total (merchant price, US domestic shipping cost, duties & taxes, international shipping cost, MercadoLibre fee) as the price buyers have to pay on MercadoLibre marketplaces.

How Does ChannelAdvisor Work with MercadoLibre Cross-Border Trade?

  • Upload Your Inventory to ChannelAdvisor – Regardless of where you maintain your inventory, you will need to keep a record of your products within the ChannelAdvisor application. ChannelAdvisor offers a variety of options based on what works best for your company. To learn more about getting your Inventory into ChannelAdvisor please refer to the Inventory Getting Started Guide .
     
  • Map Your Inventory to MercadoLibre CBT’s Format – MercadoLibre CBT, like other marketplaces, has a particular format they require sellers to conform to. ChannelAdvisor has made the process of mapping your inventory information to MercadoLibre CBT's product fields simple by providing a template to communicate your inventory data in the preferred format to MercadoLibre CBT.
     
  • ChannelAdvisor Sends Your Listings to MercadoLibre CBT – Once you have completed your inventory mapping you are ready to list products on MercadoLibre CBT. You will activate your integration between ChannelAdvisor and MercadoLibre CBT and we will begin sending files to MercadoLibre CBT with your product details and quantity. If you have opted to use their translation services, MercadoLibre CBT will translate the product content. They will also calculate the final buyer price in the local currency. Then your items will be listed on the MercadoLibre marketplaces.
     

    How item and order data is transferred between ChannelAdvisor and MercadoLibre CBT
     
  • Customers Purchase Your Products on MercadoLibre marketplaces – Now that your products are showing on the marketplaces, you should start to see sales almost immediately. Buyers will pay directly to the MercadoLibre Marketplace’s official payment gateway (MercadoPago). These funds are routed to MercadoLibre CBT and orders will be imported to MercadoLibre CBT for logistics warehouse assignments. MercadoLibre CBT will disburse payments to sellers every 15 days. For more information regarding payments, see MercadoLibre CBT Program Description .
     
  • Orders Are Imported into ChannelAdvisor – ChannelAdvisor will retrieve each order and acknowledge it on your behalf. Your orders will then be available in the ChannelAdvisor software to fulfill. You will pick and pack orders and send them to MercadoLibre CBT's warehouse in the US or direct to buyer, if using DHL.
     
  • ChannelAdvisor Sends Shipping Confirmations to MercadoLibre CBT – After you have fulfilled an order, ChannelAdvisor notifies MercadoLibre CBT that the package is on its way to the warehouse. Once MercadoLibre CBT ships the package, they will notify the marketplaces that the order has been shipped.
     

    The shipping process in the MercadoLibre CBT integration


How Do I Get Started?

Getting started with MercadoLibre CBT is easy and ChannelAdvisor can help you with this process. Begin by following these steps:

  1. Review the MercadoLibre Cross-Border Trade Program Description .
  2. Contact MercadoLibre CBT (cbt-support@mercadolibre.com) to discuss any product pricing, product listing limits, content translation and logistics strategy and concerns.
  3. Send a sample file of 1000 SKUs to cbt-support@mercadolibre.com for review. Your product data has to include price, title, description, category info, and product weight. Be sure to include a product in each of the MercadoLibre CBT categories you wish to list in. You can create that file via an inventory export .
  4. Register  for an account with MercadoLibre CBT, accept their terms & conditions, upload your logo, company FAQs , and customer service contact information to setup your MercadoLibre listing template.
  5. Contact your ChannelAdvisor Sales Representative or your Account Manager to request access to the MercadoLibre CBT integration.
  6. Follow the steps in the ChannelAdvisor Getting Started Guide .

  3. You need to be able to ship your products quickly to the MercadoLibre CBT warehouse in the US – MercadoLibre CBT fulfills all shipments to Latin America from their warehouse in the US. The package should arrive at the US warehouse within five days after purchase to avoid long shipping times to the buyer.
  4. You need to be able to print provided DHL label for orders that you will use MercadoLibre’s DHL account for shipping direct to buyer – This is easy with ChannelAdvisor as we will provide the link to download the label with in the order details.
  5. Be ready to answer buyers' questions – MercadoLibre CBT provides a tool to reply to listing and order questions in English, Spanish, or Portuguese.

How Do I Get Started?

Getting started with MercadoLibre CBT is easy and ChannelAdvisor can help you with this process. Begin by following these steps:

  1. Review the MercadoLibre Cross-Border Trade Program Description .
  2. Contact MercadoLibre CBT (cbt-support@mercadolibre.com) to discuss any product pricing, product listing limits, content translation and logistics strategy and concerns.
  3. Send a sample file of 1000 SKUs to cbt-support@mercadolibre.com for review. Your product data has to include price, title, description, category info, and product weight. Be sure to include a product in each of the MercadoLibre CBT categories you wish to list in. You can create that file via an inventory export .
  4. Register  for an account with MercadoLibre CBT, accept their terms & conditions, upload your logo, company FAQs , and customer service contact information to setup your MercadoLibre listing template.
  5. Contact your ChannelAdvisor Sales Representative or your Account Manager to request access to the MercadoLibre CBT integration.
