Selling on MercadoLibre Cross-Border Trade

MercadoLibre Cross-Border Trade (CBT) is your hub to MercadoLibre in Latin America. The program is currently open for buyers in Brazil and Mexico, and is soon to be expanded to all 14 MercadoLibre countries throughout Latin America. MercadoLibre CBT is your gateway to the thriving Latin American e-commerce market, which grew by 11% year over year and is the fastest growing internet market. MercadoLibre is the #1 e-commerce site in Latin America, where the Brazilian and Mexican marketplaces represent 60% of the total MercadoLibre GMV. The MercadoLibre CBT program supports merchants in four areas:

  1. International logistics: There are three ways you can ship your products to MercadoLibre buyers:
    1. You can ship your products to MercadoLibre's local warehouse(s) in the United States. They will take care of the international portion of shipment and deliver the item to buyers. They currently operate from two warehouses, one in Florida (for Brazil, Chile, and Colombia) and another in Texas (for Mexico). This option is only available for US sellers.
    2. You can ship your products directly to buyers using your shipment company of choice. This option is available to all sellers. Under this option, you can ship using a local postal service, such as USPS, Hong Kong Post, or Royal Mail or a courier service such as UPS or FEDEX. Local postal services do not require prepayment of taxes (DDU method), whereas couriers usually request a prepayment of taxes (DDP method).
    3. You can use DHL to ship items directly to buyers. MercadoLibre has preferred DHL rates, and you will be using their DHL account to ship items directly to buyers.
       
  2. Payments: MercadoLibre CBT, through its payment unit - MercadoPago - , can take any local payment method in Brazil and Mexico. As a result, US merchants will be able to sell products and receive payments locally without opening a local branch. Payments are settled twice a month in the merchant's US bank account.
     
  3. Localization of listings: MercadoLibre CBT makes sure the content and price of your listings are localized to each country participating in the CBT program. They offer services to translate titles and descriptions. Regarding listing prices, MercadoLibre CBT calculates all costs related to the transaction before the product is listed. Those costs are: duties & taxes, international shipping, and MercadoLibre fee. The â€˜Total Landed Costâ€™, or price the buyers see has the following components: merchant price, US domestic shipping cost, duties & taxes, international shipping cost, MercadoLibre fee.
     
  4. Fees: You don't pay listing fees or sales commissions when listing and selling your products on MercadoLibre CBT.
