MercadoLibre Argentina’s Offices

Firm
  • Contract Workplaces
  • Client MercadoLibre,
  • size 44,000 sqft
  • Year 2011
  • Location Arias, Argentina,
  • Industry Technology,

    • One of the things I’ve been noticing recently is a move by companies with open offices to provide spaces that are adequate for privacy. Which is a weird phenomenon as the last 20 years have been spend moving away from private spaces to completely open offices.

    MercadoLibre’s office is a great example of this. They have their open areas for normal desk work, but have smaller spaces that are nearby for a quick getaway.

    Their new Argentina offices were recently completed by Contract Argentina.
