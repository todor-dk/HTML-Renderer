SUmail

What is SUmail?

Official Syracuse University email accounts for students are established in a system called SUmail.

SUmail is managed by the University and utilizes Microsoft's cloud services. This partnership provides students several powerful and convenient features, including:

  • 25 GB inbox and 25 MB attachments
  • Optimized Outlook experience including customizable user interface
  • Shared calendar and contacts
  • Message tracking
  • Folders to organize email
  • Forwarding, IMAP, and Outlook Connector for mobile phone and personal computer access
  • Spam and virus protection
  • Keep your email address after you graduate

Log into SUmail

Go to http://SUmail.syr.edu

Note: SUmail utilizes Microsoft's cloud services. When asked for your "organizational account" or user name, use your official SU email address (NetID@syr.edu). Use the same password you set up for your SU NetID.


Compatible browsers

To access SUmail, use the following browsers:

  • Windows - Internet Explorer, Chrome or Firefox
  • Macintosh - Safari, Chrome or Firefox
  • Most smartphones, tablets and mobile devices can also be configured for SUmail


SUmail Setup

  • Smartphones
  • Apple


Using other email programs with SUmail

Many people manage their SUmail incoming and outgoing email directly through the SUmail Web interface (http://sumail.syr.edu).

If you use a third-party email package to access your SUmail account, please review this important information: Using SUmail with other Email Packages (i.e. Outlook, Apple Mail, etc.)


University Email Policy

The SU email policy requires that all official University email communications be sent to your @syr.edu email address. If you choose to re-direct your @syr.edu email to another account, such as @gmail.com or @yahoo.com, you do so at your own risk.
