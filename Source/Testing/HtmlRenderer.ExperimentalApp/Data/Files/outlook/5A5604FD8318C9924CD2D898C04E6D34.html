EMSI data

EMSI provides the most complete, current, and accurate employment data in the United States, which is why publications like The Wall Street Journal, Forbes Magazine, and The New York Times regularly cite EMSI data.

EMSI goes beyond state and federal data sources by mathematically restoring suppressed values, and by providing zipcode level data across the entire nation. EMSI data incorporates nearly 90 different data sources and is updated 4 times per year.
