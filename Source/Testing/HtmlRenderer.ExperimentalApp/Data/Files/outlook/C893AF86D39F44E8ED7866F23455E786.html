Office 365 Training Available
Free Outlook 2013 and Office 365 Email, Calendar and OneDrive courses are offered through Learning TECHniques. All courses are hands-on and only two hours long. To register, visit LearningTECHniques.uwm.edu

Got Office 365 Questions?
Check out the UWM KnowledgeBase where you will find answers to commonly-asked questions, and more. If you cannot find what you need, please call the UWM Help Desk at 414-229-4040 (toll-free 877-381-3459) or submit an online help request at GetTechHelp.uwm.edu.
