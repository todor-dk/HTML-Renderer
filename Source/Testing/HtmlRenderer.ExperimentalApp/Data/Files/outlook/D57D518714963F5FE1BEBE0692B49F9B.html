Outlook is built into your Windows 10 phone

It's easy to add any email address to the integrated Outlook app on Windows 10 phones.

  1. Open Outlook Mail.
  2. Tap the '...', then Settings.
  3. Tap Manage Accounts.
  4. + Add Account.
