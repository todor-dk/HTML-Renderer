FOOD AND DRINKS. FOR EVERYONE.

Right at Home with Innovation.

Awash in innovation, like the neighborhood they serve, The Envoy Hotel restos are buzzy and brilliantone up, one down, fittingly christened Lookout and Outlook respectively. Lookout Rooftop Bar outshines its entire classoverlooking the city, but not the defining details. In both, food is a forethought, handcrafted and highly creative, and whisked from kitchens with a recipe to delight. Drinks echo, with small-batch distillers, rock-star brewers and single vineyard labels all making the list. To accompany, theres a design flair in tow, a lively mix of bold spaces and clubby nooks where teams can pre-flight their presentation or revel in its success, and friends can socialize as twos or tens, agreeably. All of it down the street from the office, native to celebrations, short-listed by locals, and simply delicious.

OUTLOOK KITCHEN AND BAR

American at its roots, inspiration for cuisine will be drawn from the unique Boston landscape but tempered by seasonal variations, locally sourced food, all resulting in a one of a kind approach to our food and drink.

The bar highlights handmade craft cocktails and micro brews including a special beer brewed specifically for OUTLOOK Kitchen and Bar, small batch bourbons, single malt scotches, an interesting, carefully selected wine program, fresh juices and mixers and house made sodas.

LOOKOUT ROOFTOP AND BAR

The Neighborhoods De Facto Hotspot.

Building on top of the new POV inside our inspired hotel, we created the Lookout Rooftop and Bar, a celebration of visionary thinking mixed with maverick mojo. Up, and out, a pinnacle design unfurls against killer skyline and harbor views. Bold character oozes over the expanse, filled with cozy couches and community spaces, a railing bar and jaunty ell, modern intrigue and signature surprise. Elemental to the concept, a desire to feed the sensesdrinks masterfully rendered by virtuoso mixologists, tracks laid down on tone with the vibe, and everywhere, a vantage point on whats next.
