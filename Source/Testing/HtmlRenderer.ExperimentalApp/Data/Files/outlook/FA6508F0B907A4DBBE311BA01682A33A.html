Sabbaticals for pastors are now widely accepted, a situation that wasn’t the case even a couple of decades ago. The opening verse records that Amos prophesied during the reigns of Uzziah (783-742 B.C. in Judah) and Jeroboam II (786-746 B.C. in Israel). Although this was a relatively peaceful period for both the northern and southern kingdoms, the prophecies of Amos indicate that there were ominous signs that all was not well. Amos uncompromisingly denounced social injustice and religious hypocrisy and arrogance. He warned that God would surely punish the guilty.
