What is Quick YouTube To MP3?

Quick YouTube To MP3 (QYT2MP3) is a Windows based application written in AutoIT that allows you to download and convert any YouTube video to MP3. It's fast, it's easy, and most of all, it's free. QYT2MP3 is released under GPL version 3.

 

The latest version is 3.0. You can grab yourself a copy from the Downloads section.
