Minecraft Tower Defense is a tower defense game based on Minecraft. Begin the Minecraft game by digging a path to your house. After you have created a path, you have to construct turrets along the path to kill invading mobs before they get to you. The building and upgrading of turrets requires resources, that are obtained by killing enemies/invaders. You may also make use of your resources to position traps and barriers on the path of your invaders/enemies. You will lose a heart every time an invader/enemy gets to you, and the game comes to an end once you lose all your hearts.
