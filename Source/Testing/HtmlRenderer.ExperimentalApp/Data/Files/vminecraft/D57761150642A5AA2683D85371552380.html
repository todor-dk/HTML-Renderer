What is TheMineList?

TheMineList is a Minecraft multiplayer servers top list. A directory of Minecraft servers ranked by amount of incoming votes and by their popularity.

Our website helps you find the most popular minecraft servers of any type; Survival, PVP, Survival, Hungergames, Minigames, Cracked, Tekkit, FTB and more!
