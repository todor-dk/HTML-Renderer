This blog is a showcase of the growth and development of the DemoCraft server. We will showcase our works regularly and you will be impressed because we are awesome and you're only a little bit awesome. =3

DemoCraft is a Survival Minecraft server (with mild RPG elements). We use the Factions plugin to allow people to play as a group, on their own or however they like. Faction creation and joining is strongly encouraged! Seeing as we are a new server, we are currently looking for skilled builders/players to expand our community.

To be part of DemoCraft, submit an application through the submission box and we'll reply to it as soon as possible. Alternatively, email your applications to democraftserver@gmail.com. Include your in-game name, a few words about yourself and why you want to be part of the server and, if possible, attach a photo of something you've built (email photo's in .jpg only).

You can now apply in our minecraftforums thread!
