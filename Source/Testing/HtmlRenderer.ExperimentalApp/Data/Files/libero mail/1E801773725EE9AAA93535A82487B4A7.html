Libero Mail Login | Sign in to Libero Mail

May 2, 2016May 4, 2016 Sign in Email Libero

Libero Sign In is extremely easy. All you need to do, is visit registrazione.libero.it and if you already do not have aid, you will be prompted to create one. On clicking the ‘sign in’ tab, a form will come up asking you for some basic details, such as your name and date of birth. You will be required to create a username based on your choices and availability. That being done, you will need to set up a password, fill in your gender, mobile number and your alternate email-id, along with the name of your country. Once you agree to the terms of service, you are all set to use the services offered by Libero.

How to Login to Libero Mail

  • Sign in to Libero Account by visiting www.libero.it, simply by putting in your username and password. Once you do that simply click on the ‘sign in’ tab and you will be taken to your mail inbox.
  • If either of your username or password is wrong, you may be required to click on the ‘forgot password’.
  • However, before you do that, you can try typing in your password somewhere else, like on the notepad or MS Word, and then copy and paste it in the space provided for password. This will help you in avoiding any mistake while typing the password, since you would be able to see the characters clearly.
  • Also, since Libero passwords are case sensitive it is important to check if your caps-lock key is on/off.  Then you can try Libero Login once again.
  • In case, even that doesn’t work, then you might want to click on the ‘forgot password’ tab and recover your password through a series of verification steps as directed by Libero.
