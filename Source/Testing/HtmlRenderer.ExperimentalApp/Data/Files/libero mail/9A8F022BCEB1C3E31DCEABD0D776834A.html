User friendly download page

File transfer with a user friendly download page

As recipients receiving files via JumboMail, we often have a large number of files sent to us at once. You may want to download just some of the files, but downloading all the files at once might take a while. On the other hand, downloading each file separately can be a pain and will definitely take a time.

We've made the JumboMail download page user friendly, choose the files you want to download and with just one click you can download them all at once. You can also share those files with others without having to share them all.

It's never been easier to download received files!
