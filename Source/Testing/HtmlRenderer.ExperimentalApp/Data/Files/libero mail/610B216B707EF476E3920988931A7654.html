This is a collaborative web page through which users may share SMTP servers. A lot of users have trouble setting the right SMTP server and they ask in the forum for help. Contributing to this page will help FreePOPs developers to spend more time coding rather than supporting users. Since users can modify the content of this page there is no warranty over its contents.

Reading instructions

Your network provider (that may be confused with a mail provider, since sometimes they are the same, but they arent' always) may offer to its users an SMTP server to relay their mail. Search in the first column for your network provider and read its SMTP server name in the second one.
