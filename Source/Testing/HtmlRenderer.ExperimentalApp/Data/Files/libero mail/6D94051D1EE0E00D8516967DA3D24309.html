We have created an e-mail address on a Free and now we would like to know how to configure Free Mail to access it from our favorite devices?

We are not always available to the PC and find the web client too awkward to see from our smartphones? Then this is the right guidance!

Below we will teach you how to configure the mail client of PC, Android, iPhone and Windows Phone using the manual setting of the parameters; not always, indeed, these clients are able to correctly configure the account.

The premise: at a certain point we will have to choose whether to use the POP3 protocol or the IMAP protocol. Although both allow you to send and receive email, the difference is in the mode of message management. Without going into the details, we can say that:

Given the habits of today, we strongly recommend that you configure Free Mail using the IMAP protocol. The choice is still yours.

Having said that, let's see how to do it!

Configure Free Mail on the PC is simple: just have an e-mail client and the correct parameters! The procedure is compatible with virtually all e-mail clients, such as “Mail” to Windows 10, Outlook, Mozilla Thunderbird, Opera Mail, Airmail, and many others.

To configure Free Mail access the window for adding new accounts to our client and configuriamolo as follows:

POP3 PROTOCOL

PROTOCOL (IMAP SSL, recommended)

The IMAP PROTOCOL (without SSL, not recommended)

The easiest way to configure Free Mail on Android is to download the dedicated app from the Play Store, start it, select the Free Mail on the selection screen of the account and, when prompted, enter the username and password.

DOWNLOAD | Free Email (Google Play Store)

Alternatively, we can use any other mail client for Android, such as Outlook, the app Mail system, or even Gmail, we can do it safely.

Simply start the procedure of adding the account of our favorite mail client. Some mail app that can import the parameters without the need to enter them manually. If this were not the case, we can use the same parameters seen for the PC client, both POP and IMAP (recommended), taking care to choose the manual setting of the parameters at the time of the addition of the new account.

The easiest way to configure Libero Mail on iPhone and iOS is to download the dedicated app from iTunes, launch it, select the Free Mail on the selection screen of the account and, when prompted, enter the username and password.

DOWNLOAD | Free Mail (iTunes)

Alternatively, we can use any other mail client for the iPhone and ipad, the default client Mail. Simply start the procedure of adding the account of our favorite mail client; if we use Mail, scolleghiamoci before from the Internet – otherwise the app will try to single parameters, and not being able to detect them properly, will not allow us to go forward.

We can use the same parameters seen for the PC client, both POP and IMAP (recommended).

The easiest way to configure Free Mail on Windows Phone or Windows 10 Mobile is to download the dedicated app from the Windows Store, start it, select the Free Mail on the selection screen of the account and, when prompted, enter the username and password.

DOWNLOAD | Free Mail (Windows Store)

Alternatively, we can use any other mail client, even the default Windows Phone (Email), or Windows 10 Mobile (Mail). Simply start the procedure of adding the account, taking care to choose the manual setting of the parameters, and we will be able to use the same parameters seen for the PC client, both POP and IMAP (recommended).
