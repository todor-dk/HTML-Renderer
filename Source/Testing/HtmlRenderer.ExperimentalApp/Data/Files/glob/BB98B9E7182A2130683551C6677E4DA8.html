Please be advised that the Globe Institute of Technology has closed and permanent student records have been transferred to the New York State Education Department.

Former students looking to obtain duplicate credentials, transcripts, or help with other questions should contact the New York State Education Department, Office of College and University Evaluation at OCUEInfo@nysed.gov or (518) 474-2593.

Additional information may also be available on the New York State Education Department’s website at http://www.highered.nysed.gov/ocue/globe.htm

 

At Globe Institute of Technology, you will gain the skills, knowledge, and experience needed to excel in a rapidly changing world. Our innovative and specialized degrees offer both field-specific knowledge and real-world experiences that will prepare you to enter the workforce and achieve your professional aspirations.

Located only blocks away from Times Square and the Empire State Building, Globe invites you to experience the rich cultural diversity of midtown Manhattan. The city is your campus, providing access to galleries, theaters, museums, parks, shops, and restaurants.

During your studies at Globe, you will work with a community of passionate faculty and peers. We value the intimate exchange of ideas that occurs within our small sized classes, and we place a tremendous emphasis on preparing you for your future endeavors.

If you appreciate the friendliness and warmth of a small college environment located in the heart of one of the world’s greatest cities, Globe is the college for you. We encourage you to explore all that we have to offer.
