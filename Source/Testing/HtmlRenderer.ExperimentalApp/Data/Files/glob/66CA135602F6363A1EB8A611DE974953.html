Globo Industrial (H.K.) Ltd.

Globo Ind.(H.K.) Ltd. is a manufacturer carrying over 8 years of experiences in producing full color printing PU products for toys and gift & premium markets. We have built up a high reputation among local and international companies

Our factory is located in Wujiang City, Jiangsu, it was awarded the ISO 9001, ICTI certificate and also is a Disney authorized manufacturer. We care about the safety of toy, all of our products are comply with toy safety standard of EN71 and ASTM.

Having functioned as many famous brands manufacturing licensee for past several years, we are fully aware of the complexities and safety measures on Disney products and many other famous brands. Not just having substantial experience in producing top-of-the-line products and with the breakthrough printing technology applying to our products such as PU foam ball, bouncing ball, Frisbee, boomerang, baseball bat, bowling set etc.

We regularly develop new products to keep up with market trends and to satisfy our customers’ needs. OEM, ODM and customized products orders are always welcomed.
