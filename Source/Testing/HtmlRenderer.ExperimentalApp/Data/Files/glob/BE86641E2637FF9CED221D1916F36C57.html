GLOBO is an international leader and technology innovator delivering multi-platform enterprise mobility management and telecom software solutions. Founded in 1997, GLOBO has offices in Palo Alto, New York, Athens and London, and offers a wide range of solutions to manage and secure mobile devices in a business environments across a variety of industries including government, financial services, retail, education and more.

Since 2007, GLOBO has been listed on the London Stock Exchangeâs Aim Market, (LSE:AIM: GBO) and operates in over 45 countries internationally. Their suite of GO!Enterprise software is a complete Enterprise Mobility Management (EMM) platform that combines mobile device management (MDM), mobile content management (MCM), mobile application management (MAM), mobile application development platforms (MADP) and more, and currently serves more than 6 million users on a monthly basis.
