Dairy

Glob Marketing works in the Dairy sector with partners to co-pack and co-produce through facilities in Europe, Middle East. That is in parallel to using Glob Marketing’s own excising standard packing and brands such as Milka, Barla, and Bita. We are constantly looking for new possibilities and it is a challenge for us to develop new and tailor-made ways of packaging and applications which are based on customer requirements.

Our experience in exporting to international markets dates back to 1995. Over the years, our company’s hard work and worldwide marketing network has generated impressive results for our customers.

Meat and Poultry

For clients who are looking for US Products with private brands and labels, we can accomplish this by providing the packing material in advance and then pack in full equipped freezer facilities in USA. The advantage of such service is the flexibility in our supply, and we can source the products where it is more competitive to meet our clients’ specification.

We supply our products to the following customer groups located in each territory. Products are ideal for food service, ice cream, yogurt, Bakery and confectionery manufactures, catering companies or for reconstituting milk.

  • All government and semi- government institutions.
  • All private importers
  • Bakery, Biscuit, Snack, chocolate, confectionary, ice cream, yogurt, cheese, evaporated and sweetened condense milk factories
  • Packing factories

From the very beginning, Glob Marketing has been committed to provide its worldwide clients with premium quality products. We have been successful in establishing long-term business and personal relationships with companies and individuals that share and practice this mutual belief. These relationships have allowed us to become a reputable supplier in each territory and our export division is growing year after year.

In an effort to continue to grow, we are seeking new markets around the globe by taking advantage of the knowledge, experience and unique line of our products.

Glob Marketing’s Headquarter is located in Potters Bar, North of London with branch and associate offices in the Middle East and Africa. These offices serve the purpose of assisting our customers at the location providing to them the best possible service and filling the physical distance gap between us and our customers worldwide.
