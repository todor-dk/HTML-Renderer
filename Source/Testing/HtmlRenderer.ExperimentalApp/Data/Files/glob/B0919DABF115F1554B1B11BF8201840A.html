Grupo Globo’s areas of action

Grupo Globo has a relevant position in all the segments it acts in.

TV Globo’s programming is broadcast in almost all of the national territory through 5 own networks, partnership with affiliates. In addition, they reach over 100 countries through Globo Internacional. Recognised by its high quality standard, a trait it has employed ever since being founded in 1965, TV Globo's trajectory intertwines with the history of television in Brazil, always guided by pioneering and innovative nature.

Globo Filmes engages in the co-production of Brazilian films, releasing feature lengths that become box office hits in Brazil.

Globosat is a subscription television programmer. It has a diversified portfolio of channels, including over 30 subscription channels supplying 24 hours programming. SporTV, GloboNews, Multishow, GNT, VIVA and Gloob are the channels that stand out.

The group also acts in the segments of printed and digital newspapers and magazines through Infoglobo and Editora Globo. Infoglobo brings together daily newspapers O Globo, founded in 1925, Extra and Expresso, as well as having a share in the Valor Econômico paper. Editora Globo has 16 magazines in its portfolio, as well as editing classic and contemporary books.

Som Livre acts in the music sector, producing and selling content from Brazilian artists using traditional media (CDs, DVDs and others), as well as digital media and events.

In relation to radio, the group acts through the Sistema Globo de Rádio, with its own stations and affiliates, in the news segment (CBN) and talk shows (Rádio Globo).

Zap is an online classified portal with national reach.

All of the group’s companies develop actions in the digital environment and are in charge of expanding their brands and products, interactivity and more interaction with its audience. Globo.com provides internet related technological services and platforms for the Group’s companies.
