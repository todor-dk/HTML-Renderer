Welcome to The GLOBE Academy !

The GLOBE Academy is a dual-language immersion charter school in the DeKalb County School District of Metro Atlanta, Georgia. We currently serve students in K-6th Grade, adding a grade each year to become a K-8 school. We invite you to learn more about how we provide Global Learning Opportunities through Balanced Education!
