About Globopak

Globopak LLC, is one of the leading providers of RECYCLABLE Corrugated Plastic Packaging throughout United States, Mexico and Canada. Our main platform is in Agricultural Packaging. We serve many of the best and well known global brands of fruit and vegetable growers and distributors. The presentation and durability of Corrugated Plastic makes it the ideal packaging & marketing solution to transport your goods from the field to the consumer. With over 20 years of packaging experience, we are highly knowledgeable and experienced in other industries including Electronics, Pharmaceutical, Automotive, and Industrial applications where Corrugated Plastic has proven to be the best packaging solution. We look forward to providing you with superior service and outstanding quality for all your packaging needs.
