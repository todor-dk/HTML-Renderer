GLOBO Jobs with Part-Time, Telecommuting, or Flexible Work

Based in Fort Washington, Pennsylvania, GLOBO is a full-services cultural services and language company that provides on-demand translators and interpreters for nearly any language or culture. GLOBO's services include project-based translation, face-to-face and telephone/video interpreters, consulting and language learning, translation and localization, and global and social media. Telephone interpreting, provided through GLOBO Voice, is offered 24 hours per day, 7 days a week, in 230 languages. In just 8 seconds, companies can be connected to a Spanish interpreter, or to an interpreter in any other language in just 20 seconds. GLOBO works with companies in a variety of industries, including hospitality, government, contact centers, healthcare, legal, insurance, and financial services. As an employer, GLOBO has hired for flexible jobs in the past, such as freelance remote positions with flexible schedules.
