Montana Broadband Speed Test

Using the Speed Test

The Montana Speed Test is now concluded, but you can still measure your speed at speedtest.net. Click the "begin test" button and the website will determine and display your Internet connection speed.

Gathering speed data was one of the first steps in identifying and actually measuring the need for new broadband services in Montana. The Montana Broadband Program encouraged Montana citizens to help in its data collections efforts by participating in its on—line Speed Test. This information assisted the Montana Broadband Program — geared to increased economic development, educational opportunities, public safety and national security.

Measure your connection speed at http://speedtest.net
