Test your school in four easy steps:

  1. Enter the ZIP code or city & state your school is in and press Find Schools
  2. Select your school by typing the first few letters of your school name. If you don't see your school in the list, check to be sure you entered the correct location information or press New School to enter the name of your school.
  3. Confirm that you are at school and connected to your school's network so we know you are testing the speed of your school's Internet access.
  4. Select your role, enter your e-mail address (optional) and press Start Test.

In less than a minute you'll know the speed of your school's Internet access!
