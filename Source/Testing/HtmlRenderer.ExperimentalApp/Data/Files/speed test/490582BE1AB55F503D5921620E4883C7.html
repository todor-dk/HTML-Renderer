Greenlight Speed Test

A couple helpful hints before you get started...

  • Greenlight's Speed Test will be most accurate when your computer is connected directly with an Ethernet cable. If possible, please bypass your router before running the test.
  • Disconnect from any VPN and WiFi connections
  • The results of your speed test may vary based on network or Internet utilization, your laptop or computer, your browser, and the wiring inside your location, among other factors.
When you click the button below, you'll be taken to speedtest.net to run a speed test.

There are a number of items that will appear on your screen, but the one you want to click on is the button that says "BEGIN TEST". We've circled it in red in this screenshot.
