How do we calculate our speed test results?

We're confident that our speed test tool is the most accurate around, here's why.

  • The results we show you are your mean average speed. Many online speed tests show you the fastest speed you've achieved during the test – we think the average is more accurate and more indicative of the speeds you're likely to receive.
  • We test against well connected and uncongested speed test servers run on a network managed by thinkbroadband.com - some other testers pick the server which would give you the fastest speed at that time, not us.
