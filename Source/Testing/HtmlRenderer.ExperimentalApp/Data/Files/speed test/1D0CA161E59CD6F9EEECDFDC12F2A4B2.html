Speed Test

Recommended Speed Test

Please go to cruzio.speedtest.net to perform speed tests.

Choose the “Begin Test – Your Preferred Server” button to start the speed test. After the test completes, you can use the “Share This Result” button to save a link to the results of each test, which may be helpful for troubleshooting.

Network speed test results tend to vary. To get a more accurate gauge of your network speed, Cruzio recommends you repeat the test three times and average the results.

Do you need to talk to Cruzio about your network speed? Write down your three test results. Then, contact Cruzio Technical Support.
