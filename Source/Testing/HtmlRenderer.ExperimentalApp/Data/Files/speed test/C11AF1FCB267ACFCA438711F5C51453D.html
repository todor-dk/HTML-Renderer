8 Important Tips when Choosing an Internet Provider

Promotional pricing or the lowest-cost plans often include conditions or restrictions that you should consider before selecting an Internet service. Here are eight important areas to review when you evaluate a new plan:

  • INTRODUCTORY PRICING: Will my initial pricing increase when the term ends?
  • EQUIPMENT: Does my Internet service include a WiFi router to connect all of my devices? Are there equipment fees?
  • INSTALLATION: How is the product installed? Will I need to be home? Is there an installation fee?
  • CUSTOMER SUPPORT: Is Customer Support available anytime?
  • TECHNOLOGY: What technology does the Internet connection use? DSL? Cable? Fiber? Will it slow down during the day?
  • ADDITIONAL BENEFITS: Does my plan include nationwide WiFi hotspots? Is security included?
  • BUNDLE TO SAVE: Can I bundle my Internet service with a TV, phone, or digital home security plan to save money?
