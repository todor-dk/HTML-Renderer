Internet – Speed Test

Understanding Speed Tests

Because so many factors can cause speed tests to provide inaccurate network information and internet speed reports, Eastex has created our own speed test tool, to help you to determine connection speeds between your DSL modem and our equipment.

When you perform the test, make note of the speed result and perform the test at least three times to obtain an average. If the results are not within an acceptable margin (typically 80% of the advertised rate for your broadband package), then you will need to provide all three of the results to Eastex when contacting us.
