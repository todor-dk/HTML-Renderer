Why does testing my speed matter?

With our lives moving more and more online, periodically checking your Internet speed gets more and more important. Not only will it help you troubleshoot connection issues, but it can save you money by verifying that the speed you pay for is the speed you're receiving.

If you're not getting the speeds you pay for, or if you realize you need higher speed, you may want to explore your Internet options.
