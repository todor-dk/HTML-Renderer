Speed Test

This test attempts to give an estimate of your current connection to First Step. If you are a connectivity customer of ours, this speedtest will be different from a speedtest performed at a different location on the Internet, because you are connected directly via phone line, DSL, or wireless to our servers - you do not go through the Internet to reach our server. If you are not a connectivity customer, this is a benchmark test of connection speed to our servers. Your geographic location, the number of "hops", Internet traffic, and many other factors can and will affect the results of this test.

If you have additional questions, or require assistance interpreting the results of this test, please send e-mail to techsupport@fsr.com or chat with a technical support representative.
Speed Test requires at least version 9 of Flash. Please update your client.
