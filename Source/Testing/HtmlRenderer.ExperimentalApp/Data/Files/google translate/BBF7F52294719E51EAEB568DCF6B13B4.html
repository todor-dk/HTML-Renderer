Google Translate

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Google Translate is a free translation service that provides instant translations between 103 different languages.[1] It can translate words, sentences, and web pages between any combination of our supported languages.[2] It was launched on April 28, 2006.[3]
