Google Translate™ Exceptions

There are specific areas within the EDD website that do not provide the Google Translate™ translation service. Those areas include the Labor Market Information section, EDD online applications, and the Spanish pages that have been translated by the official translator of the EDD.

EDD Online Applications

Currently, there are web-based applications within the EDD website that do not provide the Google Translate™ automated tool. The EDD has excluded those pages and/or areas to maintain the security of confidential information.

The following areas do not include Google Translate™:

  • Ask EDD
  • CalJOBSSM
  • eApply4UI
  • e-Services for Business
  • Employment Training Provider List
  • eWOTC
  • Online Forms and Publications
  • Payroll Tax Seminars
  • SDI Online
  • UI OnlineSM

Spanish Pages

Some pages on the EDD website have been translated in Spanish by the official translator of the Department. These pages do not include the Google Translate™ automated tool and are considered the official Spanish pages. For a complete listing of the EDD’s official Spanish pages, visit Las Páginas Oficiales del Departamento del Desarrollo del Empleo en Español or for more information about some of EDD’s programs and other resources, visit Información General en Español.
