I did not know about the Phrasebook and would not have thought about browser history. Good to know!

There's at least one more way. I am a professional translator who uses s Translation Memory (TM) program. That program contains an option to send phrases and sentences to Google Translate. That service, unlike the webpage, is not free. Google charges a nominal amount to prevent users from using it for mass translations, millions of words for example. It is very cheap, but I had to set up an account to pay Google for it. This uses the Google Translate API. I get a bill every few months, for a few dollars each time. The bill also contains the character count, not the word count.

But the amount of the bill is so low I have never looked at the character count.
