Customer Reviews

!! Excellent Quality of Translation English/French/English !!
     
by FlamencoPuro

Although I speak French reasonably well, it's over 50 years since I passed my French GCE, so of course have forgotten loads of words, phrases, and meanings. Also of course, my written French is not up to the standard of the spoken word. So I particularly like this App because the translations (both ways) seem excellent. Also, you have the facility of being able to HEAR, and in good quality, the translated text - a real bonus, in my view. Also, translations of, for instance, whole paragraphs - could be more - can be saved for future reference. And I was amazed to find that a (French in my case) translation can be exported by email, without showing the original English. Very clever! Finally - and this is the only aspect where I reckon there's room for improvement - you can use your iPhone or tablet's camera to 'hover' over the foreign text, and the text appears in English! Trouble is that it's difficult to do this in anything other than daylight, since the iPad (in my case) tends to block the light to the text itself. Also, it's not easy to hold the tablet's camera lens as still as it needs to be held in order to view the translation. But that said, that's almost like picking hairs. An excellent product, highly recommended!
