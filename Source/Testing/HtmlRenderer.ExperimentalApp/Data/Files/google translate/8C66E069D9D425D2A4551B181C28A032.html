Online Google Dictionary

Google Dictionary was an online dictionary service, originating in its Google Translate service.

However, the Google Dictionary website was terminated on August 5, 2011 after part of its functionality was integrated into Google Search using the define: operator. It should have caused inconvenience to some people who like the free online Google Dictionaries. This is how this website comes out.

This site uses the Google Dictionary json API to provide service for English Learners, who prefer to read the explanations in English itself. Currently, we do not provide download of Google Dictionary, or standalone version, IPHONE/ANDROID APP, Firefox/Chrome plug-in. Note that this site is not related to Google in any ways.
