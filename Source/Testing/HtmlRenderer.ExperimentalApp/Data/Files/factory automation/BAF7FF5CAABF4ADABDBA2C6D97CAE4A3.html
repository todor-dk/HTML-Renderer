Welcome to AP UniTECH Co.,Ltd. Website

We are expertise in mechanical and electrical design with 15th years experience.

When you select AP UniTECH, you have a business partner that you can rely on and trust to get the job done right. We are committed to helping you every step of the way to succeed in your production and quality. 

Most importantly, we consider and listen carefully in order to understand your goals and share your vision. This enables us to design the perfect automated manufacturing solution to help you achieve your objectives and provide you with an impressive return on investment.

We work hard to meet the needs of customers who exceed expectations and enhance efficiency. We are able to deliver on-time due to the ongoing

management of our projects from start to finish with a modern, streamlined project management systems. We focus on establishing partnerships with our key suppliers and vendors to ensure that we have the elements necessary to meet our time. We also offer services and support in places such as China, Indonesia and Poland.

We have a highly-trained workforce of Mechanical and Controls Engineers, Certified Project Managers and experienced Assembly Teams. Through creative engineering designs and control programming, we deliver improved throughput and efficiencies to our client base. To help reduce costs, we can leverage existing designs and apply them for different applications.
