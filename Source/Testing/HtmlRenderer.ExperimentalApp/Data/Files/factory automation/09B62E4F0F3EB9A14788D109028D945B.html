    Us
  

Moorestown, N.J.-based Automation & Control Inc. (ACI) is a system integration, electrical installation and contracting, and custom control panel building shop that provides state-of-the-art industrial automation services. We offer total solutions -- from concept to completion -- with comprehensive capabilities in control and automation engineering, control panel design and panel fabrication, electrical contracting, and factory floor data collection.

Our team of engineers has 125+ collective years of factory automation and engineering experience encompassing the design, programming, and installation of complete production lines; upgrading and modernization of equipment; drive system replacements; and much more for companies in a broad range of industries. Our engineering team has vast expertise in PLC programming; HMI and SCADA design and implementation; drive system application and startup assistance; and control panel design, including the design of UL 508, hazardous-location, IS, and purged UL and cUL panels.
