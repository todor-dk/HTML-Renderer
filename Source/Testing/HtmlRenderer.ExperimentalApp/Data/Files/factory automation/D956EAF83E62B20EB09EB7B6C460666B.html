Factory Automation and Manufacturing Engineering

Koops, Inc. designs custom manufacturing automation engineering systems to help businesses ensure greater control and efficiency in their manufacturing processes. We offer full service and support for each of our systems and tailor them to our client's unique specifications.

Factory Automation for Your Business

Your operation is unique, so it pays to ensure that your automation equipment is customized to meet your unique needs. Koops, Inc. employs engineers at all levels of our organization, many of whom worked in manufacturing companies prior to Koops, Inc. We understand your needs, have the manufacturing experience to evaluate your processes, and employ engineering talent to integrate the latest technology into a custom solution that exceeds your requirements. Custom workstations, material handling systems, and robotic cells give you the capabilities to deliver a high volume product without sacrificing quality.

Our Consultation Process

From an on-site analysis of your current production process to the full integration of technology, people, and processes, we will listen to your concerns, evaluate your requirements, and engineer a custom solution to meet your needs. Koops, Inc. strives to be a partner that is a key component in the solution process. Our equipment is often designed to address and solve specific manufacturing problems. We are honored to be considered a trusted partner with our clients who provides factory automation solutions.
