Factory Automation

Factory Automation is the use of control systems as well as information technology to achieve the most efficient manufacturing processes. It includes a wide range of applications, e.g. motion control, conveyor, robotics, machine tools, and many more. Our interconnect solutions provide highly reliable signal and power transmission for standard connectors and cable assemblies, as well as for customized versions.
