Machine Safe Guarding Solutions

Schmersal Machine Guarding Safety Products: Offering safety control systems with specially designed switches that are resistant to tampering and difficult to override. Mechanically they provide positive break contacts and positively linked actuation. Safety circuit equipped switches with multiple redundant contacts. Products include: Keyed Interlock Switches, Solenoid Latching Interlocks, Door Handle Actuators, Hinged Safety Switches, Safety Rated Limit Switches, Non-Contact Safety Sensors and Cable Pull Switches.

Many designers equip their systems with Schmersal safety controllers because the potential for electrical/mechanical failure is always present - regardless of the reliability of the components in use. Safety controllers detect faults that can compromise safety system operation, thus insuring system reliability. Some of the safety modules available include: SRB & AES Safety Control Modules, Output Expanders, Mutifunction Safety Controllers, Fail-Safe Standstill Monitors, Fail-Safe Delay Timers and Input Expanders.
