Factory Automation

Automation is a key success factor for you in machinery and plant engineering
We enhance your performance and simplify your processes
Let’s connect.

 

The optimisation and progressive digitalisation of industrial production processes is placing ever-growing requirements on modern automation technology. The decentralisation and flexibility of technologies and simple operation of systems play a vital role in this regard. This results in ever more complex requirements as regards system design, installation processes and productive plant operation.

With Weidmüller as your highly experienced partner in machinery and production technology, you can rest assured that your plants will benefit from a noticeable performance boost and unprecedented user-friendliness. With our Factory Automation division, we are offering machinery specialists, production automation engineers and control cabinet manufacturers innovative added value in their application. Developed using a practical approach, our solutions intelligently blend into your industrial field of application and help you to exploit technological trends in a profitable manner.

 

A coherent complete portfolio – comprising everything from classic control cabinet wiring, functional electronics and communication electronics, to control engineering, development environments and web-based services – means we can create solutions that are tailored precisely to your specific requirements and offer you sustainable added value.
