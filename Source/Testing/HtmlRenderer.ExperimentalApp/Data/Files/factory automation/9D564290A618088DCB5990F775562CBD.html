Factory Automation Tours

 

The ability to see a product demonstrated in person helps customers make a more informed decision and experience an application in action, so we introduced a way to bring a fully functional walk-through exhibit to you. H.H. Barnum Company’s 38-foot automation trailer showcases a wide variety of new and legacy products from all major suppliers in an interactive setting.
