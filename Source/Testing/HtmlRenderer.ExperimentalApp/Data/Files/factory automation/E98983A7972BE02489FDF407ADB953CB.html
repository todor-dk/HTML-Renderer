Superior Engineering Resource

In 1991, FANUC Robotics contracted Patti Engineering to work on its customized PLC programs and its proprietary PaintWORKS, Graphic User Interface (GUI) software. Since then, robotic systems and factory automation control systems have continued to play a large role in our continued success.

Although our origin stemmed from automotive paint shop automation, we have ventured into many other automotive applications and into a diverse range of non-automotive industries, to provide expertise in control systems software and hardware.

Patti Engineering has taken the initiative to expand our brand knowledge of factory automation & robotics, PLC and HMI controls to include ABB and Kawasaki Robotics, Siemens, Allen-Bradley, Mitsubishi Electric, OMRON, GE-Fanuc and Opto-22 PLCs, and Iconics HMIs. We are a certified Siemens Solution Provider and one of a select few Certified CSIA members in the regions we serve. Our ability to tie our system control expertise into MES reporting and SCADA system integration has increased the range of our engineering scope from the factory floor into the data collection and supervisory control of manufacturing systems.

Patti Engineering is well positioned to help companies solve their difficult factory automation & robotics control system problems and/or upgrade obsolete hardware and software.
