INTRODUCTION

California Electrical Services is a licensed & insured electrical contractor with a proven track record in construction, engineering, programming , troubleshooting and systems integration. CES has an extensive history in both the industrial private sector and public works projects. License#: 474810

Celebrating 25 years

Andrzej Krol, who remains the owner of the company today, founded California Electrical Services in 1985. Andrzej has a Masters Degree in Electrical Engineering, he is a registered Professional Electrical Engineer (PE) in the State of California, he has an electrical contractor’s license in California and he is a licensed electrician.

Combined with their team of programmers, licensed electricians and PE’s, CES provides their clients with a diverse knowledge base that allows them to commission a client’s project on time and within budget, no excuses.
