vFactory Features

Built-in Features to vFactory include:

Alarms

When a machine enters alarm mode, vFactory can send an email notification to alert machine operators, supervisors, or even shop owners of machine status changes. Trust your equipment, and know that your email-enabled wireless phone or computer can notify you of issues before they become a problem. This minimizes unscheduled downtime or problems in processing, and you can have instant control over your shop floor's work flow by tracking performance like never before, from anywhere in the world.

Reporting

In addition to real time monitoring, vFactory also stores machine status and produces various extensive reports to assist your shop operation. Now your entire shop's throughput can be summarized with hard data, benchmarking your performance, highlighting any bottlenecks, and empowering your shop floor with accurate indications of performance.
