    Factory Automation Services
    Factory Automation Services is a registered business established in April 1999, operating out of KwaZulu Natal, concentrating on projects within South Africa, Mozambique, Zimbabwe and Northen Africa. FAS specialise in all spheres of process control and automation engineering, including design, construction, implementation, project management, feasibility studies and operational commissioning and factory optimisation.
    Core Business
    We undertake small and large engineering projects in field of SCADA, PLC, HMI, C&I Design, Network Architecture and System Design, Information Technology Integration, Instrumentation and Manufacturing Execution Systems also offering training on the above Systems.
