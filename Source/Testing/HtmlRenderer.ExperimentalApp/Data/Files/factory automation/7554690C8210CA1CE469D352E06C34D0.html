Since its inception in 1962, Sigma Design has been your single source solution for developing manufacturing friendly, complex machinery and innovative electro-mechanical devices.

Sigma Design is experienced at manufacturing support and is certified by NIST to help manufacturers become more competitive using Lean Manufacturing techniques. We employ this same focus on efficient manufacturing management in every machine we develop.

Sigma Design has developed hundreds of specialty machines with the capabilities to:

  • Produce machine specifications based on your input
  • Develop innovative mechanisms and geometry to achieve your machines objectives
  • Design electrical machine controls to provide you with operator-safe and reliable production equipment.

Sigma has worked with several clients to manufacture industrial machinery including:

  • Ametek
  • Johnson & Johnson
  • PALL Filtration
  • Victaulic America
