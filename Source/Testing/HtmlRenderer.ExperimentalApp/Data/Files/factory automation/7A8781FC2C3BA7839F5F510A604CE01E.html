Factory Automation Systems Applications

Factory Automation Systems Meet Market Demands

Nook's linear motion products are widely used throughout the factory automation industry. Globalization inspires today's manufacturers to find and incorporate only the best automation technology to ensure manufacturing excellence in a saturated market.

Our selection of products used in factory automation systems applications can include:

  • Modular actuators
  • Ball screws
  • Profile rails
  • Ball screw jacks
  • Machine screw jacks

Factory automation systems applications integrating Nook technology include:

  • Robots
  • Feeder technology
  • Dispensing automation
  • Laser processing applications
  • Industrial automation systems
  • Inspection and test systems
  • Elevators
  • Automated assembly systems
  • Safety platforms

For over 40 years, innovative Nook technology has powered factory automation worldwide providing reliable and cost efficient solutions for all high performance industries. Our products enhance efficiency, safety, equipment performance and process. These advantages improve overall factory automation reliability in improving companies' use of assets and their ability to meet customer demand.

 

Application Spotlight: High Level of Position Accuracy Obtained

Until the release of Nook Precision Metric Ball Screw product line, achieving the precise positioning necessary for today's demanding linear actuator applications required the use of ground metric ball screws. Nook invested in advanced thread-rolling technology, resulting in the ability to manufacture precision ball screws of superior quality in a wide range of diameters and leads. Nook Precision Metric Ball Screws provide a more cost-effective alternative for accuracy-intensive actuation and performs better than all standard thread-rolled ball screws.

Precision Metric Ball Screws offers the fine adjustment, accuracy and repeatability needed to tightly control positioning needed for manufacturing excellence.
