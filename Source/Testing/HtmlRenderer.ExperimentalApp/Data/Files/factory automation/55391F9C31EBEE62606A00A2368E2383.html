Manufacturing Industry Solutions

  • Home
  • Industries
  • Manufacturing Industry Solutions

The manufacturing industry faces a host of challenges. Fluctuations in currencies and commodity prices, increased global competition and escalating customer expectations place enormous demands on quality and safety processes and standards.

Ayoka offers a variety of manufacturing industry solutions designed to help your business improve efficiencies and enhance responsiveness with customers, partners and suppliers while controlling IT costs.

Ayoka’s core competencies include ERP (enterprise resource planning), BI (business intelligence for manufacturing) development, PLC (programmable logic controller) integration and CRM (customer relations management) software solutions. We also perform custom mobile app development to connect remote workers and partners to your industrial business.

In the manufacturing process, slight variations in the temperature, resistance and speed of industrial machinery can bring your operations to a halt and cost your business thousands of dollars in lost time.

Ayoka is a recognized leader in PLC integration and custom builds solutions including distributed and sequential relay remote monitoring of data, pressure, temperature and vibration a wide variety of fields including industrial manufacturers and aerospace and defense contractors.

Using cutting-edge application and database development techniques, we provide web browser-based HMI (human machine interface) accessibility to your core business processes.

We also offer comprehensive SOA (service oriented architecture) solutions to provide your manufacturing operations with complete, end-to-end integration between existing assets and emerging technologies in an open source environment that eliminates costly licensing fees, adapts easily to future needs and delivers an attractive ROI.
