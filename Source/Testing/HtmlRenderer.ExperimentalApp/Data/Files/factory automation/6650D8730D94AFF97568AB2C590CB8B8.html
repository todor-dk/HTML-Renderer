Factory Automation

The automated factory is considerably more efficient and cost effective than factories that rely on manual labor and manually operated machines. By automating factory processes, manufacturers are able to reduce labor costs, improve quality, increase their flexibility, and ultimately improve and increase productivity.

MESH Automation combines its diverse engineering expertise with an innovative approach to automating product assembly, building specialty equipment, and designing process systems – all of which may be customized to solve each of our customer’s specific production and process challenges.

 

Our factory automation solutions incorporate:

  • Robotic Solutions
  • System integration
  • Flexible automation
  • Automated machine control

Additionally, our areas of expertise include:

  • Assembly and joining methods
  • Fluid carrying systems
  • Web converting equipment
  • Machine retooling

Robotic Solutions from MESH Automation

MESH has integrated hundreds of robots in a wide variety of applications for many different industries. Whether you need to add robotics to an existing machine or want us to design new equipment around a robotic solution, we have the in-house resources to handle the job from concept to completion.

Our engineering team has specialized expertise in robotic assembly, palletizing, and machine tending applications.

System Integration by MESH Automation

Whether you want to implement a factory communications system that monitors your plant floor or automate machine controls on existing equipment, the MESH team can develop the best solution to meet the needs of your business.

At MESH, we know that comprehensive electrical controls design and software development are key components to a successful automation project. That’s why we’ve built a large controls department laser-focused on industrial processes, equipment, and systems.

This specialist department works with several different automation platforms including Rockwell/Allen Bradley, Omron, Siemens, BOSCH CNC and General Electric; and our engineers’ decades of combined experience working with these leading brands.

Flexible Automation Solutions from MESH

The MESH team has designed, built and commissioned many automated solutions in a variety of industries. In fact, only a few companies in the United States can match our capabilities and experience in flexible automated systems.

Whether you need individual lean cells or a multifaceted, fully automated machine tied together with a material handling solution, we can design and build a system with the best value for your specific needs.

Our professional electrical control design, mechanical engineering expertise, and software development skills provide our customers with robust equipment and systems that meet their demanding return on investment (ROI).

We have experience using various automation hardware including robots, pneumatics, smart actuators, servo systems and more.

Automated Machine Control

We can tailor an automation control solution, whether simple or advanced, specifically for your manufacturing needs.

Our team of experienced electrical engineers and technicians design systems to best-in-class standards using numerous technologies and subsystems so that your machine functions as a complete unit.

Contact us today to discuss how we can develop a manufacturing solution that could help your business optimize productivity, increase efficiency, and potentially cut costs.
