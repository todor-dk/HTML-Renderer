Plethora

Located in San Francisco, Plethora automates traditional manufacturing processes to produce high-quality machined parts with quick turnaround times. With instant design feedback, pricing, and custom automation software, Plethora helps customers cut days out of their ordering process and up to weeks out of their engineering schedule. Plethora's software is the first of its kind to provide instant feedback and connect users directly to a factory.
