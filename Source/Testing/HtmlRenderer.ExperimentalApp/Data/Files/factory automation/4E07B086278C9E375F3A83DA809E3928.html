Factory Automation & Machine Building Services


At Hye Precision Products we provide custom automation machinery design, fabrication and installation services to commercial and industrial manufacturers. We can support your operation with comprehensive, turn-key solutions. Our engineering team has a long history of experience in designing and building custom machines for a variety of manufacturing operations. Whether for assembly, production finishing or material handling, we create the most effective solutions for our customers’ unique machinery needs depending on their specific requirements. We have produced custom equipment that not only meets the extremely complex specifications of our customers, but is also easily serviceable and upgradeable.

From initial concept, engineering and prototype to the fully assembled machine through installation as well as after the sale support, we possess the advanced engineering, precision machining and fabricating capabilities to produce custom machines to automate your process. We offer a wide range of capabilities from portable bench top machines to fully automated assembly or production equipment up to 240” long, 144” wide and 200” high, in weights of up to 20,000 lbs.

Our custom machinery can automate the most complex assembly and manufacturing process. Control systems are engineered for reliability using components and hardware from top manufacturers including including: Allen Bradley, Omron, DeltaTau, GE Fanuc among others. We also have the capability to retrofit and upgrade your existing machines to automate your processes, including the re-working and redesigning of the controls, hardware and software.

We also support a wide range of platforms and protocols, utilizing the most sophisticated software programs in the industry. Committed to building long term relationships we also provide spare parts and ongoing engineering support, as well as a responsive field service organization.

Our engineering staff is skilled in all aspects of machine design including mechanical, electrical, hydraulic and pneumatic applications. We have an experienced team and the facilities to design, build and test your machinery on our premises. We design and build machines for assembly, product finishing and testing as well as other custom automation applications.

At Hye Precision we pride ourselves in developing and building robust equipment that is built to last and will stand up to years of use. In fact we’ve built machines that have been in continuous use for over 20 years and counting. We also have the capability to retrofit and upgrade your existing machines, including the control systems integration, in order to automate your processes. Let our team of experts support your project with new, innovative designs and completely integrated solutions.
