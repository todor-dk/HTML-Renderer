Factory Automation LSI

The advancement of Industry 4.0 and the Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) brings with it a growing need to boost factory productivity. The use of Ethernet-based networks in factories is becoming more widespread, as deterministic, safe communications across all levels of the factory are more critical than ever. Renesas has a comprehensive portfolio of products to support Industrial Ethernet solutions leading to increased factory productivity and reduced production cost.

Certified by PROFINET official conformance test.
Please see the TPS-1 PROFINET Communicatoin Solution page.

Product Family

Industrial Ethernet Solution w/ Multi Protocol Support (R-IN32M3)
Industrial Ethernet communication requires determinism, fast processing and flexibility to support multiple communication protocols such as EtherCAT, Ethernet/IP, Profinet (RT), CC-Link and Modbus TCP. This powerful solution is also equipped with EtherPHY and designed to address some of the unique challenges presented by Industrial Ethernet applications. The built-in R-IN engine technology has a Real-time OS accelerator and Ethernet Accelerator to achieve high-speed and high-precision communication control.

Learn More

Industrial Ethernet Communication with Gigabit PHY (R-IN32M4-CL2)
R-IN32M4-CL2 is an advanced Industrial Ethernet Communication solution with a built-in Gigabit PHY in addition to our R-IN engine technology. This is an optimal solution to provide high real-time performance with CC-Link IE Field network protocol in the Industrial Networking segment.

Learn More

Industrial Networking solution w/ PROFINET (TPS-1)
This is an industrial networking ASIC is targeted at providing a complete hardware solution for PROFINET IO Device. It has integrated dual port PHY and PROFINET IRT switch with bridge delay of less than 3us.

Learn More

EtherCAT Communication Solution (EC-1)
EtherCAT has been one of the leading protocols in industry for its high performance, low-cost, and flexible topology. EC-1 is an efficient solution that is simple to implement, yet have the processing power to handle the I/O as well as the network interface. The EtherCAT-enabled remote I/O solution is comprised of a ruggedized remote I/O board, software, documentation, and circuit diagrams.

Learn More
