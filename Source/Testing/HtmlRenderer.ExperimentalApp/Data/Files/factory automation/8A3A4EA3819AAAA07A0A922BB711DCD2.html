BCIL is a full service systems integrator that provides turnkey automation solutions to manufacturing companies. The core competency of BCIL includes design, development and implementation of solutions and high level support to customers. Our solution components are:

Machine Vision & Auto ID Based Solutions

  • Direct Part Marking (DPM)
  • Optical Character Recognition (OCR)
  • Optical Character Verification (OCV)

At BCIL, we engage deeply with the businesses in Manufacturing market verticals such as Automotive, Pharma, FMCG, Consumer Durables and also in  Ecommerce, Transport and Logistics. We study the existing processes, understand pain points and challenge areas, design, architect and build a technology road map using combination of machine vision, DPM, traceability and identification technologies.

BCIL has been on the leading edge of plant automation and having a long experience in the Development & Design of solutions for the entire production and logistics process.

 

It ensures that our solutions are ready for existing and future trends and actively support the structural shift to Industry 4.0 & IoT. Our solutions cover identification of the product, eliminating the defects, verifying the assembly and automation of the production in manufacturing.
