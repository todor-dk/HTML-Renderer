Georgia Tech enrollment

Ross Pryor and Mark Ligler enrolled at Georgia Tech and lived in the same dorm. Mark had a car, and a friendship was formed.

Mark Ligler graduates from Georgia Tech

Mark Ligler graduated from Georgia Tech with a Mechanical Engineering degree and started his career with a pump manufacturer.

Ross Pryor graduates from Georgia Tech

Ross Pryor graduated from Georgia Tech with an Electrical Engineering degree. Ross started with EZ Go Golf Car Company and later became a plant engineer with Johns Manville Corporation.

Intro to designing control systems

At Johns Manville, Ross took on his first programmable controller system. He hired a local systems house that specialized in PLC applications.

Technical Director

Ross joined the Atlanta-based systems integration company as the Technical Director and launched his career in the integration business. His first assignment? Finish the PLC project at Johns Manville!

Mark Ligler recruited

Ross recruited Mark Ligler to join the integration company as an engineering manager and later VP of Engineering.

Factory Automation Systems Founded

After the integration company was acquired, Ross left and founded Factory Automation Systems. Mark joined a few months later.

CRA partnership

FAS established a partnership with Ron Potter's company, Creative Robotic Applications. CRA offered turnkey robot solutions, and FAS provided the control systems.

First coordinated DC drive application

FAS completed its first coordinated DC drive application at a building materials company. FAS has specialized in coordinated drives and servo systems as a core competency from the founding of the company.
