Complete Factory Automation

Satisfying every customer, every day – that’s what it’s all about isn’t it? Whether you're making the materials, parts or ingredients that other manufacturers count on or the finished products that people consume, drive, use, or enjoy every day. To deliver the specs that will meet their demands, you need an ally. You need A&E Engineering.

We can make you a hero. Tell us what you want from your factory automation investment and we'll make it happen. Our approach is painless, effective, and efficient. Here's how we do it:

1. We dig into your challenges. We don't talk at you; we listen to you. And we're not afraid to probe and ask the hard questions. We know that defining the right technology solutions requires a deep understanding of the complex challenges you face:

  • Upgrading or replacing obsolete systems with minimal downtime and disruption.
  • Improving existing systems to meet ever-increasing customer expectations for quality and consistency.
  • Delivering consistent documentation and enhanced system safety to meet regulatory demands and organizational goals.


Most importantly, we will engineer solutions that work for the people who use the system day-in, day-out. Our goal is to provide a customized implementation that matches their expectations and approach – and yours, as if you did it yourself.

2. We assemble a top-notch team. A&E's manufacturing automation group has decades in combined engineering experience earned across multiple industries, including automotive, metals, and life sciences. Our expertise and services include:

  • Engineering – Electrical, Mechanical, Software
  • Project Management
  • Design
  • Fabrication & Assembly
  • Documentation
  • Installation
  • Commissioning Start-Up
  • Maintenance and On-Going Support


3. We deliver. Customer satisfaction is your goal. It's our goal too. We'll deliver efficient installation of the right technology at the lowest total project cost. We'll help you deliver the quality, customization and availability your customers demand. And we'll do whatever else it takes to make you a hero – and be yours.

 

Want specifics? Here's what we can do for you:

  • Turnkey control system design and implementation

  • PLC software development

  • HMI and SCADA system development

  • Safety system design and implementation – fully integrated or relay-based

  • Custom and commercial PC application development

  • Motion control and drives – VFD, Servo, DC, Stepper

  • Power distribution design

  • Robot application development and coordination

  • Control panel design and fabrication

  • Fieldbus implementation: Profibus, PROFInet, EtherNet/IP, DeviceNet, Modbus, etc.

  • Conveyor controls

  • Automatic handling – robotic, conveyor, gantry, etc.

  • Automatic storage and retrieval systems (ASRS)

  • Control system upgrades

  • Production tracking systems

  • Machine safety upgrades conforming to EN ISO 13849-1 or EN 62061

  • Fully integrated electrical design using EPLAN or AutoCAD Electrical

  • 3D modelling of electrical systems using Siemens PLM NX (Unigraphics)
