Factory Automation

Banner  Engineering a global leader  in process and industrial automation, helping customers increase efficiency, Rely on Banner for industry-leading photoelectric, ultrasonic and time of fight sensors, machine vision systems, FHSS radios with I/O, Ethernet and serial communication,  machine safety light curtains, safety logic controllers and relays, and a wide assortment of indicator lights, tower lights, stack lights, and enclosure work lights.

Fortress Interlocks designs and manufactures safety access & control systems. Fortress offers an unrivalled portfolio suitable for applications across a wide industrial base from power generation and distribution, steel, automotive, recycling, building materials, through safeguarding robots and palletizes. With in excess of 40 years experience in the safety market, Fortress is renowned for innovative design, robust engineering and reliability.

PULS Power is focused and dedicated to delivering the most technologically advanced compact and efficient DIN-rail power supplies on the market today. PULS offers a comprehensive product offering to include AC to DC power supplies, DC to DC converters, buffer modules and innovative DC UPS controllers to provide back-up power for AC power loss. 

Red Lion Controls has built a strong reputation in several product areas including human machine interface, Ethernet networking, M2M WiFi & cellular communications, process measurement and control, and digital measurement and control. The recent acqutision of N-Tron & Sixnet provides a automation solution package second to none. The Graphite Series of HMI's provides complete integration with PLC’s, Motion Controllers, VFD’s. With 300+ communication drivers,  simultaneous protocol conversion, data logging capabilities, built in web server functionality  make this the most powerful HMI on the market today.

Turck Incorporated is a leading manufacturer of products designed for factory and process automation industries that uphold uncompromising standards for reliability and functionality.  Turck's extensive line of products includes inductive, capacitive, magnetic sensors, linear and rotary measurement devices, temperature, pressure & flow instrumentation, connectivity,  intrinsic & zener barriers, and a complete offering of bus networking  I/O solutions.

Yaskawa America, Inc. Drives & Motion Division is the world’s largest manufacturer of ac drives and motion control products, including adjustable frequency drives, servo amplifiers, servomotors, machine controllers, and motion controllers.
