Factory Automation

Factory Automation demands technology that is engineered to operate with consistent repeatable precision at high duty cycles. System components must provide high accuracy, reliability, and repeatability, as well as velocity control and high power density, in a compact package.

Our product solutions provide:

  • High Precision Motion Feedback Technology
    • Up to 16-bit Resolution Analog-to-Digital Conversion
    • Up to 1 Arc Minute Accuracy
    • Flexible Interface Options with High-levels of Integration
  • Turnkey Motor Control Solutions
    • Proven DSP-based Architecture Enables Flexible Tuning to
         Optimize Motor Performance
    • Flexible Solutions for Various Power and Current Requirements,
         28V to 600V and up to 30A
    • Robust Software Tools and Support Accelerate Time-to-Market
