Factory Automation  

  • ← Back to product catalog
  • Save to MyST
  • Share
  • Print

Factory Automation is an highly demanding Market in which Safety, Standardization and Performance are recognized by customers as key assets.
ST has a strong background in the industrial automation and process control market and is committed to long product life-cycles and high-end technology development.
ST provides an extensive portfolio of technologies and devices to be used in factory automation systems. Our full system solutions (meaning HW and SW) are supported with development tools, evaluation boards and a rich documentation set..                                 

Key enabling products are:

  • 32-bit Microcontrollers based on ARM Cortex M4 cores
  • Standard Bridge Low-side and High-side Smart-Power Devices  for the driving of Solenoids, DC motors and stepper motors
  • Dedicated ICs for charging ,power management and actuator driving
  • One of the industry’s broadest ranges of Power MOSFETs and IGBTs
