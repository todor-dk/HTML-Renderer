Factory automation

Multi-faceted solutions for dynamic systems

The range of products and services provided by Schaffner covers the entire spectrum from engineering to complete product lines. Factory automation systems usually consist of different systems in a fully integrated process flow. Tailor-made solutions are used in virtually every manufacturing and production process. Automation is applied across industry sectors, such as

  • manufacturing, engineering or construction
  • food and beverage industry
  • textile industry
  • heavy industries
  • pharmaceutical Industry
  • automotive industry


Further areas of use include steel mills, the manufacturing of chemical and petrochemical semiconductors and electronic equipment.

Schaffner helps customers to achieve high scale volume production goals and raise their market competitiveness by helping to fulfill local and international standards, reach a low total cost of ownership and achieve a reliable and efficient production automation. Therefore, we provide components that ensure an efficient flow of energy and the reliable operation of applications.

Schaffner’s portfolio includes standard and customized EMC/EMI filters and chokes such as three-phase filters for automation systems, power magnetics components such as medium voltage transformers, auxiliary transformers, reactors and PQ harmonic filters.
