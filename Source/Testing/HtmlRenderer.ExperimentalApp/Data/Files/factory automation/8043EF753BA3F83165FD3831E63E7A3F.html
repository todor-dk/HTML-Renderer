About Us


From a humble start in 2010 now SEIMITSU Factory Automation is a renowned name in Indian engineering industry. We are a prominent service and solution provider. We are providing our services in various industrial fields like Automobiles, Pharmaceuticals, Medical, Electrical, Agriculture and Electronics. Our focus is on providing various products and services which lead to innovation, improved product quality and faster processing. Thereby increasing the overall productivity and reducing the production as well as maintenance cost in a short span of time.

We have partnerships with various Japanese engineering manufacturers to cater the needs of Indian market on one hand and creating business opportunities for Japanese manufacturers on other hand. We are pursuing them to setup their manufacturing facilities in India under MAKE IN INDIA.

Our eminent Japanese Partners are THK, Linear motion Guide Manufacturer, KHK, Gears Racks and Pinion manufacturer,CKD, Pneumatic Cylinders Pneumatic Valves Filter Regulator Manufacturer, NSK, machinery and Automotive bearing manufacturer, OILES Corp, self-Lubricating bushing manufacturer and YAMAHA ROBOTICS,Industrial Robots ( Electric Actuator ) Manufacturer .

SEIMITSU Factory Automation has not only been providing engineering products, it also provides total solution in terms of technical support, skill enhancement by conducting various technical seminars and exhibitions. We have also introduced in India new products and new technology of our various Japanese counterparts so that our Indian engineering industry can also be competent at international level and hence can compete with foreign brands.
