Welcome to Tronrud Engineering Pte Ltd

The Norwegian Engineering company Tronrud Engineering founded in 1977 has maintained an office in Singapore since 2009 to cater for the market potential in the South East Asian region. We provide service and support activities as well as sales and project management. We aim to deliver engineering services and automation solutions to a variety of industries and we are ready to take on a challenge.

 Automation Consultancy

Tronrud Engineering has delivered industrial automation projects to enable new technologies or improve HSE and production in customers facilities for 35 years and has picked up many lessons learned and best practices during these years. We can assist in factory design, mechanical design and factory automation consultancy for most industries including PLC (programmable logic controller) improvements, and propose ways to increase productivity.

Oil & Gas / Offshore

Tronrud Engineering has a long tradition in offshore / oil & gas machining services and producing automation for offshore / oil & gas, improving HSE and productivity for our customers. We have delivered anything from extremely low tolerance titanium seals to seismic activities production lines and pneumatically driven transport trolleys for moving heavy objects like turbines onboard vessels.

PV & Renewable Energy

Together with a Norwegian benchmark company in the PV solar industry, Tronrud Engineering has developed a range of PV automation for solar wafer fabrication and solar cell manufacturing as well as load / stress testing for PV modules. We continue to offer automation for this industry to the customers that wants the lowest yield loss and highest availability of their automation.

Machining

Our well-equipped machining workshop can machine any metal including titanium to very high customer specifications. We also have a metal printing 3D printer that can handle titanium as well as other metals.

Textile / Weaving

One of our products is a high speed, easy to work with, next generation loom called the TC2. It is suitable for high speed and ultra-flexible weaving where patterns can be uploaded from your PC design program in a minute. We call this digital weaving as it is an automated weave.

Aerospace

Based on our described competency we also deliver special automation and machining services to the Aerospace industry.
