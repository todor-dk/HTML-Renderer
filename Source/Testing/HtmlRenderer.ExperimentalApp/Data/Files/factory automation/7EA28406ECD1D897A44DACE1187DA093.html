P&F Factory Automation

Proximity Sensors

P&F offers a wide variety of Proximity Sensors including capacitive and inductive sensing technologies.

Capacitive proximity sensors reliably detect non-metallic objects, liquid, powder & granular materials; they can also detect materials through glass or plastic walls. Inductive proximity sensors detect metals.

Photoelectric Sensors

Pepperl+Fuchs offers a wide range of Photoelectric Sensors, fiber optic cables, & accessories. We have the photoelectric sensors you need to keep your applications running. Pepperl+Fuchs with the brand VISOLUX is an innovator of photoelectric sensing technologies, for precise, optical control of positioning, sorting, and counting processes and for custom non-standard applications.

Ultrasonic Sensors

Pepperl+Fuchs’ Ultrasonic Sensors currently encompass twelve series of mechanical designs for through-beam, direct detection and retro-reflective operation. Ultrasonic sensors present the ideal solution for non-contact position and distance measurement in all industrial areas where environmental conditions such as dust, smoke or steam may affect the sensors.

Industrial Vision

P&F offer Industrial Vision sensor solutions that range from freely programmable devices to user-specific, custom-designed devices.   Pepperl+Fuchs offers the whole range of industrial image processing from vision sensors via smart cameras to a complete system.

Rotary Encoders

Rotary Encoders can be used in all applications that require monitoring rotation rate, speed, acceleration and direction. These sensors can be utilized in many applications found in mechanical engineering, the conveyor industry, material handling and the packaging industry. You are certain to find a suitable rotary encoder for your application from our broad product offering.

Positioning Systems

Pepperl+Fuchs utilizes different technologies to provide industrial Positioning Systems for virtually any positioning task. Our product range includes: Inductive Positioning Systems Distance Measurement Sensors PosiTrack Position Encoding System WCS Data Matrix Positioning System PCV Electronic CAM-Switch Controller PAX

AS – interface

The actuator sensor interface (AS-Interface) with special yellow flat cable supplies power to the sensors and actuators as well as allowing you to transfer data to a maximum of 62 nodes per AS-Interface network. The installation time and costs are much lower compared to conventional wiring technology.

Identification Systems

The Identification System technologies allow you to choose between RFID and optical identification (Data Matrix code or barcode).  Pepperl+Fuchs identification systems make the acquisition of information in production and logistics more cost-efficient, and enhance the quality of the information.

Logic Control Units

Our Logic Control Units provide a multitude of features for use in the automation industry. We offer a wide range of indicators, counters/ timers, tachometers and other secondary switching devices, specifically designed for applications and used in conjunction with proximity sensors.  When it comes to signal and discrete pulse control and display, Pepperl+Fuchs offers you a broad line of logic and control products.

Accessories & Cordsets

Pepperl+Fuchs offers many types of Accessories for mounting and connecting all sensor types.

Plug connectors Cordsets Mounting accessories Multi-port junction boxes Reflectors…and more

Pepperl & Fuchs – Factory Automation

The Factory Automation Division is a leading manufacturer of industrial sensors designed to address specific market needs on a global basis. 

Pepperl+Fuchs introduced the world’s first industrial proximity sensor in 1958, and ever since that time we’ve been providing the automation industry with the highest quality industrial sensors.

Pepperl+Fuchs’ sensors are crafted using state-of-the-art components and the latest technologies to ensure precision, reliability, and functionality. Our industrial sensors’ combination of durability, high accuracy, and quick response time make them an invaluable tool in industrial automation in the automotive industry, doors, gates and elevators manufacturing, machine and plant engineering, material handling, packaging, print, paper and finishing and process equipment industries.

Our inductive and capacitive sensors along with photoelectric and ultrasonic technologies are the backbone of our Factory Automation product line. Other products include rotary encoders, positioning systems, AS-Interface, RFID, and barcode systems.
