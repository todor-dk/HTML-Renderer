Factory Automation

MMCI is a full service systems integrator that provides turnkey automation solutions to manufacturing companies across country. Our core competencies include design and implementation of a variety of solutions using;

  • Programmable Controllers
  • Software
  • Drives
  • Motion control and robotic systems
  • Conveyor systems, and more.

We deliver automation systems to customers from diverse industries – building products, metals, automotive, food processing, consumer products, chemicals, lubricants to name just a few.

The motivation to automate processes has been fundamentally the same for years - make more product, improve quality and flexibility, and reduce direct labor costs.

From design and integration to maintenance and training after installation, MMCI’s innovative warehouse automation systems solutions designed to fit your project needs now, and in the future.
