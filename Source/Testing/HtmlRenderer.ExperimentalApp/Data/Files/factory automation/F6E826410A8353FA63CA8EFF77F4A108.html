Factory Automation Services for Manufacturing Facilities in Philadelphia, PA

When optimizing a factory, automation is one of the keys to driving profitable results in terms of consistency and efficiency in throughput, reductions in direct labor costs and more. If you want to make sure that the factory you’re working on in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, receives the greatest benefit from automated equipment, trust the expert engineers at Turtle & Hughes. Our Industrial Control & Automation team is highly skilled at offering technical expertise in selecting, installing and providing support for factory automation equipment from the top manufacturers in the industry. With our automation solutions, you’ll not only be able to outfit the facility you’re working on with top-of-the-line equipment, but you’ll also be able to ensure that your machinery is running at peak performance.

When you turn to the Turtle & Hughes Industrial Control & Automation department for factory automation assistance for your project in Philadelphia, PA, you’ll benefit from a full array of services, including:

  • MACHINERY ENCLOSURES
  • CIRCUIT PROTECTION DEVICES
  • FIELD AND PROJECT SUPPORT
  • MOTION AND MOTOR CONTROLS
  • AUTOMATION SOFTWARE
  • OPERATOR INTERFACING
  • PROGRAMMABLE LOGIC CONTROLLERS
  • VARIABLE SPEED MOTORS AND DRIVES
  • INDUSTRIAL CONTROL AND SAFETY
  • TRAINING
  • PRESENCE SENSORS
  • AND MORE

We are a family-owned, employee-owned and woman-owned company that has been serving the industrial and electrical distribution industry since 1923, and throughout the decades, we have perfected the art of finding specialized engineers who can provide expert assistance for virtually any project, large or small. Not only do we offer factory automation services through our Industrial Control & Automation department, we also offer many other services through the various departments of our Electrical Division, including Communications, Power Distribution & Automation Solutions and more.

For more information about our Industrial Control & Automation department and the factory automation assistance we can provide for your project in the Philadelphia area of Pennsylvania, contact Turtle & Hughes today.
