Pokémon GO

Pokémon GO is a smartphone game released on iOS and Android devices in 2016. This app allows for you to go and capture Pokémon using Augmented Reality. This game uses geographic location for you to find and capture Pokémon. You can then battle other players, trade with other players and even participate in raids against Pokémon.

Pokémon GO has over 150 Pokémon available, but more will be added as time passes. There are various locations you can visit called PokéStops where you can find items and set off lures to attract wild Pokémon. There are also Gyms that you can face and attempt to hold for your team.

It can come with a special Pokémon GO Plus accessory which can be worn which allows for notifications if a Pokémon is nearby.

The game requires a constant 3G or 4G connection in order to work.
