Frequently Asked Questions

If you need answers to more eCommerce questions, try the support menu for video tutorials, online documentation, community forums, and more!

What Payment Gateways Are Provided?

WP EasyCart comes with over 30 payment gateways included.  Select from popular gateways such as Stripe.com, PayPal, Authorize.net, and First Data.  We also have many overseas partners like Payment Express, eWay, SagePay and others.  Click Here for a full list of payment options.

What kind of shipping is available?

WP EasyCart includes live shipping options.  No added cost for UPS, USPS, FedEx, Australia Post, and Canada Post, they’re all included with WP EasyCart.  Build your own table rates based on quantity, price, weight, or percentage.  Want to keep shipping simple? Try our flat rate shipping options.

Can I have customers select options on a product?

Yes. WP EasyCart offers all kinds of options and variations. Choose from combo boxes, image swatches, radio groups, check boxes, text fields, text areas, even quantity grids. Options can change pricing, up or down, multiply the product price, and more!

Can I sell downloads, retail goods, gift cards?

Yes, Yes, and Yes.  WP EasyCart is the most complete WordPress shopping cart on the market.  Sell downloads, retail items, gift cards, subscriptions, donations, and more all from one eCommerce plugin!

Is EasyCart a monthly fee?

No. Its a one-time cost. Each license is good for one website and works for life, update for free. The plugin is a one time low fee starting as low as $50 dollars…  Click Here to Compare pricing with similar products.

Will EasyCart work with my Theme?

Yes.  WP EasyCart works in any theme.  The cart is placed directly on your page using a shortcode.  Use widgets to customize your store setup to fit your theme.  Click Here to view our extension marketplace for more information on those.
