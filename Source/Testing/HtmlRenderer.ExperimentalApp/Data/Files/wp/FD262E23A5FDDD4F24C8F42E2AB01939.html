Web Publishing

Powered by WordPress, we provide an easy way for current faculty, staff and students to create a website or blog using predefined design themes and plugins. There is no charge to users.

 

LEARN

To build the site of your academic dreams with wp.nyu.edu, learn about our best features, and how to get started.

CREATE

Write, click, publish, and revel. Your words can be read by anyone in the world or only to specific users of your choosing.

CUSTOMIZE

Find the perfect theme for your site and make it your own with widgets, menus, plugins and custom design.
