English[edit]

Initialism[edit]

WP

  1. (newspaper) Washington Post
  2. (Wiktionary and WMF jargon) Wikipedia
  3. (baseball) wild pitch
  4. (software) WordPress
  5. (software) WordPerfect
  6. (military) white phosphorus
  7. (social) white privilege
  8. (military) weapons procurement
  9. (military) war prison
