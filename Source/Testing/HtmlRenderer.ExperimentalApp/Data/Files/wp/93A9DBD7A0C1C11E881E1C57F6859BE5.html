A Plugin That Works the Way You Do

You probably already use third party tools that you've come to love and rely on. This plugin isn't designed to replace those tools – it’s designed to enhance them, making them more accessible to you via your CRM.

The free version of WP-CRM System is robust on its own. We didn't want to bloat the software by incorporating extensions you may not ever use. The choice is yours for what you add and what you keep out of your WordPress CRM.

If you need extra features, we invite you to browse through our add-on plugins. These extensions make it easy to setup WP-CRM System so it works the way you do.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is WP-CRM System really free?

Yes! Get started today for free enjoying the simplicity and ease of having your CRM integrated into your WordPress website – no strings attached.

Are plugin updates and support included?

All packages include support by email, as well as plugin updates for the duration of your subscription. Plugins will continue to work if you choose not to renew, but you will no longer receive updates or support. The only exception is for lifetime packages, which will receive support and updates until the end of time.

I bet the extensions are expensive, right?

Not at all. We’ve designed the extensions to be affordable, even for the tightest of budgets.

What if I don't like WP-CRM System?

If you don’t love WP-CRM System, we’d rather you find a solution that works for you (no hard feelings). Simply uninstall the WP-CRM System plugin and any add-on plugins to eliminate it from your website.

We’d love to hear from you! If there is something specific you’d like to see changed, please reach out to us so we know how we can continue improving the system.

Starter Extensions

Starter extensions are available to all Personal, Plus, Professional, and Lifetime license-holders.

Custom Fields

This extension allows you to add custom fields to your WP-CRM System records.

Contact From User

This extension allows you to create new contacts in WP-CRM System from existing users in your WordPress site.

Email Notifications

Keep your team up-to-date with email notifications whenever a project, opportunity, or task is updated.

Import Campaigns

Upload a CSV with information on all of your campaigns into WP-CRM System.

Import Contacts

Upload a CSV with information on all of your contacts directly into WP-CRM System.

Import Opportunities

Upload a CSV with information on all of your opportunities directly into WP-CRM System.

Import Organizations

Upload a CSV with information on all of your organizations directly into WP-CRM System.

Import Projects

Upload a CSV with information on all of your projects directly into WP-CRM System.

Import Tasks

Coming from another CRM? Upload a CSV with information on all of your tasks directly into WP-CRM System.

Pro Extensions

Pro Extensions are only available to Professional or Lifetime license-holders. These license-holders will also receive any additional pro add-ons we release in the future while their license is active.

Client Area

Create a portal for clients to see the status of their projects, tasks, campaigns, and invoices.

Dropbox Connect

Add documents from your Dropbox account to any record in WP-CRM System.

Gravity Forms Connect

This extension lets you connect to a Gravity Form to capture information from new form submissions.

Invoicing

Create and send invoices to your customers and accept credit card payments through Stripe.

Less Accounting

View invoices for each customer and create new invoices for all of your projects right from WP-CRM System!

MailChimp Sync

This extension allows you to subscribe WP-CRM Contacts to your MailChimp list.

Ninja Forms Connect

This extension allows you to connect to a Ninja Form to automatically create new contacts.

Slack Notifications

Keep your team up-to-date with notifications in a Slack channel for updates to tasks, opportunities or projects.

Zendesk Connect

Stay up to date with your customer support tickets, and manage new tasks, projects, and opportunities from one place.
