Advanced room settings - Flexible pricing options and room rates

Easy WP Hotelier allows you to create two types of rooms: standard or with rates. A room with rates (variable room) lets you define variations of a room where each variation may have a different price, required deposit or conditions.

Each room (or rate) can be non cancellable and non refundable, allowing you to charge the guest for the remaining deposit (possible only with some gateways).

Three different pricing options are supported out of the box: global price, different price for each day of the week and seasonal prices.
