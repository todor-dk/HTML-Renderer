User Reviews

 
Just the first in great series!
17 May 2006 | by Ben Hendel (deltableh) (United States) – See all my reviews

The first Chad Peter film I ever saw, Sex absolutely blew me away with it's plot. However, the odd-plot-twists are a signature, and a specialty, of Chad, and a viewer sees how he got his starts with his unique genre. While obviously an amateur short, this could be expanded so much more and make a good longer film. The acting isn't the best, but it shows promise that comes through in Chad's later films. With Sex, however, I'm always left wondering what exactly happens at the end with all the characters. How people get switched back, how the main character deals with Natalie Portman in his body. I would have liked some more resolution. All and all, a good movie to watch before you have your mind scrambled by Drugs.
