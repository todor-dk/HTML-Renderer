Harry Grammes Jr.


1939 - 2016 Obituary Condolences
Harry E. Grammes, Jr., 77 of Hellertown, PA. died Sunday, May 22, 2016, at his residence. Born April 28, 1939 in Allentown, PA, he was the son of the late Harry E. Grammes, Sr. and the late Minnie (Smith) Bilheimer-Grammes. Harry was employed by the former Bethlehem Steel Corp. for 41 years with his last responsibility as manager for the coke works division until retiring in 1994. A member of Emmanuels Evangelical Lutheran Church, Bath PA.; Harry was an avid antique collector, who enjoyed attending flea markets; He was much about being with people with his weekly stop at Krock's Pub, Wescoesville, PA. to meet the gang; regular stops at Snydersville Diner; he spent much time with friend and family out dining; he loved his garden and being outdoors. Survivors: a sister, Mrs. Hilda Phillips of Berwick, PA. and canine companion Sara. Many nieces and nephews and great nieces and nephews. He was predeceased by 7 Sisters; Elizabeth, Anna, Mary, Dorothy, Quilla, Esther, Edith, and 5 Brothers; Kenneth, Dallas, Robert, William, Arthur. Services: A Funeral service will be at 11:00 AM on Thursday May 26, 2016 at Emmanuel Evangelical Lutheran Church, 3175 Valley View Drive Bath, PA. 18014 with the Rev. Elizabeth Mitchell officiating. The family will receive friends from 10:00 AM to 11:00 AM prior to the services in the church. Interment will follow in Emmanuel's Union Cemetery- Emmanuelsville, PA. Schisler Funeral Home, Northampton, PA. is in charge of arrangements. Contributions: Online condolences may be made to the family at www.schislerfuneralhomes.com
Published in Morning Call on May 24, 2016
