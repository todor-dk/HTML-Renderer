    Hi!

    I need help to translate a recipe book: when you list the ingredients, can you say "Gramme" or is it too old-fashioned?

    Further on, does "gramme" (or "gram") have to be followed by "of"?

    Example: 500 grammes (grams) of potatoes

  3. Thelb4 Senior Member

    UK English
    Even most British people these days would raise an eyebrow upon seeing the word "gramme". While the word "programme" may still be in existence, "gramme" certainly is not.
    "500 grams of potatoes" is the correct expression here.
