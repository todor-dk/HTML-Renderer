Grammes Correction

January 27, 1985|Sunday Call-Chronicle

In Saturday's obituary on Adam A. Grammes, Rodney Grammes was incorrectly identified as Grammes' brother. Rodney Grammes is Adam's cousin. Adam Grammes was the only son of Edna (Litzenberger) Grammes of Cetronia and the late Arlan Grammes.
