Le Gramme

Le 7 Grammes Bracelet

—$290
Description
Dedicated to men yet also beloved by women, Le Gramme specializes in wearable objects of irreducible simplicity. Founders Adrien Messié, a protégé of the late Andrée Putman, and Erwan Le Louër, creator of the ethical jewelry brand JEM, design each architectural piece based upon the weight of the metal from which it is made. This bracelet is handcrafted in France of recycled 925 silver (a tarnish-delaying, durability-enhancing combination of 92.5% pure silver and 7.5% copper) with a polished exterior and brushed, matte-finish interior. Each one bears the stamp of the master artisan that made it along with a laser-engraved number corresponding to the weight of the bracelet, its provenance, and a unique serial number.
