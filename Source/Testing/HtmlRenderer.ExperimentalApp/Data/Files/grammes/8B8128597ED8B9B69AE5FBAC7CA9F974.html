Region 6A North 

Highlanders finish 7th in the Region; qualify 4 for the 6A State Tournament along with one alternate. Senior Alex Wassel joins the Century Club en route to his 3rd place finish at 106lbs. Junior Calvin Zug finishes 6th place at 132lbs to qualify for the state tournament. Sophomore Yazeed Salameh finished 7th place at 126lbs and will be an alternate. Seniors, Gavin Legg (170lbs.) and Brendan Grammes (152lbs.) both win their second Regional Championships. Brendan Grammes awarded the 6A North Outstanding Wrestler!

Conference Champion!

#1 seed, Alex Wassel, claims his first conference championship after three first period pins. Wassel did not allow a conference opponent to score against him this season. Sealing his 99th Varsity win with a 0:57 pin over Langley's Nikhil Patel.

Freshman Region Champ!

One week after claiming his first Liberty District Championship, Conor Grammes wins his first Northern Region Championship in his first year in High School. Along the way, Grammes did not allow a single point to be scored against him en route to his 45th victory of the season in the Regional Finals.

3rd Place Finish

Forrest Stieg places third at the Northern Region Tournament. Stieg never trailed in a single match throughout the entire tournament. Following an unfortunate circumstance, Forrest was DQ'd in the quarterfinals while leading 12-1. Overcoming the DQ, Stieg wrestled his way through 5 wrestlers to reach 3rd place, and a gain his way to the State Tournament.
