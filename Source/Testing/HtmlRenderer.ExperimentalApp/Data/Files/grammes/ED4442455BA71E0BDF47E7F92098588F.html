                AlamakGirl • 03 May 2016 • 370 Reviews
                Dessert

                The outlet is located under a HDB block, next to a coffee shop. This is the second outlet of Twenty Grammes. The outlet offers a variety of ice cream on display, as well as, a range of soft serve. The outlet was designed with a minimalist design, unfurnished wall and flooring. Menu is a simple 2 page filled on a clipboard. After placing order, a hard metal capsule was given. Food will served to your table. Remember to help yourself to the cutlery at the counter. Help yourself to the water from the tap net to the counter. Queen B ($12.90) Chocolate lava cake, cookie butter soft serve, caramelised cookies The molten filling in the cake was rich and moist. The soft serve melted rather fast. So remember to take photos as fast as possible. Overall the food was not bad. The place had a regular flow of customers, even when it was not located quite near to the MRT station.
