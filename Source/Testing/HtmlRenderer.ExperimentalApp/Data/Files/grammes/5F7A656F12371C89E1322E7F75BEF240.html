Louis F. Grammes, CLU®, CFBS

Louis F. Grammes, CLU®, CBFS is a Certified Family Business Specialist with uFinancial Group, a general agency of Massachusetts Mutual Life Insurance Company located in Mechanicsburg, Pennsylvania. He has forty years of experience counseling his clients ininsurance strategies, wealth preservation, business succession and selective benefit plans. Working with his clients’ advisors, he is known for being a highly creative professional in the development of business succession plans and wealth preservation techniques. 

Lou contributes unselfishly to his industry and his community. He has been an active member of the local NAIFA chapter since 1966 and has served as president of four Life Underwriters Associations, two Estate Planning Associates and also served as Pennsylvania State Associate Legislative Chair for 20 years.

Lou has gone well beyond the industry in serving his community through his involvement with Rotary, the American Trauma Society, March of Dimes, The Children’s Playroom, his church and is currently serving as a member of the Asbury Communities Board of Directors and Chair of the Compensation Committee.

Recently Lou had an article published in Forbes Magazine regarding business succession planning.

Louis F. Grammes is a Registered Representative of and offers Securities, investment advisory, and financial planning services through MML Investors Services, LLC, Member SIPC (www.sipc.org). 5001 Louise Drive, Suite 300, Mechanicsburg, PA, 17055. 717-791-3300.
