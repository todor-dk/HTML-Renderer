A gram is a unit of weight equal to 1/1000th of a kilogram.

A gram is the approximate weight of a cubic centimeter of water.

A pound is a unit of weight commonly used in the United States and the British commonwealth, and originally descended from Roman times.

A pound is defined as exactly 0.45359237 kilograms.
