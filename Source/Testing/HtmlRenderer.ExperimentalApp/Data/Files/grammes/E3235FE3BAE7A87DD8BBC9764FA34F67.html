2017 (As A Freshman):
• BIG EAST Weekly Honor Roll (2/20)

Prior to Xavier: 6A North Region First Team as a senior... hit .500 with a .587 on-base percentage as a senior... also slugged .655 to go with nine doubles, 22 RBI and 17 runs... recorded consecutive seasons with at least a .982 fielding percentage... made just three errors in 49 games over sophomore and junior seasons... improved at the plate in each of his first three seasons, raising his batting average every season he's played varsity – from .305 as a freshman to .395 as a junior... a two-time all-conference selection, Grammes was selected to the Virginia Futures Prospect Game in 2015 and was named McLean High School's Most Outstanding Male Athlete... outside of baseball, Grammes was a back-to-back state champion wrestler in 2015 and 2016... he graduated as McLean's all-time wins leader in wrestling...
