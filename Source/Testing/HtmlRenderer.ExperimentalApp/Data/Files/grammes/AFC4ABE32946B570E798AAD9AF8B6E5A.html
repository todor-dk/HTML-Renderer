English[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • gram (chiefly US, Canadian, Australian, New Zealand, international)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ɡɹæm/
  • Rhymes: -æm
  • Homophones: gram, graham (GenAm)

Noun[edit]

gramme (plural grammes)

  1. (Britain, dated) gram (unit of mass)

Anagrams[edit]

  • gammer
