The Facebook Login plugin is the easiest way to let your users connect using their Facebook account. It’s a lightweight but powerful plugin that will improve users registrations on your site. It’s fully Compatible with BuddyPress, WooCommerce, Easy Digital Downloads and Ignition Deck

The plugin comes with a powerful login / register widget that you can place anywhere on the site by simple using the sidebar widget, a shortcode or a simple php function.
