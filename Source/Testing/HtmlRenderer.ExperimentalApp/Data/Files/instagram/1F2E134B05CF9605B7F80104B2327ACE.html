Instagram Sign UP or Login Online PC & Create Account

Share:

This article will cover the Instagram Sign UP and Login process and will guide you on how to create Instagram Account Online from your PC. Lets start with a brief introduction to Instagram before tackling the main issues.

With modern smartphones and gadgets you can make your world as pretty as you want and you can share your perspectives with people near and far using the power of the Internet. Applications like Instagram can help make this possible by helping you to share and broadcast creative and beautiful things with your friends.

An Instagram login can be obtained from your iPhone or Android devices and devices supporting OpenGL ES 2.  Once logged in to Instagram, you can take photos on your mobile phone and share them super-fast to all your friends.

With 100 Million users, this application is the fastest growing of its kind. So, sign up to Instagram and start enjoying the wonderful features of this amazing app.

Creating an Instagram login is completely free of charge and costs nothing, so even if you are just curious, you might as well sign up – but you must create the account from an iPhone, iPad or Android device since you can’t login to the website from your computer without having done this.

So, let’s start with the simple procedures required for getting started.

Instagram Sign UP Requirements :

In order to browse your Instagram account from your PC, you must first create an account via your iPhone or Android smartphone. You can download it free of charge from both Google Play store and Apple iTunes stores so you know that the programs are free from bugs and viruses.

  1. Google Play:  Google Play is a repository for Android apps for use on Android devices. You will find most applications on Google store including the Instagram app.
  2. Apple Store: You can also find the iOS version of Instagram on iTunes and also read other people’s reviews of the app.

You need to download the app before following any of steps as stated below.

Creating the Account

Before using Instagram, you need to create an account. All the required steps for Instagram Sign Up and Login are below.

  1. First step after downloading, open the Instagram application. After opening the application you will get a message with two options whether to register or sign in to Instagram.
  2. You need to choose register from those options and your registration process will begin.
  3. You will have option of registering using a Facebook account or by filling all information in manually. Either way will work but if you are doing so with Facebook, you have to allow them to use your basic information and if you decline then you won’t be able to login to Instagram.
  4. You have to choose a unique username and password to get your login credentials for this application.
  5. Lastly you have to click on the Register button and your process of Creating an Instagram Account will end here.

Login to Instagram from your PC or Laptop

Once you have an account, you can login to Instagram from a PC by visiting the official site – instagram.com – or web.stagram.com in your favourite web browser. After entering your Username and Password you will be able to view all your photos and your friends’ uploads.
