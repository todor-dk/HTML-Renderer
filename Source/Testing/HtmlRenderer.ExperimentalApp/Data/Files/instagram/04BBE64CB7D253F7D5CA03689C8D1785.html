Instagram is a free software (app) that allows you to share photos with your friends. Users can take pictures with it, Then the app filters the photos to give it a retro look then users can share it on social networks, such as Instagram itself. Instagram delivers unlike the standard ratio pictures of mobile phones, (which have 4:3 aspect ratio) square photos, just as the Kodak Instamatic and Polaroid. The program was initially only supported for the iPhone, iPad and iPod touch. From April 2012 also worked on smartphones with Android version 2.2 or later.
