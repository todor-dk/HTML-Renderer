Convert PRT to DWG

Design Presentation offers you SolidWorks (prt) to AutoCAD (DWG) conversion [STL to DWG] enabling you to access and edit the file. We use a parametric, feature-based approach to creating 3D models and assemblies.

At Design Presentation we usually start building a model with either a 2D or 3D sketch. The sketch consists of geometry such as lines, arcs, conics etc. We add dimensions to the sketch to define the size and location of the geometry. We provide highly organized design tables & feature trees for easy navigation and revising, accurate part and assembly modeling as well as drawings supplied to your standards and specifications.

At Design Presentation we understand the deadline pressure a project can create. We can provide you the transformed files within the same day or overnight without any rush charges. We believe that our success is based on the success of our customers which is why we strive to deliver a support system that enables us to provide error free files.

We can translate your existing PRT file to DWG or vice versa so that you become more productive and profitable. You can forward anything from hand sketches to full production 2D drawings, scanned paper or 3D files.

Let Design Presentation take the hassle and aggravation out of your next conversion job. Just upload your drawings via ftp and send us your instructions and we can assure you delivery on time, every time. Email or fax us your requirement today.
