Community Currencies in Action was a transnational partnership (2011-2015) helping to lay the ground for cross-sectorial currency innovations designed for the common good.

Providing a package of support structures to develop initiatives across North West Europe, we are promoting community currencies as a credible vehicle for achieving positive social, environmental and local economic outcomes.

 

Learn more about our pilot currencies  and the Who, What, Why and How of our project.
