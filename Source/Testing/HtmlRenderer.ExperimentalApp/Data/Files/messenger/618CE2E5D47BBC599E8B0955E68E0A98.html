  • THE MESSENGER

    In ancient times, humans looked to the flights and songs of birds to predict the future. Today once again, birds have something to tell us.

    WHERE TO SEE THE FILM

THE MESSENGER is a visually thrilling ode to the beauty and importance of the imperiled songbird,
 and what it will mean to all of us on both a global and human level if we lose them.

About:

SongbirdSOS Productions Inc. is a Canadian independent production company. We produce documentary films that explore the rich and varied human connection we share with Songbirds. Our current productions are international treaty co-productions between Canada and France. Films à Cinq is our Paris-based partner. The productions are supported by CBC TV, Canal D and ARTE with the financial support from the Canada Media Fund, Ontario Media Development Corporation, Rogers Documentary Fund, The Canadian Film or Video Production Tax Credit, CNC, PROCIREP, Angoa, Rogers Telefund and DOC’s Giving Voice. Development was made possible by the CFC/NFB Feature Documentary program.
