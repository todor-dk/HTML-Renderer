How it works

Messenger for Desktop is a wrapper for the official client messenger.com. By "wrapper", I mean that it works like a regular browser whichÂ can only navigate to the messenger.com web app. Thus, MFD doesn't touch your messages, account or personal data. All that is handled securely by Facebook.
