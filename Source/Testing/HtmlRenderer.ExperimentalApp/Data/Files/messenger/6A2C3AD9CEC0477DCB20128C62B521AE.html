Quik Trak Messenger Service has been providing same day, fast and reliable delivery service to more than 1,000 companies in Manhattan and the Tri-State area for 31 years.

How fast? Our bike, truck or walker courier can pick up and deliver your rush package within 20 minutes! Whether it's an envelope or a truck load you need delivered, you can count on our courier service to make you look good with our timely and courteous service.

Why would you choose Quik Trak Messenger Service over other messenger services?

  • Dependability comes to mind first. It’s important to have messengers who are professional, courteous and competent. After all, the messengers are delivering to YOUR customers. Quik Trak screens all messengers before hiring - most messenger services hire anybody who walks through their doors.

  • Insurance covers every delivery. Can your courier service provide you with a certificate of insurance within 24 hours?

  • Knowledgeable Customer Service Representatives answer the phone promptly. At Quik Trak, a machine won’t answer your call, and you won’t be put on hold forever. We provide instant answers to all of your service and billing questions.

  • State of the art technology enables us to customize any billing needs you may have, and to track your packages from pick-up to delivery by either phone or online.

  • Personal service assures quality. We’re not too large, allowing us to know our customers very well - but we are also not too small to handle many deliveries at a moment’s notice. Unlike other delivery services, we charge no fee to open and maintain an account.

  • Speed is our specialty. We offer the fastest deliveries in the city and surrounding Tri-State area. In fact, our regular service is faster than most of the competition’s rush service.

  • Time Service hire a messenger or driver by the hour.

  • Refrigerated delivery we deliver perishable items in our refrigerated vans to business or residential addresses.  On demand or scheduled.

  • Light Warehousing we can store your items and ship them out for you.

  • One Stop Shopping provides you with bike and truck deliveries. We service the entire Tri-State area and beyond.  All your messenger and trucking needs can be taken care of by Quik Trak.

Quik Trak Messenger Service was established in 1986 and is absolutely committed to levels above the highest quality. We are members of the New York State Messenger and Courier Association, Manhattan Chamber of Commerce and Transportation Alternatives.
