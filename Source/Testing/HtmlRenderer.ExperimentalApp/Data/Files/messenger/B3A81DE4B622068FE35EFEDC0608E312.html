Tenba Messenger Collection

The MESSENGER collection is all about speed, stealth and versatility. Get at your camera and laptop gear quickly, with bags and accessories that have nearly infinite uses, and do it all without anyone knowing that you have expensive equipment with you.

When building a bag to be used every day, there is no choice but to use the best materials and hardware. All Messenger bags are made with water-repellant, military-grade 1000 denier nylon, YKK® zippers, Duraflex® clips, steel D-rings and a waterproof bottom panel. That's what it takes to keep a bag looking good while carrying everything you need to a photo shoot or an office; close to home or on location.
