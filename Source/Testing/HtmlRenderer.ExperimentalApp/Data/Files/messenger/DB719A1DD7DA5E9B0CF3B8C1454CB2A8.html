Messenger Schedule Update & Other News

Messenger Schedule Update & Other News published on April 26, 2017Read more posts by the author of Messenger Schedule Update & Other News, BugbyteNo Comments on Messenger Schedule Update & Other News

Hey there! It’s been a while! Messenger has been kind of pushed to the back burner for longer than I’d expected, but I’m planning to return to regular updates on Saturday, May 6. Thanks for your patience!

As a side note: I’m currently offering Create-a-Chio commissions for Follower, which include customizing a Chio character which will later appear in the comic. If you’ve ever wanted to add your own character to the Messenger/Follower-verse, check out this page for more information! Note: These commissions are “officially” closing on May 3, however, I’m quietly leaving them open as long as it’s realistically still possible to complete one to add to the comic. Custom Chios will start appearing in a few weeks, so if you’re interested, get a note in ASAP!

And if you’d like to help me keep updating on a more regular schedule, you can also support me via commissions, Patreon, or Ko-Fi. The biggest hurdle between me and comics is time, and when you donate to/shop from/commission me, that’s time I don’t have to spend doing other freelance work and can make comics instead!

Thanks for reading, and see you in two weeks!

Schedule update! Guest art opportunity!

Schedule update! Guest art opportunity! published on July 17, 2016Read more posts by the author of Schedule update! Guest art opportunity!, BugbyteNo Comments on Schedule update! Guest art opportunity!

Hi there! Due to some unexpected real-life goings-on, Follower & Messenger have gotten a little off track! I sat down with my planning notes and a list of everything I need to finish for various upcoming deadlines and made a plan.

The short of it is, Messenger will resume regular updates on August 6, 2016.

The long version is, I have a host of deadlines for small (but exciting!) anthology projects coming up quickly, and I want to knock those out of the way so I can focus on regular comics again. Patreon reward art is a priority of course, and Patreon backers will also continue to get in-progress comic updates as I work through all this stuff.

In the meantime, if you’d like to contribute any kind of fan art or fiction in this unplanned break, I’d love to show it off here on the site. Link me to wherever you’ve posted it, or send me a note at: bug at messenger-comic dot com.

Thank you very much for your patience while I sort through all this stuff, and we’ll be back and better than ever in just a couple of weeks!
