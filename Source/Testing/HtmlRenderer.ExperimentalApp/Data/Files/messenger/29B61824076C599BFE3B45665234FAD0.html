We sell exclusively to Licensed Funeral Directors only.

Make it fast. Make it easy. Make it personal.

Messenger is pleased to introduce a completely new printing and design application, called Make It! Personal. This new application gives Funeral Homes the ability to easily create and print high quality, professional looking and highly personal memorial products within a matter of minutes!

In addition to the new application, Messenger has developed a whole new line of Make It! Personal coordinated products - including NEW Personal Photo Books and Video Slide Shows - that continue to feature our flagship artwork and designs while providing flexibility for the Funeral Home to create beautiful personal remembrances for families. Now, Funeral Homes have the best of both worlds – beautiful Messenger stationery product designs and the creative control they need to provide a meaningful, personal memorial service!
