We never forget notes in our backpack.

Kids are kids. They have enough to remember without all those class notes about field trips, homework assignments or requests for a dance chaperone.

Class Messenger makes it effortless for teachers to send home important notes and updates about the day’s learning experiences. They can even see exactly which parents have read each note. And whether via app or email, communication through Class Messenger is always private.

How does it work?

We help kids by keeping teachers and parents in sync.

Everyone wants the same thing - the best results for kids. The only problem is that everyone has the same limitation - not enough time.

Class Messenger is the only service available that enables a greater connectivity between teachers and parents, without asking for more time from either. In fact, the easy-to-use and versatile communication tools can actually reduce teacher workloads. And they keep more parents involved by requiring only the time it takes to glance at a phone or click a mouse.

We give parents a view into the classroom.

Class Messenger is a breath of fresh air for every parent who’s ever asked "What did you do at school today?" and been told, "Nothing much."

Now parents can effortlessly stay informed about the amazing moments and the challenges their kids face in the classroom each week. From triumphs at the science fair, to updates about upcoming tests, teachers can share text, photos and even video with individual parents or the whole class.

Class Messenger keeps you in sync from your computer, phone or tablet.
