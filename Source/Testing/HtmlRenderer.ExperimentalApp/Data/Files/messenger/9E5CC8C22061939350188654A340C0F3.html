2017 Underwriters

2017: Golf and Alpine Experience

The Messenger Cup two-day golf tournament will take place on the East and West courses at the five-star Broadmoor Resort in Colorado Springs. But for those of you who prefer non-golf adventures, we’ve added an Alpine track involving world-class zip lines and white water rafting in the Broadmoor’s backyard: the great Rockies.

Last year, the generosity and faith of our guests impacted millions of lives through leadership development and anti-trafficking initiatives. We can’t wait to see what will happen this year!

The Tournament

The Messenger Cup is a two-man scramble on the beautiful East and West Courses of The Broadmoor. After the first day is complete, several flights will be created to make sure everyone has a shot at first place. This tournament is for every type of golfer, from the PGA Tour pro to the amateur who simply enjoys chasing his little white ball through beautiful foliage. Let us know if you do not have a partner—we will be more than happy to set you up with one!

The Alpine Experience

Day 1: The Broadmoor Seven Falls Zip Line Tour

Begin your Alpine exploration in the canyons above The Broadmoor Seven Falls with a brand new zip line course and rappel that provide stunning views of the canyon hundreds of feet below. This spectacular (and safe) guided adventure ends at the foot of Seven Falls where you will enjoy a lunch at The Broadmoor’s Restaurant 1858.

Day 2: Royal Gorge White Water Rafting

The fun continues on day two with a white water rafting adventure on the Arkansas River. You’ll travel through The Royal Gorge, navigating a chasm that’s over a thousand feet deep. The ten-mile trek is world famous for its breathtaking scenery and thrilling rapids. All food and transportation will be arranged by us.

Day 1 or 2: The Broadmoor Spa

If the thought of flying through mountains or navigating fast-moving water makes you tense, then spend the day at The Broadmoor’s Five Star Spa. At the Spa you will be able to enjoy a service of your choice (arranged ahead of time by our team) and complimentary amenities including: gender-specific dry saunas, steam rooms, aromatherapy rooms, and fireplace lounges. Their co-ed Mountain View Room overlooks the lush golf course and offers space to enjoy quiet reflection and light refreshments.

The Broadmoor Resort is the longest-running consecutive winner of both the AAA Five-Diamond and Forbes Travel Guide Five-Star awards. The 5-star Colorado Springs resort is located on 3,000 lush acres under the shadow of Cheyenne Mountain and offers an award-winning day spa, fitness center, two swimming pools, three outdoor hot tubs, one lap pool; 54 holes of championship golf, six tennis courts with tennis camps; 24-hour room service, valet parking, and 24 specialty retail shops.
