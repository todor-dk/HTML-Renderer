Features

People Nearby

Find people around you. Select 'People Nearby' in the 'Users' screen. You must enable yor location services to be able to use this feature.

Find Users

Browse through all the users in B-Messenger Community. Online users are indicated by a blue arrow in the lists. You can also search users by name.

Video Chat

Broadcast your video or watch other user's video streams. Either in a public room or in a private chat. Enjoy the feeling of being together.

Free Calls

Make free voice calls via your internet connection. Save money when talking to your friends and beloved ones. You can use video, voice and text together.

Push Notifications

Get notified when someone sends message or calls you. Even when B-Messenger is not running on your device, our push notification service notifies you if you need.

Privacy

B-Messenger has several options where you can set or change permissions and notifications about videos, calls, messages and listing specifications.
