    Description

    IM+. One app, all your messaging. IM+ supports all major IM services, including Facebook, Google Talk, AOL/AIM/iChat, Yahoo!, ICQ, Vkontakte, Mail.Ru Agent, Odnoklassniki, Yandex chat, Mamba.Ru, Mig33, SINA Weibo, Renren, Fetion, Gadu-Gadu, MeinVZ and Jabber. IM+ features text messages and photo sending, supports group chats in AIM and ICQ, allows to add multiple accounts per service, lets you show your mood with personal status messages and customize application appearance, supports typing notifications and we're not done yet, new IM+ features will keep coming! Follow @implus on Twitter and Like www.facebook.com/plusim to get the latest updates from IM+ team! IM+ uses your personal data to login to your instant messaging accounts. You will be prompted to authorize IM+ to use your personal data upon app launch.

    Works... I guess.

    This client does work. It allows the user to feed all their chats (AIM, Yahoo, etc) into one handy login and screen, but it lacks some basic functions that other similar programs offer. 1. You cannot customize your font or windows. You can choose between provided themes, or change the font size, but you cannot change the themes, colours, or font face. 2. Functions like 'shift + enter' to break your line of text and create new lines of text do not work. Similarly ctrl + B, ctrl + I, etc, do not work to change the emphasis on your text. Really, if you don't care about these typing functions, and if you like how it looks, it's not a bad program. It DOES WORK! But for me, basic typing functions and some degree of customization are necessary to make a chat client worth using.
