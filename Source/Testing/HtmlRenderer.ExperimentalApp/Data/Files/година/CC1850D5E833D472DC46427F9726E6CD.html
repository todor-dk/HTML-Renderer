Boy Scout Troop 1488, Magnolia Texas

For over a century, Boy Scouts of America has helped boys (ages 11 to 18) develop leadership skills and friendships that carry beyond childhood.

At Troop 1488, we strive to continue that tradition and to go beyond! It is a BLAST (Boy Led Adult Supervised Troop).

Boy Scout Troop 1488 is in Magnolia, Texas, and is a participating member of the Orion District with the Sam Houston Area Council.  It is chartered (sponsored) by the Knights of Columbus Council 11866 at St. Matthias Catholic Church on 302 Magnolia Blvd.
