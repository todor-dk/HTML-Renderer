Games Vision

The Tokyo 1964 Games completely transformed Japan. The Tokyo 2020 Games, as the most innovative in history, will bring positive reform to the world by building on three core concepts:
"Striving for your personal best (Achieving Personal Best)"
"Accepting one another (Unity in Diversity)"
"Passing on Legacy for the future (Connecting to Tomorrow)"
