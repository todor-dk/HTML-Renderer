Reinventing TV with X1

The best way to experience Rio is on our X1 platform. For the first time, we have built a destination that will combine live television, online streaming and on-demand content, athlete profiles, and up-to-the-minute stats in one integrated NBC Olympics dashboard.

Every Minute. Every Screen.

The 2016 Rio Olympics will be streamed on NBCOlympics.com and the NBC Sports App. In addition, NBC Olympics will co-produce the NBC Olympics Discover channel on Snapchat with Buzzfeed, and NBC Olympics social partner for the Games. The channel will generate content that covers all aspects of the Games, co-produced by Buzzfeed's dedicated team on the ground.
