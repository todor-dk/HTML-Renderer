About Infinite Flight

Infinite Flight is a mobile flight simulator created by Flying Development Studios. It features realistic flight physics, multiple aircraft with a wide variety of liveries and upcoming features of global flight and satellite imagery.

The Olympics

The Olympics is a competition based on the community forum of Infinite Flight. It features multiple events testing teams of varying numbers. Most information on the Olympics can be found on the forum or on this website.
