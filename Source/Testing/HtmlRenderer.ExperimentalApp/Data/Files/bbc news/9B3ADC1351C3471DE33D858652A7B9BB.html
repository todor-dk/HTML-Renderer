City female hotelier caught up in raunchy WhatsApp post

Posted on Jul 3, 2017 58889 Views

By EDDY KAGERA

A hotelier in Nairobi has been caught up in a “Bro Ocholla” moment after she posted toe-curling details of her sex life in a WhatsApp group.

The group, Hospitality Marketers KEz, brings together professionals in the industry.

It seems everything was going on well with one person writing, “it shall be well and God is in control.”

The next person then posts: “On matters hospitality.”

SEXTING

The woman, who appears to have been sexting with another person, then sends a message detailing how she likes it between the sheets. We cannot name her for legal reasons.

PHOTO | COURTESY

*The message reads: “Rubbing your (obscenity) on my (obscenity) and licking it up then slowly sliding it back in.”*

Group members immediately react with astonishment forcing her to leave the group. A screenshot of the message has been shared countless times on social media.

She becomes one of many Kenyans who have sent embarrassing information to WhatsApp groups.
