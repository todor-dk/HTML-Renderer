About BBC World News

Broadcasting international 24-hour news and information channel across the globe

BBC World News is the leader in global breaking news, with journalists in more countries, we break stories from more places than any other international news broadcaster.

Reflecting the BBC’s commitment to global broadcasting, BBC World News delivers impartial, in-depth analysis of international news including sport, weather, business, current affairs and documentaries.
