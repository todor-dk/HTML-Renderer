ryanair (FR/RYR)

Ryanair was set up in 1985 with daily flights on a 15-seater Bandeirante aircraft. This operated daily from Waterford in the south east of Ireland to Gatwick, London. The airline's aim was to provide low cost flights to all passengers. The company quickly grew from a small airline providing short journey flights to one of Europe's largest carriers. In 1997 it went public and the money raised was used to expand the airline into a great value-for-money pan-European carrier. As one of Europe's most popular airlines, Ryanair flights operate daily from 86 bases connecting to over 200 destinations in 33 countries.
