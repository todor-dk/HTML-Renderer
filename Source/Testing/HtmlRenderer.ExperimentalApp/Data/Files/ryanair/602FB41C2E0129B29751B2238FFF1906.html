What to do in Porto?

As a former European Capital of Culture, Porto has plenty to offer with its elegant concert halls, art galleries and diverse calendar of exciting events. It is also an architecturally stunning city with many sightseeing spots. Travel to Porto today with Ryanair – Europe’s favourite airline!

Fantastic Variety in Porto

  • Enjoy one of the many exciting festivals in the city each year. St John's Festival (Festa de São João do Porto) presents street concerts and great food on the 23rd of June, lasting until the early hours of the morning.
  • Take a sightseeing trip through the city to experience some of its unique architectural sights. The House of Music (Casa da Musica) is one of the city’s world-renowned buildings and is well worth a visit.
  • Porto is home to many art galleries and public sculptures. The “She Changes” sculpture (known locally as “anémona") on the waterfront is the best known of its kind.

Regional Gastronomy

Porto proudly offers some of Portugal’s finest cuisine. Be sure to try the local tripas (tripe), which consists of beef stomach and white beans and is considered one of Porto’s signature dishes.
