Ryanair Ltd. (ISEQ: RYA, LSE: RYA, NASDAQ: RYAAY) is an Erse law-cost airline heidquartered in Swords, a suburb o Dublin, Ireland, wi its primar operational bases at Dublin an London Stansted Airports.
