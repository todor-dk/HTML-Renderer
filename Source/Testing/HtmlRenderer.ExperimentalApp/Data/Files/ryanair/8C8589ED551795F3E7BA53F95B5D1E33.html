EU261 Disruption Compensation
You may be eligible to claim monetary compensation if your flight was delayed more than 3 hours on arrival or cancelled within 14 days of departure. However, if the delay or cancellation was unexpected and therefore outside of our control (extraordinary circumstances) no monetary compensation is due under EU Regulation 261/2004. For validation please provide us with the last 4 digits of your credit/debit card number used to make your booking.
