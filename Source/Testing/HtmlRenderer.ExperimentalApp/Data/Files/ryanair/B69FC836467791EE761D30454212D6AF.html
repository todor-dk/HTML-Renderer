How To Save Time On Your Journey With Ryanair

They have an online check-in system and it's available from 5 days up to 4 hours before the scheduled flight is due to depart.

Terms and conditions of the Ryanair online check-in system

The online check-in system saves you time because you can choose your seat and print your boarding card from home or from your office. Also, when you get to the airport, you will be able to board ahead of other passengers who have not used the on-line system.

At Girona, Ryanair have a large presence. They fly there from all over Europe and offer some bargain deals. If you search 2 to 4 weeks in advance you are most likely to find the cheapest flights.

Flying To Girona With Ryanair If You Are Disabled

You have to advise Ryanair at the time you book your flight if you are disabled, visually impaired or blind. This is so they can take care of any arrangements you may need for special assistance.

At Girona airport you can't take a battery operated wheelchair right up to the plane. You have to check it in with the rest of your luggage and then the airport staff will take you to the plane in an airport wheelchair.
