Introduction to Ryanair

Ryanair founded by Tony Ryan is of Irish descent and is distinguished by its low prices. In addition, Ryanair reached the highest number of flights that arrived on time, compared to other European airlines.

When you fly with Ryanair you have to pay a fee for extras such as luggage, food and drinks. This gives you the certainty that you pay a low ticket price. Ryanair flies to over 194 destinations and offers 1600 flights on a daily basis. Can't wait to fly with Ryanair? Book your tickets now and fly to your favorite city!

Practical information

Before you start your journey with Ryanair, you must provide a valid ID and possibly a visa. If you need special assistance, you are able to arrange this up to 48 hours on the Ryanair website. The airport can only provide the requested service when you report at the special assistance desk at least 1 hour and 40 minutes before flight departure. Youngsters, hearing and visually impaired should travel with a companion that is over 16 years old. If you are pregnant and you are between 28-36 weeks, you can only travel when you have a Fit to Fly letter that confirms you are fit to fly.

What is the Ryanair hand luggage allowance?

You can bring two bags with you as cabin baggage. Bag number 1 must not exceed 55cm x 40cm x 20cm in size and 10kg in weight. This bag must also fit in the sizer and should easily fit in the overhead locker. Bag number 2 is a small bag e.g. handbag, laptop bag etc. which must not exceed 35cm x 20cm x 20cm and should easily fit under the seat in front of you.

What is the Ryanair checked luggage allowance?

Each passenger may check in up to three checked bags of either 15 kilos or 20 kilos which is decided at the time of the initial booking. After the booking is made, checked baggage can be added to a reservation via the My Booking facility up to 2 hours before the scheduled flight departure time. The checked baggage fees are charged per person/per one way flight. Prices can vary for checked luggage.

What time do you need to check-in for a Ryanair flight?

Check-in at Ryanair is quick and easy via the check-in counters at the airport, the self-service kiosks, via your computer or your phone. We recommend that you check-in baggage as early as possible. The Bag Drop desks open two hours before the scheduled flight departure time. The Bag Drop desks close strictly 40 minutes before the scheduled time of departure. The boarding gate opens 30 minutes before the scheduled flight departure time, and 20 minutes before the scheduled flight departure time. Passengers who arrive late will not be permitted to board the plane.
