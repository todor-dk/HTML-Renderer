Vin Diesel Shares Video from the xXx Set

xXx: Return of Xander CageMovie News samuel l. jackson Vin Diesel
By Silas Lesnick
ON April 6, 2016
SHARE

Vin Diesel and Samuel L. Jackson talk humble beginnings and taking on Harrison Ford in video from the new xXx set

With cameras now rolling on xXx: The Return of Xander Cage, leading man Vin Diesel is back on social media, taking fans behind the scenes on the new xXx set. In a video posted to his Facebook page, Diesel joins franchise costar Samuel L. Jackson who, in the new film, reprises his role of NSA Agent Augustus Gibbons. They talk about their humble beginnings and the path that has led them to become movie stars, while Jackson explains that his impetus for wanting to do the film is to once again overtake fellow Star Wars franchise star Harrison Ford at the box office. Check out the video, straight from the xXx set, in the player below and take a look at a new xXx set still, also posted by Diesel, in the gallery viewer at the bottom of this page.

The third explosive chapter of the blockbuster franchise that redefined the spy thriller finds extreme athlete turned government operative Xander Cage (Vin Diesel) coming out of self-imposed exile and on a collision course with deadly alpha warrior Xiang and his team in a race to recover a sinister and seemingly unstoppable weapon known as Pandora’s Box. Recruiting an all-new group of thrill-seeking cohorts, Xander finds himself enmeshed in a deadly conspiracy that points to collusion at the highest levels of world governments. Packed with the series’ signature deadpan wit and bad-ass attitude, xXx: The Return of Xander Cage aims to raise the bar on extreme action with some of the most mind-blowing stunts to ever be caught on film.

Joining Diesel and Jackson are Ruby Rose (Orange is the New Black), Donnie Yen (Rogue One: A Star Wars Story), Toni Collette (Krampus), Tony Jaa (Furious 7), Nina Dobrev (The Vampire Diaries) and 2015 Miss Universe contestant Ariadna Gutierrez.

D.J. Caruso (I Am Number Four) is directing xXx: The Return of Xander Cage. The film is written by Chad St. John (London Has Fallen) with current revisions by F. Scott Frazier (Collide). The threequel is getting a January 20, 2017 release from Paramount Pictures.
