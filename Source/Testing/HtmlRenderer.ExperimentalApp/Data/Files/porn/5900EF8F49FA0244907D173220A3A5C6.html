Intro

Right off the bat we can all recognize that Porn.com has an excellent domain name which is always nice to have. That being said it's still up to them to put together a package that would work well with that title and I think what they have is fitting. Porn.com is a big hardcore network that also functions as one big paysite. Content includes exclusive niche sites with video episodes, downloadable DVD movies, and photo galleries. Generally speaking paysites take an either/or approach in that they are either a network of exclusive video/reality sites or a DVD download sites. Here it's both plus they throw in photo galleries so there is a lot to see.


The Pros

  Huge Archive of Downloadable DVDs, Videos, & Pictures.

One of the big content items here is the DVD archive. They have dedicated section with thousands of titles in all sorts of different niches. It's the type of layout and collection you'd expect to see from a dedicated DVD download site. While the site is network with different individual niche sites when you login directly to Porn.com the DVD area functions more like one site or collection that's broken down by category instead of site. A dedicated Pictures section has a similar amount of photo sets and both the DVDs and picture galleries can be accessed thru the model index for members who prefer to search by performer.

  Lots of Exclusive Content Niche Sites with HD & 3D Videos.

One of the tabs on the navigation header is "Exclusives" which is a section of video/reality niches sites with original content. Dozens of sites are included with downloadable hardcore episodes hitting on all the popular niches like teen, mature, latina, solo toy, blowjobs, lesbian, etc. Newer additions her include HD level MP4 files at 4000k 1280x720. There is a 3D site in the mix too which does have a few dozen hardcore 3D videos. I used my old school glasses and the 3D worked just fine.

  Full-Scene Video Downloads & In-Browser Flash Streaming,

Content options do vary a bit which I'll get into later on below however I like the fundamentals solid and they're relatively consistent. Whether you're looking at the exclusive episodes or DVD scenes there are full-scene downloads in MP4, WMV, or XviD. Sometimes they have both MP4 and WMV and other times it's just one choice. In-browser Flash streaming buffers quickly and allows for immediate jumping ahead. For the picture galleries those can be viewed online or saved in zip files.

Other Good Stuff:

  • Updates are made daily and they seem to be adding material across the board to just about all the different content sections.
  • The design is sharp with clear exclusive, DVD, and pictures sections. There is also a pornstar model index, niche categories, and a keyword search.
  • Separate dedicated sections are available for Mobile and Gay content. These are on distinct tabs at the top of the member's area so it's easy to jump back and forth between Porn.com and either of those sections.
  • These guys have set up a special discount price for TBP users which makes the cost lower than their standard rate.

The Cons

  Video Quality & Options Vary, Some Movies in MP4 Only.

Along with the range of content from exclusive stuff to non-exclusive DVDs that go back years the video quality levels and options vary along with that. Many movies seem to have just the MP4 for downloading while others have WMV as well or sometimes instead of the MP4. The HD level movies in the exclusives section are just in MP4. Quality on the older DVDs is less impressive than the newer ones as some of these are from years back. Picture quality is only average at around 1024x683 for newer images and more like 800x600 back in the archives.

  Lots of Non-Exclusive Content, Some Exclusive Sites Not Updating.

The exclusive content section is just one part of the total member's area and the DVD section is a non-original collection of hardcore titles from various studios. Some of the exclusive network sites themselves are stagnant and not updating as they have moved on to producing content for their newer additions. The number of scenes on the exclusive sites varies quite a bit with some having just a handful of episodes and others with dozens and dozens.

Minor Negatives:

  • The login system is overly complex requiring four fields to be entered: username, password, PIN, and security code.
  • File size information that's posted on the movies is almost always wrong. This is just inherent to this comany as it's the same across the board on the individual network niche sites as well as this Porn.com archive.
  • There are some member's area upsells and ads. The navigation can also be a bit overwhelming as they have so much stuff crammed in here.

The other
  • Porn.com is a network incorporating all sorts of this company's niche sites but for the most part when you log in directly to Porn.com it acts more like one big site rather than a network of separate sites.
  • Members can post feedback and comments on the scenes. Depending on the shoot there can be a nice string of comments or none at all.

Bottom Line

A gigantic collection that includes exclusive network reality/video sites, DVD downloads, & photo galleries.

There is just a ton to see here as they have dedicated and full collections of both exclusive realty niche sites and a DVD download archive. Throw on top of that the photo galleries and mobile section and the site is pretty complete. Because things are so varied you do have to put up with different video quality levels and available formats but overall they've done a good job keeping things organized and relatively consistent with the set-up. Because there is such a big archive and the price is quite low they do deserve a good rating.
