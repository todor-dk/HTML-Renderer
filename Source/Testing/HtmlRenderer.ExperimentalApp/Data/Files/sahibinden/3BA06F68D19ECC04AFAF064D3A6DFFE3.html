Tweet 2004 Skoda Octavia 1.9 TDI-PD

The Skoda Octavia 1.9 TDI-PD is a front wheel drive motor car, with its engine positioned in the front, and a 4 door saloon body. The power is produced by a turbocharged engine of 1.9 litre capacity. This unit features single overhead camshaft valve gear, 4 cylinder layout, and 2 valves per cylinder. It develops 104 bhp (105 PS/78 kW) of power at 4000 rpm, and maximum torque of 250 N·m (184 lb·ft/25.5 kgm) at 1900 rpm. A 5 speed manual 'box transmits the power to the wheels. The claimed weight at the kerb is 1395 kg. Its maximum speed stated is 192 km/h (119 mph), officially quoted fuel consumption figures are 6.3/4.1/4.9 l/100km urban/extra-urban/combined, and carbon dioxide emissions are 132.0 g/km.
