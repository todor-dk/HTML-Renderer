The e-Okul Vbs is the official app published by the Turkish Ministry of Education for parents to monitor all aspects of their child’s education.

If you have a child that’s being education in Turkey e-Okul VBS is a MUST. This app will allow you to examine exam results, check absentee roles and find out just about everything you could ever want to about your child’s education from this app.

It’s not just about making sure your child is attending class though – it’s also about being more involved in your child’s schooling. e-Okul Vbs keeps you up to date with what books need to be read for which courses, important school announcements (holidays, closures, special tests, etc.) and more. If you want to understand your child’s curriculum better, this app is definitely for you.

It’s important to note that this app is for Turkey, so it’s in Turkish. There is no official English translation available; if you don’t speak Turkish you might want to skip this app or find a friend that can help you translate.

In conclusion, if you’re living in Turkey and want to keep up-to-date with your child’s education, you need e-Okul Vbs. Check out a quick list of pros and cons down below!

Pros

  • Official Application by Turkish Ministry of Education This is the official app and is promised by the government to be safe, secure and easy to use! If there are any problems you’ll be able to get help fast!
  • See Everything About Your Child’s Education You’ll be able to find out if they’re attending classes, see their grades, what the upcoming curriculum is and more.

Cons

  • Turkish Only This app is only in Turkish, not English, Chinese or Russian. If you need to use e-Okul Vbs you’ll need to find someone to help you translate.
