Berroco Modern Cotton™ DK

One size

FINISHED MEASUREMENTS
Approximately 60" long x 50" wide

YARN
BERROCO MODERN COTTON DK (100 grs): 6 hanks #6645 Point Judith (MC), 3 hanks #6611 Rocky Point (CC1), and 1 hank #6603 Piper (CC2)

NEEDLES and NOTIONS
48" and 29" circular needles, size 8 (5.00 mm) or size to obtain gauge
Optional – spare needle, size 8 (5.00 mm)

GAUGE
18 sts and 28 rows = 4" in Pat St

NOTE
Piece is worked flat – circular needle is used to accommodate large number of sts.
