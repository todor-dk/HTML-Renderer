The app for your writing class.

Designed by writing teachers, Marca offers everything you’ll need for your writing class in one friendly, easy-to-use place.

Sign Up

Leave rich feedback in a single click.

Give every student rich, detailed feedback—even links to handbooks, exercises, and more—with a single click. Marca comes with a great library of markup tags, and it’s quick and easy to add your own favorite comments, too.

Prep for your classes in record time.

Reusable content modules make teaching multiple sections of the same course (or even the same course from one semester to the next semester) a breeze. Share a class calendar or post documents, links, and announcements to multiple classes with ease.

Peer reviews, portfolios,
and more: the entire
writing process, built in.

Discuss topics in the Forum, write privately in the Journal, and gather your information in Notes. Peer Review and instructor Feedback tools make it fast and simple to leave great suggestions. Then, collect and show off your work in a beautiful Portfolio.
