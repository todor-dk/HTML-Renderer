  • Motif 1741 marble effect with surprising superimposition effects.
  • Textile Extra The research activities that have always characterised Marca Corona 1741 gave life to Textile Extra, a brand new collection of fine porcelain stoneware that skillfully blends textile patterns with concrete.
  • 4D 4D is a project based on structures or rather a collection of projects that suggests a new manner of interpreting a collection of ceramic tiles so that these can be matched with other materials.
  • Essences 1741 The warmth of wood gains geometric textures and powdery colours, with patterns of different sizes and threedimensional elements.
  • Essences Extra Essences Extra is a porcelain stoneware collection that is designed to resemble wood. A range of colours and wood types are available to match different furnishing styles. From Nordic to vintage 1950s looks and more contemporary offerings, the wood effects can give any setting a fresh, timeless look.
  • STREET In Street collection there are four concrete-inspired takes on porcelain stoneware. There are various tones and surfaces, offering maximum freedom for designers. The selection presents an unparalleled interpretation of the latest urban trends.
