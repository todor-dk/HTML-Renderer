Enroll in Our Cosmetology School near Hamilton

Marca is the only L’Oreal concept beauty school in Canada. Prepare for an exciting career in hairstyling and esthetics with Canada’s industry leaders centered around a bustling cityscape, focused on leading techniques, trends and unparalleled training. Partnerships with international leaders in the beauty industry, Pivot Point International, L’Oréal Professionnel & Matrix and Dermalogica, provides our students with a competitive education that is recognized worldwide.

Spring into a New Beauty Career with Our Cosmetology School in Hamilton and the Toronto Area

Cosmetology schools give you an advantage when pursuing a career in the hairdressing and beauty industry. In a top school, you gain crucial talents and experience that enlarge your resumé and launch you into a career. Take advantage of the success-building opportunities available to you in our cosmetology school.

At Marca College, you will find many of the opportunities you require to begin your new path. Marca College is the only L’Oréal-sponsored school in Canada and has partnerships with Dermalogica, Pivot International, and Matrix. Our partnerships give you enriching opportunities that open up your future. Build yourself a dynamic experience at our cosmetology school in Hamilton and Toronto.

Expand Your Skillset

Our exciting hairdressing and esthetics program will provide you with new abilities you can use immediately. Our creative learning experiences and renowned instructors show you how to make your job an art form. Take advantage of our course offerings ranging from thermal styling, hair removal, and massage to become an expert in your chosen field.

Gain Experience at Our Salon

Our campus locations offer professional salon treatments. As a student, you have a dynamic environment to put your new knowledge into practice. As a customer, you will enjoy professional styling at marked-down prices from our skilled students and talented supervisors.
