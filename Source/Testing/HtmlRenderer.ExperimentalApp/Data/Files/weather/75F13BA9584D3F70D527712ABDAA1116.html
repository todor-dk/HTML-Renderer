      Happy Wednesday!

      Headlines:

      -Wednesday: Hot and humid, few P.M. showers/storms possible

      -Thursday: Near 95, 30% chance for P.M. showers/storms

      -Friday: Highest rain chances with our next cold front

      Forecast:

      The heat and humidity will hang around through the end of the week. Find a way to stay cool and hydrated!

      We'll warm up for Wednesday with highs topping out near 94 this afternoon under mostly sunny skies.

      The heat index will be near 95 in Lynchburg and Roanoke, with heat index values at or above 100 in Southside.

      A few pop up showers and storms are possible this afternoon. Rain chances are only 20% so most remain dry.

      Were partly cloudy and mild overnight with lows near 71.

      Thursday will also be hot and humid. Highs hover around 94 with partly cloudy skies.

      Heat index values will range from 95 to over 100 (values over 100 especially on the Southside).

      Tomorrow brings a 30% chance for afternoon showers and storms.

      Friday brings our best chance for showers and thunderstorms as a cold front heads our way.

      Highs for Friday will still climb to around 90 in the afternoon.

      Looking ahead to the weekend, highs will remain in the upper 80s to near 90 with lows near 67. A few showers and storms are possible.


      Chief Meteorologist George Flickinger will have a full update at 5pm!
