Today's Forecast

  • 81°
  • 60°

Happy Hump Day, everyone!  This is Gary, with another one of those "pretty good, but not perfect" forecasts.

A weak front will be dropping southward through the area today.  For the most part, we are going to see a decent day with lots of sunshine, and it will be a bit less humid, too, especially the farther north you are.  But we still can't rule out a pop-up shower or thunderstorm, especially the farther south you are.

As that front starts to move back northward again tonight, we will get a better chance for showers throughout the day on Thursday, and possibly a few thunderstorms.  It will be a little cooler, too, on Thursday, especially north.

An area of low pressure riding along the front will bring another round of showers & thunderstorms on Friday, especially later in the day.

Then, over the weekend and into the start of next week, we will be right on the edge of another stalled-out front.  So, each day will feature some sunshine, but also the chance for showers.

And so, this showery weather pattern continues!  -Gary
