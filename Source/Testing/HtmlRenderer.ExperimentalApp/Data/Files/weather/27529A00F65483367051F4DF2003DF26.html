    Metro Detroit Weather: Watch for very heavy rain into Thursday

    Showers are moving toward us this Wednesday morning. Rain could be moderate at times until late this morning. That batch of rain will move out later this morning but will likely be followed by strong or severe storms this evening or tonight.

    Between storms today, the combination of heat and humidity will make it feel like its as hot as the mid 90s late today.

    OUTLOOK:

    Today: Storms with some moderate rain are possible in the morning and then storms should take off again late in the day or in the evening. Skies will turn partly sunny and it will be humid. There's a risk for severe weather after about 7 pm and through Wednesday night. Highs: 87-91 / Winds: SW 5-15

    Tonight: Thunderstorms likely, some with damaging wind, torrential rains and hail mostly ending by about 2am. A Flood Watch is in effect from 8 pm this evening until 11 am Thursday. Lows: 69-73 / Winds: SW 5-10

    Thursday: More clouds than sunshine with a 60% chance for showers and thunderstorms. It will be still be humid. Highs: 81-85 / Winds: SW 5-10
