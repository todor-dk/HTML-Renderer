Very warm and humid with a shower and a t-storm in the area this afternoon

Warm, humid and unsettled through Friday

  • Lauren Due

Posted: Jul 12, 2017 10:28 AM EDT

Updated: Jul 12, 2017 10:28 AM EDT

TODAY: Very warm and humid with some sunshine; a shower or t-storm in the area. High: 89

TONIGHT: Warm and muggy with patchy clouds; a stray shower or t-storm, mainly this evening. Low: 72

THURSDAY: Very hot and humid with some sun; a shower of t-storm around (some heavy t-storms). High: 93 Low: 71

***EXCESSIVE HEAT WARNING FOR THE GREATER PHILADELPHIA AREA FROM THURSDAY MORNING UNTIL THURSDAY EVENING.***

This warm, humid and unsettled pattern, typical for summertime, continues into this afternoon. Times of clouds and sun as high temperatures hit the upper 80s but with humidity really feeling like the 90s. A shower or t-storm in the area this afternoon into this evening. By tonight, just patchy clouds. Conditions stay warm and muggy overnight, low temperatures in the low 70s.

Tomorrow is very hot with widespread 90s expected and with the humidity some areas, especially the greater Philadelphia area, could feel close to 100 degrees. Otherwise, some sunshine with a shower or t-storm around. Some of these storms may be heavy with some gusty winds.

Friday’s showers and t-storms could also be heavy and gusty, so we’ll need to monitor where the localized flooding potential sets up. Otherwise, variable cloudiness with highs in the mid 80s and humid.

Right now, a drying pattern looks to be building in for the weekend. At this point, still can’t rule out a shower in spots Saturday morning. Then the rest of the weekend should turn out mostly sunny with highs in the mid 80s with less humidity.
