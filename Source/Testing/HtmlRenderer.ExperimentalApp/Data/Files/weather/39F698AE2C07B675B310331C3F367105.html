11 Breaking Weather Team Forecast Discussion

Wednesday, July 12th

Wednesday morning will be quiet, with storms developing early in the afternoon along the mountains. Those storms will then spread eastward across the Plains during the afternoon and evening. The ingredients are better Wednesday for storms to be on the stronger side. The main threats will be small hail, heavy rain, gusty winds and lightning. Temperatures will be slightly cooler in the 80s and 90s.

Storm chances are also expected Thursday and Friday and into the weekend but they will become less frequent through the weekend. With the increased rain chances we can expect cooler temperatures; highs will be in the 80s across Southern Colorado. It will be warm but not as hot.

We'll start to warm back up into the start of next week with highs in the upper 80s and mid 90s.
