KY3 Weather Forecast

Good morning…another warm and humid day is on tap and central Missouri will see the worst of it. We’ll be sunny early with clouds developing by late morning. An isolated storm or two is possible but most of us will be dry. Temperatures will be in the lower 90s, except a few middle 90s north of Springfield where the heat index may reach 105 here and there. Thursday looks similar but a cold front will approach from the north and we could see a few storms. Friday still looks like our best shot at rain, but it won’t be for everyone. Saturday we may see a few storms left over but another dry streak is in store by Sunday into much of next week as the upper ridge reasserts itself over the central US. Highs will be in the 90s for most of the forecast period.
