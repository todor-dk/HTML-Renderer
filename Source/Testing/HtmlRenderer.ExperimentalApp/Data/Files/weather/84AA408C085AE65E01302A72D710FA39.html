After the first 11 days of July, our average temperature at RDU is running about 1.1 degrees above normal. It's too soon to know whether the remainder of the month will leave us above, near or below normal overall for July, but it's interesting to note that June ended up a little on the short side of normal, though only by .8 degrees. What's more notable about that is that it was the first month to come in below normal at RDU in just over a year. May 2016 was the last previous month to do so, and even that was only a half-degree below. The twelve months in between were all above normal, some by just a little, but some by huge amounts from a climatological standpoint. January, February and April were 5.5, 8.8 and 6.3 degrees above normal, respectively!
