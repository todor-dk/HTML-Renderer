High heat and humidity will continue through the remainder of the work week, sending our heat index into the 100°+ range each afternoon through Friday. FORECAST:

Wednesday: Mostly sunny and hot with highs in the low to mid 90s. Heat index: 100-105. Rain chance: 10 to 20%.
