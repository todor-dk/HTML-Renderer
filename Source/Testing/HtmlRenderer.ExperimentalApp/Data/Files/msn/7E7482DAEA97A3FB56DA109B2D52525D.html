About World of Solitaire
World of Solitaire has over 50 solitaire games, including Spider, Klondike, FreeCell and Pyramid.
A 100% free online solitaire game with full undo support, multiple decks, statistics tracking, custom backgrounds and more.
Created with HTML5 and JavaScript, this online website works everywhere!

My name is Robert and I created World of Solitaire.
It started in 2007. My goal was to create a free, high quality, no hassle site to play solitaire on.
I was just hoping to have 30 happy players each month.
Today, over 250,000 different people come to the site every day!
It is absolutely thrilling to me to know that so many people enjoy what I have created.
I truly hope that you all continue to enjoy playing here for many years to come.
If anyone has any comments, questions, ideas or bugs please e-mail me: robert@telparia.com

Offline VersionThe offline version is several years old and is missing many games and features.

Some day I hope to create a proper installer so it is easier to use.
