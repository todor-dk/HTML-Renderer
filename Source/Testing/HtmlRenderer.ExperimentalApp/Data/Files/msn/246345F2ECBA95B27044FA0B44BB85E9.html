Empower Your Passions: Five Specializations for Your MSN

Earning your Master of Science in Nursing is more than a way to advance your career. It’s an opportunity for you to shine. You get to develop expertise in an area that interests you, choosing from five specializations, each in high demand. Our MSN program is CCNE-accredited, DEAC approved and 100% online, giving you the flexibility you need to balance work, school and home.

Specialize with Confidence

Our goal is to equip you with the latest knowledge and market-relevant expertise, no matter which specialty track you choose. You can count on American Sentinel to constantly update and enhance our master’s degree curriculum with direct insights from industry partners and leading healthcare organizations.

  • Case Management
  • Infection Prevention and Control
  • Nursing Education
  • Nursing Informatics
  • Nursing Management and Organizational Leadership

Tailor-made for Working Nurses Like You

American Sentinel is one of the few universities in the nation dedicated solely to educating working healthcare professionals online.

  • Monthly start dates
  • Course length: 8 weeks
  • Work at your own pace with no mandatory login times
  • Personal, one-on-one support: MySentinel® Student Success Advisor
  • Relevant curriculum, aligned with Quality and Safety Education for Nurses model
