TSA Pre✓Application Center - MSN Airport

Temporary Location Will Allow Onsite Enrollments July 17 - July 21, 2017

Enroll in the Transportation Security Administration’s popular TSA Pre✓® Application Program onsite at Dane County Regional July 17 - 21, where officials will host a temporary enrollment center.  Read more...

Frontier Adds Non-stop Service To Las Vegas

Beginning August 15, 2017, travelers from Madison will have three weekly nonstop flights to Las Vegas McCarran International Airport (LAS) year round. Flights will depart MSN on Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Sundays at 2:00 p.m. in the afternoon and arrive in Las Vegas at 3:42 p.m. Ticket options will be available for sale beginning May 4, 2017. www.flyfrontier.com

Airport Wins Excellence In Airport Concession Award

To menus featuring fresh, locally grown, sustainable fare from a nationally acclaimed chef, the result is an authentic Wisconsin experience that no other airport can replicate. The new concessions offer a memorable full-service "gastropub," a much-lauded local coffee roaster, Wisconsin-inspired shopping, and grab-and-go food from a grocer who has served the local area for nearly a century; this artful melding of diverse tastes and goods offers travelers a world-class experience with a uniquely Wisconsin flavor.  Read the press release.
