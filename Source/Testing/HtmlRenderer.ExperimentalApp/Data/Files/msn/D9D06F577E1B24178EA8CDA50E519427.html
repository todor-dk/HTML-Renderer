Installation applies to Internet Explorer, Firefox, Chrome and Safari.

To make MSN your default homepage, follow these simple steps.

  1. Click More actions (...) on the address bar.
  2. Click Settings.
  3. Under Open with, select A specific page or pages.
  4. Select MSN from the drop down menu.
Also make Bing my default search engine

To make Bing your default search engine, follow these simple steps.

  1. Click More actions (...) on the address bar.
  2. Click Settings.
  3. Scroll down and click View advanced settings.
  4. Under Search in the address bar with, select Bing.
