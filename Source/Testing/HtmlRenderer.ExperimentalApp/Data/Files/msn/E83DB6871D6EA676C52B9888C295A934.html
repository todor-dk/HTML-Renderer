                        Master of Science in Nursing (MSN) Programs

                        Home » Master of Science in Nursing (MSN) Programs

                        Expand your nursing career and salary options with a master’s degree

                        With Herzing University's online MSN programs, you can pursue the advanced degree that you need to advance your nursing career.  At Herzing we support your dream of seizing a leadership role in patient care, administration or education by providing an accredited and relevant online nursing master's program.

                        Program highlights

                        • Family Nurse Practitioner and Nursing Education concentrations
                        • CCNE* accredited program
                        • Flexible online scheduling to support your busy lifestyle
                        • Expert teaching faculty with extensive practical experience
                        • Earn your MSN in less than two years

                        Herzing’s MSN program is for licensed, registered nurses who hold a Bachelor of Science in Nursing.  

                        Choose the concentration that is right for you

                        • MSN - Family Nurse Practitioner
                        • MSN - Nursing Education

                        *The Master of Science in Nursing - Family Nurse Practitioner and the Master of Science in Nursing - Nursing Education programs at Herzing University Online are accredited by the Commission on Collegiate Nursing Education (CCNE, One Dupont Circle, NW, Suite 530, Washington DC 20036, 202-887-6791, www.aacn.nche.edu/ccne-accreditation).

                        Classes start August 7.
