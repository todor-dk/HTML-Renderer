How to make MSN your homepage on

Internet Explorer

  1. On the Tools menu, click Internet Options.
  2. On the General tab, type http://www.msn.ca in the Address text box, then click OK.

Internet Explorer

How to make MSN your homepage on

Firefox

  1. On the Tools menu, click Options.
  2. In the left pane of the Options dialog box, click Main.
  3. In the Home Page text box, type http://www.msn.ca, then click OK.

How to make MSN your homepage on

Mac Firefox

  1. Select Firefox from the top menu and then Preferences…from the drop-down list.
  2. In the left pane of the Preferences dialog box, click Main.
  3. In the Home Page text box, type http://www.msn.ca and then close the Preferences window.

How to make MSN your homepage on

Chrome

  1. Click on the Chrome “wrench” icon, located in the upper right hand corner of your browser window. When the drop-down menu appears, select the choice labeled Options.
  2. In the Home Page section, type http://www.msn.ca in the Open this page text box, then click Close.

How to make MSN your homepage on

Safari

  1. Select Safari from the top menu and then Preferences… from the drop-down list.
  2. In the General section, type http://www.msn.ca in the Home page text field, and then close the Preferences window.

How to make MSN your homepage on

Netscape

  1. On the Tools menu, click Options.
  2. In the Browser Options section, click General.
  3. In the Home Page section, type http://www.msn.ca in the Location(s) text box, then click OK.

How to make MSN your homepage on

Opera

  1. On the Tools menu, click Preferences.
  2. Select Start with home page in the Startup box.
  3. In the text box, type http://www.msn.ca, and then click OK.
