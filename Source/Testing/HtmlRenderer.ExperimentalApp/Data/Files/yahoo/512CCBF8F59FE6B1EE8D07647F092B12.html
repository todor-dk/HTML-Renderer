    Ad Interest Manager

    To make our ads more relevant and useful for you, we make educated guesses about your interests based on your activity on Yahoo’s sites and services. Some of the ads we show you reflect these interests. You can opt out of interest-based advertising, analysis of communications content for advertising purposes, and the sharing of your information with partners for data matching and appends using the tools on this page. Your choices within Ad Interest Manager do not affect interests you expressly declare to Yahoo elsewhere. Learn More.

    The opt-out for BrightRoll is managed separately.

    Your Advertising Choices

    Your Interest Categories

    We use information about many of the pages you have visited, ads you have seen and clicked, some of your searches on Yahoo, and your use of our services to create interest categories that help us choose the kinds of ads and content you’d like to see. You can edit or de-select categories or opt out of interest-based ads and other features altogether. See All Standard Categories

    Your Device & You

    We may customize some ads based on information sent to us by your device and/or information you provided during registration. Your opt-out does not apply to usage of this demographic or geographic information for targeting. This is how you appear to us.

    Your Other Devices

    We use login information to better serve you relevant and useful ads. These are the devices that Yahoo has seen you log in with previously, and the devices Yahoo will show ads that are tailored to your interests on. If you no longer expect to use these devices with Yahoo, please remove them from the list.

    Ad-Supported

    At Yahoo, we believe that our ads can be part of an exciting, immersive experience, and we empower our users to shape the ads they see to be as relevant as possible for them through our Ad Interest Manager and the Ad Feedback mechanism.
