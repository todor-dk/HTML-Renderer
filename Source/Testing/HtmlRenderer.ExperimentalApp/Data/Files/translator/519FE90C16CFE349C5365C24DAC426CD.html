Connecting the world since 1989.

LinguaLinx, Inc., a language translation provider recognized by Inc. Magazine as one of America’s fastest-growing companies, acquired Language Translation, Inc. (LTI) in September 2016. LTI is a California-based company, and its location will increase LinguaLinx’s west coast presence.

LinguaLinx’s book of business is roughly 60% long-term government contract and 40% corporate clientele. LTI’s portfolio is based on working with corporate clients; combining the two portfolios will lead to the creation of a balanced book of business. Additionally, the acquisition will provide positive growth for companies that rely on LTI for their translation services. LinguaLinx has developed a continuity plan for LTI’s current clients so that there are no service disruptions or pricing changes.

LinguaLinx, Inc. is a language service provider that offers document translation, website localization, multilingual layout / typesetting / graphic design, eLearning localization, interpreting, video voiceover, subtitling and cultural consulting services in over 200 languages. More information and case studies can be obtained by visiting lingualinx.com.

Telephone Interpreting

Telephone interpreting, also known as over-the-phone interpreting (OPI), is especially useful when the interpreter is needed for a short period of time, such as for a medical appointment or to communicate overseas by telephone.
