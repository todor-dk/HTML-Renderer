Who is LanguageLine Solutions?

With more than 35 years of experience, LanguageLine Solutions® is the trusted language services provider for more than 27,000 clients across the country.  We provide the highest quality phone, video, and onsite interpreting, document translation services and localization, as well as bilingual staff and interpreter testing and training.  We deliver the industry’s fastest and most dependable access to highly trained and professional linguists in more than 240 languages 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, facilitating more than 33 million interactions a year; that’s a new connection every second!
