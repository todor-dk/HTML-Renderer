Microsoft Translator User Forum for Businesses and Developers has moved

Do you have questions or feedback about the Microsoft Translator Speech or Text APIs, the Translator Hub, the CTF, or Microsoft Translator move to the Azure portal? You can now submit them to our new Translator UserVoice forum in Cognitive Services at aka.ms/TranslatorForum.

As always, our UserVoice forum lets you report any issues and get answers to your questions. You can also vote on new ideas to help guide our product features and developments, and stay up to date on new product features. 

If you posted a question or comment to our public forums, the question has been migrated to the new Cognitive Services UserVoice site.

For support with the Microsoft Translator apps for Windows, iOS, and Android; the Microsoft Translator Edge extension; Bing.com/translator; the Translator Web Widget; or other consumer products please click here.

You can read more about all Microsoft Translator product updates on our blog, or by following us on Twitter and Facebook.
