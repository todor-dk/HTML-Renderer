    • How much does a Translator make? The median annual Translator salary is $45,067, as of June 28, 2017, with a range usually between $39,754-$51,049, however this can vary widely depending on a variety of factors. Our team of Certified Compensation Professionals has analyzed survey data collected from thousands of HR departments at companies of all sizes and industries to present this range of annual salaries for people with the job title Translator in the United States.

This chart describes the expected percentage of people who perform the job of Translator in the United States that make less than that annual salary. For example the median expected annual pay for a typical Translator in the United States is $45,067, so 50% of the people who perform the job of Translator in the United States are expected to make less than $45,067.
