    Geoffrey Norman: Froth, The Politics Of Pointlessness

    The President of the United States has, of late, been in a mud wrestling match with a television news network. CNN, to be precise. The larger context is simple enough: the network and the president are enemies. And this is not a policy dispute where the president has taken a position on, say, immigration and the network opposes him. This fight is neither idealistic nor self-interested. It is primitive and instinctive. Like that thing that happens between a cobra and a mongoose.
