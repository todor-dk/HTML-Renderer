Man survives jump from Daytona’s Seabreeze Bridge

DAYTONA BEACH — A man attempted suicide Wednesday morning when he jumped from atop the Seabreeze Bridge, officials said. The unidentified man survived the plunge and firefighters used two personal watercraft borrowed from a nearby rental business to rescue him from the Halifax River about 10:45 a.m., said Sasha Staton, Daytona Beach Fire Department spokeswoman. The bridge's apex is about 60 feet above the Halifax. "When they got there he started swimming away from them like he didn't want to be helped," Staton said...
