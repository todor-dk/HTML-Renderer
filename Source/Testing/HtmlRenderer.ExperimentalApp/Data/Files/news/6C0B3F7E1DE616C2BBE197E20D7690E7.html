News

The Office of Communications is available to assist the news media with information to accurately report on issues involving Virginia's public education system, the Virginia Department of Education and the Virginia Board of Education.

Education reporters are encouraged to contact the director of communications or the communications manager to receive electronic news releases and media alerts.

Use the menu to the right to navigate sub-sections of this topic:

  • Educational Initiatives – Large-scale priority issues impacting statewide educational structure and vision.

  • Media Resources – Office of Communications contacts, glossary of educational terms and links to additional state education documents and resources useful for background research.

  • News Releases – Library of VDOE news releases and educational releases issued by the Governor’s Office.
