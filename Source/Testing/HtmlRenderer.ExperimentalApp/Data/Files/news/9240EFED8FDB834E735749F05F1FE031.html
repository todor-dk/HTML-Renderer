Myrtle Beach officially moves youth curfew earlier

Myrtle Beach’s earlier curfew is officially in effect, after city council passed it for a second and final time on Tuesday. The curfew affects all people under 17 starting at midnight, an hour earlier than the previous time. It includes multiple exceptions, but youth on public property at night will not longer be able to avoid a penalty by saying they were sent out on an errand by a parent.
