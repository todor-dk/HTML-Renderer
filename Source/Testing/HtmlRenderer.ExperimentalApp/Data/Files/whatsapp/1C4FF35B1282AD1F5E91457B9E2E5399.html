How do I get the WhatsApp Messenger app on my Samsung Galaxy device?

Last Update date : 2017.02.24

Downloading the Whatsapp app

 

Follow these steps to install the WhatsApp Messenger app on your Samsung Galaxy device.

 

At the moment, WhatsApp has only made the app available on devices running Android 2.1 or later, and devices that are able to receive messages or calls during the verification process.

 

WhatsApp does not officially support Wi-Fi only devices.

 

Please note: if you have trouble with an app we recommend contacting that app's developer. Third-party applications are not supported by Samsung.  You can see a list of FAQs from WhatsApp here.

 

Not quite what you were looking for? See the bottom of this article for a list of related and top content.

 

1. From the Home screen on your device touch Apps.

 

 

 

2. Touch Play Store.

 

 

 

3. Enter "WhatsApp" in the search bar at the top and then touch WhatsApp in the pop-up auto-suggest list.

 

 

 

 

 

 

4. Touch Install.

 

Note: you will need to have enough spare memory on your device. You can clear space on your device by deleting apps you don't use or by moving them to an SD card.

 

 

 

 

5. Touch Accept.

 

 

 

 

6. Touch Open once it has installed and the app will open, presenting you with the log in screen.

(You can either do this by pressing the pop-up notifying you that Whatsapp has installed, or it will now be available on the screen after you tap Apps)
