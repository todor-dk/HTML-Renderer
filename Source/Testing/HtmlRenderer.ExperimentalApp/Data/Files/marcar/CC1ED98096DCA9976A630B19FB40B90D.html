Marcar Steel & Engineering Ltd

Marcar Steel & Engineering Ltd act as exclusive agents and distributors representing some of Europe’s leading producers in their fields which cover a very diverse range of steel and engineering products. The industries into which we sell include materials handling, power generation, aerospace, automotive and transport.

Stainless steel products are supplied as precision strip to the spring steel industry, the deep drawing industry and stamping industry. Stainless Steel extrusions are supplied into the aerospace industry and are used in many other applications.

The Marcar Company is divided into specialist business units. In this way each unit is able to adapt precisely to the particular needs of the market segment that it services. For further information please click through the web links below.
