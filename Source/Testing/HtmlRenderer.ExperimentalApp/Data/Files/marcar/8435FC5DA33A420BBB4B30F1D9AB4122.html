Why Renewable Energy?

 
The power of nature has been harnassed by people since the creation of time. Renewable energy includes solar thermal, photovoltaic and wind.  Here at MarCar Solar we understand and appreciate the fact that our lives require energy.  We strive to help people gain access to responsible ways to the energy they need with the smallest impact on our enviroment.  It is our responsibility today to help preserve and protect our world for tomorrows children.    
 
 
 
 
 
 
 

Incentives
Energy Audits
 
In an effort to help lessen the financial burden, the government is offering incentives for the installation of renewable energy.  The average residential and commercial installation is eligible for a 30% tax credit, however, there are special programs that may be applicable in addition to this.  Visiting the incentives link will bring you to a listing of possiblities by state.  
 
In addition to federal incentives, there are also incentives offered by state.  If you have questions about incenctives, please email us at marcarsolar@yahoo.com.
