  The Tempo Omni Marker provides an improved method to electronically mark and locate underground facilities. Unlike other marking devices that use just a single coil, the Omni Marker contains three orthogonal tuned circuits. When excited by any standard marker locator, these passive circuits produce a uniform, spherical RF field in every direction. Because of their unique patented design, the Omni Marker offers benefits that are superior to any other electronic marking system.
   
  Product Features
- Electronically Marks and Relocates Buried Facilities
- Unique Design Produces a Uniform, spherical RF Field in Every Direction
- Easily Field with a Signal Peak Directly Above the Marker Provides Precise Location
- Wider Field with a Signal Peak Directly Above the Marker Provides Precise Location
- Requires No Particular Orientation When Buried
- Greater Range than Standard 4" Ball Markers
- High-Reliability Designs Lasts as Long as Your Buried Plant
- Color Coded by Utility Application
- Lifetime Limited Warranty
- Patented
