Amazon Company Information

Amazon.com Overview

Amazon.com is an e-commerce company that sells books, movies, electronics, clothing, home decor, and hundreds of other categories of consumer products from its website. The company sells products of its own, but it also facilitates consumer-to-consumer (C2C) transactions. It generated nearly $90 billion in revenue in 2014 and holds over $50 billion in assets. More than 222,000 professionals work for Amazon.com.

Amazon.com Job Opportunities

Many of the jobs through Amazon.com are based at one of the company's dozens of fulfillment centers. These warehouses serve as storage centers for Amazon merchandise as well as a hub from which the company sends out orders to customers. Fulfillment center employees spend most of the day on their feet and are responsible for a portion of the supply chain.

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), laborers and materials handlers can expect to earn a median hourly salary of just over $11. Material moving machine operators command slightly higher salaries, with a median wage of $15.16 per hour. These professionals operate forklifts, front end loaders, and other machinery.

Outside the fulfillment centers, Amazon.com jobs include positions in legal, accounting, human resources, sales, and logistics. The company also hires seasonal workers to keep up with the demand during the holidays. Every year, thousands of people flock to fulfillment centers to earn extra cash while they ship packages to anxious customers.

Amazon.com Work Culture

Professionals who are interested in Amazon.com jobs should know that the company values innovation, creativity, and passion. In fact, the company offers its fulfillment center workers a cash incentive to quit every year. If their employees want to do something else, Amazon.com wants to help make that happen.

Amazon.com employees should expect excellent benefits. Apart from part-time and seasonal workers, all employees have access to paid time off, insurance, retirement plans, and other perks.
