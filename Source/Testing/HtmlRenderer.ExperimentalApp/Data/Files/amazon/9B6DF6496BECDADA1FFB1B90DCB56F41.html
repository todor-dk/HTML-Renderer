WHO WE ARE

Amazon is all in on games. At Amazon Game Studios, we believe the evolution that began with arcade communities a quarter at a time, growing to the live streams and esports of today, will continue to a future where everyone is a gamer and every gamer can create, compete, collaborate and connect with others at massive scales. Our game teams in Seattle and Irvine are developing games that harness the power of AWS and Twitch to create bold, new game experiences.
