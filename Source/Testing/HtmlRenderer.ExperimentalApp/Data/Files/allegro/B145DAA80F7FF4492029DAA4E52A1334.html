About Us

Caffe Allegro is a rustic Italian restaurant that speaks tradition through the menu and the ambiance and beckons the true Italian food lover. Both of our locations are nestled in small historic downtown areas. Whether you are dining on our patio, or cozied up inside at one of our tables, you are surrounded by the old-world feel of another place and time. Caffe Allegro La Verne was opened in 1993, the creation of owner and chef, Alain Fournier. Twelve years ago a second Caffe Allegro was opened in Historic Downtown Upland. Caffe Allegro is an inviting place where those who love traditional Italian cuisine will find a home. Caffe Allegro has many options for dining in or for take-out. Our upland location accepts reservations by telephone only, and in La Verne we accept reservations only on major holidays. Please contact us if you have a large party. We also offer catering and we are eager to help you create the perfect menu options for your event. Please call for pricing and menu options, so we can create a menu that fits your specific needs.

Wine Selection

Caffe Allegro offers a wide variety of wines from a number of regions. Everyone can find something they will enjoy form our wine list. We have white wine selections that include chardonnay, pinot grigio, and sauvignon blanc. We also have an extensive list of red wines that include pinot noir, a variety of cabernets, and chianti, just to name a few. We also have some organic choices. Please see our wine menu for all of our red and white choices. Caffe Allegro also serves beer, your server can tell you our current domestic and import beer choices.
