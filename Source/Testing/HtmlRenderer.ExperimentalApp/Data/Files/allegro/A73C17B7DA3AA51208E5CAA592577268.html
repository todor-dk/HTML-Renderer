An Independent Full Service Investment Bank

Allegro Advisors is a leading investment bank that provides a complete range of financial advisory services to marquee of Indian corporates, financial institutions, family offices and professionals.

Founded in 2002 by a team of highly experienced finance professionals, Allegro leverages its in-depth domain expertise, well-seasoned experience and access to industry leaders and financial institutions to offer be-spoke & unbiased solutions across a full suite of services. It offers its clients global reach through partnerships with investment banks Petsky Prunier in the US and The Mountain Capital Group in China.
