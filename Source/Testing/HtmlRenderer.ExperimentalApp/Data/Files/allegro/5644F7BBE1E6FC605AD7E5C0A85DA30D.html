2017 Allegro

You’ve got places to go and sights to see. Bottom line: you’re going to be spending quality time in your Allegro. That’s why we loaded it with comforts to ensure you enjoy every mile. The Allegro has an impressive report card, surpassing industry average in design, reliability, quality, warranty, and parts support. Not to mention it’s our most affordable model. This year, we’ve added more floor plans and redesigned the exterior to give the coach a sleek modern look. Now in its 44th year of production, the 2017 Allegro is definitely our best one yet.
