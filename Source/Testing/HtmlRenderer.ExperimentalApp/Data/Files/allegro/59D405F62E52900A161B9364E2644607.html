Product Description

Medical curtains are flat with grommets at the heading spaced every 6". Select a curtain size at least 10% wider than your track size.

Two styles of medical curtain are available:

  • NO MESH (the curtain panel is solid fabric)
  • WITH MESH (with 20" of mesh at the heading of the curtain to meet fire code when fire sprinklers are present). Example: 84" high curtain = 64" fabric + 20" mesh.

Pattern ALLEGRO is 100% inherently flame resistant polyester, anti-microbial and is washable. Check out the Cubicle Curtain Care page for care instructions.

Click here for medical curtain measuring instructions.

Tie Band

  • A tie band is made from matching fabric.  It is flat with a grommet in the center and a Velcro tab at each end.  
  • Screw through the grommet to attach the tie band to the wall, wrap around the curtain and secure with the Velcro tabs.  
  • Click here to see a photo of a tie band.
