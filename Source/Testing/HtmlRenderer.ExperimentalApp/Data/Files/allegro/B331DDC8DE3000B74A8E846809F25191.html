Allegro Summer Academy 2017

Mon, 05/08/2017 - 13:21 — amasone

Academy is a training program geared toward high level players who want to prepare for their upcoming seasons. Unlike a summer camp, players work with coaches to develop an individual training plan based on their specific needs and goals. Each practice includes individual skill work as well as strength training/conditioning. Players meet 3 times per week. The player to coach ratio is kept very low (about 6 to 1).  The schedule is flexible in order to accomodate busy summer calendars. (We encourage players to train as many weeks as they can, but you only need to sign up for a week at a time.)

Body Sculpting: A high energy workout designed to shape and solve your problem areas!

Congratulations to Allegro 18 Black

Thu, 06/23/2016 - 13:06 — amasone
 
 
 
Allegro would like to wish our graduating seniors from 18 Black all the best next year and beyond! All eight girls will be attending college in the fall and they just wrapped up their high school career’s in amazing fashion! This season, Allegro 18 Black won 1st place at a GEVA tournament in March, finished the season ranked third overall in GEVA, and took fifth place overall at Penn State! Talk about a great season! Our eight departing seniors include:
  • Andie Ackerman (Rutgers)
  • Alyson Lawrence (Susquehanna)
  • Sarah Lynch (County College of Morris)
  • Kate Shirkey (Ramapo College)
  • Riley Krauss (Texas)
  • Janelle Rossi (Temple)
  • Kayleigh Duke (Dayton)
  • Amanda Morrison (Champlain College)
 
Congratulations on an awesome season and wonderful careers! Coach Carl, Coach Zach, & the rest of us at Allegro will miss you all dearly! Make sure you come back to visit us!
