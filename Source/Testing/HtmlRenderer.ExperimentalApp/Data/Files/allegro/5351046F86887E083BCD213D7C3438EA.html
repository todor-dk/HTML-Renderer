Four Seasons Hotel Prague welcomes you to CottoCrudo, a modern Italian restaurant, bar and lounge in Prague. Located by the Vltava River and entered via Veleslavinova Street, the chic urban setting showcases traditional and authentic Italian dishes with a modern touch.

For reservations:

  • Online: CottoCrudo website
  • Email: rezervace@cottocrudo.cz
  • Phone: +420 221 426 880

Weekend Brunch

CottoCrudo’s new weekend brunch begins October 1. Fill your plates with a Chef’s selection of prosciutto, salame and ricotta to start. Guests can choose from a selection of egg dishes including eggs Benedict with San Daniele, Prague ham or smoked salmon or and eggs Florentine with black truffle. Guests also have the option to choose a main course that changes weekly and offers a fish, meat and pasta option. Those with a sweet tooth can enjoy the sweet and hot tastes of either ricotta and lemon pancakes with cranberry compote or a Belgian waffle with maple syrup. Executive Chef Leonardo Di Clemente and his team will also be dreaming up a weekly sweet special. 

The brunch price includes a selection of fine teas and coffee for CZK 890 without a main course or CZK 1090 with a main course.

Culinary Adventure

Take a savoury journey through CottoCrudo Italian restaurant in Prague with visits to the raw bar, cheese cave and wine cellar, salami and prosciutto tower and the cocktail bar.

CottoCrudo

CottoCrudo is open daily for breakfast, lunch, cocktails and dinner, CottoCrudo offers 142 seats in a dining room, lounge and bar. 

  • Visit CottoCrudo's website

Authentic Italian

CottoCrudo's extensive menu of Italian and Mediterranean dishes includes fresh seafood, homemade pasta, choice meat cuts and artisan products from the Piedmont and Tuscany regions of Italy.

From an early-morning breakfast to a late-night nibble, our In-Room Dining menus offer tempting items, served in the privacy and comfort of your room or suite, at all hours.
