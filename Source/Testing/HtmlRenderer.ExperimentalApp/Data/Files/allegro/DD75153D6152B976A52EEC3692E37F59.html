About Us

Allegro Credit is a California-based enterprise providing retail financing throughout the United States to consumers of musical instruments, primarily pianos and keyboards. Allegro also offers patient financing through its AllWell program in select healthcare industries. Financing is offered through its more than 3000 participating dispensers/dealers under the trade names Allegro Acceptance, AllWell, Keyboard Acceptance (KAC) and Music Acceptance (MAC).

Allegro Credit prides itself on its fast, courteous and personal service to both its dispensers/dealers and finance customers. Credit authorizations are routinely handled either instantly on-line or in minutes by an authorizer, seven days a week. Payments to dealers for contracts purchased are always expedited.

Standard financing programs are very competitive; special programs are always available on request. Financing is based on fixed-amount installment payments which are particularly well-suited to the type of products being financed.

Allegro Credit is proud to be a wholly-owned division of Sherman, Clay & Co., one of the oldest and most respected names in the retail music business. The company was founded in San Francisco in 1870 and has offered retail financing since 1960.
