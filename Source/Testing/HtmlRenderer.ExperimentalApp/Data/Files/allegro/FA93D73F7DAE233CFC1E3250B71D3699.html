Please consider a donation.

We’ll use the money you donate during this period to fund school outreach programs for low income (Title 1) schools. Your money goes toward matching the funds we receive from the Fort Worth Arts Council and the Texas Commission for the Arts.

Annually we serve 15,000 – 20,000 children in their neighborhood schools. Our outreach programs expose students to classical music as well folk music from around the world. Our “Career Day” program encourages students to understand that a good education is the best way to earn the freedom to become anything they are willing to work for.

Any amount will be greatly appreciated and will go to a great cause.
