Welcome to Allegro Playacar

Allegro Playacar - All-Inclusive, Playa del Carmen, Riviera Maya

Situated on the best stretch of white sand beach in Playa del Carmen, Mexico is our Allegro Playacar Resort. A family  friendly all-inclusive hotel surrounded by lush tropical gardens looking out to the crystal clear waters of the Mexican Caribbean. This resort is also ideal for small leisure groups, family reunions and gatherings.

 

Hotel Features

  • Unlimited meals, snacks and beverages
  • 4 Restaurants
  • 4 bars and a Disco Bar
  • 3 swimming pools
  • 2 lit tennis courts and a basketball court
  • Land and non-motorized water sports including volleyball and scuba diving lessons
  • Kids Club with supervised activities
  • Day and night time entertainment schedule
  • Taxes and gratuities included

Things to do in Playa del Carmen

At the Occidental Allegro Playacar Hotel guests can enjoy a complete list of sports and activities during their stay.

Included activities

  • Basketball, volleyball, tennis and ping pong
  • Billiards and darts
  • Kayaking, snorkeling, body boarding, sailing and windsurfing
  • Nightly entertainment for adults and program of activities organized for children

Optional Activities

  • Scuba diving
  • Deep-sea fishing
  • 10% off spa treatments
  • Golf
  • Motorized water sports

All Inclusive Weddings at Occidental Allegro Playacar

With your head in the clouds and your toes in the sand, Allegro Playacar Resort offers the perfect backdrop for your very special day. With the breathtaking location the resort offers Riviera Maya weddings along the translucent Caribbean shores, all-inclusive amenities and personalized service, this is the perfect setting for a picturesque wedding occasion. The Allegro Playacar offers distinct locations on property for ceremonies and receptions, including a secluded garden sanctuary. Our professional wedding planning staff dazzles couples by designing Riviera Maya wedding packages that know no boundaries with customizations like adding mariachis, photography and unique delectable menus for extravagant receptions.
