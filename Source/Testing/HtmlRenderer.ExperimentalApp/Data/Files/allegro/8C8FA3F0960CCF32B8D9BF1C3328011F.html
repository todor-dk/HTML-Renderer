A Family Business Built Around Yours

Meet the family

Over the years, our family has had the pleasure of meeting many of our wonderful customers. They’re truly among the nicest folks in the world. So, if you’re thinking about buying a Tiffin coach, don’t be shy about asking our owners to tell you about their experiences. After all, no one knows our coaches better than the people who drive them. Of course, we’d love the chance to get to know you, too, so give us a call, visit one of our friendly dealers, or just stop by the office if you’re ever in Red Bay. Safe travels!
