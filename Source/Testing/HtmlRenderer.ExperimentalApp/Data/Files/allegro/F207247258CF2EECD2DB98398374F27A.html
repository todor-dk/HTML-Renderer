ALLEGRO BISTRO is located in the heart of
the Central Business District of New Orleans.
on the ground floor of the Energy Centre.  

Our Award winning Chef has created a menu which complements both the beauty
and complexity of our diverse city.  The best of classic New Orleans recipes have
 been thoughtfully prepared, with the best local ingredient, in order to create
an exciting collection of Creole inspired dishes in an upscale Art Deco atmosphere.
