      Most of my dance experience is with Latin dance, primarily salsa, tango and a bit of cha cha cha. The teachers at Allegro are amazingly accomplished dancers and teachers. Gary Johnson in particular teaches salsa like no other. Though he tends toward Cuban style he names his approach "open style," his goal being to have all different sorts of dancers be able to dance with each other. He begins at the real beginning, whever that is for an individual. There is no shaming, only kind correction.Big on improvisation, Gary demonstrates how a supposedly failed move can lead to another, perhaps even more interesting move. He teaches his students how to break rhythm through taps, exchange of accents, etc. The result looks amazing when an accomplished dancer manages it. Perhaps best, Gary has the lightest and clearest lead of any dance instructor I've ever worked with, Take a moment to think about the ramifications of that! Thank you Gary, and Viola too!

      Specialties

      Open daily, Allegro offers a full range of classes for all levels, taught by top-ranking instructors throughout the week. Classes include American and Int'l Ballroom, Salsa, Argentine Tango, Swing, Hustle, Nightclub, and other popular dances.  Please visit our website to view schedule.

      Weekends come alive with a variety of speailized weekend parties, and a number of special events are hosted throughout the year.

      Check our schedule for FREE programs:  
      - Saturday Afternoon Youth Dance Program for ages 10-18 yrs
      - Thursday & Sunday Tea Dance - Discount for Seniors

      Allegro is a great place for beginners to get started in a relaxed and friendly atmosphere.

      History

      Established in 1994.

      Allegro is owned and operated by Allegro Dancers, Inc. a non-profit, membership operated, public benefit and educational corporation [501(c)3]. Its mission is to serve the community by supporting and promoting the preservation, teaching and exhibition of social dance art forms commonly known under the general categories of Ballroom, Latin and Swing, as well as other popular forms of social dance including Salsa, Bachata and Kizomba.

      It is our members who make Allegro different from all other ballrooms. As a membership run facility, we rely on the commitment and support of the hundreds of members who come to Allegro to take classes, private lessons, practice, help out, participate in leadership, or simply hang out and chat. Allegro thrives on the creative energy that comes from this spirit of friendliness and volunteerism. Through on-going volunteer efforts we have raised and donated, to date, more than $252,000 to charitable causes.
