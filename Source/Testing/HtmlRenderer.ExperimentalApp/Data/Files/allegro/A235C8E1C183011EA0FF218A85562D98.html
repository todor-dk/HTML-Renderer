One of our top picks in Bern.

Located in the centre of Bern, with an exceptional view of the medieval Old Town and the impressive Alps, the 4-star superior Hotel Allegro Bern features a casino, 3 restaurants, 1 bar and 3 terraces. Free WiFi is available.

All rooms at the Hotel Allegro are of distinctive design and décor, and many offer panoramic views of Bern and the distant Alps.

The Gourmet Restaurant Meridiano is situated on the 6th floor and specializes in authentic European cuisine made from the finest natural ingredients. The chef is Jan Leimbach, whose culinary creations have earned 17 Gault-Millau points. The Meridiano also features a Michelin star.

The Restaurant Yù serves Asian gourmet specialities, while the Restaurant Giardino offers fine Italian and Mediterranean cuisine.

The allegro bar offers fine drinks and, in summer, a stylish lounge area with views of the pond. A smoker's lounge is also available.

Guests have free access to the fitness centre with cardio machines. Massages can be organised on request and for a surcharge.

At a surcharge, guests can rent bicycles and electric bicycles. Underground parking is available for a surcharge.

The Bern-Wankdorf motorway exit is 1.2 miles away, and the main train station is 5 minutes away on tram line 9, stopping directly in front of the hotel. Guests benefit from free public transport in Bern.

This property also has one of the top-rated locations in Bern! Guests are happier about it compared to other properties in the area.

Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 9 for a two-person trip.
