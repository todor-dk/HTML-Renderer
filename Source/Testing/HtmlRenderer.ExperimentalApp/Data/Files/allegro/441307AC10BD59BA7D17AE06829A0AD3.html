Allegro

Outside, the energy of Columbia Heights. Inside, the cool sophistication of the ultimate urban residence. Allegro’s stunning interiors and unique collection of amenities offer stylish sanctuaries that feel miles from the hustle of the city. Choose a studio, one-bedroom, two-bedroom or two-level penthouse loft home.

  • Located close to the Columbia Heights Metro and the U Street, DuPont Circle, Adams Morgan, Penn Quarter and Georgetown neighborhoods
  • The Lounge, featuring wireless connectivity, fireplace, club-inspired seating and modern décor
