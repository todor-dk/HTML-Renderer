Tiffin Motorhomes

Tiffin Motorhomes

When you buy a Tiffin motorhome, you become part of the family. The brand was founded by Bob Tiffin in 1972, and Bob and his sons still run the company today as a family business, with an unwavering focus on their extended family of Tiffin RV owners.

All RVers will find an open-door policy at the Tiffin Motorhomes plant, where customers are welcome to wander through the manufacturing and service areas to ask questions and meet the technicians. The company also actively solicits customer feedback at rallies and other events.

Tiffin Motorhomes produces nearly 30 different floor plan configurations, giving you plenty of options to choose from. You’ll find maximum space and optimal organization in a relaxing, elegant atmosphere, with commercial-grade countertops and solid hardwood cabinetry. Tiffin goes the extra mile for their customers.

Given the company’s long history of quality, innovation and customer care, it’s no wonder Tiffin Motorhomes is consistently honored with the DSI Quality Circle Award. Lazydays was founded upon the same family tradition as Tiffin Motorhomes. Both companies understand that a sale is just the beginning of a relationship, and both treat their customers as family – which is why Tiffin Motorhomes and Lazydays build customers for life.
