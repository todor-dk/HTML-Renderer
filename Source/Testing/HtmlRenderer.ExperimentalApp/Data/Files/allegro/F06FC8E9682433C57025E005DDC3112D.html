Who We Are

Welcome and thank you for visiting Allegro Pilates & Dance!

 

Allegro Pilates & Dance is a state-of-the-art equipped Pilates, CoreAlign®, and Ballet studio located in Medford, New Jersey, just off Route 70. We offer a wide variety of small group Pilates, fitness, and ballet classes for adults and children. We are the only studio in the area that offers Flexistretcher® conditioning training and Pilates-based training specifically geared towards youth, athletes, and dancers. At Allegro Pilates & Dance we care about your individual needs and customize your exercise program based on your specific goals. We will help you discover the world of movement that will nourish your body, elevate your spirit, and clear your mind!
