We cater to the following

Hotels & Casinos

State of the art fully automatic machines with built in grinders and automatic milk frothing system. Serve espresso, brewed coffee, cappuccino and cafe latte by pushing a button. Minimal training. Intended for many customers.

Restos & Cafes

Automatic and traditional espresso machines. We train baristas how to operate traditional machines to ensure that your customers get consistent quality coffee. We offer menu development and total coffee solutions.

 

Chain Restaurants

We have the largest chain accounts in the country. Our machines have a wide range of production capacities. Nationwide service, parts availability and monthly preventive maintenance give you peace of mind.

 

Home & Office

For those seeking the ultimate espresso experience for their personal use, we provide simple and easy to use machines. Enjoy a high quality espresso with freshly ground coffee beans. No experience required.
