About Allegro
Fast train Allegro is the easiest way to travel between Helsinki and Saint-Petersburg. Travel time is only 3.5 hours and all border formalities are carried out on the train. Allegro has 1st & 2nd class, a restaurant, children's play area, power sockets at seats - everything you may need for a comfortable trip.Read more about Allegro trains...Allegro Train Layout
