Classic bar, classic cocktails

171 W. Randolph, Chicago, IL 60601 ● 312.325.7120

Hotel Bars Dowtown Chicago

There’s no better way to unwind after a day on the town than with a sip and a snack at Bar Allegro. A classic American bar with a contemporary twist, we’re raising the bar on lobby lounging with bottled cocktails, chef-driven snacks, local spirits, an ample nightcap program, and wine on tap. Pull up a seat and relax at the bar or take your vittles to your room. Digestif or aperitif, we’ve got your thirsts covered.
