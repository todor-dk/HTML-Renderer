Our Mission

Allegro exists to Bring the Arts to Life. We believe the arts should be in every living room in our community. It is our desire to inspire our students and empower each of them to share their love of the arts with everyone they meet.
Learn More

Performances

Allegro is all about performance and sharing music: first, by teaching and training musicians; second, by providing opportunities to perform; and third, by encouraging interaction and community with other musicians in the Warrenton area.

Inclement Weather Policy

Morning classes (such as Early Childhood) will be cancelled in the event of a school delay or closing.

Afternoon & evening class cancellations will be determined and listed on the Allegro Facebook page (which can be viewed on our website or at https://www.facebook.com/Allegrocsa), through email, and on the phone system.

We do not follow the school system for normal weather cancellations.
