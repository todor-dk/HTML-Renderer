Why Dance at Allegro?

The advantages of dance are vast!  Physical exercise, flexibility, kinesthetic and cognitive awareness, musicality, self esteem, performance skills name just a few!  Combine these benefits with ASDM's culture of tradition and innovation and your student will soar.  

Students receive:
Positive Adult Role Models
Proven & Progressive Curriculum
Age Appropriate Music & Costumes
Peer Learning
