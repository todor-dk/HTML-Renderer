Allegro Resorts


Use the quote box at left to price Allegro hotels.

Allegro Resorts is part of Occidental Hotels & Resorts, founded in 1982. With locations in Mexico, Central America and the Caribbean, Allegro offers all-inclusive vacations in beautiful tropical settings. The Kids Club for guests ages 12 and under provides supervised daytime activities, making for an ideal family vacation. Guests at Allegro Resorts typically can choose from a variety of water sports and recreational activities. After a day spent at a sun-kissed beach and sparkling pool, dine at a choice of restaurants and mingle at the disco or lounge.
