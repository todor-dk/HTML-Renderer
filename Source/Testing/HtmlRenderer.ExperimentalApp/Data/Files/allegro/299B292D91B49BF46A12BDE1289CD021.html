Allegro II 14

Natural convection, energy savings and space saving ... all in one!

The Allegro II 14 is the lowest convection heater from the Allegro II line, Convectair’s best natural convection wall-mount heaters! This efficient electrical natural convection heater is the ideal choice for those seeking maximum heating comfort in their home without having much vertical space. Use less wall space, save on energy costs, and benefit from the comfort delivered by this wall-mount electrical heater.

Available in 750 to 2000W.
Only offered in white.

View price and dimensions

To know more about the Allegro II 14

For silent & consistent comfort

Natural convection heaters, such as the Allegro II 14, create a consistently comfortable ambiance in the room. Their heating elements are designed for exceptional heat conductivity.

This way, air circulates gently and continuously throughout the room, in perfect silence.

Its precision

Thanks to the Allegro II 14’s electronic backlit thermostat, which is ultraprecise to 1/5ºF (1/10ºC ), and its thermal sensor, located 4 in from the floor to detect the coldest air, you will thrive in a stable, precise and consistent environment.

Programming to suit your needs

Looking for a heater that will lower its temperature when you leave the house or office? The Allegro II 14 offers you many programming options, enabling you to use your heater in the most efficient and economical ways possible.


Comfort in complete safety

Our clients’ security is very important to us. The Allegro II 14’s design therefore features a grill that isn’t sharp, rounded corners and heating elements that are inaccessible to children’s fingers. Its thermostat is lockable and endowed with thermal protection in cases of overheating.

Convectair warranty

2 Years against all manufacturing defects.
5 Years on heating elements
For reliable and guaranteed installation, Convectair recommends consulting a certified electrician. For a listing of electricians recommended by Convectair, consult the “Where to buy” section.

Why do a calculation?

The Allegro II 14 is available in 750 to 2000W. Calculating the wattage of your heating needs is essential. Making the right choice will bring you optimal comfort. To determine the necessary wattage, go to the “Wattage calculation” section.

Technical informations

Heating element

Total capacity : 750 à 2000 W

Convection : X-shaped, monoblock element made of surface-treated extruded aluminum.

Thermal Protection : Manual reset overheat protection.

Temperature control

Electronic thermostat : Lockable built-in electronic proportional-action thermostat ultraprecise to 1/5ºF (1/10ºC ) with digital controls.

Programmer : Control panel designed to accept the 7392 Programmer module.

Control panel

Location : On top right hand side of the unit with lockable protective cover.

LCD screen with digital controls lockable by key combination.

Mode button with 4-settings:  Comfort; Economy (setback from 6.3°F / 3.5°C from your comfort settings); Frost protection (45°F / 7°C);

Program (automatic setback when connected to the Convectair Programmer).

« + and - » button : Set the temperature and lock the controls.

On/Off button.

Heating indicator light.


Cabinet

Steel construction with polyester/epoxy anti-rust powder coating and resistant to UV rays.

Cabinet : Bottom-mounted protective pan with rounded edges.

Louvres : Front-louvered air outlet that hides the unit interior and directs heat towards the centre of the room.

Color : FS = White

Installation

Installation : Quick-mounted surface installation with easy snap-on bracket that also serves as marking template.

Connection : 240v, flexible cord connection,including junction box cover. Installation requires a 2 x 3 wall box (not included).

Installation height : 4 in (10 cm).

Clearances : Front: 20 in (50 cm) - Sides: 6 in (15 cm) - Above: 4 in (10 cm).

Note : 3-conductor cable or 2-conductor cable plus a third wire should be used if unit is to be linked with the Convectair Programmer module (7392 FP).
