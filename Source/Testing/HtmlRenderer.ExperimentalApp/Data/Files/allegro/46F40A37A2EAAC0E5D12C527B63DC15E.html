Our Allegro choirs are available for community performances throughout our performance season. (October - June) Also, consider our "CHOIR FOR HIRE," Brillante. The 24-voice high school group serves as ambassadors within our surrounding metro area at private, corporate and community events of any size. Their repertoire includes:

1. National Anthem

2. 20-30 minute concert set with variety of audience appealing music to entertain all ages

3. 20-30 minute set of Holiday Music, favorite carols.

Performances are booked on a first come basis. Requesting a performance 3 months in advanced increases the possibility of securing a spot on our calendar, as we must take into account the current commitments of singers and families when scheduling additional performances.

A donation to the Allegro Scholarship Fund is appreciated.

Please submit the form below if interested in having one of our ensembles perform for your event. We will contact you.

Allegro's mission is to enrich the lives of Kansas City students through outstanding choral education & performance opportunities.

Allegro inspires its youth to envision and achieve success in all facets of their lives through music.

Allegro is committed to helping singers grow to their full potential in an energetic and motivating musical environment.

ABOUT ALLEGRO

Since its creation in 1999, Allegro has grown from one youth choir of 38 singers to over 250 singers in six ensembles. Today, our singers represent students in grades third through 12th. Drawn from across the Kansas City metro, our talented young singers come from private, public and home-schooled environments. Allegro Choirs delight audience members each year - locally, nationally and abroad - with its varied repertoire and heartfelt singing.

Our singers come from a variety of backgrounds but share this common value that every individual voice matters as the singers work together to achieve one sound. Our singers are proof that “lives are changed through song” as individual character, standards and friendships are molded for life.

Every individual on the Allegro staff believes passionately in Allegro’s standard of excellence, methods of instruction and the untapped potential of each singer who walks through the door. The Allegro staff has been hand-selected so that every member brings a level of excellence and utmost professionalism to their position combined with unbounded creativity, inspiration and a desire to teach.

Excellence is all that matters…  In life, in character and in song.

An appreciation of fine arts – Self Discipline – Teamwork – Self Expression – Confidence – Skill Mastery – An understanding of one’s self and how we relate to the world – Respect – Compassion – Commitment – Self Assessment – Emotional Maturity – Musicality – Understanding of Diversity – Community - Service – The importance of hard work – Self Esteem

 

Life values, friendships and a love of music that last a lifetime.

Inspiration within Allegro is contagious, and we strive to make a difference in our community and our world. Partnering with foundations such as HALO as well as providing special performances for those in need, not only transform our lives but positively impact those around us through service, soul and song.
