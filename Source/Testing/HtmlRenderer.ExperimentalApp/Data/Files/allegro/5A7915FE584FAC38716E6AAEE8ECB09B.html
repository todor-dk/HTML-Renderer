The Lagotto Invasion!! Flagstaff Kennel Club 2015.

conformation and Performance dogs

Tucson, Arizona

​

​After 25 yeas of exhibiting, trialing and breeding Rottweilers, we are off on a new adventure with the Lagotto Romagnolo. The lagotto is an Italian dog from the Romagna region of Northern Italy. Once a duck hunter, they are now the only dog breed in the world bred specifically to hunt for truffles. We are excited to be a part of introducing this rare breed into America, and full AKC recognition. We have been members of the Lagotto Romagnolo Club of America since 2008 and  since acquiring our first lagotto in 2009 have shown Lagotto in Italy, Switzerland, Germany, Sweden and Puerto Rico.
