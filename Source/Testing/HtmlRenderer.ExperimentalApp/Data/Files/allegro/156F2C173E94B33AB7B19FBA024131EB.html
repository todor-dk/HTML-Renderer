A leading provider of solid waste and recycling collection in the
metropolitan area specializing in commercial and industrial waste.

The mission of Allegro Sanitation Corporation of New Jersey and Accelerated Waste Systems of New York is to provide the highest quality customer service, solid waste collection and recycling within the commercial and industrial sector.

Allegro is committed to being green. Our goal is to keep as much residual waste as possible out of our landfills through the use of our state-of-the-art Materials Recovery Facility (MRF). We believe this will ensure that “Our Children’s Tomorrow” will have a cleaner and healthier environment.

We are committed to forming client relationships that will last a lifetime.
