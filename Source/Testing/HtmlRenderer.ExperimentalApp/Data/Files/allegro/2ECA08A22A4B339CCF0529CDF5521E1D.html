Radiolucent Imaging Tables

Allegro®

For Progressive Minimally Invasive Image-Guided Procedures

The Allegro® is an advanced imaging table with 2 speed proportional, 4-way motorized table top movement. It offers the surgeon precise control, superior imaging, optimum patient safety and excellent surgical access.

Product Features

– 2 speed proportional, 4-way motorized table top movement with full patient load at any angle
– Motorized table top movement ensures the user does not need to meet any physical requirements to travel the table top. There is no need to manually (float) push or pull the table top
– 2 speed Joystick Controller – mounts on either side of table for ease of use
– Flexibility in patient positioning- supine, prone and lateral
– Edge to edge radiolucency
– Excellent C-arm and O-arm® access
– High stability, allowing patient head orientation at either end with 450 lb. ( 205 kg.) patient weight capacity
– Electric floor locks
– AC or battery operation
– Hand Pendant can be connected to either the front or back of the pedestal of the table
– Hand Pendant controls Height, Lateral Tilt(roll) and Trendelenburg/Reverse Trendelenburg and Table Top Travel at a single speed, Return to Level, Top Home
– Tempur-Pedic® Medical table pad technology
– Swivel Mount for Hand Pendant – allows pendant to be mounted horizontally

 

O-arm® is a registered trademark of Medtronic, Inc.
