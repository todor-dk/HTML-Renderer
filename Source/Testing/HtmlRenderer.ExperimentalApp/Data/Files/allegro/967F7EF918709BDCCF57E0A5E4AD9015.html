Allegro Limited

Company Info

Allegro Limited is a full-service interior design firm specializing in merchandising. Allegro has more than 100 years of combined design experience with a customer-first approach that sets it apart from other interior design firms. Allegro has built its success on the personal referrals of well established clients by thoroughly understanding the needs of the customer. Allegro has never missed a deadline, never been over-budget, and always performs to meet the expectations of the client.

Carol M Flores, President of Allegro Limited, has been designing and merchandising residential and commercial properties since 1976. Her experience collectively incorporates the interior and exterior design of hotels, banks, strip centers, retail stores, condominium complexes, restaurants and business parks. Her experience also includes the merchandising of model homes for five major production homebuilders, 38 custom builders and personal homes for high-end homeowners. Additionally, she has retail experience in the sales of flooring, furniture, and window treatments. Carol’s professional experience in the business has won her recognition in major industry publications, television media, and more than 50 local and national design and merchandising awards.

Allegro is led by Courtney H Doty, Director of Operations and Designer. Courtney has been with Allegro since its inception. Her experience includes leading design teams from specification through installation. She has been responsible for the completion of over 800 projects.

Allegro has a full staff including designers, support staff, delivery and installation crews. We are well positioned to help you with any project in a completely flexible and mobile fashion.

Trust, experience and quality you can count on.
