OnCore Enterprise Research and Allegro CTMS are two very different systems. If you’re unsure which one would best address your needs, consider the typical Allegro and OnCore organization profiles:

An organization that uses OnCore typically:
  • Conducts 60 to 500+ active trials
  • Is an academic research center, cancer center, or health care system
  • Has need for functionality in two or more of the available modules:
    • Clinical Research Management
    • Billing Compliance
    • EDC
    • Biospecimen Management
    • Registries Management

Learn more about OnCore »

A site that uses Allegro typically:
  • Conducts 10-100 active trials
  • Is a dedicated research site or department, physician practice, or community hospital
  • Focuses on managing the operational data of their trials with tools common in most CTMS systems (calendars, financials, patient recruitment, visit tracking, etc.)
