Our Commitment

If you are a parent, beginner or player we stand ready to answer your questions and provide you with guidance in choosing a Violin, Viola, Cello or Upright Bass to meet your budget and performance needs. We personally select, set up and stand behind every instrument we sell. We also have the fairest violin rental programs in the Portland metropolitan area. Our shop provides an alternative to high priced specialty stores and internet drop-shipper nightmares. We are all about value. Value is defined as an excellent instrument set up and adjusted properly for inspiring performance at the best possible price.

All instruments come with a one year limited warranty*. All instruments come with a full value trade-up**. Feel free to call or email for bow, case and string upgrade options.
