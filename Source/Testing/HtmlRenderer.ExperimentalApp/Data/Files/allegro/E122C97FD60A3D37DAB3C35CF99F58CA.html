    Modern Downtown Style

    The glamour and pageantry of America’s Golden Era is alive and well at Kimpton Hotel Allegro. A historic gem in the heart of Chicago’s downtown Theatre District, surrounded by the Loop’s foremost sights like Grant Park and State Street’s shopping Mecca, this boutique haven boasts lavish theatrics of its own. One step through our welcoming doors will transport you back in time to an era of elegant hobnobbing and comfortable luxury.

    Highlights

    Meetings + Events

    If you’ve got a meeting or event, we’re ready to meet your needs. You and 10 to 400 of your cohorts. With 14,000 square feet of event space and customizable catering menus, we’ll be your new go-to for business or pleasure.

    Weddings

    Before saying “I do” at the altar, say it to our wedding event space. Celebrate that special day in style with a ceremony or reception with up to 220 guests. Italian-inspired food by 312 Chicago is icing on the wedding cake.

    Bar Allegro

    There's no better way to unwind after a day on the town than with a nice Manhattan. Or a craft beer. Or a glass of wine. Or one of each. Saunter up to our neighborly lobby bar and relax with a sip and a snack, like candied bacon, southern fried chicken, or oysters on the half shell. It's the downtown bar we all dream of; the kind of chic watering hole that makes you feel at home.

    Randolph Street

    Our central Loop location on Randolph Street makes Kimpton Hotel Allegro the perfect home base for a day of fun in downtown Chicago. Millennium Park, State Street, Grant Park, and the Chicago River, not to mention one of the best Theatre Districts in the country, are all just a hop, skip, and a jump away.
