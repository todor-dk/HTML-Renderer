        Margareten
        This former working class district blends hip eateries
        and avant-garde cinema with a time travel into 1950s
        Vienna. Feel the district’s pulse at Margaretenhof and
        Schlossquadrat. 19th-century Margaretenhof is a local
        icon, yet has very non-Viennese architecture with its
        brick structure and cast-iron verandas. The nearby
        urban Renaissance castle of Schlossquadrat houses
        several restaurants. In the summer you can sit in its
        ...More
        romantic shady courtyard over Schnitzel and Kracherl
        (traditional fizzy syrup). Siebenbrunnenplatz and its
        surrounding shops and grocery stores have remained
        virtually unchanged in the past 60 years: taking a
        walk there is like stepping into a 1950s film set.
        Speaking of movies, head to avant-garde cinema
        Filmcasino in the evening. It often stages foreign
        language cult films.

              From the airport I took a taxi booked from the Austrian Airlines website (RedCab or RedTaxi, I don't remember exactly :)) with one day in advance and paid with my credit card, it costs 29 euro, the taxi driver waited for us... More
              From the airport I took a taxi booked from the Austrian Airlines website (RedCab or RedTaxi, I don't remember exactly :)) with one day in advance and paid with my credit card, it costs 29 euro, the taxi driver waited for us on the passengers exit in the airport with my name written on a paper. The car was a Mercedes minivan so we travel in excellent conditions and we've been at the hotel in 15-20 minutes. When we left Vienna a friend told us to take the tram 1 to the Stephan Platz than subway to Wien Mite than a train to the airport. It was quite cheap around 10 euro for both of us but also it took around 1h. So if you travel with your family I recommend the taxi, it's faster and more convenient if you have kids and luggage :), if you are alone ir with your partner and travel light but on a budget you can use the public transportation system which is one of the best I've been used.
