                                            Allegro Playacar


                                            Email | Share |
                                            All Inclusive    

                                            Exceptional Value!
                                            Extra Value: Receive up to a $500 Resort Credit (booking dates, travel dates, duration, and usage restrictions may apply).

                                            Allegro Playacar Resort is situated on the best stretch of white sand beach in Playa del Carmen. This family friendly, all-inclusive resort is surrounded by lush, tropical gardens looking out to the crystal clear waters of the Mexican Caribbean. This resort is ideal for small groups, family reunions, and gatherings. Allegro Playacar Resort is located approximately 40 miles from Cancun International Airport.

                                            Features include unlimited meals, snacks, and beverages at four restaurants and four bars. Enjoy the disco, three swimming pools, tennis, basketball, and volleyball. Non-motorized watersports include scuba diving lessons. Young vacationers will love the Kids' Club, offering supervised activities away from mom and dad. After sunset, Allegro Playacar Resort's nightly entertainment includes local talent, staff shows, and a weekly Manager's Cocktail Party.

                                            HOTEL DETAILS
                                            The Allegro Resort is composed of charming thatch-roofed villas that combine an exotic Polynesian touch with the comforts of home. Set within a tranquil environment full of vegetation and Caribbean flavor, the resort offers 300 air-conditioned rooms. Because the villas are centralized in the midst of a very lush, tropical garden area, the guests are able to enjoy a laid back and relaxed atmosphere. Features include unlimited imported & domesticdrinks, nightly entertainment, and daily activities. Beach: The Allegro Playacar is situated on a long white sand beach with clear, calm waters, considered among the finest in the Caribbean. Check-in: 3:00 pm
                                            Check-out: 12:00 noon
                                            Total Rooms: 300
                                            Year Opened: 1993 Credit Cards: Visa, Master Card, American Express
                                            HOTEL RESTRICTIONS
