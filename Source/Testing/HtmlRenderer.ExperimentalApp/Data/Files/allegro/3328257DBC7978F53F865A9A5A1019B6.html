 Key Allegro Property Management

The Rockport area is one of the country’s most beautiful coastal communities. From a mild climate to pristine shorelines to verdant peninsulas to abundant wildlife, we enjoy an unparalleled charm and lifestyle.

Key Allegro Property Management is the company to turn to when it comes to renting or leasing property in the tri-county area.

Whether it’s a luxury home, vintage cottage, home site, condominium, or hotel for a vacation rental, Key Allegro Property Management is here to assist you.

We proudly serve the towns of Rockport, Fulton, Lamar, Holiday Beach, Aransas Pass, and neighborhoods including, but not limited to, Bahia Bay, Cape Velero, City by the Sea, Harbor Oaks, Key Allegro, Neptune Harbor, Oak Terrace, Peninsula Oaks, Rockport Country Club, Spanish Woods, and Boardwalk at St.Charles Bay.
