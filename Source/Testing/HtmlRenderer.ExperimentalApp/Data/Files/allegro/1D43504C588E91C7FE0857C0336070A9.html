Welcome to Allegro at Tanoan

You will find the ultimate in apartment home living at Allegro at Tanoan. Our private, gated luxury community reflects a tradition of elegant living and creates an atmosphere of serenity, enhanced by breathtaking views of the Sandia Mountains and lush landscaping. 

Comfort, convenience and style are just a few of the features you'll find at Allegro at Tanoan. Choose from one of our spaciously designed floor plans that will suit any lifestyle, and you'll experience a wide array of upgraded interior features that make everyday life easier. Our beautiful apartment homes are contemporary, yet comfortable.  Our community amenities include a sparkling pool, inviting spa, relaxing sauna, 24-hour state-of-the-art fitness center and much more.

Beyond the beautiful residences and scenic views, you'll find a vibrant neighborhood of shops, restaurants, top-rated schools and entertainment in Albuquerque’s Far Northeast Heights.  Discover hiking and biking trails just beyond your front door.

Whether you're an Albuquerque native or relocating to New Mexico, come home to Allegro at Tanoan today!
