WELCOME TO ALLEGRO INTERNATIONAL SERVICES, INC.
Allegro International Service, Inc., is based in Gardena, California. Established in May, 1991, we are a recognized international freight forwarder & NVOCC.
  • C-TPAT Certified
  • ACH Account in U.S. Customs
  • CNS Endorsed IATA Member
  • OTI Bonded NVOCC filed with FMC
  • AMS (Automated Manifest System) Equipped
  • ISF (Importer Security Filing) Compliance
We have been providing customers a comprehensive as well as price competitive service since 1991. Our professional staff includes specialists in all areas of cargo logistics. With Allegro, your shipments are closed at cargo source and the dedicated Allegro staff handles all cargo. Allegro is designed specifically to "One-Stop-Shopping" to simplify the delivery of cargo.
