Valentin Bachvarov

Valentin Bachvarov is an accomplished world-class professional latin and ballroom dancer and instructor with experience teaching, competing and performing in Europe, Asia and most recently, North America. He has experienced great success as a professional dancer and competitor .One of his most recent achievement is becoming 2008 US Professional National Latin Bronze Medalist. Currently Valentin works and coach professional couples and continues to complete with his Pro Am students in United States and Internationally. Born on December 29, 1980 in Burgas, Bulgaria he is 35 years old, and has been dancing for over 28 years.Professional North American Results
