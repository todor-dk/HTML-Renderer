Models

Affordable, Comfortable and Customizable


The Allegro is Tiffin’s flagship motorhome and an iconic favorite among RVers. Tiffin has a legendary reputation for quality and craftsmanship in every single motorhome they make. The Allegro comes in a variety of intuitive floor plans with features like easy-care tile flooring, energy efficient lighting, hand-crafted cabinets, leak-resistant roof and residential style living and dining areas. Allegro owners can also choose among many different exterior paint and design options.

WHY CHOOSE Allegro

  • Extraordinaire AC System

Manu-Facts:

A family business, built around yours.  Tiffin Motorhomes was founded by Bob Tiffin back in 1972, and since has manufactured and sold over 65,000 motorhomes.  With a simple mission of, "building lifetime relationships by doing our best to exceed the sales, service and overall ownership expectations of Tiffin Motorhomes owners and dealers", Tiffin has earned its reputation for having the best products and customer service in the industry.

Since day one, Tiffin has been a family business.  The company is filled with employees who have made working at Tiffin Motorhomes a family tradition.  The Tiffin family believes the company is more than just a business, its more like a calling.  A calling for their family to serve your family with good service and a product you can feel proud of driving down the road.

The innovations never end at Tiffin.  The company is always looking down the road for new ways to build more value into your Tiffin.  They have long been known for their impeccable customer service and have also been a leader in industry innovations introducing a number of groundbreaking products and processes.

Tiffin believes in building their products well, striving to make them better, backing each with good service and treating their customers the way they would want to be treated.
