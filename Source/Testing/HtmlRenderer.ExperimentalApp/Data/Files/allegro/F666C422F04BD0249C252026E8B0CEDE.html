Services

Allegro’s broad range and depth of experience provide the basis for sound business judgment. Our experience and related services include Fortune 1000 corporate real estate outsourcing, tenant representation, site selection, sale/leaseback analyses, economic incentive negotiation, and development consulting services.

The following components of our business model have consistently generated superior results for our clients, especially when contrasted against conventional commercial real estate companies and models.

  • Tenant Representation Services
  • Corporate Services
  • Strategic Advisory Services
  • Investment Services
  • Facilities Management Services
  • Executive-Level Counseling▾

    • The principals’ history of management consulting and experience advising corporate boards of directors uniquely positions Allegro to provide comprehensive and executable solutions and ensures that our team is capable of serving as a trusted advisor to business executives.

  • Fee Structures Aligned with Client Objectives▾

    • We understand the subtle, but important, difference between commission-based and service-based fees. As such, we align our compensation structures with our clients’ objectives to guarantee our services are delivered without conflicts of interest.

      Further, unlike our competitors, Allegro employs a salaried staffing model, which eliminates the internal conflicts inherent in commission-based firms. This allows Allegro to provide appropriate resources and assign the best team to address each client challenge. And it puts the client’s interest first.

  • Global Services through Managed Resources▾

    • Our independence from global firms empowers us to select the most appropriate vendors for each of our clients’ projects. As evidence of this, each year Allegro transacts real estate not only in all 50 states, but globally. Our independence from a national broker network is the key to engaging the most appropriate team in each market to address each client’s unique real estate challenge.
