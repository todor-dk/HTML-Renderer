Welcome to Allegro Dance Arts
 

The staff at Allegro is committed to dancers learning and growing through the dynamic and exciting world of dance. We continually strive for dancers to discover, develop, and share their talent and creativity. We work to find ways for our children to be nurtured by the dance family that is Allegro Dance Arts.

What makes us unique is our commitment to:

Individual Success

Each child has the opportunity to grow and learn; to have their life enriched by artistic expression, health, and accomplishment. We offer unique mentoring opportunities through our assistant teacher program and competition team.

Healthy Environment

Our dedicated staff and family of dancers meet in spacious, fully equipped studios designed to promote learning, safety, and health.

Support & Service

Our caring and efficient office staff is available during class hours, by phone 10 a.m. to 9 p.m. and always available by e-mail.

Curriculum of Merit

Each of our fully-trained and dedicated staff teaches from their unique professional experience, education, talent and skill base. Following our training time line, our teachers guide and inspire dancers to reach their goals and dreams.
