About

We offer tenant representation, corporate real estate services, consulting services, facilities management, and investment services. More importantly, we do it differently than anyone else. Our principals have more than a century of experience. We built a salary-based team to ensure everyone works in the client’s interest.

We chose to build a team composed of individuals with incredibly diverse backgrounds: a scientist, banker, attorney, and a broker to name a few. The team’s diverse backgrounds allow us to bring to the table a unique set of problem-solving skills, expertise, and knowledge of commercial real estate to solve any problem presented to us. We work collaboratively to identify how real estate can help achieve corporate goals.

We complete transactions in every major U.S. market each year. As well as select markets on five other continents.

Want to know how we can help achieve your objectives through real estate? Let’s talk today.

Company History

Allegro Real Estate Brokers & Advisors was founded in 2001, but our story begins in the early 1990s.

The company’s founding principals encountered a wide variety of real estate challenges while working together at prior companies. Through experiences at Big Four consulting firms and small corporate real estate outsourcing firms, they felt a better way to deliver great client service was possible. Each loved what they were doing, but not the way it was being done.

A spark turned into an idea.

An idea turned into a conversation.

Scores of conversations later, in Cleveland, Ohio, Allegro was formed. It was formed to provide a smarter way to help companies optimize the value of one of their largest assets and expenses — real estate.

Drawing on our team’s problem-solving experience, we have encountered and solved a wide spectrum of real estate challenges to address the needs of corporate owners of real estate, tenants/users of real estate, the public sector and non-profit organizations, and real estate investors.

Collectively, the principals’ working relationship and respect for each other’s vast knowledge, skills, and experience continue to provide the foundation for Allegro’s success and the ability for the company to effectively guide strategy, support decision making, and execute transactions consistent with the strategy.
