Fresh Seafood and Authentic Portuguese Cuisine

In Italian, allegro means happy. In our restaurant, casual elegance, luxurious appointments and delicious cuisine combine to make sure that you always leave us happy. Our executive chef prepares spectacular, daring, and tasty dishes served amidst an open kitchen in a Portuguese dining courtyard setting. When you walk through the doors into our dining piazza, you will notice our magnificent saltwater tank, a lovely outdoor patio surrounded by bamboo, palm trees and a luxurious waterfall. These exotic amenities place Allegro a cut above other Essex County restaurants. As soon as you experience our fine, hand-tooled décor and delectable cuisine, you will believe that we are without a doubt one of the prominent restaurants in Essex County.  

We are located in the IronBound section of Newark NJ.  Our dishes are made fresh to order including a wide selection of signature dishes!  Allegro Seafood Grill offers a full service bar which is open late. Call to inquire about catering and private parties. Patio tables are on a first come, first served basis.  Reserve a spot today to experience one of the most stellar restaurants Essex County has to offer.

For your convenience valet parking is available and reservations are highly recommended.
