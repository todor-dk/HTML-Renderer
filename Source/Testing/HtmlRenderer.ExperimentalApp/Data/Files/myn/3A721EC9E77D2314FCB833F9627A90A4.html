About our Publisher Program

In June 2012, myON launched the Publisher Program to expand our library of enhanced digital texts for learners from PreK through Grade 12. With over 60 publishers joining the program, the myON collection now exceeds 13,000 enhanced digital books from Capstone and our respected publishing partners -- and that number continues to grow as more titles are added on a regular basis.
