Elevator Pitch:

Founded in 2016 by Zora Tech, MYN is an innovative advertising platform that uniquely connects content creators, retailers and consumers to exactly what they want to see online.

With customers, media consumers and retailers now spending more time than ever before on their mobile phones, laptops and tablets, MYN makes it possible for consumers and viewers of content to easily identify what product they want to buy, opening up a catalogue of all available products in the video and taking them to a direct point of sale.

A revolutionary online advertising platform which helps;

- Retailers cut their cost of acquisition and target the right buyer to their products

- Content Creators, Influencers and YouTubers monetise their media creation and influence by up to 5x more than on an ordinary advertising platform

- Consumers, Customers and MYN Taggers find exactly what they're looking for without being bombarded by unrelated advertisements, and let them tag products to monetise their internet browsing.

MYN supercharges your customer connections and online sales, monetises your content and internet activity and establishes real time results and analytics.
