Your convenient connection to the
Froedtert & the Medical College of Wisconsin health network

Welcome to MyChart®, convenient and secure access to your electronic health record.

Your health is important to you — all of the time. That’s why Froedtert & the Medical College of Wisconsin health centers and hospitals offer MyChart. With this Internet connection to your doctor’s office, you can request, cancel and view appointments, see your health history, get test results and request prescription renewals from your provider online — any time!

Mobile MyChart

You can now download MyChart for iPhone® and MyChart for Android™. To download, simply visit the Android Market or the App Store on your mobile device and search for "MyChart."

CAUTION: Not for Urgent Medical Matters!

MyChart should never be used for urgent matters. The anticipated turnaround time for response to electronic messages is two business days. If you believe you are experiencing an urgent or life threatening health condition, please call your physicians office, go to an emergency room of a local hospital or dial 911.
