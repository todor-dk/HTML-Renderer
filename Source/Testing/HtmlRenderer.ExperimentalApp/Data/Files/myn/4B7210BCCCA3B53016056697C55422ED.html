Welcome to MyHBUHSD

The purpose of this tool is to provide parents and students with a single location to monitor class progress. Teachers have the option of using various programs to manage their assignments and grades. MyClasses was developed with the goal of gathering that data from the various programs and then presenting it to you in a convenient way, without requiring you to have multiple logins.
