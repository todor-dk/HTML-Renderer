Upcoming Repair Work at Penn Station Affecting LIRR, NJ Transit, and Amtrak Train Schedules
Written by Lyn S. Hill   
Friday, 07 July 2017

Amtrak will be performing emergency repair work at Penn Station from July 10 – September 1, 2017. It is estimated that approximately 20% of rush hour service on MTA Long Island Railroad, New Jersey Transit, Amtrak trains will be cancelled during this time.

We encourage NYP staff members who will be affected by the service changes to leave extra time for your commutes. In addition, due to the repair work, roads may be more crowded than usual, and staff may want to consider participating in the 511NY Rideshare program. Below are links to the Rideshare Program, as well as to railroad webpages for updated information.

Congratulations to our over 5,900 employees who completed the 2017 Steps Challenge!
Written by Catherine R. Treacy   
Tuesday, 27 June 2017

Congratulations to our over 5,900 employees who completed the 2017 Steps Challenge! Our NYP Family collectively walked more than 1.4 billion steps, which is equivalent to:

·         715,934 miles

·         Circling the Earth’s equator about 27 times

·         Walking from Manhattan to Los Angeles and back 128 times

·         Walking from the Earth to the Moon 3 times

We would like to recognize everyone who completed the Steps Challenge for their outstanding performance! Click steps_challenge_2017_prize_winners to see the employees randomly selected to receive prizes. If you see your name and have not received an email with instructions for receiving your prize, please email nypbehealthy@nyp.org.
