This is Tregiffian

Please visit our new site www.thecornishway.co.uk.

At the far west of Cornwall there are abundant rewards for those prepared to travel. Tregiffian sits above Gwenver beach, near Sennen, West Cornwall and for me it is the most beautiful part of the county. It is outstanding thanks to its rugged cliffs, secluded coves, dramatic beaches, wooded valleys and stunning views. Much of the area is designated as an Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty.

Brilliantly placed for you to enjoy the surrounding beaches and countryside is the ideal place to stay – Tregiffian.

From Easter 2012 we will be able to offer both Myn Tea and our other luxury self-catering holiday cottage New Forge.
