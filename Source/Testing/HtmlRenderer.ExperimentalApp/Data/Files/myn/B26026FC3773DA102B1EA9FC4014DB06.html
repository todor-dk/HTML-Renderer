 MYN Dividend History & Description — BlackRock MuniYield New York Quality Fund, Inc.

BlackRock MuniYield New York Quality Fund, Inc. is a non-diversified, closed-end management investment company. The Fund seeks to provide shareholders with as high a level of income exempt from Federal income tax and NY State and NY City personal income taxes as is consistent with its investment policies and prudent investment management by investing primarily in a portfolio of long-term municipal obligations the interest on which, to the issuer, is exempt from Federal income tax and New York State and New York City personal income taxes. As of July 31 2015, Co.'s investment portfolio totaled $903,540,585 and assets totaled $913,509,603.
