Healthcare at your Fingertips

Go paperless with MyChart, a secure online health connection. MyChart is an electronic medical record that provides easy access to your health care information from your computer or mobile device.

With MyChart, you can:

  • Request or cancel an appointment
  • Schedule an appointment with a primary care provider
  • See your past and upcoming appointment dates and times
  • See your test results
  • Request a prescription renewal
  • See your health information and medical records, including immunization records, allergies and past After Visit Summaries
  • Communicate easily with your care team and get your questions answered in a timely manner
  • Use eCheck-In at participating sites

eCheck-In now available at some clinics

Your clinic may use eCheck-In service allowing you to check in from home and complete visit questionnaires before your appointment. This allows for more valuable time with your care team. You'll receive a message to log in for eCheck-In instructions.

I want to sign up!

To use MyChart, you must receive a MyChart access code from your clinic. You may request this at your next appointment or call 855-513-5513 to ask a MyChart representative for an access code.
