A New Scheduling Option

March 30, 2017

In addition to the Self-Scheduling tool available through your My IU Health account, you now have another convenient way to schedule appointments online.
WELCOME TO MY IU HEALTH
x

My IU Health lets you self-schedule appointments with select providers, send messages to your care team, view lab results and pay a bill—online and on your time. It is secure and easy to use.

Watch the intro video to learn more about how My IU Health helps you conveniently manage your healthcare from your computer, tablet or mobile device, at home or on the go. Have a question about My IU Health? Get an instant answer with our Help Guide.

QUICK PAY

Sign In or Create an Account to see your current balance, ask questions, and manage your medical bills with My IU Health. To pay your medical bill without an account, use Quick Pay.
