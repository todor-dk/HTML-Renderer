Nabors and C & J Transaction Related Systems Changes

On May 26, 2015, all computer systems for Nabors Completions & Production Services (now known as C&J Well Services, Inc.) ("NCPS") and Nabors Production Services Ltd (now known as CJ Energy Production Services – Canada Ltd.) ("NPSL") will be separated and using their own systems under myCJES.com.

Attention to NCPS and NPSL Employees: You will no longer be allowed to use myNabors.com or other Nabors systems. Please use www.myCJES.com

Attention to Suppliers: Please see the additional information below:

  • If you provide services to both C&J (NCPS and NPSL) and Nabors companies, note that your transactions (POs and Invoices) have been split between myNabors and myCJES systems. You will only see relevant transactions in the corresponding systems. For C&J transactions, you must access www.myCJES.com and for Nabors transactions, you must access www.myNabors.com
  • If you provide services to only C&J (NCPS and NPSL), your access to myNabors.com has been disabled and transactions (POs and Invoices) have been moved to myCJES systems. You will be allowed to access www.myCJES.com
  • If you provide services to only Nabors (not to NCPS and NPSL), you will not have access to myCJES.com and you will continue to have access to www.myNabors.com and all your transactions (POs and Invoices) will be available
