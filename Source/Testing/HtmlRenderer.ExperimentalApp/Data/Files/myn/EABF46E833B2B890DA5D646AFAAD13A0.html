MyBrandman

Current Students: MyBrandman

The goal of MyBrandman is to provide easy access to the essential student resources you need to succeed. Explore the site and learn more about academic advising, disability services, our military community and more. The center section of MyBrandman features the most commonly used resources including:

  • Blackboard Tutorial
  • Career Services
  • Online Writing Community
  • Course Syllabi
  • Registration (Add/Drop Classes)
  • Financial Planning & Payment Tools
  • Technical Support

The Quick Links section on the top-right of the page provides access to many of the key online systems you’ll use throughout your academic career such as the virtual library, Blackboard and Self Service.

Logging In:

Full Website     Mobile Friendly

Depending on your web browser and computer preferences a variation of an authentication box will appear on your screen. Type in your username (make sure you include @brandman.edu) and password and click submit.

Having Trouble Accessing Your Account?

Visit our Account Management page. Or, contact the Brandman Service Desk at 949-341-9801 or toll-free at 855-553-3007 or help@brandman.edu.

To maintain the security and privacy of your student and academic records, keep your PIN/Password confidential. Never include your password in e-mail correspondence.
