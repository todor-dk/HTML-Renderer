Privacy Statement

Information collected on this page and within the Learning Management System (LMS) is collected under the authority of the Memorial University Act (RSNL 1990 Chapter M-7). The information you provide on the login page is used by Centre for Innovation in Teaching and Learning (CITL) to authenticate your login information through our secure system and provide access to the LMS. Information collected within the LMS will be used by instructors for communication or evaluation purposes unless otherwise noted. Usage of the LMS is governed by the University Appropriate Use of Computing Facilities policy. If you have any questions about the collection and use of this information, please contact Colleen Collett, Manager of Client Relations, by phone (709) 864-6940 or e-mail ccollett@mun.ca.
