Resources for Current Students

Summer Hours: During the summer semester, College services close at noon on Fridays.

MyTNCC

Sign in to access Blackboard, college email, and the Virginia Wizard. Click SIS to access your Student Center, which allows you to register for classes and contains your class schedule, the graduation application, your To Do List, and access to Quick Pay to pay your tuition.

Sign in to MyTNCC Mobile MyTNCC

Navigate

Sign in to start the College on-boarding process. Use the Major Exploration tool to connect your goals to the right program at Thomas Nelson. Use the Scheduler tool to build a class schedule around your responsibilities outside of class and then register for those classes in MyTNCC.

Sign in to Navigate

Announcements & Events

  • Annual Summer Opera Opens July 14

    Thomas Nelson Performing Arts will present their fifth annual summer opera: Iolanthe by W.S. Gilbert and Arthur Sullivan at the Hampton campus from July 14-16 & 21-23. Purchase tickets online.

  • Classes for Recent High School Grads

    Our fast five-week class session starting on June 27 is a smart way for you to get a head start on your college education. Don’t wait, register now.

  • Evening Classes in Newport News

    Take evening classes this fall in Newport News at our Southeast Higher Education Center.

  • Summer Classes

    Priority registration for current students starts February 27. Open registration starts March 6. Browse our class schedule.

  • Monthly Drawing

    Enter our monthly drawing for a chance to win a $25 gift card from the Thomas Nelson Bookstore. You may enter once per month.
