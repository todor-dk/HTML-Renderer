About MYN Magazine


In 2015 Arabian Wings introduced and launched the First Saudi Art Magazine “MYN”. It is a Visual Art Magazine that has been designed and printed according to the highest standards. Its content is centered on the term “Art Revolution Today”. MYN Magazine is meant to inspire and support art and build a bridge between different cultures and nations in order to bolster a Cultural Exchange. Art Revolution Today and Cultural Exchange, will give us a formula called MYN Magazine.

 

Read More ..

With the snap of a finger every second there is a new sensation that appears in the world of art. These revolutionary sensations that initiate researches. The Art fever has turned into a fervour giving life to a varied range of Art magazines and journals all over the globe.

Now Saudi Arabia will have its turn to bask the artistic sun. With the launch of the first Saudi Visual Art Magazine “MYN”, Arabian Wings has taken a leap into the forefront of cultural and visual art research with the cooperation of world renowned art lovers who have a flair for the business. It need not be mentioned that the revolutionary nature of such interaction. It will be an impetus to cultural growth through communication. Such communication brings nations together a global village where there is no room for hatred and prejudice.

MYN Magazine is an essential source of communication to understand other cultures and art. We can no longer cut ourselves off as we become more intertwined and interdependent. Art work has more influence than any military task forces or political delegations.

For centuries Art has been an excellent way of presenting culture, tradition, art, understanding of thinking and a rich form of inspiration from Modern to Contemporary art, which paints content and concept on canvas.

The articles of different writers are an excellent canvas of showcasing of different colors of MENA Culture exchange, sharing and Art Revolution Today as Visual arts and cultural exchange by facilitating readers with two languages Arabic and English.

The magazine offers 60% images and 40 % informative and research based articles as bridge for nations. It is a source of inspiration, satisfaction for art lovers, enhancing knowledge and skills and at the same time providing complete information.

It contains articles in Arabic about countries outside the MENA Region providing a deep picture of their culture, art work and artists; on the other hand, articles in English are full spectrum of knowledge and picture of Arab world for those, who don’t have any idea about the Arab countries.

Through this revolutionary magazine of art, architecture, history, art work and artists of the whole world will get exposure while reaching out to every nook and corner of artistic endeavors.
