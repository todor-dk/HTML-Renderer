Login to MyNM's MyChart to manage your health records and communicate with your doctor's office online. You can also use MyNM's MyChart to pay your bill. 

Attention Patient Connect Users: On June 7, 2015, we transitioned your patient portal from Patient Connect to 
MyNM's MyChart. Your Patient Connect account is now closed. You will need to sign up for MyNM's MyChart to access health information related to your hospital-based care, including your lab results and discharge instructions.
