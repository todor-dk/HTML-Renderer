Welcome to Suffolk County Community College

For Students:
At this secure site, you will be able to receive important announcements; access information such as your schedule, grades, course descriptions, holds and degree status; and perform many interactive functions, such as registration, transcript ordering, changing your information and performing degree audits.

For Faculty, Staff and Administrators:
MySCCC is the College's primary point of access for information and services pertaining to all your roles at Suffolk.

Hours of Operation:
MySCCC operates 24 hours by 7 days. Individual services within MySCCC may be occasionally unavailable for short periods of time between midnight and 7:00 a.m. for maintenance and upgrades.
Note: WebPay and Registration close at 10 p.m. each evening.


Browser Requirements:
Internet Explorer version 11 and higher are supported.
The latest versions of Firefox, Chrome, and Safari are all supported.
