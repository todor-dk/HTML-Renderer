MyBC3 Portal


Current Students

Actively using the portal through the semester is crucial to student success. Students can access the portal online or through the mobile app.

  • Username: b + student ID number.
  • Password: 8-digit date of birth the first time you login. You can change your password after logging in for the first time.
    • Setup the Portal Password Reset options to reset your own password if you forget it.

MyBC3 Portal Login   

Here's what you can do:

  • Check your student email.
  • Find classes. The best schedule is online!
  • Register for classes and drop/add.
    When you find the classes you want, you can add them to your cart. View your cart to drop classes.
  • View/print your schedule under the My Class Schedule menu.
  • Find your academic advisor. Go to My Profile to see your assigned advisor based on your program of study.
  • Keep up with assignments on Blackboard.
  • Pay your tuition and fees under Account Info/Pay Bill menu.
  • View financial aid/tuition balance under Account Info/Pay Bill menu.
  • Set up BC3 EZ Pay/EZ Refund under Account Info/Pay Bill menu.
  • Check midterm and final grades (when available).

Questions & Answers

What is my Student ID number?

For access to your student I.D. number, download and fill out the Student ID Request Form (PDF).

What is my password to access MyBC3 Student Portal?

Get password help. For first time users, please follow the login instructions above in Portal Services ONLY.

How do I get my transcript if I’m not a current student?

Students who have not attended BC3 for more than 2 years and would like a copy of their Academic History will need to request a transcript from the Records and Registration office.
