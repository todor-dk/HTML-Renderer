Announcements

Fall Classes start on Monday, August 28

If you have a hold on your account preventing you from registering for classes through MyNCTC, please follow up with the office and instructions specified in your Hold Statement. Returning students who have not taken classes at NCTC during the Fall 2016 or Spring 2017 semesters will need to re-apply for Admissions.

Any other issues related to online registration, please e-mail counseling@nctc.edu with the following information:

  • Your name and NCTC Student ID number (7 or 9 digit number/password) in the subject line
  • Your date of birth (if an ID number has not been assigned)
  • Picture of a photo ID attached to your e-mail
  • Description of registration problem you are having, or other questions
  • Exact course numbers, section numbers, and semester for which you are trying to enroll

 

Important Notices

Online Financial Aid system

For the 2016-2017 awards year, we converted to an online financial aid system.  You will be able to:

  • Check the status of your FAFSA Application
  • View a list of documents we've received from you and a list of the documents still outstanding 
  • View your financial aid awards 
  • View your student loan history from NSLDS
  • Check the status of your student loans for the current year.
