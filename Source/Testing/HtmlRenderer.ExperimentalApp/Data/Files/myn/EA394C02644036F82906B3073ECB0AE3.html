Product Description

Victoria & Albert Museum, London dated to the 15th century. The original of this 15th century ring is inscribed in old German 'myn genyst' (meaning 'my heart') around the outside of the ring with a decorative motif of birds and leaves. Available immediately in sterling silver or gold by special order. Whole sizes only 5-11. Band width measures 3/16th of an inch.
