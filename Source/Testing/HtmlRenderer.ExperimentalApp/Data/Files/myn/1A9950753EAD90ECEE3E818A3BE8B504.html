NAHT & You

As a NAHT Certified health care provider, you join a community of highly qualified healthcare professionals whose abilities and skills have demonstrated to be the best in its class. Becoming certified is the next step in demonstrating your level of excellence in the healthcare field.

NAHT Candidates

Healthcare professionals seeking to improve their qualification can do so by seeking certification. Holding an NAHT certification reflects a continuous effort towards professional growth and development.

NAHT Institute

For Healthcare Professionals

To fulfill the CE course requirements, certificants can complete NAHT CE courses, listen to archived NAHT webinars, complete NAHT specialty courses, or submit documentation of completion of CE courses from other providers. Outside CE courses are subjected to an approval process. Additional information on outside CE courses follows in this guide.

Recertification

NAHT Certificate holders are required to renew their certification every two years in order to remain active. Each member must complete 12 hours of continuing education and submit a renewal fee prior to expiration.
