MyChart

MyChart is your secure link to your personal medical record 24 hours a day. 

MyChart offers many features that allow you to conveniently:

  • Make an appointment
  • Send non-urgent medical messages to your physician
  • View test results
  • Renew prescriptions

Please note, not all physician’s offices offer access to MyChart. Please check with your physician’s office to see how you can access MyChart. MyChart is not to be used for urgent medical needs. For medical emergencies, dial 911. Patients who have been admitted to Covenant may also set up access to MyChart to view their Covenant medical records.

If you have login instructions from your physician's office or are a registered user, click here for MyChart. If you have an access code, you may also use this link to create your account.

For your convenience, we have provided the answers to MyChart Frequently Asked Questions, along with a MyChart User Manual.

Questions? Please contact your physician’s office or call the MyChart Patient Support Line at 989.583.0488, Monday through Friday during normal business hours.
