Welcome to MyNM’s MyChart, the online portal for Northwestern Medicine. With MyNM’s MyChart, you can use the internet to:
  • Send secure electronic messages to your doctor’s office
  • Access health information related to your care.
  • Request prescription refills.
  • Manage your appointments.
  • View your lab results, Past Visit Summaries, and Hospital Discharge Instructions
  • View statements and pay your bills.
