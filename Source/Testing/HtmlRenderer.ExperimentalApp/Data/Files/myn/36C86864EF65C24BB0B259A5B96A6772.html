Applicants

  • If you have applied to Trent, or have received an Offer of Admission, you can activate your MyTrent account here. You will need your Trent Identification Number provided in your application acknowledgement email. If you are unable to find your ID, you can retrieve it here OR please contact admissions@trentu.ca.

NEW - Alumni Access:

myCommunity: Read alumni news, update contact information and communication preferences, register for events, access online alumni directory, make donations and review your giving history.
and
myTrent: Access to @trentu.ca email, read university news, access academic records, request transcripts and more.
Only one Trent account login is required to access both myCommunity and myTrent. For new accounts or to re-activate your previous Trent account, go to the registration page. For password resets, visit the password retrieval page.
For further login assistance email mycommunity@trentu.ca or call 1-866-449-0072.

NEW - Donors and Community Members Access to:

myCommunity: Register for events, read news, update contact information and communication preferences, make donations, review giving history and more.
For Donors or Community members that are not current Trent students or alumni, you can request an account or reset your password by visiting the user registration page. For further login assistance email: mycommunity@trentu.ca or call 1-866-449-0072.
