Welcome to the NCWC Online Campus Portal

Instructions to Access the NCWC Online Campus Portal

MY.NCWC.EDU is your personalized campus web site at North Carolina Wesleyan College.

FOR ASSISTANCE, please contact the Helpdesk at 252-984-5000 or helpdesk@ncwc.edu. For walk in assistance on the Main Campus, visit the Information Systems Department, located in the Spruill Building.

To log in:

  • Go to http://my.ncwc.edu.
  • At the top of the page, find the username and password boxes. Enter your information in the appropriate box.
    • Your username is the first letter of your first and last names, followed by your student ID number. Example: John Doe with student ID 123456 would have the username JD123456.
    • Your initial password is your date of birth in the format MMDDYYYY.  Example: December 12, 1988 would be 12121988.
  • Click the log in button.
  • After logging in for the first time, you will be prompted to create a new password and set up hint questions for forgotten password retrieval.
    • If you are not prompted to create a new password, Immediately, click on the password management tool to change your password and set up your hint questions
  • You must use your NCWC Student email for password retrieval.

Instructions to Access NCWC Student Email

To access your email:

If you would like to use a phone or tablet to access your email, you MUST download the Outlook or Office 365 App from either the Play Store for Android or the App Store for Apple.

  • Once you have logged into my.ncwc.edu, you can access your college email account.
    • If you have just logged into my.ncwc.edu and changed your password and set up your hint questions, you will need to wait several minutes for that information to sync with Office 365.
  • On the left side of the page, click the “Student Email” link in the “Quick Links” section.  This will take you to your new email page, which is hosted by Microsoft Office 365.
  • You will log in using your full college email address (username@my.ncwc.edu).
    • Your username is the first letter of your first and last names, followed by your student ID number. Example: John Doe with student ID 123456 would have the username JD123456 and the email address JD123456@my.ncwc.edu.
    • Your password will be the same as the one you use to login to my.ncwc.edu
  • When you login for the first time, click on the "Mail" icon in the App List.
    • Select your preferred Language (English is the default) and Time Zone (NCWC is located in the Eastern Time Zone).
    • Click on save and the next screen should be your student email Inbox.
  • Please browse thru the Apps and take full advantage of your Student email account powered by Office 365.
