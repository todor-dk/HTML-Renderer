Detailed Analysis

By Frank B.

Minecraft is a really famous game that is joins the contruction games, with platforms and accion and which puts you creative capacity to the limits to create your own world. In Minecraft you are in front of a world that you need to build and its evolution only depends on you. You will have to collect all the available resources to build and survive day after day, as durin the nights, you will have to face monsters like Creepers.

The graphic aspect of the game is quite simple, but it is a good chractertistic of the game. All is created from blocks: bridges, rivers, mountains, buildings, forests and the materials with which you are going to build your wolrd will also be cubes. If the "retro" aspect of the game is surprising, it is worth to look in the web for texture package that you like with which you will be able to change easily the aspect of the game and its graphics.

According to your preferences, you will be able to play in the "survival mode" and defend yourself against a hostile world or to the "creative" mode, where you will be able to fly and have any object available to make the most amazing constructions. Minecraft is a game that at the beginning seems simple to play, but it offers great possibilites as you go further in it. Thanks to the multiplayer mode, you will also be able to play with your friends using its servers.

With Minecraft you are going to have several games in just one and, above all, freedom to choose how you    want to have fun. Pay attention to the game, as there are frequently new features added thanks to the updates it has.

Requirements

- Needs a 1.3 Ghz processor
- Needs a 2 Gb RAM Memory
- Need a graphic card of at least 256 Mb.
- You will also need free space available in the disk of 90 Mb.
- Register in web de Minecraft (the process is completely free).
- Java Runtime Environment 6 or later.

Restrictions

- You need to have a Premium account, which is a payment account. If not, you will be able to play only for five days for each world created.
