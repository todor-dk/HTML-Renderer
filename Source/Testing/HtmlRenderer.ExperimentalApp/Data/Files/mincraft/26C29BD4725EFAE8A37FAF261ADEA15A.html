What's Happening With My Account?

You now have the option to merge your Minecraft Forum and Twitch accounts! If you decide to merge accounts, you’ll be able to log in to to Minecraft Forum with your Twitch username and password.

  • Your Minecraft Forum display name will stay the same

  • You will not lose any of your forum posts, site activity, or account information

  • Merging accounts is completely optional
