Welcome to PaperMinecraft!

Create Minecraft papercraft with just a scissor, paper and glue! Now you can easily print your skins, blocks, mobs and much more. If you have printed enough blocks you can start building. Play Minecraft like never before and share some pictures in the comment section below or look at other people's creations!
The menu will show up if you hover over the logo at the top. Don't worry about printing. Everything is downloadable as PDF and fits on your paper. Have fun!
