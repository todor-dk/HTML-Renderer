What are Minecraft Games?

Minecraft games are based on the blockbuster (no pun intended) hit, Minecraft, a game created by Notch (real name Markus Persson). The gameplay can be different from actual Minecraft, but the graphics are pretty much the same. The point of these games is to take the classic we all know and love and add something fun and refreshing.
