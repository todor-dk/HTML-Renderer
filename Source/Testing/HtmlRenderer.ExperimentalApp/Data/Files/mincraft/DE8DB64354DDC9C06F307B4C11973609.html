Awaken Your Inner Hero

Adventure games are everything Bilbo Baggins would have ever wanted: all the fun of an epic journey without any of the nasty inconveniences of leaving home. When you play adventure games online, you can live out the life of a hero from another universe on your way home from work or school. 

Chart out new worlds in the Troll Tale, an adventure game for the Age of the Internet. Or create your own in Level Editor: The Game. You can explore the dark side (with a sword) in the platform runner Dark Lands. Take out alien invaders in Earth Taken. Or maybe your adventure is running a business? Try your hand at running a burger joint in Burger Restaurant 4 or at building a sushi empire in Youda Sushi Chef, to name a couple.

More into visual exploration? We have tons of point-and-click adventure games, like the Arabian fairytale Sinbad's Journey or the spooky Vortex Point series. Delve into beautiful imagery in whatever setting you feel like immersing yourself in. Or find your way out of a secure spot in Escape games like Dakota Winchester's Adventures Part 3, where you have to help the explorer get out of the basement of an abandoned fort.

Endless quests await (hundreds of them, at least). If you were looking to avoid fun, you are past the point of no return. You're just a click away from everything you ever wanted to be and will never get to do in the real world. The adventure begins here.
