Minecraft Toys! Can You Dig It?

Minecraft is a 3D video game that enables players to build their own creations out of cubes, plus craft, explore, gather resources, and battle in a world of open-ended choices. The brainchild of Swedish game designer Markus "Notch" Persson, and further developed and published by Mojang, Minecraft has generated collectibles by the ton, along with spinoff games Minecraft: Story Mode and Minecraft: Education Edition, and the 2019 film Minecraft: The Movie.

First released on May 17, 2009, the game has boomed to become one of the most popular video games in history. This exploding enthusiasm has resulted in a vast array of Minecraft merchandise that runs the gamut from LEGO sets, card games, masks, and role-play weapons and toys, to key chains, wallets, books, jewelry, and believe it or not, cookie cutters! Thankfully, there is no shortage of these awesome items to choose from at Entertainment Earth, including those cute and cuddly Minecraft plush that are so much in demand by gamers today.
