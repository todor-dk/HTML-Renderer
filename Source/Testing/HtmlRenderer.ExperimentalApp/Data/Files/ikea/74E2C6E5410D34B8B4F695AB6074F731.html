IKEA® Delivery

We deliver IKEA® furniture from the store in Charlotte to the greater Raleigh area to save you time and money. All you need to do is create your shopping list on IKEA®’s website and then come back here to order through us. We run to the Charlotte IKEA® every week.

What does it cost to drive to IKEA® yourself?

The roundtrip from the Triangle to IKEA® Charlotte takes 5-6 hours of driving time across 300 miles and around $30-$60 in gas.

  • Time cost: 5-6 hours
    • 300 miles
  • Gas cost: $30-$60

We can get products they only sell at the store

Many of IKEA®’s products are not sold online and only available at the store. We can pick up any item they sell.
