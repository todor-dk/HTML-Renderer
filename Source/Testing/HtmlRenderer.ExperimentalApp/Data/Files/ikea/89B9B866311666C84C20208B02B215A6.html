    IKEA: Here's the right way to pronounce it

    2016-05-24T17:55:00.000Z
    Amy Eley
    TODAY
    share
    tweet
    pin
    email

    When we think of IKEA, we think of Billy bookcases, the store’s maze-like layout and meatballs galore.

    What we don’t think of is the proper pronunciation of the store's name. After all, how hard could it be? “Eye-kee-ah,” right? Wrong!

    RELATED: Are you saying 'Adidas' wrong? A-Z guide to brands you're mispronouncing

    An IKEA representative confirmed to TODAY that Americans, in fact, say the Swedish store’s name differently than its actual pronunciation. Get ready for IKEA 101!

    The proper way to say IKEA is (drum roll, please): “ee-kay-uh.”
