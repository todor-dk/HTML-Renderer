  • Our shared vision – to create a better everyday life for the many people – is our greatest motivator. Over 183,000 IKEA co-workers are dedicated to bringing it to life.

  • The IKEA culture and values shape everything we do. Our enthusiasm, togetherness and willpower stem from our roots in southern Sweden and are inspired by IKEA founder Ingvar Kamprad

  • We share a business idea: to offer a wide range of well-designed, functional home furnishing products at prices so low that as many people as possible will be able to afford them.

  • Inter IKEA Systems B.V. franchises IKEA systems, methods and proven solutions to franchisees worldwide. That’s how we bring the IKEA Concept to more and more people!

  • The foundation of all product development is the idea that even with a thin wallet people should still be able to create a beautiful home with functional, safe and healthy products.

  • From forests, to sawmills, to production factories, our standpoint is always to do good business with common sense, based on honesty, respect, fairness and integrity.
