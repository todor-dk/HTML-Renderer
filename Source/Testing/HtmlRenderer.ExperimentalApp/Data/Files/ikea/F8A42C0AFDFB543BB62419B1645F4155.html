IKEA store openings

The history of IKEA goes back over 70 years. It took 15 years before the first store opened. Expansion has been slow compared, to other big box stores, but IKEA has been global from its' early days.

To watch the expansion of IKEA through the years scroll or press ►

IKEA founder Ingvar Kamprad began selling various items delivered on his bike years before the first catelogue and flat-pack furniture came along.

IKEA expands outside Scandinavia with the Zurich & Munich stores before openings in Hong Kong, Australia and Canada.

IKEA finally reaches the US in 1985.
