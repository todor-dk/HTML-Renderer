Take a look at our product range. Today, we have close to 10, 000 articles in various categories and materials in our range. We want our basic range to cover all the functions of the home. In addition we offer a range of products that meets the needs of our local markets.

We want to offer a wide range of well designed, functional home furnishing products at prices so low that as many people as possible can afford them.

To make this possible we are looking for partners who can supply products, components, raw material and services.

“The wife of the IKEA founder, wrote us a letter on 3 May 1952. She asked us if we would be interested and able to supply good quality products at low prices, with good availability and in large volumes. If we would bring good quality products, then IKEA would bring the market. Our founder responded positively to the letter.
He packed his small pick-up and went to Älmhult, the small town in southern Sweden to show IKEA some of our products. We got the deal and were very happy for the ‘big order’ of 20 chests of drawers”.

Our vision is to create a better everyday life for the many people. What does this mean? It means thinking about home furnishing beyond form and visual appeal and designing products in a way that really does solve some of life’s daily challenges. It means reaching out to as many people as possible and helping them achieve their dream home at the lowest possible cost.

Just look at our business idea: to offer a wide range of well designed, functional home furnishing products at prices so low that as many people as possible can afford them.
