Meet the Squad

Our team of IKEA® experts are traveling across the country helping families tackle home challenges with design makeovers that have smart solutions to make everyday life at home better.

Anthony Bell

Designer

Anthony is excited to join the Home Tour Squad this year as a designer and to have the opportunity to bridge both his creative ambitions with those tied more solidly in connecting and creating relationships with people. An artist at heart, he loves a good paint brush, but can be equally tempted by a good meal or a good show (best if combined).

Rocky Brewer

Director /Manager

Rocky has a passion for helping families achieve a better life at home. He enjoys working with a team to help families tackle their design challenges while creating fun and engaging videos that empower so many different people to do the same. In his spare time, Rocky loves traveling to new cities, composing music, making short films and spending time with his husband. 

Whitney Hutchinson

Designer

Whitney is a Southern Californian IKEA Interior Designer. She believes the perfect space is the one you feel most comfortable in and represents your own unique personality. She is grateful to have a job that allows her the freedom to express herself and looks forward to many new experiences as she travels across the country doing what she loves. 

Stephanie Recupero

Designer

Stephanie has a passion for encouraging people to tackle home challenges in a smart and simple way. Working with families and inspiring others to improve their home is a rewarding and enlightening experience. Along with interior design, she enjoys printmaking, ceramics and has a fondness for cats and squirrels. 

Jesse Myatt

Editor / Camera Operator

Jesse began his IKEA journey in 2010 while pursuing his passion for media production in film school. Now he can combine his love for film and IKEA by being a camera operator and editor on IKEA Home Tour for his 2nd year. He looks forward to exploring each new city on his long board and is always looking for great music festivals.
