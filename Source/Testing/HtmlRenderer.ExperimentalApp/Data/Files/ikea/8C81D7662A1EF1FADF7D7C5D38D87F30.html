Assembled in Boise

Welcome to Assembled in Boise, where IKEA is delivered to Boise. You'll find we are eco-friendly, less expensive to ship than IKEA Direct, and you don't have to leave Boise to get your POANG chair!

 Assembled in Boise will drive to IKEA in Draper, Utah (Salt Lake City), pickup, and deliver your order on a bi-weekly basis.  Should you decide to use your time on something else besides hex wrenches and screwdrivers, ask us about our assembly service. Professional design services are also available for your home or office project.

 Assembled in Boise is an eco-friendly way to shop. Since we drive one truck to IKEA to pickup your order, we not only have your order, but also the orders of other folks in the Treasure Valley, taking that many vehicles off the road.

 Our pricing is straightforward also. If your order amount, including Utah Sales Tax of 6.85% is LESS than $999.99, we charge 30%. If your order amount is over $1,000, we charge 25%. Please ask us about pricing for kitchen orders and large projects. 

Because we deliver, you don't have to leave Boise, take a day off from work, wade through a sea of people inside of IKEA, and then drive back tired from your day of shopping. 

 For information about our next trip to IKEA, like our Facebook page and you'll get all of our updates there.
