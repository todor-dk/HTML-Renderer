About Inter IKEA Group

The overall purpose of Inter IKEA Group is to secure continuous improvement, development, expansion and a long life of the IKEA Concept. The Inter IKEA Group is composed of three core businesses - franchising, range and supply and industry. The entities will work together to build a stronger franchise system. To build an even better IKEA offer together with retailers and suppliers based on existing and new strengths in our value chain. The aim is also to provide franchisees with best possible conditions for implementing and operating the IKEA Concept, and to create a strong platform for future expansion and growth. Learn more about Inter IKEA Group.
