About

IKEA West Chester offers a wide variety of family-friendly services to make your shopping experience fun for not just Mom and Dad but the kids as well! Smaland, a supervised children's play area, keeps kids entertained while parents shop. IKEA offers a wide range of well-designed, functional home furnishing products at affordable prices. The inspirational room settings in the showroom give great ideas on how to use the furniture and accessories in your own home.

Nobody likes shopping on an empty stomach. Be sure to visit the IKEA restaurant, featuring traditional Swedish meatballs, chicken fingers and more. The restaurant also includes a seating area just for kids, baby food, bottle warmers, high chairs and meals for the kids at just .99 cents.
