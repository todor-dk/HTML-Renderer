Gmail

Embed Smartsheet project workflow directly in Gmail

Use Gmail with Smartsheet to streamline processes and get quick updates on tasks. When you integrate with Gmail, row details sent to your inbox can be edited from directly inside Gmail. Save valuable time and increase team efficiency when you pair these two apps together. For Google Apps users only.

Key Features:

  • Work in your email - Quickly edit Smartsheet rows sent to your inbox from within Gmail.
  • Seamless - Integration with Google means easy collaboration on tasks, files and comments.
  • Easy contact management - Import Google Contacts from Gmail to your Smartsheet account.

How to get it:

  1. Make sure that all Smartsheet accounts are set up under the users' Google Apps email addresses (users can change their Smartsheet email address from the Personal Settings menu if needed)
  2. Ask your Google Apps domain administrator to review the Smartsheet listing in the Google Apps Marketplace and follow instructions to install Smartsheet for Google Apps
  3. Once the app is installed, any emails generated by Send Row, Notification, and Reminder features will include the "Edit Rows in Smartsheet" button, and recipients will be able to click the button to edit rows directly from Gmail without logging into Smartsheet
Built-in Integration

Integration with Gmail is built into Smartsheet.
