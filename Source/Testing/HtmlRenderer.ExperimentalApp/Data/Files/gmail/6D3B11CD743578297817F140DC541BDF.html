Create Account Gmail



Gmail is perhaps the world’s most popular email service, and it does not come as a surprise that many internet users have a need to create account Gmail out there. It is definitely not an arduous task, and you can easily create a Gmail account by simply following the guide below. Enjoy.




Follow our video-guide on how to create a Gmail account:


  • Step 1. Enter the website of Google by inserting http://google.com in the address line of your browser.

The minimalistic appearance of Google’s main page

  • Step 2. In a tiny, minimalistic menu of Google located in the up-right corner, click on the “Google apps” icon, which looks like a figure consisting of nine quadrates (see the screenshot).

Give a click to the “Google Apps” icon

  • Step 3. In the newly-opened pop-up menu with 9 icons, click on the “More” button in the bottom of the menu.

Scroll down or click on “More”

  • Step 4. Out of the presented icons, choose the icon of Gmail and click on it.

Select the service of create account Gmail

  • Step 5. In the opened window, click on the “Create account button”, located right below the window that offers you to sign in.

Get registered

  • Step 6. After you have done it, you have to fill in the typical registration form. In particular, give a name to your email account, enter your first and last name, create a password, and specify the date of your birth and gender. You have also a choice: specify either the existing email account of yours or your mobile number, which is needed for your security. Indeed, giving your mobile number will make the possibility of stealing your account less likely.

Fill the information about yourself in

  • Step 7. Then, type the symbols from the Captcha picture in and put a tick near the last line, thus agreeing with the policy of Google. Click on the “Next step” button.

Your account is created!

  • Step 8. Congratulations! Now, you can freely use your new email account.
