Managing Your Account

Use your Maricopa Enterprise ID (MEID) to sign into MCCCD resources such as Google Mail, Canvas, and the Online Student Center. Technical help for account issues can be found at my.maricopa.edu, which has support options and links to change your password or recover your account if you forgot your password.

Protect your privacy and account security. MCCCD will never ask you for your password. Do not share your password with anyone, even if the message requesting it looks official.
