TU Gmail

We have recently switched from an on campus federated authentication system to Google’s native authentication system. In most cases, your TUNetID and password have been synced with Google’s authentication system. If you have not changed your password recently, you may need to manually reset your password by visiting account.utulsa.edu/change-password before you can access your Gmail inbox.

After you have changed your password, we encourage you to enable 2-step verification for your TU Gmail account by visiting https://myaccount.google.com/security.

You can access your TU Gmail directly by visiting https://mail.google.com/a/utulsa.edu. Google will prompt you for your email address. You will need to enter your TuNetID followed by @utulsa.edu For example: abc123@utulsa.edu.

Login to TU Gmail

You may opt to have a different password for your TU Gmail account by changing your password via the Gmail settings, but be aware that anytime you change your password at account.utulsa.edu, that password will also overwrite your TU Gmail password.

If you have any questions you can email help@utulsa.edu or call the IT help desk at 918-631-3500.
