Keep up-to-date with all the latest scores, results, news and video with the brand new Sky Sports Football Score Centre App.

This season, the Sky Sports Football Score Centre Apps for iPhone and Android will bring you highlights from every Premier League and Football League game.

In-game goal clips from every Premier League game will also be available to Sky TV customers with a Sky Sports subscription and will be delivered using an improved, interactive push messaging service. We've also completely redesigned the overall look and feel of the App to bring you even more content than ever before including a dedicated video section showcasing all the best action.

Whether it's the latest headlines, live scores, fixtures and results or tables and standings, it's all here with up-to-the minute updates. You can even find your nearest Sky Pub venue by using our pub finder, take part in Budweiser dream goal or play Fantasy Six-a-side and Super 6 with Sky Bet.

The 'My Scores' page allows you to select specific matches from an array of leagues and competitions so it's easy to follow the games that matter. You can even personalise your home page with up to the minute information on your favourite team, including relevant team videos, trending videos with news, league tables and results all at your fingertips.
