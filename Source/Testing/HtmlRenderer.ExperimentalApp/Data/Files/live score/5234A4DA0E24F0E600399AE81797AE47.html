About Cricbuzz

Cricbuzz is the most popular cricket score website in India. It provides live cricket scores, live streaming, ball by ball commentary and much more. It also features match schedules of different series such as ICC World Cup, IPL T20, T20 World Cup, ICC Champions Trophy, Asia Cup, Commonwealth Games, ODI Series, Ashes, International Test Series, e.t.c.

News: It covers latest Cricket news and updates in it’s news section. Player news, rumors, match results, player ranking changes are also covered.

Results: Series and match results are updated regularly. You can also read match analysis, ball to ball highlights and previews in this section.

Rankings: ICC Rankings for Test, ODI, Twenty20 and Women are featured in Cricbuzz.com website. You can check team and player rankings.

Videos: You can watch live cricket streaming of upcoming matches, series for free. It also includes match highlights, interviews, previews and reviews.

Series: This section contains information about ongoing and upcoming series. Series stats, player statistics, points table, records are covered.

Scorecard: Of all these pages, Cricbuzz live cricket score card is the most popular one. It is more detailed than it’s rivals like ESPN Cricinfo.

Cricket Live Streaming

You can also watch live cricket matches from these popular live cricket streaming websites. Star Sports and Hotstar are the two official internet partners in India. They are completely free and do not require any registration. USA and UK fans can watch live matches at Willow and Sky Go. However, you need to pay a small subscription fee to watch live streaming of matches.

There are many other international websites like Fox Sports, Astro Cricket, Sky Sports and SuperSport. They allow you to watch live IPL, ICC T20, Worldcup and ODI matches online.
