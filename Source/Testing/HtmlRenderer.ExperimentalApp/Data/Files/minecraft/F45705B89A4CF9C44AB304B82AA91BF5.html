Date added: 2012-10-22
Description: This is the last version of Minecraft Tower Defense. Minecraft Tower Defense based on 2D Minecraft game by Mojang. To start the battle in the game, you need to use the shovel to dig a path through the ground into your house. Remember, the short wavy paths are better than long straights paths. To protect your house against the attacks of enemies, you must use four kinds of tower defenses to construct along the road, and four types of traps to set up on the road useful. Try to defeat the enemies such as skeleton, zombie, spider, creeper, and more to take the blocks. These blocks are used to upgrade your towers. When one enemy reaches your house, you will lose one heart. Once you win the battle in Island, you can unlock 2 battles in Dungeon and Nether.
Instructions: Press R key to reset your background.
Use the mouse to construct the tower defenses.
Click a block along the path. Choose one of 4 tower defenses to build.
Click on the road to open traps menu. Select one of them.
