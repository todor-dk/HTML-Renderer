Minecraft Games

What are Minecraft Games?

After the most famous sandbox game of all time Minecraft was released in 2011 many Minecraft Games followed its big success. In difference to the original game made by Mojang these block-styled games are reminiscent of Minecraft, but the gameplay and functions can be completely different. Whereas the original game has no storyline, except for survival, hardcore, creative and adventure modes, custom and fanmade Minecraft games offer a big variety of new features. From 2D-styled block games to driving cars through the world of minecraft, here you can play the craziest Minecraft Games for free on your pc or your mobile device.

How many free Minecraft Games are there?

We offer a selection of more the 100 free block games for kids. Whether you want to build a house to survive day after day or join an awesome Minecraft shooting game and lead your team to victory, the choice is yours!

Pixel Warfare is a series of an online multiplayer block-themed FPS shooter. Join a team und fight in a lovely NPC Village against the other team. The fraction with the most kills wins the road. You can switch between several weapons from pistol to rocket launcher. Aim precisly and don't miss your targets and dominate the game. The newest version of this Minecraft Shooter is Pixel Warfare 5.

Ever wondered what Minecraft would look like in 2D? Mine Blocks is a perfect clone of the original game with all its ressources like coal, gold and diamond in a 2D world. You can go left or right, jump and dig into the ground and even craft everything just like in the original game. Harvest wood to craft a Crafting Table and start building tools and weapons. Build a house to survive the night and protect yourself from zombies, creepers and skeletons. You move with your WASD keys and use the mouse for mining and building. To open your inventory klick on the little arrow next to your items list. You can create a new world and even safe your progress. Explore the endless world of this cool 2D sandbox crafting game.

Tired of fighting for the good? Take control of a creeper in Creep Craft 2 fight your way through another 2D-styled block world full of obstacles and opponents.
