Minecraft Pokemon

OUTSTANDING Minecraft Mod Combines Pokemon and Minecraft

Pixelmon is a fan-created mod in Minecraft. This mod is heavily influence by modern Pokemon game titles.

Outstanding Gameplay

Just like the original Pokemon game titles. It is built to let people to discover, catch as well as train numerous Pokemon for battle.

However there are unique features to Pixelmon which makes it's gameplay totally unique and something quite extrodinary

Pixelmon Getting Started

Pixelmon starts off by supplying the player to power to select from a bunch of starter Pokemon, for example Charmander, Bulbasaur, Squirtle, and a few others.

As soon as the one is finished choosing a starter Pokemon, then they will be able to start playing.

When the game begins, players will be able to catch, trade, train and battle with their Pokemon.

Pokemon Evolution

While a Pokemon battles more, it acquires experience, once the Pokemon receives the right amount of experience, it evolves into a much larger and more powerful version of itself.

Pixelmon Battles

Spread all around the game world are additional computer controlled characters that feature their own Pokemon.

In the event that a player makes contact with these characters, a battle starts. The player needs to remain the battle until it is actually over, which is once all of the Pokemon on a single team faint.

Players may also be tasked with developing their very own Pokeballs, which are items utilized to catch Pokemon which are not presently owned by other trainers.

Unique & Customizable

Pixelmon is highly customizable and playable on a variety of servers whereby players participate in activities, such as gym battles .
