Game Overview

Imagine it, and you can build it! The critically acclaimed Minecraft comes to PlayStation®3 system. Create your very own game world and explore, build and conquer. When night falls the monsters appear, so ensure you’ve made yourself a shelter before they arrive. Make it through the night and the world is only limited by your imagination you choose what you want to make or what to do. Play on your own, cooperatively with friends in 2 player and 4 player split screen modes, or online with up to 8 players. Split screen option requires HD TV with HD connection.

Tumble Mini Game

Break blocks and brings your foes low! Tumble is the second mini game we've released for Console Edition -- a frantic, competitive clash in which players destroy the very floor beneath their enemies' feet, sending them plummeting to their doom. There are two variants to choose from: one with players launching snowballs, the other equipping combatants with shovels. It's smashing fun!

Battle Mini Game

Compete with your friends in Battle, a mini game for Minecraft Console Editions where you get to challenge up to eight players online or four players in splitscreen in a fight for survival. Speed, strategy -- and sometimes just pure luck -- will make you the winner! Get Battle with its three base maps in the latest free game update, or add more options to play with additional map packs.
