Minecraft Review

“Minecraft is a sandbox adventure game”
A review of Minecraft by Fernando Ortega

Minecraft is a creative mining game which revolves around a randomly generated map. Players can build large structures and spend literally countless hours playing the game because there is never an end in sight with Minecraft. The game is extremely addictive and fun.

Beginning the journey in Minecraft is really all about exploring and mining things which give you the ability to build stuff. If you've ever played Terraria, you might notice where they got a lot of their ideas for building that game.

The worlds in Minecraft are absolutely huge. Several updates have improved the worlds and a new realm which resembles hell gives you a quicker, albeit more difficult way to travel throughout the world.

A new mode in Minecraft called the "Creator Mode" really gives you god-like abilities like invincibility to build and create throughout the world without fear of death. With this new mode, you are also able to fly around.

Framerate issues happen sometimes depending on your CPU and more importantly, your graphics adapter and DirectX, but they are normally few and far between when you consider the size of the worlds created in Minecraft.

Though not impossible, it may become difficult at times to find good and working Minecraft server lists so it might take a bit of searching to find the Minecraft community suited to you.
