Minecraft Unblocked Game

Minecraft Unblocked is an addictive flash game, created by Marcus Persson in 2009. Minecraft unblocked unites destruction and creation in an enchanting way that will make you spend hours staring at the screen. You are able to play the game online or to download it and play on your computer.

How to Play?
These are the controls which you need in the game:

  • - [W] to move up
  • - [S] to move down
  • - [A] to move left
  • - [D] to move right
  • - [Space] to jump.

You will get easily used to these controls, if you haven’t already mastered them in other flash games.

Game Modes
To make it more interesting, Minecraft Unblocked has a few modes, which give you the opportunity to change the way you play and experiment with the ways of having fun. The game has the following modes:

  • - Adventure mode – this mode does not allow you to crash the blocks and to manufacture but for me this is the most dynamic mode and I find it the most interesting one. So, I usually choose to play Minecraft Unblocked at this mode. Just like the name says, it is a real adventure and also great fun.
  • - Creative mode – this is the most popular mode of the game, but for me – less intriguing than Adventure. Here, you cannot attack and hurt the other player. You can only manufacture or crush the structures.
  • - Survival mode – here you have weapons to protect you from the beast and staying alive is your main purpose.

Along with choosing the game mode, you should also choose the game difficulty – easy, normal or hard. The easy one is kind of boring, so you would better start with the normal one. When you get used to the game, start playing on the greatest difficulty – this is the biggest fun.
