About the site

Minecraft Server List is showcasing some of the best Minecraft Servers in the world to play on online.

Scroll down and find a good minecraft server that seems right for you - click on the server, copy the Server Address (IP & Port) and paste it into your minecraft client at the Multiplayer option.

Server information

We ping each server often to ensure they are online and ready to play on. More than 370.000 Minecraft Servers monitored since the list started in 2010.

If a server goes offline it will first be displayed with a red number and after a couple of hours of downtime it will be removed from the list.
If it remains offline for 14 days, it will be deleted from the system.
