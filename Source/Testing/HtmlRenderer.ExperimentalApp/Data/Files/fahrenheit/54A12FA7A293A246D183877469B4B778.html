Fahrenheit

by LordMune

Publication date 2008
Topics let's play, fahrenheit, trainwreck, lordmune
Publisher Let's Play Archive
Q:What is Fahrenheit?
A: Short answer, the European title of the dramatic adventure game Indigo Prophecy. Long answer, it's the second game developed by Quantic Dream and directed by David Cage. It's the trainwreck that comes after body-hopping David Bowie (Omikron: The Nomad Soul) and before the Uncanny Valley (Heavy Rain).

Q: What's Mayans and Hobos?
A: For the first few chapters of the game, I'm doing two separate playthroughs to show of the interactivity of the environments and the flexibility of the storyline. The two feature basically the same situations, but the commentary and character behavior is drastically different. When much of the flexibility and interactivity is lost and only one viable path remains open, which happens disappointingly soon, I'll be doing one Maybos video per update, alternating between Mayans and Hobos commentary style.
