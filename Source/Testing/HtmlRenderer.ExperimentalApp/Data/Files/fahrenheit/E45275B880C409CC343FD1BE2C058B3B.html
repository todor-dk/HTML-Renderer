Make a lasting impression with Fahrenheit by Christian Dior, an exciting cologne for men that is sure to turn heads and attract attention. This unforgettable fragrance mixes honeysuckle with the rich scents of sandalwood and balsam. Introduced in 1988, Fahrenheit is the perfect blend of fresh and warm scents. Splash it on your skin in the morning before you leave for an important business meeting at the office and enjoy the energy it brings to your day.
