About Fahrenheit Colognes

Created by Jean-Louis Sieuzac and Maurice Roger in 1988, Fahrenheit cologne is a uniquely blended, intense perfume with floral, spicy, and fruity tones harmonized to produce a classic sensual scent. Named after the commonly used temperature measuring scale, it distinguishes itself with heavy woody and leathery notes mixed in with delicate florals such as bergamot, violet, and patchouli. Worn in winter, it provides a warm and fiery foundation that can linger on for a comforting sensation. Reviewed by fans as a mysterious combination of spicy and leathery aromas followed by a fresh flare of earthy and fruity hints, Fahrenheit cologne is a bold choice that makes a definite impression. Another powerful fragrance by Dior, Fahrenheit 32 is a fusion of citric orange blossom, earthy vetiver, and warm vanilla. Adorn this for an instantly fresh masculine mixture sprayed from a bottle that is named after the freezing point of water. You can also experience the fresh and fiery intermingling of grapefruit, spearmint, violet, and vetiver with Aqua Fahrenheit. The fragrance comes in splash or spray bottles, available from the vast inventory on eBay.
