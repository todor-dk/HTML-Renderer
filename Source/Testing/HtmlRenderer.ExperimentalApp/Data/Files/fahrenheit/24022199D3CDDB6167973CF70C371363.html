How do you convert celsius to fahrenheit?

If you have a temperature in Celsius, the equation to convert is: = 32+ (T * 9) / 5

If you have a temperature difference in Celsius (for example, an increase of 1C is how much in Fahrenheit?), the equation to convert is: = (T * 9) / 5
