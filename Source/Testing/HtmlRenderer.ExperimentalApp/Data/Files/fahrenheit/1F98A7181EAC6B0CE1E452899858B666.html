Commercial and Residential HVAC

Welcome to Fahrenheit Mechanical Systems: Middle Tennessee and Nashville’s choice for indoor climate and commercial refrigeration expertise. We provide commercial and residential HVAC services in the Murfreesboro and Nashville areas. From new equipment installations to emergency repairs, our team of factory trained and certified technicians blend old school values with today’s innovation to deliver 100% satisfaction guaranteed! When you partner with the Fahrenheit Mechanical Systems team, we put your comfort and budget first without sacrificing workmanship and service. We supply the customer focused service you deserve!

Fahrenheit’s comprehensive residential and commercial offerings include:

  • Air conditioning and heating installation
  • Air conditioning and heating repairs
  • HVAC preventative maintenance programs
  • HVAC retrofits and upgrades
  • Indoor air quality consultations and solutions
  • Air filtration installations and repairs
  • Commercial Refrigeration installation and repairs
  • Gas piping repairs and service
