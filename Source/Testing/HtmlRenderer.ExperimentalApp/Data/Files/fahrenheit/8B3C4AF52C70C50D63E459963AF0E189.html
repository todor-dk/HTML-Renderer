Roller Coaster Stats

  • Angle Of Descent: 97°
  • Height: 121 feet
  • Inversions: 6
  • Length: 2,700 feet

Fahrenheit Facts

Features: 90-degree ascent up the 121-foot tall lift hill

Inversions: 107-foot Inverted Loop, Inverted Corkscrew Roll, 75-foot Cobra Roll, Airborne Inverted S-Roll, Inverted Corkscrew Roll

Opening date: May 24, 2008

Estimated cost: $12.1 million

Train has three cars with two rows each, seating two abreast per row.

Ride capacity: 850 passengers per hour
