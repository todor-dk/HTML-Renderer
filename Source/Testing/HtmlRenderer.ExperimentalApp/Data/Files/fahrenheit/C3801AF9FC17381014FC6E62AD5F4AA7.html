We Are °Fahrenheit 250

We are located in Sacramento, California, 7042 Folsom Blvd Sacramento CA 95826.
We serve handcrafted BBQ forged from wood, fire & steel.

We respect our guests and our craft, which is why we believe in using the freshest and highest-quality meats and ingredients throughout our menu. These quality meats are smoked low and slow at a constant 250 degrees with locally seasoned oak and fruit woods, making our restaurant a pellet-free zone. Every item on our menu is made from scratch, from our four unique barbecue sauces to dip your ‘que in, to our mouth watering sides and fresh-squeezed lemonade. Our cocktails are made with fresh ingredients and feature house-infused liquors.
