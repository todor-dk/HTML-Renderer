The Oiligarchy of Fahrenheit 451-America is a massive, efficient nation, ruled by some bigoted old white man with an even hand, and notable for its flagrant waste-dumping, sprawling nuclear power plants, and complete absence of social welfare. The hard-nosed, hard-working, humorless population of 4.47 billion Fahrenheit 451-Americans are either ruled by a sleek, efficient government or a conglomerate of multinational corporations; it's difficult to tell which.

The relatively small, corrupt, pro-business government juggles the competing demands of Defense, Law & Order, and Industry. It meets to discuss matters of state in the capital city of Corrupt-er Washington DC. Citizens pay a flat income tax of 36.6%.

The frighteningly efficient Fahrenheit 451-American economy, worth a remarkable 1,259 trillion dollar bills a year, is broadly diversified and led by the Arms Manufacturing industry, with major contributions from Information Technology, Uranium Mining, and Automobile Manufacturing. Black market activity is rampant. Average income is an amazing 281,760 dollar bills, but there is an enormous disparity between incomes, with the richest 10% of citizens earning 1,601,961 per year while the poor average 24,141, a ratio of 66.4 to 1.

Fur coats have become the latest fashion trend, councils up and down the country wrangle over legal matters, the new foreign policy is why bother invading neighbours when they can be bought wholesale, and Eau de Trashcan has become a wildly popular perfume. Crime, especially youth-related, is totally unknown, thanks to a well-funded police force. Fahrenheit 451-America's national animal is the Mechanical Hound, which is also the nation's favorite main course, and its national religion is Television.

Fahrenheit 451-America is ranked 2,297th in the world and 1st in Arena Est Infernum for Largest Agricultural Sector, scoring 9,838.13 on the Mu-Bah-Daggs Productivity Index.
