Since 2007, Fahrenheit Seafood and Grill Restaurant has provided Residents in Edenavale with an extraordinary seafood and Grill dining destination. Under experienced and efficient Management, Fahrenheit exhibits vitality, energy and warmth not previously associated with fine dining establishments. These seasoned professionals present the finest elements of a restaurant experience to all who dine at Fahrenheit, but they do it through the means of their own personal charm.

OUR VIBE
Our ambience is Tranquil yet full of energy projected by our knowledgeable and friendly waiters, who will always tend to a diners needs.

 

OUR FLAVOUR
Absolutely unique and authentic. We offer diverse and delectable taste sensations to satisfy even the most particular of pallets. Here you will find a setting that suits a style that is classy, romantic and family orientated. Our Seafood is fresh and brings with it nostalgia of seaside holidays; our Grills; Legendry.

OUR VENUE
The serene environment set in a central midtown location provides an ideal setting for business lunches or a memorable night out, giving patrons an exceptional venue to enjoy luxurious food and impeccable service. Our Lapa magnificently set in a tranquil garden setting with lush verdant surroundings, ensuring your visit to be a warm and relaxed experience.

 

Functions and Buffet!
Our exclusive venue and facilities are available for all your Weddings, Engagements, Christenings, Birthdays, Conferences, etc... and offers the perfect atmosphere and services. Accommodating approximately 100 people in the garden Lapa, and 150 in the dining area, that means a combined total of up to 250 people you could have share your event.

Fahrenheit is set in a tranquil, lush garden surrounding, combined with a warm, relaxed atmosphere, the perfect setting for any occasion. Guaranteed our Buffet will leave you with an exceptional experience of world class dining and unique tastes with a variety from all parts of the world.

Please make an Appointment with our Function coordinator to discuss all of your Function requirements.

Directions


Travelling North or South towards Edenvale on the Eastern Bypass. Take the Modderfontein turn off then turn right onto Andries Pretorius. Before crossing to Van Riebeeck Road, turn left onto Terrace Road. We are situated on the corner of Terrace and Hudson Street on your left.
