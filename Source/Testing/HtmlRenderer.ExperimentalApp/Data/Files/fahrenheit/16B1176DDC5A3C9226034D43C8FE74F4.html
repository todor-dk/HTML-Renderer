Fahrenheit Labs

WELCOME TO FAHRENHEIT LABS, the first and only incubation program available for eligible students of the Maricopa Community Colleges. Developed in partnership with the Maricopa Small Business Development Center (SBDC), Fahrenheit Labs' mission is to provide space, resources and access to emerging student companies for the purpose of helping them grow their business.

Jeff Saville, CEI Executive Director, explains how Fahrenheit Labs will assist student entrepreneurs during the program's kickoff event in August 2013.
