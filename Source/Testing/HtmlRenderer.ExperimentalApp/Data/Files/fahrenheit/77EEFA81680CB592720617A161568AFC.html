Celsius and Fahrenheit Conversion Formulas

Celsius to Fahrenheit conversion formula:

Celsius (Centigrade)to

Fahrenheit [°F] = [°C] × 9⁄5 + 32
Kelvin [K] = [°C] + 273.15
Rankine [°R] = ([°C] + 273.15) × 9⁄5
Delisle [°De] = (100 − [°C]) × 3⁄2
Newton [°N] = [°C] × 33⁄100
Réaumur [°Ré] = [°C] × 4⁄5
Rømer [°Rø] = [°C] × 21⁄40 + 7.5

The next simple approximation works well within the range of normal weather temperatures [°F] = [°C] × 2 + 30 (or [°C] = ([°F] − 30) / 2) , underestimating by 6°F at −20°C and over by 4°F at 30°C.

Celsius [°C] = ([°F] − 32) × 5⁄9
Kelvin [K] = ([°F] + 459.67) × 5⁄9
Rankine [°R] = [°F] + 459.67
Delisle [°De] = (212 − [°F]) × 5⁄6
Newton [°N] = ([°F] − 32) × 11⁄60
Réaumur [°Ré] = ([°F] − 32) × 4⁄9
Rømer [°Rø] = ([°F] − 32) × 7⁄24 + 7.5
