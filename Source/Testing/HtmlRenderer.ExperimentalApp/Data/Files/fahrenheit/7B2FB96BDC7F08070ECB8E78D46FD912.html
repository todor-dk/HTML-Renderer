Our Divisions

At Fahrenheit Beachsports we have so many awesome activities that we have divided them into specific divisions.

Surf School Arabia

Surf School Arabia is a division of Fahrenheit Beachsports offering a wide variety of Surfing and Paddleboarding activities in the UAE. The Surf School is introducing a range of fresh and innovative activities such as SUP Polo, SUP Sisters, XL SUP, Kids SUP, Night and Full moon SUP paddles, WindSUP as well as private and group Surfing and SUP lessons. Surf and Paddleboarding activities are taught from the ages of 5 years old.

Visit Website

Grom Squad Arabia

Grom Squad Arabia, a division of Fahrenheit Beachsports, has a full range of kids’ specific watersports activities to ensure kids have an awesome time in the ocean. From Kids SUP (Stand Up Paddleboard) classes with kids only boards, Kids birthday parties, Schools (After School Activities and Enrichment Programs), Surfing lessons, Clear Kayaks, Snorkelling, SUP XL, Wakesports, WindSUP and Towsports (donut tows and banana boat rides) and a range of floats to enjoy on the water, Grom Squad has the kids covered.

Visit Website

Fahrenheit Fitness

Fahrenheit Fitness is the fitness division of Fahrenheit beachsports. Fahrenheit fitness has a unique offering in which almost all activities are done either on or near the beach and in the sea. At Fahrenheit Fitness the Sea & the beach is our gym so you can expect a host of exciting new beach and sea based fitness classes.

Visit Website

Liquid Edge Arabia

Liquid Edge Arabia is the motorized activities division of Fahrenheit Beachsports offering a wide range of fresh and active motorized watersports. Guests can enjoy wakesports such as Wakeboarding, Wakeskating and Wakesurfing as well as popular ocean activities such as Flyboarding, Kneeboarding, Donut & Inflatable tows, Banana Boat rides and Waterskiing as part of the experience. Operating in a sheltered bay in Jumeirah 1, Liquid Edge Arabia is gearing up to be an epic motorised watersports location.

Visit Website

Big Kahuna Arabia

Big Kahuna Arabia is the Corporate, Team Building and Group bookings division of Fahrenheit Beachsports and has the best line-up of group watersports activities to tailor make corporate days out. Big Kahuna also has a wide range of packages from rewarding staff to motivating the team, to tailor made solutions for groups of people with activities designed to have fun but also to test skills. Life’s more fun in a group.

Visit Website

Aloha Arabia

In the Hawaiian language, “Aloha” stands for much more than just “hello” or “goodbye” or “love.” It’s deeper meaning is “the joyful (oha) sharing (alo) of life energy (ha) in the present (alo).” This is what we want to spread around the Middle East through our trips, and share with you. So join us on a surf or SUP trips in the UAE, Oman, Sri Lanka and the Maldives.

Visit Website

What We Do

Fahrenheit Beachsports offers the largest & freshest range of active watersports in the region run by watersports professionals. Surfing, Paddleboarding (SUP Polo, XL SUP, SUP Yoga, SUP Tours, WindSUP), Flyboarding, Wakesports (wakeboard-wakeskate-wakesurf), Night activities, dedicated kids division, Tow sports (banana boat, donut), Kneeboarding & Skiing), Fitness Classes, Corporate & Team building activities are just some of the huge range of activities we offer.
