About Fahrenheit Studio

We build brands that bring you results

Fahrenheit Studio is a branding and design agency based in Los Angeles. We harness the power of new media to build living brands that bring you results.

Founded in 1995 by Robert Weitz and Dylan Tran, Fahrenheit Studio has developed complete branding and marketing strategies, websites, and online applications for companies such as Virgin Records America, IBM, and Warner Music Group.

We help you connect with your customers

At Fahrenheit Studio, we don't simply design logos or build websites: we build relationships. We use a mix of online, print, email, video, and social media channels to tell your brand's story in a way that resonates with customers and forms lasting bonds.

Whether you're a new company just starting out or an established business looking to revitalize your brand, we can help you sharpen your message, refine your image, and connect with customers.
