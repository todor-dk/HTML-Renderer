Ingredients Nutrition

  • Servings
    1
  • Units
    US
  • 1 1⁄2 tablespoons Malibu rum or 1 1⁄2 tablespoons coconut rum
  • 1 1⁄2 tablespoons Grand Marnier or 1 1⁄2 tablespoons Cointreau liqueur or 1 1⁄2 tablespoons triple sec
  • 2 1⁄2 tablespoons cranberry juice
  • 1 1⁄2 tablespoons pineapple juice
  • Sprite, splash

Directions See How It's Made

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice and add rum, liqueur and juices.
  2. Shake well and strain into a chilled martini glass.
  3. Top with a splash of Sprite.
