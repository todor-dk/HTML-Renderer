Convert Celsius to Fahrenheit

Converting a Celsius temperature to Fahrenheit is easy with this online converter. Just enter the Celsius temperature in the box and hit the Convert button.

Celsius Temperature:      

You also can convert the temperature by hand, if you use this formula:

  1. Multiply the Celsius temperature by 9
  2. Divide the answer by 5
  3. Add 32

Convert Fahrenheit to Celsius

To convert a Fahrenheit temperature to Celsius, enter the Fahrenheit temperature in the box and hit the Convert button.

Fahrenheit Temperature:      

You can convert the temperature by hand by using this formula:

  1. Subtract 32 from the Fahrenheit temperature
  2. Divide the answer by 9
  3. Multiply that answer by 5
