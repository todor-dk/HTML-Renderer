Learn how to convert temperatures

Formula for conversion from celsius to fahrenheit:
°F = ((9/5) x °C) + 32

Formula for conversion from fahrenheit to celsius:
°C = (5/9) x (°F-32)


What is 80 °F in °C?
°C = (5/9) x (80-32) = (0.556) * (48) = 26.7 °C

What is -40 °C in °F?
°F = ((9/5) x -40) + 32 = (-72) + 32 = -40 °F
so, -40 °C = -40 °F, an interesting coincidence!
