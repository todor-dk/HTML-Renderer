2017 FESTIVAL    PENNSYLVANIA, USA

JUNE 8TH-11TH 2017

Fahrenheit is a 4 day fire and flow arts retreat that boasts fantastic workshops throughout the day and incredible performances and fire dancing throughout the night! Come learn from some of the top flow arts instructors and fire performers from all over the East Coast! With classes in just about every prop and skill level, this is not to be missed!

COMMUNITY INVOLVEMENT IS THE BEST

Fahrenheit can’t happen without help from its participants. Everyone who holds a ticket is a valued member of our community and we expect participation and interaction through volunteering. Once you have your ticket, please use our volunteer scheduler to find a shift or two you could donate your time to and do your part to make Fahrenheit a clean, happy, and safe event for everyone!
