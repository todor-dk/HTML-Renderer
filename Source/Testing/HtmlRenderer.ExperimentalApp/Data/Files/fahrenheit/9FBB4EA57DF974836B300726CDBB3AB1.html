    From Our Editors

    For familiar food you're sure to love, head to Fahrenheit for American-style cuisine in Charlotte. Fahrenheit offers great gluten-free alternatives to fan favorites. You'll find a wonderful selection of drinks from this restaurant's full bar to top off your meal. Surf the web from your tablet or laptop on Fahrenheit's complimentary wifi. Enjoy the cool summer breezes on Fahrenheit's seasonally available outdoor seating.

    Slip into something more comfortable before dining at Fahrenheit, where dress code calls for business casual.

    Valet parking is always readily available for Fahrenheit's diners. You'll also find plenty of safe spaces to lock up your bike if you prefer to cycle to the restaurant.

    Fancy snacks do come at a higher price, but wow are they delicious. When you're looking for a bite of the classics, you know there's no better place than Fahrenheit.
