We're continuously improving our website's architecture.  As a result, some webpages were recently moved or deleted.

  • Class schedules migrated into the Registration Information webpage. 
  • A new website section was created for the Employee Service Awards. 
  • Institutional Assessment and Institutional Effectiveness webpages were redesigned.
