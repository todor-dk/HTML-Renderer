General

What is Fahrenheit: Indigo Prophecy Remastered?

Fahrenheit: Indigo Prophecy Remastered is the definitive version of the supernatural murder mystery game Fahrenheit (known as Indigo Prophecy in North America), originally developed by Quantic Dream. This newly remastered edition features updated graphics, full controller support, and is based on the uncut/uncensored international version of the game.

What’s with the name?

To make a long story short, because the game is known as Indigo Prophecy in North America and Fahrenheit in the rest of the world, we simply combined them to reduce any potential confusion with the new version. The name’s a little longer, but we think it works quite well.

Is this the full, uncensored version of the game?

You bet. There is absolutely no cut or censored content in this version. Consider this the definitive version of Quantic Dream’s vision.

Was Quantic Dream involved in the port?

While we at Aspyr Media did all of the technical work, the lovely folks at Quantic Dream gave us invaluable feedback during the process (as well as their blessing).

Cool. So… who is Aspyr Media, exactly?

We specialize in bringing AAA and fan-favorite games to new platforms. Our historical focus has been on the Mac operating system, though we have branched out in recent years to include iOS, Android, Linux, and, now with Fahrenheit, even Windows! You can learn more about us at http://www.aspyr.com.

What’s new in the Remastered version?

Updates to the game include:
  • Remastered HD graphics
  • New touch-based control scheme for phones/tablets
  • Full controller support across ALL platforms
  • Seamless switching between graphical modes

How have the graphics been improved?

Our primary focus was increasing the texture detail and image resolution. We gave every texture in the game a facelift, in many cases increasing the detail by 4x. With native support for resolutions up to 2880×1800 (the original was capped at 1024×768), as well as true widescreen support, this is the best looking version of Fahrenheit you can get. (Note: To fit within the 2GB size restriction on iOS, the mobile version contains only textures twice the size.)

On which platforms can I play the game? How much does it cost?

Fahrenheit: Indigo Prophecy Remastered is available on iOS, Android, Windows, Mac, and Linux. It sells for $9.99/€8.99/£6.99 on all platforms.

Is there a home console version in the works?

Sorry, there are currently no plans to release Fahrenheit: Indigo Prophecy Remastered on any home consoles, including Playstation 4 or Xbox One.

Are there any differences between the mobile and desktop versions of the game?

All versions of Fahrenheit: Indigo Prophecy Remastered have the exact same game content and features, no matter which platform you play it on.

If I buy both the mobile and desktop versions of the game, can I play one version and continue my game on the other?

Sorry, due to platform restrictions, we can’t support synced game saves between mobile and desktop versions. However, you will be able to sync game saves between all desktop platforms and between iOS devices.

Which languages does the game support?

Fahrenheit: Indigo Prophecy Remastered features full support for English, French, German, and Spanish on all platforms except Android. This includes full audio, subtitles, and user interface. (Note: iOS users will need to download non-English language audio content via free IAP.) The Android version features full text and audio support for English, and text support for French, German, and Spanish.

I’m having technical difficulties with the game. Who do I contact for support?

Whether you’re having trouble downloading the game, are running into crashes, or are experiencing any other bugs on ANY platform, Aspyr Support will be more than happy to assist you in any way they can. Please visit http://support.aspyr.com to open a support ticket or start a live chat with one of our representatives.

Mobile (iOS & Android)

What are the hardware requirements to play Fahrenheit: Indigo Prophecy Remastered on iOS?

Fahrenheit: Indigo Prophecy Remastered requires iOS 7 or newer. As for hardware, the game is compatible with iPad 2, iPad mini, and iPhone 5 and newer. THE GAME WILL NOT RUN WELL ON EARLIER DEVICES!

Is the iOS version universal?

Absolutely. You only need one copy of the game to play on iPhone and iPad.

Can I transfer saved game data between iOS devices?

Yes, game data may be synced between iOS devices using iCloud. iCloud saves can be uploaded to the cloud at any time by tapping the iCloud upload button on the main menu. Please note only one save can be available on the cloud at any time. Once a save is in the cloud, the save can be accessed on any device that that iCloud account is logged into.

What are the hardware requirements to play Fahrenheit: Indigo Prophecy Remastered on Android?

Fahrenheit: Indigo Prophecy Remastered requires one of the following Android OSes or later:
  • Jelly Bean (4.1 – 4.3.1)
  • Kit Kat (4.4 – 4.4.4)
  • Lollipop (5.0 – 5.1.1)
  • Marshmallow (6.0)
  • FireOS
As for hardware, the game is compatible with a number of devices from manufacturers like Samsung, LG, HTC, Motorola, Google, Nvidia, Sony, and Amazon. There are far too many devices to list here, but the app store you purchase it from (either Google Play or the Amazon Appstore) should indicate whether your device is compatible or not. If you’re still not sure, contact Aspyr Support at http://support.aspyr.com.

I saw the game supports French, German, and Spanish but I’m only hearing English. What gives?

Fahrenheit: Indigo Prophecy Remastered on iOS comes with subtitles for French, German and Spanish. However, to access localized audio, you’ll need to download a Language Pack via a FREE In-App Purchase. This audio can be downloaded at any time. On the main menu, tap the Languages icon to download the Language Pack. This will require 1GB of free space on the device. Android users are restricted to English audio only.

How do I switch between the old and new graphics?

This can be done any time by tapping the HD/SD icon that it is on screen.

What controllers are supported and/or have been tested for mobile devices?

While there are other controllers not listed that might work, below is what is officially supported.
  • iOS — Tested on MadCatz CTRLi, Logitech PowerShell, MOGA REBEL, MOGA ACE POWER, SteelSeries Stratus
  • Android — Fahrenheit: Indigo Prophecy Remastered for Android features full HID controller support.

Originally released in 2005, Fahrenheit (known as Indigo Prophecy in North America) was a breakthrough in interactive narrative, teetering between the worlds of cinema and gaming while also embracing them, carving its own unique genre in the entertainment landscape. This newly remastered edition features updated graphics and full controller support for all platforms, and is based on the uncut and uncensored international version of the game.

With a rich multilayered narrative, innovative presentation, and a chilling musical score by famed Hollywood composer Angelo Badalementi, Fahrenheit: Indigo Prophecy Remastered serves as the definitive version of the supernatural murder mystery and (re)introduces the groundbreaking title to old and new fans alike.

Key Features

  • Interactive Drama — An intuitive control scheme and spectacular split-screen presentation complement the game’s mysterious noir narrative.
  • Extensive Replayability — With branching dialog and multiple endings, each playthrough of the game yields a different outcome.
  • Multiple Characters — Take control of the narrative through four different playable characters, each illuminating a different perspective of the story.
  • Fully Voiced Script — Every line from the extensive script is voiced by professional actors in English, French, German, and Spanish. Voice files may require free in-game download.
  • Cinematic Score — Fahrenheit features a fully orchestrated score composed by Angelo Badalamenti, famous for his extensive work with director David Lynch.
  • Uncut version of the game — Unlike the original North American release, Remastered contains all of the game’s original scenes, uncensored and uncut.

 

Key Upgrades

  • High-definition Graphics — All of the in-game textures have been meticulously recreated in HD for mobile and desktop.
  • Widescreen Support – A true widescreen (not cropped) view strengthens the game’s cinematic qualities while also providing players with a better sense of their surroundings.
  • Elegant Touch Controls — An intuitive control scheme has been implemented that works on both phone and tablets, and brings the player closer to the narrative.
  • Full Controller Support —Support for MFi hardware controllers on iOS, as well as various popular controllers for desktop. See system requirements for full list of supported controllers.
  • Live Graphics Comparison — Players can switch between the updated visuals and the original art assets with the touch of a button.
