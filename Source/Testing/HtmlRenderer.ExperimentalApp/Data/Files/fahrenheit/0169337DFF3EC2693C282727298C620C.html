Fahrenheit Slim Line Rubber Iron

The plates are made from 100% titanium which heats up quickly and gives your hair a beautiful shine. The Slim line flat iron is the best for extremely coarse hair. With temperatures ranging from 140f – 450f.If you have fine hair, then this iron will work well for you since we all know how hard it is to find an iron which has such low temperatures. The plates are a standard 1 inch. The long plate length makes styling the back of your head; less of a hustle .the iron only weighs 1.6 pounds to give you a sturdy feel with a comfortable grip on the handle. The looped end of this iron allows you to store it more efficiently. The worst that could happen is if you’re trying to style at an angle, but your cord keeps getting tangled up .the Fahrenheit slim line has an 8-foot cord rotates 360 degrees to prevent those types of moments.

The only reserve about this iron is that it doesn’t open up much. Therefore, if you have very curly hair, you might want to invest in a pair of heat protectant gloves or a spring clamp flat iron that is more adjustable. All in all, this is a great mid entry level iron worth every cent.

Far infrared energy and ionic technology will straighten your hair and reduce frizz. Delivering consistent heat to the hair, it conditions it from the inside out and reduces bacteria while straightening.

Double Trouble Pink Zebra Fahrenheit Heat Wave Ceramic Iron

With this combo, you don’t have to worry about what to do when you travel, and your favorite iron can’t fit in your carrier bag with everything else. The mini flat iron is just as good as the larger one and is travel size. The plates are made of ceramic and heat up within 60 seconds of turning it on. This saved time counts, especially in the morning. The maximum temperature on the iron is 450f while the mini iron heats up to 400f.

The long swivel cord on the flat iron ensures no tangles while straightening your hair. The solid ceramic plates on both irons use ionic technology to straighten the hair from the inside out giving it less frizz. Far infrared technology distributes heat evenly while reducing bacteria, leaving you with a sleek and smooth look.

When using this iron, carefully part your hair into small sections to avoid tagging because the iron is overcrowded. This also means you can use a lower heat setting to avoid snapping your locks.

These two Fahrenheit irons have a dual voltage of 110-240v making them suitable to use overseas. The ergonomic design makes it not only comfortable to hold but convenient to use. These irons also have a cute pink zebra print that screams chic!
