FACTS: On the Fahrenheit scale, water freezes at 32 degrees F (0 degrees C), and boils at 212 degrees F (100 degrees C). Celcius was previously referred to as Centigrade.

The average temperature in Tokyo is 15.9 degrees Celsius, from an average low of 5.8 degrees Celsius in January to an average high of 27.1 in August.

Common misspellings: celcius, farenheit, farenhait
