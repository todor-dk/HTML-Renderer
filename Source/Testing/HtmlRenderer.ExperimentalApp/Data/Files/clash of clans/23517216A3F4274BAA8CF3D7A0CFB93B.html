To download Clash of Clans, you need one of the following devices to be able to play the game:

  • iPhone 4, 4S, 5 or 5S with iOS 4.3 or higher
  • iPad 2, 3, 4, iPad Air or Mini
  • Any Android phone or tablet running version 4.0.3 or higher

Download for Android

Clash of Clans is free to download for every iPhone and iPad user. To download this amazing game for your iPhone or iPad, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the App Store and search for “clash of clans”
  2. Click on the first result
  3. Now click on the Free button to start the download.
  4. You will probably be asked for your Apple ID password. So enter this to continue.
  5. Congrats! you have now Clash of Clans on your iPhone or iPad

Download Clash of Clans for Android

Installing Clash of Clans on Android is also very simple. To install the game on your Android tablet or Phone, follow the next steps:

  1. Open the Play Store on your device
  2. Click on the search button and search for “Clash of Clans”
  3. Click on the first result
  4. Click on the Install button to start the installation

This should install the game on your phone or tablet.

About Clash of Clans

Clash of Clans is a multi-player, real time , freemium , online strategy game that is developed by Supercell. In the game, players to protect their villages against attackers by building various defensive mechanisms, while at the same time produce an army in order to attack. Players can join clans that can be consisting of 50 players.

It is an addictive combination of strategy and fast-paced competitive battles. Imagine an army of barbarians, wizards, dragons and other powerful warriors. Suit you to a clan of players and work your way up through the ranks, or create your own clan together to fight for. Ownership of the empire Reducing the Gobelins is just the first step – your mission is not over until your clan reigns over all other clans!

The game is funded from in-app sales of “jewels” that can be used in the game to speed up the development progress significantly.

Game rules

You prepares your viking-like people for securing villages of other players, but you can also be attacked by them. The more you build, the more opportunities you have for attack and defense.

The heroes go hand in hand with the new material dark elixir. It is the resource that you need to recruit your heroes. Getting dark elixir is done by drilling into the ground and building a depot and is possible from city hall level 8. But, beginning from Hall level 7 you can build a dark elixir depot where you can store your material that you conquests of other players.
