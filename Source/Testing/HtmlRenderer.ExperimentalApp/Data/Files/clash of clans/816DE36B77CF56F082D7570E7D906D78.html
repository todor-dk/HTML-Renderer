FAQ

What is Clash Royale?

Clash Royale is a fast paced, card based PvP brawler. Collect Clash troops, and use them to wreak havoc on your opponent’s towers!

Was Clash Royale created by the same game team as Clash of Clans?

Several founding members of Clash Royale are also past members of the Clash of Clans game team. However, there are currently no developers working on both Clash Royale and Clash of Clans. We plan to keep these teams separate going forward.

Is this Clash of Clans 2?

Clash Royale is not Clash of Clans 2. We will continue to develop Clash of Clans and Clash Royale as completely separate games, with two distinct teams!

Will the new Clash Royale characters be part of Clash of Clans?

You never know what might happen! We’ll always develop Clash Royale and Clash of Clans as their own games, with separate teams, but we’re very excited about the opportunities to expand and build upon the Clash Universe.

I’m having a problem with Clash Royale, can you help?

Please head over to our support page for assistance with any problems you are having with Clash Royale.

What’s TV Royale?

TV Royale aggregates the latest, greatest Royale replays.

By searching for unusual decks, upset matches and close calls, TVR features only the most heart pounding Arena battles.

Flip to TV Royale for strategies, card previews and regal schadenfreude! Whatever that means.

How does the King keep such perfect facial hair?

Elixir based beard oil. Pine scent.

What’s in the game shop?

Cards: with fresh options daily, the Shop features 3 card classes. Choose between Common, Rare and Epic troops. Prices vary based on strength and multiples purchased.

Check in each “Epic Sunday” for double the selection!

Treasure Chests: want more than one card at a time? Try chests! With three options available, buying in bulk never felt so majestic.

Gems: used to speed up game progress, gems immediately open Chests or purchase shiny new ones!

Gold: use it to upgrade and buy troops!

Where can I find Clash Royale merchandise?

You can find Clash Royale merchandise on the Supercell Shop (BETA)!

How often will cards be added to the game?

We hope to roll out new cards with great frequency, so keep your eyes peeled for fresh faces, spells and short-lived defense buildings.
