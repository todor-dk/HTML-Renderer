Clash of Clans Mod 1.7.10

Author: dragonragedavid123 ❘ August 19, 2016 ❘ 79,047 views

In Clash of Clans Mod 1.7.10 you will find troops and defenses that you see in the ios game Clash of Clans! These troops and defenses are coded just like the real game to attack certain buildings! You also can build your own villages & structures & can set up defenses to keep mobs & barbarians out of your house & village. This Minecraft mod also adds in cannons which will act like turrets and defend your base from raiders! In the future you will be able to raid other villages and will have more defenses, mobs, & structures from Clash Of Clans!

Mod Showcase:

How to install Clash of Clans Mod 1.7.10

  • Download and install Minecraft Forge.
  • Download the mod.
  • Go to %appdata%.
  • Go to .minecraft/mods folder.
  • Drag and drop the downloaded jar (zip) file into it.
  • If one does not exist you can create one.
  • Enjoy the mod.
