    Clash of Clans - The Iconic Strategy Game for Mobile Devices

    There are many games out there in which you can establish a village and make sure that it flourishes and grows, but there's only one Clash of Clans...

     

    Clash of Clans is a strategic mobile game that allows you to go up against other players from all over the world - wherever and whenever you want. All you need to play this strategy game is a working internet connection, but first thing’s first. You can download Clash of Clans for your iPhone, iPad, or Android device completely for free. If you are not interested in spending any money and keeping it free, then the microtransactions can be turned off in the settings of your app.

    Build Your Empire

    In Clash of Clans, you will start on a very small scale. At the beginning of the game, you will establish your village and invest your time and energy into making sure that it constantly grows and flourishes. Your number one incentive should always be to turn your establishment into an impenetrable fortress. Many dangers constantly await your settlement. Goblins are always lurking, hoping to attack at any given time.

    While you may find yourself strong enough to ward off these ghoulish creatures, you will soon notice that it gets quite difficult to fend for yourself in the long run, especially against other clan leaders. In this online game, it is of utmost importance to team up with other players and form a powerful alliance. Only then will you have better chances at fighting against the powerful Goblin King.

    Massively Multiplayer

    Battle it out against millions of other players from all over the world and try to steal their Victory Points. That will make it possible for you to climb the international ranking. In a feature called Clan Wars, you fight with your clan against other teams of players and collect great amounts of bonus loot. The sheer amount of players in the game means that there will always be someone online to raid to get your hands on some of that tasty loot and rewards.

    Train fantastical military units, such as war wizards and dragons, equip your settlement with cannons towers, bombs, and traps, and battle it out against monsters and other players. This strategic mobile game will keep you busy wherever you go. The only thing you have to make sure is that you have a working internet connection and nothing can go wrong.

    Dynamic Game World

    There’s never a dull moment when playing Clash of Clans, as the game is constantly changing and evolving into some even more awesome. Since the launch in 2012, there has been a whole load of new features added, and Supercell are always bringing out new updates. Here are just a selection of the awesome features you can find in Clash of Clans:

    • Battle Replays: If you ever want to rewatch an epic battle that you’ve had with friends or against enemies, then this is a perfect feature to optimize tactics.
    • Competitive Leagues: Here you can earn trophies to show off just how good you are. From Bronze to Legend, there’s a league for all.
    • Clan Wars: At the heart of the game is the social part. The Clan Wars let friends and clans engage in epic battles to determine who is the strongest of them all.

    If Clash of Clans seems like the game for you, then grab your iOS or Android device, and get involved with the mobile game for free by clicking the “Play Now” button.
