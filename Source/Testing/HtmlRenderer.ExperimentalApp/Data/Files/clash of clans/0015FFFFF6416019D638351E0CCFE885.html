Clash of Clans is a popular iOS game created by SuperCell. It has been available internationally on the iTunes store for free since its initial release on August 2, 2012.

Clash of Clans is a strategy game where, like many other strategy games out there, the purpose is to build one's village, unlock different warriors, raid resources from other villages, create a clan and much, much more.
