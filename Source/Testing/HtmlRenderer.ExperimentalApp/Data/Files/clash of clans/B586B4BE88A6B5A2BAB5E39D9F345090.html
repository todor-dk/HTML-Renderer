About the Game
One of the online multiplayer games based on strategy that has taken the internet gaming community by a storm is of course none other than Clash of Clans. It has been developed and launched by Supercell.
In this game you will need to create a community where you have to train your troops so as to attack troops of other players. The winners get to earn gold as well as elixir. Elixir is useful in building defenses that can protect the players from various attacks whereas Gold is the resource used to train or upgrade the player’s troops. The game also has a feature of pseudo-single player in which the players need to attack many villages of goblins and destroy their forts. Before attacking it is advisable to build a thorough army and upgrade to them to the fullest. Training is one of the most important aspect of this game and training can also take a lot of your time.
The troops are categorized into various tiers that consist of barbarians, goblins and even archers. There are heroes that can be trained only once and remain immortal throughout the game. As a player you will need to build mines of gold and storages for gold and elixir. Gold is required to build defense walls to stop protrudes into the player’s village. As in almost every strategy game you will need to have resources to continue and these resources are in the forms of gems. Apart from having Elixir and Gold you will require Gems also to make it big in the game.
