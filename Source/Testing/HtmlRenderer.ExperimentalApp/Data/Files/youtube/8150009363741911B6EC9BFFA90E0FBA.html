Watch the Pope on You Tube

his section allows users from across the world to watch news events about the life of the Church and about Pope Francisc, through the Vatican's page on You Tube.

Provided by Vatican Radio and the Vatican Television Center, these video clips represent a new, quick and open way by which one can educate oneself and become passionate about the happenings of the Church in the world,  through a social and global perspective.
