Robbie's Truck and Auto - Speedometer Testing
Getting a speeding ticket can make your day go from great to awful. The fines, possible court date, and the inevitable rise in insurance premiums can take a toll on anyone. Did you know that ticket may be the result of faulty speedometer calibration?
Robbie's Truck and Auto offers full service speedometer testing and calibration. We can test your speedometer to determine if it is accurate and repair the speedometer if it is not accurate. 

Our speedometer testing equipment was custom made here in Raleigh.

   




Robbie's truck and auto is the Triangles only speedometer testing site. Whether you are in Raleigh, Durham, Cary or anywhere outside of the Triangle call us today to schedule your vehicle's speedometer test!
