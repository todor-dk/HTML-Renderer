How to check TM Streamyx broadband speed

How to check TM Streamyx broadband speed

Last updated on April 16, 2017 by Barzrul

Check TM Streamyx broadband speed with TM Speedometer and Speedtest.net

Learn how to check your TM Streamyx broadband speed with TM Speedometer and Speedtest.net. TM Speedometer is provided and recommended by TM, while Speedtest.net is provided Ookla, the global broadband speed test provider. TM Speedometer is updated and currently supported by Ookla. The test may take a few moments. Your screen will refresh when the test is completed.

This speed test will transfer data between your computer and the selected server. Data will be sent to your computer through your internet connection to test the speed. The speedometer also uploads the proxy data to TM’s server as part of the speed measurement. This allows TM to measure the upload and download speeds and provide absolute measurements of your internet speed.

If the result of the speed test shows your speed is less than 80% of the package you subscribed, you can and recommended calling TM to make a speed complaint. According to their policy, TM guarantees at least 80% of the subscribed speed. And make sure to not use your connection for any other activity while the test is in progress.

How to check TM Streamyx broadband speed

There are two places you can check your Streamyx speed, both are recommended. Since the last update, now TM is using the speed test supported by Ookla. However, you still can try both speed tester and see which one probably give you more accurate measurement.

Method 1: Test TM Streamyx broadband speed with TM Speedometer

Step 1: Make sure your device is connected to the internet. On your device, open any internet browser.

Step 2: Copy the following URL: http://speedtest.streamyx.com.my http://speedtest.tm.com.my/tmspeedtest.html and paste into the address bar and press “Enter”.

Step 3: Click “Begin Test” to start the speed test.

Method 2: Test TM Streamyx broadband speed with Speedtest.net

Step 1: Make sure your device is connected to the internet. On your device, open any internet browser.

Step 2: Copy the following URL: http://www.speedtest.net and paste into the address bar and press “Enter”.

Step 3: Click “BEGIN TEST” to start the speed test.

Note: To get the most accurate speed, I would advise you to repeat the speed test for at least 3 times and calculate the average speed. Make sure to not use your internet connection for any other activity while the test is in progress.
