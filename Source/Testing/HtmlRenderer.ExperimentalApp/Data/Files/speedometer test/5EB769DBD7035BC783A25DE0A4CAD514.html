Thunder Heart Smart Link EFI Owner’s Manual
Thunder Heart 2005 Seminar
Thunder Heart Speedometer Models EA1640, EA1641
Manual
Hello all, I contacted Thunder Heart concerning the functions of the Speedometer they made for the 2007+ Bandera and Judge. The PDF is for a model SIMILAR to the 2607 model that is on the Bandera and Judge.  I believe the main differences are that the one on the Bandera and Judge does not show the tachometer function.  Well I know mine doesn’t have it. Thought this could be helpful to some of your readers. Warrior71459 Thunder Heart’s Larry Hulsey - "Here are the instructions to a similar speedo but the Iron Horse did not have all the calibration functions the I640 has. There were only speed calibration settings, and miles can only be put on the unit with a code generator. If you remove the back half of the handlebar mount there are two access screws that hold it to the front part of the mount. We never made a manual for the [Ed. AIH] speedo because it was an OE proprietary unit."
