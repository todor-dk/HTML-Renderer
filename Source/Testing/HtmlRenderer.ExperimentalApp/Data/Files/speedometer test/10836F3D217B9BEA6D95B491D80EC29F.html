Speedometer not working....
  • Report
  • Follow

Asked by gregw44 Mar 26, 2009 at 09:17 PM about the Chevrolet TrailBlazer EXT

Question type: Maintenance & Repair

I have a 2003 Trailblazer EXT LT.  I bought this vehicle used with a very limited warranty.  I have encountered a problem with the speedometer not working properly or not working at all in some cases.  I called my mechanic about this issue and he had me call the local dealer because they heard about a recall on the trucks for the same thing.  The dealer was unsure if my vehicle would be covered and suggested that I call GM for more information.  GM stated that there is a recall in place... However, not for my vehicle.  It seems that this problem has happened on every other vehicle in this class made by GM but, for some reason, the trailblazer with the exact same problem is unrelated and not covered by this recall.  GM went so far as to tell me that this is the first time that they have heard of this problem and there is nothing that they could do to help.  I found that interesting considdering my local dealer service manager (mind you... i live in a very small town in KY) told me that he sees 2 or 3 of these a week.  That being said, I was responsible for the total cost (with no help from GM) for a vehicle diagnostic (run at the request of GM) ($85) and the replacement instrument cluster and labor for a grand total of $453.26... Again... Out of my pocket with no help whatsoever from GM.  I did a little research online and found that this is a very common occurance.

If you happen to share in my troubles and also have this problem, I have filed a complaint on the NHTSA website (National Highway Traffic Safety Administration)

Here is the website: http://www.nhtsa.dot.gov/

Please go there and file a complaint... If enough people do this, we may be able to create a recall so we could recoup at least some of our losses.

Thank you.
