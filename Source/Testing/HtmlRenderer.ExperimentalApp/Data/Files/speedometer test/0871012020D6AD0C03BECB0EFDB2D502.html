How can you test the speedometer?

Only after fitting on the vehicle, it will give a reading. Let some one follow you maintaining some uniform distance and then compare the readings to know the accuracy. There are contact type and non contact type. The factory made vehicles have their speedometers made exclusively for that model only. If you replace with any other model or make, you are bound to get a wrong reading as the wheel size is different.
