The Ultimate Replacement for the CJ dash
Easily replace your old, broken CJ gauges with modernized Speedhut CJ gauges! Each CJ gauge is water resistant and drops right into your CJ dash. Best of all, our CJ speedometers use GPS technology, require no calibration, and you'll never have to worry about broken speedometer cables again. Our CJ Jeep Speedometer Clusters include a built-in temperature gauge and fuel level gauge, and we offer models that include a built-in Tachometer or Inclinometer that sound a warning when you are about to tip.
CJ Gauge Features:
  • Replaces old CJ gauges and drops right into dash
  • Water resistant
  • GPS technology
  • Requires no calibration
  • Built-in temp & fuel level gauge
  • Built-in tachometer or inclinometer notifies you when you tip

  • Direct replacement of OEM gauge
