    Finally found the speedometer parts under the propulsion section. Called dealer and a paddle kit is available (paddle, spindle, and hardware) for about $30. Still having a hard time figuring out how it works because the paddle and spindle appear to turn without being connected to anything. One pic I found for a similar paddle looks like there is metal on the ends of the paddle fins. Does the spinning create some kind of magnetic field that registers with the sensor? Reason why I was asking about magnet because I was wondering if you can take a magnet and hold it near the sensor to see if the speedometer registers any. Just an idea. Maybe this is why people question how well they work, but when mine was working, it was always within a mile or two when I checked it against several different boat speedometers.
