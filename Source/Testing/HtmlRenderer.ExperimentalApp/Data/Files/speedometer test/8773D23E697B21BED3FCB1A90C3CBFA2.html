All New Mitsubishi Pajero Sport Dakar Test Drive: Great Power To Overcome Hilly Roads!

Ridwan Hanif
19 May 2016

Bali, AutonetMagz – As we’ve got a chance to test drive the new Mitsubishi Pajero Sport Dakar along with 20 other journalists. Mitsubishi invite us to try their new car for 3 days two nights with their prepared itinerary.

To add more info, you could read our other Pajero Sport review in this article:

First Impression Review Mitsubishi All New Pajero Sport Indonesia, Part 1 : Exterior
First Impression Review Mitsubishi All New Pajero Sport Indonesia, Part 2 : Interior

Get in car

Arrived in the airport, we instantly grouped into 7 team with 4 people in each team, and I’m in the 6th group using a Pajero Sport Dakar with Silver color. Inside the cabin, I thought the engine are in off condition, but its not, as the noise and vibration are very smooth for a Diesel engine, as we think its the most quiet Diesel engine compared to its rival, such as Toyota Fortuner or Isuzu MU-X.

As another journalists turn to drive the car, I’ve got a chance to sit at the second row seat, so let’s discussed it, as the 2nd row seats now are more spacious compare to the previous model, its wider, the seats are more comfort, good headroom and legroom. Too bad the 2nd row seats couldn’t be adjusted for sliding movement, despite have a good reclining settings. We do think the 2nd and 3rd row seats of the new Pajero Sport are more spacious than its competitor, Mitsubishi really improve the cabin spaciousness.

Besides a comfortable seats, the suspension also become softer, as seating at the 2nd row seats is just like seating in a MPV such as Mitsubishi Delica, Nissan Serena or Mazda Biante in terms of suspension comfort. A really SUV with MPV tastes, if only they have a captain seat, probably it will be truly felt like a MPV. The sound proof level also improved, although not up to a luxury saloon standard.

Our first trip is from the airport to the Secret Garden Village located on Baturit, a quite far journey with straight roads like Cipali highway, only more narrows. In this road, the new Mitsubishi Pajero Sport felt stable and comfort, what I do like is the wind and tyre noise are minimum in the high speed.

Arrived at the secret garden and seeing the process of making coffee and cosmetics, as we continue the journey, I decide to drive the car, starting the car felt normal, as just press the push start stop button, the engine turn on with a smooth vibration and noise, looking at the idle, the rpm at around 700 rpm, which is a very low rpm, and most of the rough engine vibration happened in this rpm, but it felt ok in the new Pajero Sport.

Press the electronic parking brake then the handbrake are released electronically, as to make it more safe, when releasing the parking brake, we have to press the brake pedal first.

Seating at the drivers seat, it felt comfortable, as it soft, big and fitted with a comfort contour for the long journey ahead. Its not difficult to find a comfortable seating position in this car, as it fitted with an array of seating settings. The speedometer now fitted with a MID screen like the Outlander Sport, its not that attractive but ore than enough to display the information. The steering wheel meanwhile looks luxurious, futuristic and comfortable, but too bad no cruise control, so the right side of the steering wheel felt empty.

One of the details we love from Mitsubishi is the paddle shift who are made of magnesium metal, and its fitted into the back of steering wheel, so it follows the steering wheel movement when you turn the steering wheel. While for the dashboard, its not felt too superior and lack of luxuriousness, especially an application of the faux wooden panel color. As the dashboard design itself basically is an improvement compared to the previous model, not to mentioned the high console model are easy to use, along with a futuristic transmission lever, a center console box who could be functioned as an arm rest, and a cold Dual Zone AC. The material also apply a good plastic with a good fit and finish but too bad no soft touch to elevate the ambiance.

For the engine, the new Pajero Sport Dakar apply a new engine with 4N15 code and a 2.4 liter 4 cylinder of displacement made of aluminum block to make it more lighter. The application of a variable geometry turbo make the car produce 181 Ps on 3,500 rpm and 430 Nm of torque at 2,500 rpm. The best part of this car is not at its engine, but on the new 8-speed transmission who have a close ratio and suitable to be applied to a Diesel engine, considering Diesel engine have a lower rpm compared to a gasoline, as the redline of this car is reaching only 4,000 rpm.

Continuing the journey, from Secret Garden Village, we have to pass a climbing and twisty roads through Buleleng, Gitgit up to Sukaraja. This engine works really well, as passing by a steep hilly roads, the car went through it with an rpm at 1,000 to 2,500 rpm, its rarely the engine works up to 3,000 rpm except I press the throttle more, and manually change the transmission.

In terms of climbing, the new Pajero Sport Dakar have a more than enough torque plus a good transmission to overcome those condition. This new transmission also emphasize more on fuel efficiency by letting the engine works at a lower rpm, but for the 7th and 8th ratio, we felt its too high, as in one chance I drive the car in a speed around 80-90 km/h, the transmission still won’t move to 7th gear manually.

Like I said before, the suspension are more softer and comfortable like a MPV, which affect a bigger body roll, but the steering still felt good and have a nice feedback. One thing that we like is the turning radius is much better with only 5.6 meter, which is the best on its class, while the car itself measures 4.8 meters of length.

As the bigger body roll felt, I still think that its still consider as an acceptable one, indeed if you compared it with other MPV, but the brake dive of the new Pajero Sport felt like the new Innova. As passing a twisty roads with a sharp corners didn’t offer although challenge for the driver and passenger of the new Pajero Sport.

Other good thing is the car equipped with an Active Stability Control who become standard across 3 continents, not to mentioned with a car as big as this car, ASC should be a mandatory features. While the Hill Start Assist and Traction Control also available as standard in the Pajero Sport Dakar. Other good information is Mitsubishi blandly said that the car curb weight is at 1,935 kg or almost 2 tonnes, which is a similar weight as the previous version of Pajero Sport Dakar, looks like the improvement on the aluminum engine and a lighter transmission make it that way.

As went through a twisty road around an hour, we arrived on a gas station in Sukaraja, as according to the guide, the journey will take around 2 hours to go, I decide to seat at the 2nd row and sleep until we arrived at Octagon Menjangan Taman Nasional Bali Barat.

After going back from Octagon at night, KTB Mitsubishi ask the group to stop the car in the night to take photograph in the middle of the forest, as they want to take the rear tail light pictures who according to them looks amazing, and my pictures of many Pajero Sport tail light become a winner in this event.

From Octagon to Naya Gawana Hotel Bali Barat, our road is a bad stone off road condition, I myself are confuse how come I felt a sleep passing those kind of road, probably due to the comfort suspension maybe?

2nd Day

At the 2nd day, we change into the 5th car, a white color Pajero Sport Dakar, who IMHO, the silver color is the most suitable color for the new Pajero Sport, how its felt? just the same as the no 6 Pajero Sport we experienced on the day before.

Departing from hotel, again I sit at the rearm and enjoy the audio, what crazy is, on the car we found a left behind USB, who have a crazy playlist from Ed Sheeran to Chicago who are two generation difference, and Ora Minggir Tabrak from kill the DJ x Lebertaria or Javanese house Music who appears on the new AADC 2 movie.

Speaking at the music, the head unit of the new Pajero Sport Dakar still similar as the previous model, the bass are sufficient, treble and mid also ok, as we think the sound quality is sufficient without any audio update. The head unit use a 6.5 inches touch screen Clarion with Bluetooth, USB, AUX, and DVD Player, plus GPS navigation, and a rear parking camera. For the 4×4 variant we will got a multi around view camera like the bird eye view from the Nissan X-Trail.

After a quite far journey, finally we arrived at the Sukaraja gas station where I drive again, and this time I try to test the car by pressing the throttle much deeper, using a manual mode, and over revving as late changing the gear. Its most of caused by the engine sound is too quiet at a high rpm, so anyone will think that its not the time to change the gears. Despite the transmission is smooth, the engine reacts well when you pressed down the throttle, it surely give you a jolt to your seat every time you do that.

In terms of braking, it also felt good due to the application of 4 discs brake, but in terms of pedal feel, the previous Pajero Sport felt more softer and nicer, this version felt a little bit too sensitive.

Speaking about the turbo whistle, this new Pajero Sport Dakar engine have a quiet to none turbo whistle compared to the old 4D56 engine.

3rd Day

On this day, I didn’t drive at all , as I enjoy the rear seats. The trip from Sanur and stopping at several places before the airport felt quite far. In here we have a chance to pass the Bali Mandara highway before going to airport, but too bad we didn’t explore the car more than 100 km/h to test its performance

Looking at the Google maps, the distance from airport to Octagon Bali Barat through Singaraja are around 151 km, which is 302 km back and forth. With stopping at several routes, and a couple of times the car left behind in engine on position, our car has passed over 350 km. We do felt happy when seeing the fuel indicator still at a half to 3/4 position, as we push the car and passing a climbing roads. Mitsubishi claimed that the car are 17% more efficient, with a fuel consumption of 12.5 km/liter, as we leave it to you to believe it, due to Mitsubishi recently got a problem regarding this fuel consumption claims. But for a car with around 68 liter of fuel tank capacity, and used through a long road during 3 days without any refueling, we think the car fuel consumption are very frugal, as it didn’t consume up to a half of the fuel tank.

Conclusion


As me and my family did use a Pajero Sport for a long time, this car is our favorite cars to travel out of town, as I did bring this car to Bromo, and Lampung in a solo driving, and felt comfortable with the previous Pajero Sport. If the new Pajero Sport customers is just like my family who loves to do a long journey as a family, than the improvement done by Mitsubishi are just right, as it become more powerful, more efficient, more comfortable, better handling, more spacious cabin, and more advance. If only the car have a more luxurious interior, and a better rear design, plus a similar active safety features as the one fitted in Thailand and Australian model, it will become a more value for money.

In overall, the new Pajero Sport Dakar is a good car for you who want to use a 7-seater SUV, who are comfortable to be used by your family for a trip or homecoming journey. Up to now, Mitsubishi said that they manage to get more than 9,000 units of booking order since it first launched several months ago.
