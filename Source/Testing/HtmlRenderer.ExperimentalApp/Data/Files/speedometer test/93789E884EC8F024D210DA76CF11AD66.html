Proudly located in Austin, TX.

Accepting repairs from around the world.

  • Speedometers

    We know speedometers. We will repair and service your speedometer in a professional manner, and do so with a rapid turnaround. We guarantee our work, and customer satisfaction is a high priority with us.

  • Odometers

    Odometer failures are our most frequent repairs. Our team works tirelessly to solve even the most troublesome repairs.

  • Tachometers

    Most of our tachometer repairs are mechanical and performed primarily on BMW motorcycles. Inoperable tachometers are frequently overlooked if the customer has a working speedometer.

  • Dash Clocks

    We are usually successful in bringing your clock back to life with a combination of cleaning, lubrication, and possibly some electrical repair. If our efforts fail we can install a quartz movement. We convert any clock foreign or domestic with the best available components.
