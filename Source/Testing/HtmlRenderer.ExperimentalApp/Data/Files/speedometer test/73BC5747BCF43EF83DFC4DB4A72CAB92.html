Speedometer Calibration Test Hickory, NC

Have you received a speeding ticket, and feel like your incorrect speedometer is to blame?

Did you know you can fight it?!

Remember, it's not just the ticket and court costs...it's your insurance costs for the next 3 years!

Traffic Courts often reduce a speeding ticket based on a "improper equipment plea." With our MD Chassis Dyno, we can perform the most accurate speedometer calibration test around. it measures to the tenth of a mile per hour.

We can test your speedometer calibration accuracy "while you wait" right here at our facility in Hickory, NC. Save hundreds of dollars in speeding ticket fines and insurance costs!

Please note there is an additional fee to actually correct the inaccuracy if any is found.
