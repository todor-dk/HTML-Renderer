Check Yes Internet Broadband Speed

November 21, 2016 By: Admin

Check Yes Internet broadband speed provided on Yes Internet speed test page which also based on Ookla speed tester. Yes Internet is almost the same as P1 but apparently Yes has more coverage than P1 in the West Malaysia. Before subscribing to one of their Internet plans, make sure to check if there is coverage in your area from either one of their dealer or through their website. The speed that you will get is based on your area, plans that you’ve subscribed, the modem placement, number of devices using it, etc. Disconnect all devices that are connected to the Internet except for your laptop or computer and connect the LAN cable to both the modem and your PC. Let’s move on to the steps to check your Yes Internet speed below.

Check Yes Internet Broadband Modem Speed

  1. Open your chosen browser and visit Yes Internet speed test website here or manually type http://www.yes.my/v3/speed-test.do. You will see something like the image below. To proceed, just click the “Begin Test” button. 
  2. While Yes Internet testing your Internet speed, you will be able to see the progress of the “Download speed” and “Upload speed” as soon as it is fetched. Once confirmed, it will show two columns that consists of both results.

There you go. It’s as simple as that. Checking or test the Yes Internet speed once in a while is good especially when it lags a lot. But no worries since Yes Internet is always going to help you with any problem occurred with either their Yes broadband or modem.

How To Check P1 WiMax Broadband Modem Speed

May 11, 2016 By: Admin

Check the P1 WiMax broadband or modem speed on the official P1 Speed Test website which are provided by the famous Ookla. Your P1 Wimax speed depends on the plan you have taken, your location and P1 server as well as the total number of people using it at the same time. Telekom Malaysia(TM) has co-partnered with P1 to deliver a wireless Internet connection to users across Malaysia so you can get a good result out of P1 product. If you don’t, P1 customer service is available to help. P1 Monthly unlimited Internet plans that are available is RM89 and RM139 inclusive GST. There’s also the RM39/month plan but that only provide 5GB of Internet quota. The cheaper you get, the less speed you will get as well. 1MB is good enough for household that consist of two(2) peoples.

Check P1 WiMax Broadband and Modem Speed Test

Step 1 – Visit the P1 WiMax speed test website.

Step 2 – Click the purple button with the “Begin Test” wording to start the speed test. There will be three(3) notifications shown while the process is running – “Testing Latency”, “Preparing Download Test”, and “Preparing Upload Test” as shown on the image above. That image is just to show you how the process is done and we are not using the actual P1 product right now. How To Check TM Streamyx Broadband Internet Speed With SpeedOMeter

December 30, 2015 By: Admin

Check Streamyx Internet speed on TMnet Streamxy speed test page(known as SpeedOMeter) to find out the current speed of your Streamyx Internet line with two(2) simple steps. Your Streamyx Internet speed depends on your location and the total number of users per household. Apparently, you will get slow Internet connection when more people share the same network. Streamyx used different modem such as Innacomm W3400V, Innacomm D2100 and Innacomm W3100 but they do support other type of modems as well which they listed on their Streamyx Certified DSL Modem page. Before you start doing the speed test Internet process, please close all your tabs, other opened browsers, any software updates, downloads and uploads, etc. You only need to open one browser and one tab that needed for the speed test. These are initial step to get an accurate results.

How to check TM Streamyx broadband speed with TM Speedometer

Step 1 – Use any supported Internet browser and go to this link -> http://speedtest.streamyx.com.my. Copy and paste that link to your browser or just click this TM Streamyx Broadband Speed Test page which will open in a new tab.

Step 2 – Next is to click the blue button which has that “Start Speed Test” wording. The process might take a while but usually it only take 1 to 2 minutes. You will know the process is in progress when you see the “Running download test” and “Running upload test” notifications.
