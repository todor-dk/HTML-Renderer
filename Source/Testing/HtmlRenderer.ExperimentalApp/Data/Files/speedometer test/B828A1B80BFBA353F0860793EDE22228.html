Content

This top-of-the-line Teltek digital truck speedometer gauge comes with a brilliant LED display available in either blue, or red.  The LED is auto-dimming for night time viewing, but is bright enough to see under direct sunlight. The aftermarket speedometer gauge bezel comes in black or chrome. Teltek Gauges are made in the USA, and come with a lifetime warranty.

Our Teltek digital speedometer gauge is designed to work with the existing MAG sensor in your truck. The precise gauge reads speed from your sensor ranging from 0 to 150mph. The Teltek digital speedometer gauge comes with an odometer and trip meter. Installation and calibration is simple. Teltek USA gauges come with easy installation instructions and toll free number for installation or operation questions. The stylized chrome or black bezel gauge fits in either a 3 1/8” or 3 3/8” mounting hole in the dashboard. It can also be placed in the radio area, the driver's side pillar, or in a mounting bracket.

Speedometers have an interesting history in mechanics. The first speedometer was invented in 1888 by Josip Belusic from Croatia, and was actually named a velocimeter. They were first seen in automobiles in the 1900’s and then became standard a decade later. Modern digital speedometer gauges are installed on the rear of the truck transmission. The sensor is a flat metal plate with serrations placed between a helix and a sensor that reads magnetic fields. The serrations move between the helix and sensor creating a magnetic the field. The pulse of the magnetic field is turned into a speed reading and sent along to the digital speedometer gauge.

Odometers record the distance traveled by a truck over the lifetime of the vehicle. They can be both mechanical and electronic. The Teltek USA gauge is an electronic digital truck odometer gauge. The mechanic gauges show numbers on six to seven wheels, each wheel having 0 through 9 imprinted on them. The lowest digit in the U.S.A. is usually tenths of a mile, while the highest digit is either tens of thousands, or on newer diesel trucks, hundreds of thousands. Modern electric odometers are more difficult to tamper with, as compared to their mechanical counterparts. All new odometers add mileage driven in reverse to the vehicle total. Trucks are required to have odometers in order to be resold. The digital truck speedometer gauge includes an odometer to make that process easier.

 A trip meter, or trip odometer, is like an odometer, but it can be reset at anytime by the truck owner. Trip meters are sometimes utilized to calculate gas mileage at different truck loads, or to record mileage for mileage reimbursements by trucking clients. When following directions that are indicated by miles between actions, a trip meter can help show when the next action will occur. For these reasons, the trip meter is included on the digital semi speedometer gauge. Install reliable Teltek digital speedometer gauges on your trucks and never worry again about the accuracy of the speedometer, odometer or trip meter.
