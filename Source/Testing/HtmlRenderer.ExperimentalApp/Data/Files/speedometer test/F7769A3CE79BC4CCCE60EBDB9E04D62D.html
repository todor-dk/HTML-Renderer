Welcome to Carolina Speedometer

Carolina Speedometer is your one-stop speedometer supply, repair and service specialist.  Carolina Speedometer has been in business since 2003 and offers same-day service.  We pride ourselves on outstanding customer service and prompt delivery.

We will replace, reprogram, or repair your instrument cluster, or provide parts for the “do-it-yourself” customer.  We use the highest quality and latest part revisions and carry the complete Chevrolet and GMC full-size truck line, including Kodiak and Topkick clusters.  What we don’t have in stock, we will locate for you.  We offer an exchange program or outright purchase.  All repairs are done in-house.

Shipping through UPS, FedEx, DHL, or USPS.

Hyundai and KIA Dealers Welcome for odometer programming.

Carolina Speedometer has a wide variety of clusters in stock.  We will replace or repair clusters, usually on the same day that they arrive for service.
