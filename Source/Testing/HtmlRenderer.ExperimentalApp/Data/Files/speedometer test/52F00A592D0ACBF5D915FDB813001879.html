We carry the Cable-X Speedometer Adapter that allows you to retain your classic cable driven speedometer when using modern day electric VSS signal. This takes the VSS signal and converts it to a cable driven output to your gauge cluster/speedo, allowing your old cable driven speedo to work with an electronic VSS signal that modern transmissions use.
