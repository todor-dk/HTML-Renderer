Livorsi GPS SPEEDOMETERS

Tailored for either race or ski boat application

posted Jun 5th, 2015 at 2:38pm
Comments

Livorsi GPS Speedometers are more accurate than ever with the new GPS antenna with a 10X update. What does this mean? It means your GPS Speedometer will show your true speed faster because the antenna updates 10 time per second. There is a memory recall function. Pointer lag is eliminated and there is no calibration required.

Antenna Features:

Fast 10 Hz 10x update

4 ft harness with ring terminals

NMEA 0183 compliant

No calibration required

For marine, ski boat and automotive applications

For the skier and wake boarder in mind. The GPS Ski speedometer ranges from 0 to 50 MPH with an expanded scale from 10 MPH to 40 MPH. The expanded scales indicates every MPH, accuracy is achieved using GPS technology to determine speed.

GPS speedometers work with the NMEA 0183 antenna that receives satellite signals to determine speed over land. GPS speedometers are more accurate than a regular speedometer using a pitot tube, as there is no need to calibrate for wind current. Plus, this system can be interfaced with GPS plotters and fish finders.
