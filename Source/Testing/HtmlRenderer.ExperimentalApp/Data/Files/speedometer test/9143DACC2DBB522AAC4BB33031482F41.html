    Re: Speedometer stopped working

    I have torn apart 3 O2J'S (transmission code)

    This black ring is what the sensor for the RPM's reads off of (METAL)


    This is the sensor in the transmission that is easily removable to replace. It is located on the back side of the transmission by the shifter bracket. (LEFT SENSOR) This is more than likely your issue. The best bet is to scan with Vag Com and it will tell you what sensor is faulty even if it has no check engine light triggered.


    There are no plastic gears inside the transmission.
