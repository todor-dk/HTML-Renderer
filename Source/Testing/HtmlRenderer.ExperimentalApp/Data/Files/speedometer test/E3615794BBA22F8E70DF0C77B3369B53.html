Customer Reviews

Love the app, HATE the adds
     
by KW0828

When the app works, it's awesome! Simple design, perfect when the phone is dimmed down at night, and accurate with only the slightest GPS delay. The only issue I have is its constant need to switch over to the App Store, every 10-30 seconds. This is a serious hazard for people who rely on this app to know their speed. I drive a lifted 1969 K10, when my speedometer says I'm going 35 I'm really going 62. It would be much cheaper to offer an add free paid version of this once 6 star app (yes I know there's only 5 stars, but that's how much I used to love it), then it would cost me to tear apart my transmission and replace a few sun gears.
I've searched for a paid version, but haven't found this exact one made by you. Please consider making an add free app!!!

I rescind my previous statement. The last time I'd checked the App Store there was no paid version, but I've found it linked to this app under the related tab.

Perfect!
     
by Lucas Lindner

I almost never review apps, but this app is so top notch and to the point I have to make an exception for it! Very simple design and extremely large text so as not to distract you from the road, but I highly recommend a dash mount if you are using this application as your primary speedometer for driving. Very accurate so it can be used to determine if it's time for a vehicle repair or could very well become your main speedometer if your daily driver just isn't worth the price of a repair. Lastly, this app does not drain your battery as much as similar apps although it likely won't be an issue when used on long commutes with a car charger.

Could be so great if this one thing is fixed
     
by CEO Bobo

I'm sorry but I have to delete this app. It's absolutely perfect for riding my motorcycle and knowing what speed I'm going. It's always dead accurate and I love the simplistic and aesthetic digital display. Only problem is the ads open of their own accord, without anything touching the screen. I've had pop up ads and ads that take me completely out of the app. Either way, it renders the app useless because I can no longer see my speed. And it happens A LOT. Any smart motorcycle rider wears gloves while riding, so it is impossible to exit the ad or go back to the app while riding. And even if I'm in a car, it's extremely dangerous to mess around with my phone while driving. So I have to delete this app and rate it with one star because it is just plain UNSAFE to the user. Fix your popup ads and you're golden.
