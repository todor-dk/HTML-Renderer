Welcome to Speedometer Repair Pros, your home to the West Coasts best speedometer repair shop.  We have been operating in Beautiful Manhattan Beach California since 1960!  Our repair technicians can handle your New, Late Model and Classic Car speedometer repair.  With over 50 years of experience and stock we offer our customers a one stop repair facility.

Have your changed tire size or re-geared your axle?  Have you recently received a speeding ticket?  Our vehicle DYNO provides us the ability to accurately measure your vehicles instrument to within +- 0.01%.  We can then recalibrate your vehicle to the correct speed and mileage insuring your next court appearance will not be for speeding!

If you are planning on sending us your speedometer already removed from your vehicle we have numerous bench testing machines to ensure the accuracy of your rebuilt instrument.  Our test equipment works for both Electronic and Mechanical Speedometer/Tachometers.
