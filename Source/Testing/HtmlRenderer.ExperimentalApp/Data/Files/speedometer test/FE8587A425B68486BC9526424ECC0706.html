Our Services

Hamp's Speedometer aims to provide quality service to our customers with the utmost integrity. We understand that our reputation is determined by our quality of work and this is why every job receives the same excellent service we offer to all of our customers.

Speedometer Repair

Every driver needs to know the speed of the vehicle they are operating, and Hamp's Speedometer is here to make sure this is a realization for every driver on the road. We provide services to diagnose and repair any issues that are causing your speedometer to not function as intended.

Hamp's Speedometer also specializes in speedometer calibration. An inaccurate speedometer can be the result of a number of issues, but we are here to fix your problem.

Instrument Clusters

Your speedometer is not the only gauge a driver needs to be functioning while behind the wheel of an automobile. From a light being out to the entire circuit board failing on an instrument cluster, Hamp's is here to resolve your problem and get you back on the road with the comfort of knowing your entire instrument cluster will be functioning properly.

Additional Services

Hamp's is here to provide additional services that can arise outside of the instrument cluster and speedometer gauge. We also service factory radios, mechanical and electronic ratio adapters, GM digital ration adapters (DRAB), distance measuring devices (DMI), fuel injection control modules for heavy duty Ford diesel trucks, and provide speed checks.

Hamp's also builds custom speedometer cables and housings. Ask us how we can transform your driving experience with a custom unit.
