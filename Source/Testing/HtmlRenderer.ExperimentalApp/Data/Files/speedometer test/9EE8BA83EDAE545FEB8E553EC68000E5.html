Description

Driving License Practice Test is for people to prepare the driver license theory test in United Kingdom. Features: ********** Practice & Mock test More than 150+ test questions gathered from formal test, including traffic sign, driving knowledge, laws and etc. Questions will be generated randomly from APP database We encourage you take the practice & test as many times as you can. ********** The Highway Code The Highway Code is in your pocket for daily reading (under Open Government License v2.0) It is well organized and easy to read, easy to navigate to chapters. ********** Bookmark During your practice or test, you can add questions into bookmark. Especially for questions you made wrong answers, bookmark them and save for further review. ********** Good luck to your driver license test! Driving is a Privilege, Not a Right! Be a safe driver, not only for your safety, also for others.
