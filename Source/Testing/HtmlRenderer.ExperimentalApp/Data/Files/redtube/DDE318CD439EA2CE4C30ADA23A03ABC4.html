How to Delete Redtube

March 31, 2015
By: Diana Braun
  • Share
  • Share on Facebook

Redtube is a free adult website that features adult videos. If your computer is infected with the Redtube virus, your Web browser home page is automatically set to Redtube. This is a marketing ploy to get you to visit Redtube and affects both the Internet Explorer and Mozilla Firefox browsers. Change the default Home page of your Web browser to delete Redtube from your computer.

Internet Explorer

Step

Click "Start" located in the bottom-left of the screen. Click "All Programs" and then click "Internet Explorer."

Step

Click "Tools" from the top menu and then click "Internet Options."

Step

Click the "General" tab.

Step

Click and highlight the redtube.com website in the "Address" field.

Step

Type the URL of the desired Home page for Internet Explorer.

Step

Click "OK" to save.

Mozilla Firefox

Step

Click "Start" located in the bottom-left of the screen. Click "All Programs" and then click "Mozilla Firefox."

Step

Click "Tools" from the top-menu and then click "Options."

Step

Click the "General" tab.

Step

Click and highlight the redtube.com website in the "Home Page" field.

Step

Type the URL of the desired Home page for Mozilla Firefox.

Step

Click "OK" to save.
