Yahoo Sign Up Using Computer And Mobile Phone – www.yahoo.com

Deporclick 10:27 pm
 

This post will guide you on yahoo sign up using a computer and mobile phone. With over 281 million registered users, Yahoo mail is the third largest web-based email service provider in the world.

To register yahoo account mean you have to provide a valid mobile number and other information to register your account. Provided you have a yahoo account. you will be able to us Yahoo products like Yahoo Messenger, Fantasy Sports, Flickr and much more.

Now let’s see how to set up yahoo account with your computer and as well with your mobile phone.

Yahoo Sign Up Using Computer

Remember that it’s only on Yahoo official website (www.yahoomail.com) that you can create yahoomail account. Just follow the steps below to get started.

1. With your computer browser, visit the Yahoo Sign up page.

2. Enter your correct information in the required fields.
(Incase the text you entered in the User Name field turns red, it means that the Yahoo ID you choose is not available, and you must try something different.)

3. Once you are done, then in the “Mobile phone number” section, select your country code and enter a valid cell phone number.

4. Click Continue.

5. To verify your mobile number, click Text me a code (or Call me with a code).

6. The account key code will be sent to you on your mobile device. Depending on the method you choose to receive it.

7. Enter the code in the field and click verifyCongratulations! you have successfully sign up for yahoo account, just proceed to login and access your account.

Sign Up For Yahoo Account On a Mobile Device

1. Logon to the mobile registration page (edit.yahoo.com/registration).

2. Enter the information requested on the form.

3. In the “Mobile phone number” section, select your country code and enter a valid cell phone number.

4. Tab Continue to proceed.

5. On the next page, tap Text me a code.

6. You will receive an account key code on the mobile.

7. Enter the account key in the “Code” box and press Verify.

How To Sign In Yahoo Account

Beleive me, the whole process is just as easy as A,B,C. It’s a simple task you can easily complete. With your login details (username and password), you can access all the Yahoo services with a single yahoo account.

On the yahoo login page, type in your yahoo user ID, followed by your password, then hit the sign in button to access your account.
