Yahoo! Mail

By Yahoo! Inc  (Free)

Yahoo Mail is one of the world’s most prominent and long standing webmail services. This free service provides plenty of storage, general features and security measures, which keeps it one step ahead of most of its competitors.

Key features include:

  • Personalised Inbox.
  • Yahoo! Search.
  • Help and support.
  • Secure.
  • Messenger services.
  • Large storage capacity.

Yahoo Mail makes it very easy to sign up, especially so if you already have a Google or Facebook account. This is because Yahoo's email service is compatible with your existing login credentials for those services.

Yahoo Mail lets you personalize your inbox with different themes and lets you organize your mail how you want. You can quickly access the tools you use everyday like your Calendar, Notepad, Contacts, Instant Messages, and Search. The message toolbar and quick actions makes it simple to sort, mark, and delete emails in only a few clicks.

Composing emails with Yahoo! Mail’s interface is a walk in the park. It lets you create and enhance your messages with beautiful link previews and formatting options. It also has Dropbox and Flickr integration that lets you attach files, documents and photos quicker than ever.

Yahoo Mail gives you the most free storage of any email provider. With 1TB of free space, you’ll never have to worry about deleting emails to save space again. The search functionality of Yahoo! Mail is also powerful. It makes it easy to locate the exact message, document or photo that you need, no matter when it was sent or received.

Overall, Yahoo! Mail has everything you could need from an webmail service. It has top-notch security scanning tools that are provided by Symantec. It has mobile apps on multiple platforms, and an instant messenger service too. You can see why Yahoo Mail provides hundreds of millions of people worldwide with free email accounts.
