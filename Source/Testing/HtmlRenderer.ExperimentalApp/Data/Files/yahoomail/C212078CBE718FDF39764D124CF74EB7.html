Yahoo Registration in 4 Steps

STEP 1: You need to visit the yahoo registration page (I will show you the simplified way of getting this done) Visit www.yahoo.com or www.yahoomail.com. This will take you to another url which looks like this https://login.yahoo.com/config/login_verify2?&.src=ym&.intl=us.

Allow page to fully load and click the Sign up tab. (You won’t miss it)

STEP 2: Click Signup which will redirect you to a form-like page known as the yahoo registration page. Input all necessary details like your full name, DOB, desired email address, etc.

STEP 3: Once you have filled all relevant spot and accept Yahoo Inc. terms of service, you can click the send button.

STEP 4: A completed and successful registration will take you to your mail box with a congratulation message. If your registration was not successful, you will have to be redirected back to the yahoo registration page to input information’s you omitted.

How to Change your Yahoo Password?

Finally, you will need this feature due to many scenarios. Just follow this simple step.

  1. Firstly, Visit the Yahoomail.com sign in page
  2. Secondly, Click Account Information – You have full access to change your account details here including your email preferences amongst others.
  3. Thirdly, find the “Change Password bar” and click the same
  4. Fourthly, type in and confirm your new password.
  5. Finally, continue by submitting your new password.

How to Delete a Yahoo mail Account

In conclusion, if you so wish to delete or deactivate your yahoomail account, the following tutorials will assist you:

  1. Log-on at https://edit.yahoo.com/config/delete_user?.intl=in
  2. Type in your password in the bar provided
  3. Hit the “terminate this account” bar (make sure you are logged on to the main account you want to terminate.
  4. You will be given a 90 days grace to either re-activate your account or to have it permanently deleted.
