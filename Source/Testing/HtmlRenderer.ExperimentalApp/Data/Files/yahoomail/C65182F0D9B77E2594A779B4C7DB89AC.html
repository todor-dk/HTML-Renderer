Best Answer:  To change your Yahoo password. When you sign into your account click on “Account Info” or on your email page click on your ID at the top of the page and click on “Edit my Info“. You will be asked to enter your password to confirm it is you.

Click on "Change Password"
Enter your password in the first box.
Enter your new password in the second box.
Confirm your new password in the third box.
Click the "Save" button. Hope this helps!!!

  • Change your Yahoo password

    Go to the Yahoo Account Information page. ( https://edit.yahoo.com/config/eval_profi... )
    Click Account Security | Change password.
    Enter and confirm your new password, then click Continue.
    - You'll see a message that confirms your password change.
    Click Continue to finish.
