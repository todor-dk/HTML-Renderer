How do I Set Up Free Storage for My Galaxy S6 on Microsoft OneDrive?

Your device does not support a microSD card, but you have access to 15GB of free cloud storage with Microsoft OneDrive. Samsung also offers 2 years of free access to an additional 100GB of OneDrive storage*. *Due to carrier limitations, users who do not have OneDrive preloaded on their device will not have access to this offer.

Download and Install OneDrive

It may be necessary to download and install OneDrive from Google Play Store.

If the OneDrive application is not preloaded on your mobile device, you can download it from Google Play Store.

  1. From the Home screen, touchApps.
  2. TouchPlay Store.
  3. Touchand search forOneDrive.
  4. TouchOneDrive.
  5. TouchINSTALL.
  6. Review the application permissions and touchACCEPT.
  7. Once the download and installation is complete, touchOPEN.

Sign Up for OneDrive

If you do not have a Microsoft account, you will need to sign up before using OneDrive.

Once you have downloaded and installed OneDrive, or if the application is preloaded on your device, you can sign up for anaccount.

  1. From the Home screen, touchApps.
  2. TouchOneDrive.
    Note: The OneDrive shortcut may be located in the Microsoftfolder.
  3. Swipe the screen to the left to learn more about OneDrive.
  4. TouchSign up now.
  5. Enter the required information and touchCreate account.
  6. A verification code will be sent to the email address you registered. Enter the code and touchNext.
  7. TouchOK to get an extra 15GB of free storage from OneDrive, or touchNot now to continue to your OneDrive account.

Sign In to OneDrive

Once you have signed up for OneDrive, or if you already have a Microsoft account, you can sign in to your account.

  1. From the Home screen, touchApps.
  2. TouchOneDrive.
    Note: The OneDrive shortcut may be located in the Microsoftfolder.
  3. TouchSign in.
  4. Enter youremail addressand touchNext.
  5. Enter yourpassword and touchSign in.

Turn On Camera Backup

Once you have signed in to OneDrive, you can set up your cloud storage preferences.

  1. From the Home screen, touchApps.
  2. TouchOneDrive.
    Note: The OneDrive shortcut may be located in the Microsoftfolder.
  3. Touch>Settings.
  4. TouchCamera backup.
  5. Touch theCamera backupslider to turnon the feature.
  6. TouchUpload usingand chooseWi-Fi onlyorWi-Fi and mobile network.
  7. Mark theIncludevideosandUpload only while charging checkboxes if you want to turnon these features.
