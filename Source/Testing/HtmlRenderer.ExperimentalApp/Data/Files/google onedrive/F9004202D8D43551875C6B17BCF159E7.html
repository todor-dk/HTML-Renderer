  • Google Drive and Android, even better together

    With Drive now part of Android, integrating Drive services into your Android application has never been easier. With sync and offline support, we make it easier for you to focus on writing great apps. Simply read and write files and we’ll handle the details.

  • Reach millions with Gmail attachments in Drive

    Hundreds of millions of people use Gmail every day to send and receive files of all types. With Drive directly in Gmail, developers can have their app recommended to open Gmail attachments. Simply integrate your app with Drive and create a Chrome Web Store listing and your app could become someone’s new favorite.
