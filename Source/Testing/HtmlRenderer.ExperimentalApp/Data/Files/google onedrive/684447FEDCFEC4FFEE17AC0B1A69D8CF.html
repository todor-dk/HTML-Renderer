About OneDrive

Microsoft OneDrive is a file hosting service which provides its users the possibility to synchronize files. Access OneDrive is possible for PCs as well as mobile devices. Stored files can be shared with the user’s contacts or publicly. For companies, Microsoft additionally offers One Drive for Business. The competitive products of OneDrive are Apple’s iCloud, Google Drive, ownCloud and salesforce.com.

About Google Drive

Google Drive is a file hosting service which provides its users the storage and share of files as well as the collaborative editing of documents. Google Drive includes Google’s office suite Google Docs, Sheets, and Slides. With this bundle you can create the mentioned documents. According to Google, Google Drive was having 240 million active users in October 2014.
