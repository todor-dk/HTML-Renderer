Google Drive and OneDrive
Presented by Shannon Holden, Assistant Principal, Republic Middle School, MO

Our February TechTools webinar featuring Evernote & Dropbox was one of the largest webinars of 2014.  During the webinar, many of the participants were asking “What about Google Drive?” and “What about OneDrive?”  Join Shannon Holden as he compares and contrasts the features and functionality of these two cloud-based storage platforms.
