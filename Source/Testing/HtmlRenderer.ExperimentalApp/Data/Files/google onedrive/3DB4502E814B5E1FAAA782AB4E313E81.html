Main Content

This tutorial assumes you have the data from Google Drive that you want to upload to OneDrive at SIU.  If not, please see this tutorial.

  1. The files on your computer should be extracted to a folder or list of files that looks like the following:
  2. Sign into your SIU E-mail account at http://office.siu.edu.  You will see your e-mail inbox, then click on the "tile" box in the top-left corner
  3. Click on OneDrive
  4. Click on "Upload" at the OneDrive screen. 
  5. Click on Folder (or, if you didn't create a specific folder of your files to upload, click on Files)
  6. Navigate to the location of your folder and click on it.  If you have multiple folders, CTRL + left click on each one.  Then Click 'OK'
  7. After the files have sucessfully uploaded, you should see them in your OneDrive folder. This can take minutes or longer depending on the amount of files you have uploaded.
  8. Once complete, you should be able to see them in OneDrive.  You may have to reload the page.

Congratulations!  You have now sucessfully uploaded your documents to OneDrive at SIU!
