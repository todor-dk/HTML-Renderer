Google Drive to OneDrive Migration

CloudFuze makes bulk file transfers easy. Once you register with CloudFuze and sync your existing cloud storage accounts, such as Google Drive and OneDrive, you can migrate files to and from different clouds.

Start Your Free Trial >

Below, learn how to transfer files from Google Drive to OneDrive in 3 simple steps.

Choose it, Move it, Check in.

Step 1:

Click the ‘Migrate’ button located on the left-hand toolbar of your CloudFuze dashboard. This will take you to the file transfer page where all of your integrated cloud accounts should appear.

Step 2:

Under ‘Source Cloud,’ click the Google Drive account containing the files you want to move.Once you click the icon, CloudFuze will show you all of your Google Drive files. Check each box corresponding with the folders, files, or photos you want to transfer. If you enter a folder and need to go back for additional selections, click the black arrow located above your opened list of files.

Then, under ‘Destination Cloud’ on the right-hand side of the dashboard, click on your OneDrive account. Select one folder in OneDrive where you would like to place the transferred files.

Step 3:

Once you make your selections, click, ‘Migrate.’ A new window will pop up asking you who to notify by email when the file transfer from Google Drive to OneDrive is complete, as well as an option to delete the source files. Make your selections and click, ‘Confirm Migration.’

You’re done!

After you click to confirm, CloudFuze will take you to the ‘Migration Reports’ page where you can check on the status of you file migration or wait for the confirmation email.
