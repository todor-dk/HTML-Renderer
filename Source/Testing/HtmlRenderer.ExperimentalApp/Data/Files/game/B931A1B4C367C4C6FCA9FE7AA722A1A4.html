Dice Masters Batman Draft – 7/8

Posted on July 5, 2017 by Game Universe
When:  Saturday, July 8th at Noon
Where:  Game Universe – Franklin
Entry: $15
What:  Players will be given twelve packs. They’ll open them six at a time ending up with two stacks of twelve cards and sorting their dice in the middle of the table. Players pick a card then pass their stack to the right until they’re out of cards. Repeat the process with their other stack of cards but passing to the left. After which, players obtain 1 corresponding die per drafted card and build a team out of 8 characters and/or non-basic actions they drafted. Bring two basic actions from any set to complete your team and come have fun!
Prizes:  There will be a pick of prizes from the remaining packs after the tournament, plus players’ weekly promo participation picks.
