Create a Game in Cogswell's Game Studio Course

Check out Wizard Prison, a project completed by the on-campus, project-based game devleopment course Game Studio. Throughout the Game Design & Development Program, you will learn to create engaging game and level design, experience industry-like production pipelines, and understand project management. You will build your skills in 3D modeling, animation, rigging, lighting and texture mapping to create believable worlds, characters and props. Explore your passion for creating video games - Cogswell will help you Create Something Extraordinary.

Hear What Students Are Saying

The Student Perspective on the Game Studio project "Tangram Jam", with Sean Langhi, Engineering and Design Lead, and Bugi Kawaga, Art Lead. "Tangram Jam" which is a Unity based action/puzzle game for mobile devices. During the project, students got first-hand experience with collaborating with students of different personalities as well as different art, engineering and design skill sets. Together they created a production-quality game for a real client that will be available on the App Store and Google Play.
