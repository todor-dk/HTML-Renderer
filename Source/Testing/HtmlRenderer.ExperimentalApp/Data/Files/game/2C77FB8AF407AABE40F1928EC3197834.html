Balance 1.2.8 + Characters + Event Follow-Up
Tuesday, September 1, 2015 at 02:37 by Kostas

Yo, guys. A bunch of stuff today. Let me start by saying August was a busy month for us outside the game, but we didn't want to let it go without an update. So let's start with some things. First, balance update. Second, two new characters. Third, a temporary event character.

 

For those who don't remember, earlier this year the mods held a contest in the forums, and the winner of which was eligible to work with us to enter a temporary event character into the game. The contest was the 1st Season of the Naruto-Boards Official Character Contest, and Sahedy won it. Sahedy submitted to us Mecha Naruto, and for the next week or two, Mecha Naruto will be in the game. We made two minor changes to the character, but they are still Sahedy's own. Note: Event characters are subject to swift balance updates without notice if there is something horribly wrong with them.

Edit: REMINDER! We recycle old skills, so if you see some old and odd pictures for skills, clear your browser image cache, or your temp internet files to see the new ones. 

 

Nara Shikaku
The father of Nara Shikamaru, and the head of the Nara clan, Shikaku is a cool-headed Jounin of Konoha who will go to any lengths to protect his friends and family. In battle, Shikaku is merciless, combining his impressive shadow imitation skills with his unparalleled intellect to grab victory for his team.

 


Uchiha Shisui

A member of the prestigious Uchiha clan, and the best friend of Uchiha Itachi; Shisui is a highly trained ninja that is utterly devoted to Konoha. Shisui is one of the few known ninjas capable of using one the most powerful of genjutsu: Koto Amatsukami.

 


Mecha Naruto is a mechanized replica of Uzumaki Naruto; it is a mindless savage, attacking everything in its path. As a machine, he can manipulate his body in a variety of manners, even assuming a three-tailed form of its own. Created by: Sahedy

 

Balances

  • Nerfs: Hyuuga Hiashi
  • Reworks: Zaji, Uzuki Yugao, Yamanaka Fu
