inXile Scores $4.5M for Open World Survival RPG VR Title

inXile Entertainment has received an investment infusion from Japanese publisher Gumi to the tune of $4.5M. The funds are to assist with the development of an "open world survival RPG VR" game something along the lines of ARK: Survival Evolved. The game will be based on one of inXile's existing worlds such as Torment: Tides of Numenera, Wasteland or The Bard's Tale.
