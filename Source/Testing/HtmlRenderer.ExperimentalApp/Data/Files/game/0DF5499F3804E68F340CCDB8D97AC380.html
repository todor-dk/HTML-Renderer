Our Goals
At Game 7 Basketball, we are concerned about giving quality teaching to players who are serious about the game.  Our expert staff will drill the players in the physical and mental fundamentals of basketball by focusing on building the new skills they need to gain the confidence necessary to succeed on and off the court.  Our staff will demand the best from each and every player - there is no try - there is only "DO".

    "If you're not making mistakes, then you're not doing anything. I'm positive that a doer makes mistakes." - John Wooden
  2. Dedication
    “The difference between a successful person and others is not a lack of strength, not a lack of knowledge, but rather a lack in will.” – Vince Lombardi
  3. Success
    “The will to succeed is important, but what’s more important is the will to prepare.” – Bobby Knight
