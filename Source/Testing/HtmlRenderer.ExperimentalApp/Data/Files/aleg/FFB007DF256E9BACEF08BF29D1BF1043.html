alegar

al·e·gar

 (ăl′ĭ-gər, ā′lĭ-)
n.
Vinegar produced by the fermentation of ale.
[Middle English, blend of ale, ale; see ale, and vinegar, vinegar; see vinegar.]

alegar

(ˈeɪlɪɡə; ˈæ-)
n
(Elements & Compounds) malt vinegar
[C14: from ale + vinegar]
