The Holy Empire of Aleg is a massive, environmentally stunning nation, ruled by John God Emperor of Mankind with an iron fist, and notable for its frequent executions, digital currency, and infamous sell-swords. The hard-nosed, cynical, humorless population of 4.139 billion Alegians are ruled by a mostly-benevolent dictator, who grants the populace the freedom to live their own lives but watches carefully for anyone to slip up.

The large, corrupt, well-organized government juggles the competing demands of Defense, Education, and Industry. It meets to discuss matters of state in the capital city of Aleg City. The average income tax rate is 97.0%.

The frighteningly efficient Alegian economy, worth 760 trillion Lengs a year, is driven entirely by a combination of government and state-owned industry, with private enterprise illegal. The industrial sector, which is fairly diversified, is dominated by the Information Technology industry, with significant contributions from Arms Manufacturing, Book Publishing, and Beef-Based Agriculture. Average income is an impressive 183,788 Lengs, and distributed extremely evenly, with little difference between the richest and poorest citizens.

Movie references are legally admissible in court, three-year-olds have tantrums because they don't want to be drainage technicians when they grow up, political scientists despair as the national mascot election eclipses all others in voter enthusiasm, and political talk shows often have children as guests whenever they need commentary on world religions. Crime, especially youth-related, is totally unknown, thanks to a capable police force and progressive social policies in education and welfare. Aleg's national animal is the Unicorn, which is also the nation's favorite main course, and its national religion is Cult of John God Emperor of Mankind.

Aleg is ranked 12,730th in the world and 143rd in 10000 Islands for Largest Agricultural Sector, scoring 5,739.28 on the Mu-Bah-Daggs Productivity Index.
