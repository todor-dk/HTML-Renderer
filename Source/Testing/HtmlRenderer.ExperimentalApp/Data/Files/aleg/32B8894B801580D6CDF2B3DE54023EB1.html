ALGE S4 TIMER
ALTERNATIVES
OVERVIEW
The venerable Alge S4 Timer continues to provide the hard-to-beat combination of exceptional reliability, ease-of-use, and economical pricing in a compact desktop form factor.
DESCRIPTION
  • Extremely accurate timebase based on Alge's temperature-compensated quartz crystal
  • Three timers in one! S4 features three completely independent timing channels, so you can simultaneously time three competitors on the same course without the need for computers or software
  • Excellent when used as a timebase in conjunction with a PC-based timing solution
  • 18-channel capability
  • RS-232 output-capable
  • User-selectable baud rates for compatibility with numerous aftermarket software packages
  • Supports scoreboard-output
  • huge memory -- up to 8,000 splits
  • Printers: works with Alge printer models P4 & P5, and Seiko DPU-411/414
  • High tolerance to weather extremes -- rated from -20C to 55C (-4F to 122F), but we've used this device in -40C (-40F) with no problems, though we recommend the printer be turned off to conserve batteries
  • Built-in speech amplifier for use with Alge Q34 headsets
  • Works with either internal rechargeable batteries or alkalines (6 x "D")

PRODUCT CERTIFICATION
This timer has been officially homologated for use in international competition by the International Ski Federation (FIS). In order to comply with the certificate, the timer must be connected to a functioning printer.
