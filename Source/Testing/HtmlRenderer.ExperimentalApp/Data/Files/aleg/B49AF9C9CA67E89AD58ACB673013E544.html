Alex G is a Colorado-raised, LA-based singer-songwriter, dreamer, visionary, and leader.

 

After years of writing music and the release of two EPs and a self-titled album, Alex released her Share Your Story EP, in which Alex wrote songs about her fans stories.The EP marks a massive shift in Alex's personal journey and artistic presence in the industry.

 

With honesty and courage, she handcrafts her songs to inspire love & hope in her listeners.
