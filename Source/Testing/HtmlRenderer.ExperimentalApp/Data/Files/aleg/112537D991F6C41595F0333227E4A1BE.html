Vintage Glamour

You’ll find classic AGA good looks and a multiple oven design in the artful AGA Legacy 36″ Dual Fuel Range. A versatile cooktop and flexible electric ovens indulge those with the highest culinary standards. With vintage style and modern convenience features, it’s easy to see why this AGA out-looks and out-cooks other pro-style ranges.

5 GAS BURNERS
3 OVENS
7 COOKING MODES

3 Separate Ovens Totaling 4.5 cu. ft.

  • 7-Mode Multifunction Oven
    Offers ultimate flexibility with 7 different cooking modes. Turn the convection off, leave it on or go somewhere in between. You tailor the oven’s performance to your needs by selecting from the convection bake, convection broil, fan assist bake, conventional bake, top brown or base heat modes.
  • Programmable Convection Oven
    Continuously circulates hot air through the oven cavity with true European convection for faster, more even cooking. Features 1.8 cu. ft. of capacity, with four heavy duty oven racks and a removable plate warmer.
  • Powerful 8-pass, 2,300-watt Broiler
    Ready to cook in 5 minutes! The Full Broil mode provides complete coverage using the full eight passes. The Half Broil mode uses the four right-hand passes and is perfect when cooking for one.

My wife and I bought your AGA Legacy a few years ago and have greatly enjoyed it. Best range we have EVER bought. Last Thanksgiving cooked four casseroles and the turkey all at once…wonderful. Not a bit left over.

Sebe W., Mississippi

5-Burner Cooktop
Features sealed gas burners, matte porcelain finish burner caps and continuous cast iron grates. Ranging from a low simmer to a 15,000 BTU burner, the AGA Legacy cooktop is versatile in power and performance. Available in natural gas with an LP conversion kit included.

Clock Timer
Delayed baking feature allows you to program the convection oven. Includes a locking feature to prevent little hands from accidentally turning on the ovens.

HandyRack™ Shelf
A signature Legacy feature. No more pulling out hot, heavy racks to check foods. The HandyRack is a removable shelf located in the door of the left multifunction oven. Adjustable to two positions, this rack provides safety and convenience allowing you to place dishes where they are easiest to access.

36″ Legacy Dual Fuel Range 
DIMENSIONS 35 1/2″ W x 25 5/8″ D x 35 3/8″ H.
WEIGHT 315 lbs. – Shipping Weight 343 lbs.
FUEL Natural gas or LP (LP Conversion Kit included)
ELECTRICAL REQUIREMENTS 7.4 kW @ 240 volts. Your electrician must consult local codes to determine electrical requirements.
COLORS Stainless Steel, Black, Cranberry, Ivory, Vintage White
MATERIALS Solid steel chassis with high gloss enamel
OVENS
  • 7-Mode Multifunction Oven with convection, fan assist, fan assisted broil, conventional, browning, base heat or defrost mode
  • Programmable Convection Oven
  • 8-pass, 2,300-watt Broiler
CONTROLS
  • Independent control of all cooking zones including 5 gas burners and 3 ovens.
  • Time delay programming function when you need timed or delayed cooking.
BURNERS Natural Gas (LP Conversion Kit included):

  • 2 x 9,200 BTU
  • 1 x 5,000 BTU
  • 1 x 15,000 BTU
  • 1 x 12,000 BTU
HOOD RECOMMENDATION Aga 36″ 600 CFM Vent Hood
OPTIONS Handle Sets:

  • Classic handles supplied with Vintage White and Stainless Steel models.
