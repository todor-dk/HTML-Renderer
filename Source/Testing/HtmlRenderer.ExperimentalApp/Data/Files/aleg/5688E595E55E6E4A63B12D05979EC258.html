Aleg On (1962-) - Composition III
Aqua engraving in colour
signed, numbered 38/40 in pencil by the artist.
Size: 49 x 63 cm.
Titled in the work in pencil
Dry stamp AQUASTAMP PARIS
Very good condition. Shipping flat.
Sent by international mail with tracking.

This technique is characterised by the simultaneous creation of the paper and the etching. The artist engraves and sculpts his drawing in low relief in a slate of wax, wood, metal, linoleum...
A flexible mould is created from this base. The pulp is prepared and placed on a base upon the press. The mould is directly coloured and pressed on the pulp. The pressure is gradually increased up to several tons.
Then comes the very long drying process. Each print is retouched by hand.
