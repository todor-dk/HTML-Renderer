    Who we are...

    Wishes Dance Studio is committed to offering the finest dance education and instruction emphasizing on proper technique, self discipline, coordination and musicality while building strong, self confident dancers. We pledge to provide a positive and nurturing environment, where our experienced, educated, and dedicated teachers will foster the passion for dance and encourage creativity with every Wishes student. We promise to always follow our number one rule......HAVE FUN!

    What we offer...

    Pre-School Classes

    Pre-School classes include Little Dancing Friends, Tiny Tappin Feet, Broadway Babies, Combo Tap/Ballet, Combo Jazz/Hip Hop and Petite Ballerina. Classes are designed for students ages 18 months to 6 years.

    Youth, Teen, and Adult Classes
