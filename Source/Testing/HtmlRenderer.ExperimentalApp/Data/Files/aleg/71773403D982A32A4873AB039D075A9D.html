Aleg is the capital of the Brakna Region, Mauritania. It is located at 17°3′0″N 13°55′0″W﻿ / ﻿17.05000°N 13.91667°W﻿ / 17.05000; -13.91667Coordinates: 17°3′0″N 13°55′0″W﻿ / ﻿17.05000°N 13.91667°W﻿ / 17.05000; -13.91667.

The town includes the village of 'Elb Jmel.

Aleg is known as the birthplace of former President of Mauritania, Sidi Ould Cheikh Abdallahi.
