Romanian[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Latin allegere, present active infinitive of allegō (“choose; recruit”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [aˈle.d͡ʒe]

Verb[edit]

a alege (third-person singular present alege, past participle ales) 3rd conj.

  1. (transitive) to choose, to select
  2. (reflexive, accusative) to be chosen
  3. to elect
