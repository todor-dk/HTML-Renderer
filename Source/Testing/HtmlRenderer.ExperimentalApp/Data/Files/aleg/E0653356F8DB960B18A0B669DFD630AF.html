I’m super excited to announce that Sydney will be hosting a special screening of Late Night Work Club’s new anthology “Strangers”. It will include my new film Born in a Void as films from Sean Buckelew, Loup Blaster, Jeanette Bonds, Caleb Wood, Nicolas Menard and Kirsten Lepore (and some juiiiicey extra animation from Charles Huettner). 

The Sydney screening will also see special guest appearances from Greg Sharp, Dave Carter, Peter Milliard and Jennifer Zheng

We’ll be hosting it at the artist run Red Rattler Theatre, a short walk from Sydenham Station. Its a free event, RSVP -here- 

Tuesday 29th November
Doors at 7pm
Red Rattler Theatre, Marrickville
