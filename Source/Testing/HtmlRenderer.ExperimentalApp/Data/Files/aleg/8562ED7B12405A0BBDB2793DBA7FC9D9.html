FASHION FORWARD, FAIR TRADE CLOTHING - HELPING EMPOWER WOMEN IN POVERTY

Liz Alig produces fair trade clothing and believes that slow, recycled fashion is the key to helping communities in developing countries out of poverty. We use recycled, handwoven, or high quality sustainable textiles in our designs. We think clothing should be easy to wear and effortless - which is why we love pockets!
