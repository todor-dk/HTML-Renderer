      14. Mutec MC-3+

        Aleg replied to *progear's topic in DAC - Digital to Analog Conversion

        I Think you are mixing up word clock generators and 10 MHz reference clocks. The single purpose of a 10 MHz reference clock is to offer a more stabel clock signal to a word clock generator. So 10MHz --> Antelope LiveClock and then using two word clock outputs --> RedNet --> Mutec is the standard way of using a 10 MHz clock in an audio chain. Because the Mutec is a Word Clock generator in itself, it can also accept a 10 MHz input, but apparaently the Antelope LiveClock is a better word clock generator than the internal one of the Mutec
        • August 6, 2016
