Dogology

Dogology is our belief that if you take a pet into your life, you have an obligation to fulfill this life by feeding their mind, body and soul. We have studied, adored and appreciated dogs since we were children and it is this life-long love of dogs that has led us to create our “Dogology”.  

What's in Store

  • Wholesome food, treats, supplements, nutritional guidance and therapeutic diet plans geared to whole body health for your pets
  • Award-Winning Dog training in private and group lessons, puppy playgroups, pack walk & more
  • Practitioners certified in canine massage and acupressure
  • Dog adoptions through local, reputable rescue organizations, including Boot-N-Kit and Dog Star Rescue
