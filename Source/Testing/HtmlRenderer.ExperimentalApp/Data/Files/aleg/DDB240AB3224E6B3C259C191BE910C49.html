Summer Camp Mentoring Opportunities

The Summer Mentoring program at Shake-A-Leg Miami provides an opportunity for high school students to interact and mentor children of all abilities. Mentors participate in diverse activities that reinforce school year learning in a fun, thought provoking, and interactive environment. Grow your mind and exercise your body through subjects such as literacy, science, art, music, physical fitness, sailing, outrigger canoe, swimming and more. We look forward to receiving your application and welcoming you aboard!

 

MENTOR EXPECTATIONS

 

  • SALM is looking to you, Mentors, as LEADERS for our participants:
    • Take responsibility for your position as a Mentor
      • Be on time (call if you are going to be late)
      • Fulfill participation commitments
      • Follow SALM rules
    • Mentors (not parents) are responsible for calling with questions and bringing up any issues that may occur or be necessary to resolve.
      • You are benefiting from the program and we want to interact directly with you.
  • Mentors need to set examples for participants:
    • Attire must follow SALM guidelines and be safe
    • Behavior must follow SALM guidelines, and be energetic and inspiring
      • Inappropriate behavior (including not following rules, acting out, not participating energetically and/or engaging in program assignments, and others as may occur) will not be tolerated and may cause termination of your Mentorship.
    • Have fun! and the participants will have fun, too.
  • Benefits of being a Mentor:
    • Rewards of helping and inspiring someone younger than yourself
    • Community Service Hours
    • Learn from the organization, and especially from the children you are helping
    • Developing contacts who may serve as references
    • Training in areas such as life skills, interpersonal skills, program specific skills, job skills
    • Possibility of future employment with the organization
  • Mentors need to have full knowledge of and follow SALM SAFETY and SECURITY rules at all times.
    • Study Safety handout and be prepared to be tested on it at any time during your Mentorship
    • Any violation of the Safety and Security rules can cause immediate termination of your Mentorship
    • Training is a requirement for certain program areas and interns without those specific trainings will not be able to work in those areas until proper training is achieved.
