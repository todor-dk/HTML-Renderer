ALEG36DFCRN AGA 36" Legacy Dual Fuel Range with Broiling Oven - Cranberry
Explore the features of the ALEG36DFCRN dual fuel range from AGA

The Aga Legacy ALEG36DF range is more moderately priced than most 36-inch professional ranges, the Aga Legacy 36 gives serious cooks something new to look at. No other professional range has this much functionality in such a small footprint.

Features Sealed 5-burner gas cooktop
  • Offers heat output from 850-15,000 BTU
  • Integral 15,000 BTU wok burner
  • Field convertible to LPG

    Dedicated Broiling Oven
  • 8 pass, 2300 watt element
  • Preheats in just 5 minutes

    7- Mode Multifunction programmable 2.2 cu. ft. oven
  • Convection defrost - fan forced air without heat
  • Convection bake - rear convection element with fan forced air
  • Convection broil - top element with fan forced air
  • Fan assist bake - top and base element with fan forced air
  • Conventional bake - top and base element heat
  • Top brown - top element heat only
  • Base heat - bottom element heat only
  • Removable HandyRack™ in oven door
  • 5 interior oven rack positions

    Programmable convection 1.8 cu. ft. vertical oven
  • 2500 watt element with fan
  • Can be programmed for start/start operation
  • Key lock safety feature

    Range ships Natural Gas. Propane conversion kits are included but must be installed by a certified gas fitter.

    Approximate Dimensions (Inches)
    Height: 35 3/8
    Width: 35 1/2
    Depth: 25 5/8

    Consult product installation guide for exact dimensions.
