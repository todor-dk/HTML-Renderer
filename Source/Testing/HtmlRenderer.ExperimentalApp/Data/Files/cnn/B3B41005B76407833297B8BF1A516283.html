    Centennial Park District

    Downtown Atlanta has so many options when it comes to things to do, where to stay and what to eat, but don’t fret! The Centennial Park District is a vibrant, active place to be during your stay and they have put together everything you need to plan your trip to downtown Atlanta on the Centennial Park District website.

    To further enhance your visit, the Atlanta Convention and Visitors Bureau has gathered all things Atlanta in one helpful and convenient location at atlanta.net . They provide an events calendar, top things to do in Atlanta, hotel packages and more! To discover Atlanta and plan your visit, the ACVB will be able to help with every detail.
